title: "The Thavnairian Coast"

---

**The Thavnairian Coast** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Thavnairian Coast.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Thavnairian Goby
- Qeyiq Sole
- Fate's Design
- Shadowdart Sardine
- Paksa Fish
- Chalky Coral
- Jhinga
- Durdina Fish
