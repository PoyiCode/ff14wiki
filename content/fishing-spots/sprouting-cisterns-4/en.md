title: "Sprouting Cisterns"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sprouting Cisterns** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sprouting Cisterns.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Topiary Gibal
- Fishform: Nutrigrowth
- Drifting Dragon
- Auroral Petalfish
- Fishform: Aqualevel
- Topiary Serpent
