---

**Iq Br'aax Reservoir** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Iq Br'aax Reservoir.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Shimmering Shadow
- Ut'ohmu Dawnfish
- Iq Br'aax Sailfin
- Hunmu Ob
- Iq Ebaji
- Deep Canopy
