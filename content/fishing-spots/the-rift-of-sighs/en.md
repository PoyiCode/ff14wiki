title: "The Rift of Sighs"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Rift of Sighs** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Rift of Sighs.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Eighteyes Eel
- Hard Candy
- Abyssal Snail
- Water Ball
- Albino Caiman
- Sweetheart
