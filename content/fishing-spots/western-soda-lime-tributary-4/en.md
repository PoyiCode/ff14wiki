**Western Soda-lime Tributary** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Western Soda-lime Tributary.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- White Starburst
- Skippingway
- Glass Coral
- Shallnot Shell
- Full-blown Bubble
