title: "Sirensong Spectral Current"

---

**Sirensong Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sirensong Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Mermaid Scale
- Broadhead
- Vivid Pink Shrimp
- Sunken Coelacanth
- Siren's Sigh
- Black-jawed Helicoprion
- Impostopus
- Jade Mantis Shrimp
- Nymeia's Wheel
- Taniwha
