title: "Raincatcher Gully"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Raincatcher Gully** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Raincatcher Gully.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Copperfish
- Lamprey
- Mitten Crab
- Giant Bass
- Discus
- Loyal Pleco
- Thunderbolt Sculpin
- Archerfish
- Agelyss Carp
- The Terpsichorean
