---
title: "Sylphlands"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sylphlands** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sylphlands.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Giant Bass
- Velodyna Carp
- Oakroot
- Common Sculpin
- Southern Pike
- Mushroom Crab
- Magicked Mushroom
