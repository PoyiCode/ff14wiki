---

**Ruby Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Ruby Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Fleeting Squid
- Bowbarb Lobster
- Pitch Pickle
- Senbei Octopus
- Tentacle Thresher
- Bekko Rockhugger
- Yellow Iris
- Crimson Sentry
- Flying Squid
- Hells' Claw
