title: "Southern Diadem Lake"

---

**Southern Diadem Lake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Southern Diadem Lake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Zagas Khaal
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Goldsmith Crab
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Common Bitterling
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Skyloach
