---
title: "Bitterbark Cenote"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Bitterbark Cenote** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Bitterbark Cenote.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Mudfish
- Yak T'el Catfish
- Blind Brotula
- Yak T'el Caiman
- Cenote Oyster
- Bitterbark Caiman
