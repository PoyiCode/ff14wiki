title: "Sweetbloom Pier"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sweetbloom Pier** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sweetbloom Pier.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Crayfish
- Striped Goby
- Pipira
- Maiden Carp
- River Crab
- Dark Sleeper
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Dark Ambusher
