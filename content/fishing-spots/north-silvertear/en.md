---
title: "North Silvertear"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**North Silvertear** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: North Silvertear.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lamp Marimo
- Silverfish
- Ignus Snail
- Sludgeskipper
- Assassin Betta
- Ilsabardian Bass
- Goldfish
- Heliobatis
- Aetherlouse
