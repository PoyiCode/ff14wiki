---
title: "Isles of Umbra Northshore"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Isles of Umbra Northshore** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Isles of Umbra Northshore.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Ocean Cloud
- Fullmoon Sardine
- Lavender Remora
- Red Coral
- Silver Shark
- Wahoo
- Pike Eel
- Sunfish
- Helmsman's Hand
