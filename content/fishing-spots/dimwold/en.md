---
title: "Dimwold"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Dimwold** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Dimwold.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Miounnefish
- Bone Melter
- Fallen Leaf
- Padjali Loach
- Cave Killifish
- Wraithfish
- Chakrafish
- Sapphire Fan
