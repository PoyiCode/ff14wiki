---
title: "The Flounders' Floor"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Flounders' Floor** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Flounders' Floor.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Stormfish
- Deep Purple Coral
- Caliban
- Bubble Angler
- Ondo Harpoon
- Ondo Sigh
