---
title: "Parata's Peace"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Parata's Peace** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Parata's Peace.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grip Killifish
- Silverfish
- Ignus Snail
- Sludgeskipper
- Boltfish
- Ilsabardian Bass
- Vampire Lampern
- Bat-o'-Nine-Tails
