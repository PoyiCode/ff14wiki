---
title: "Kugane Spectral Current"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Kugane Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Kugane Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Snapping Koban
- Silkweft Prawn
- Stingfin Trevally
- Swordtip Squid
- Mailfish
- Idaten's Bolt
- Maelstrom Turban
- Shoshitsuki
- Spadefish
- Glass Dragon
