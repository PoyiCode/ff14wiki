---
title: "Costa del Sol"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Costa del Sol** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Costa del Sol.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Angelfish
- Blowfish
- Bianaq Bream
- Indigo Herring
- Ash Tuna
- Fullmoon Sardine
- Lavender Remora
- Silver Shark
- Little Thalaos
- Shark Tuna
