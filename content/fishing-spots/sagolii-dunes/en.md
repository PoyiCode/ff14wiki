title: "Sagolii Dunes"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sagolii Dunes** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sagolii Dunes.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sand Bream
- Desert Catfish
- Dustfish
- Storm Rider
- Antlion Slug
- Dune Manta
- Caravan Eel
- Olgoi-Khorkhoi
