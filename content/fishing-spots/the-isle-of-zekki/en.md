---
title: "The Isle of Zekki"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Isle of Zekki** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Isle of Zekki.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ruby Shrimp
- Daio Squid
- Koromo Octopus
- Zekki Grouper
- Zekki Gator
- Rakshasa
