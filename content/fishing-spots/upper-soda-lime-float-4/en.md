**Upper Soda-lime Float** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Upper Soda-lime Float.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Frosted Shell
- Untitled Work No. 98
- Pendantfish
- Untitled Work No. 432
- Sodiment Licker
- Soda-lime Streamer
