---
title: "Niikwerepi"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Niikwerepi** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Niikwerepi.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bonytail Chub
- Niikwerepi Trout
- Pyariyoanaan Cichlid
- Shaaloani Salmon
- Hwittayoanaan Cichlid
