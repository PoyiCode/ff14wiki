---
title: "Palus Arsenici"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Palus Arsenici** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Palus Arsenici.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lunar Scorpion
- Moonwhip
- Polypus Arsenici
- Arsenic Flower
- Arsenic Salamander
- Eyeballingway
