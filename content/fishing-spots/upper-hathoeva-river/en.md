title: "Upper Hathoeva River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Upper Hathoeva River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Upper Hathoeva River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Rainbow Trout
- River Crab
- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Faerie Bass
- Dark Sleeper
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Black Eel
- Velodyna Carp
- Vip Viper
