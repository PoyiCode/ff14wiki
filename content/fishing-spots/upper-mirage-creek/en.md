title: "Upper Mirage Creek"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Upper Mirage Creek** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Upper Mirage Creek.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Abalathian Bitterling
- Broken Crab
- Miounnefish
- Gyr Abanian Trout
- Bloodsipper
- Hookstealer
