title: "Upper Black Tea Brook"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Upper Black Tea Brook** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Upper Black Tea Brook.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crayfish
- Pipira
- Gudgeon
- Maiden Carp
- Moat Carp
- Carp Diem
