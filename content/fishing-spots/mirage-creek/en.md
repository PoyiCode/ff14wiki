title: "Mirage Creek"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Mirage Creek** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Mirage Creek.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Highland Perch
- Mirage Chub
- Gravel Gudgeon
- Rhalgr's Bolt
- Mirage Mahi
- Scarlet Frog
- Corpse Chub
