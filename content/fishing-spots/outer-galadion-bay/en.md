---
title: "Outer Galadion Bay"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Outer Galadion Bay** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Outer Galadion Bay.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Galadion Goby
- Galadion Chovy
- Leopard Eel
- Jasperhead
- Tarnished Shark
- Rosy Bream
- Cyan Octopus
- Heavenswimmer
- Spectral Megalodon
- Drunkfish
