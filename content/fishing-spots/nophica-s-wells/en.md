---
title: "Nophica's Wells"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Nophica's Wells** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Nophica's Wells.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Crayfish
- Dusk Goby
- Gudgeon
- Brass Loach
- Maiden Carp
- Copperfish
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Glimmerscale
