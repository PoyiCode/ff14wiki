---
title: "Prism Lake"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Prism Lake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Prism Lake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Yellow Prismfish
- Blue Prismfish
- Violet Prismfish
- Red Prismfish
- White Goldfish
- Firelight Goldfish
- Indigo Prismfish
- Green Prismfish
- Warden of the Seven Hues
