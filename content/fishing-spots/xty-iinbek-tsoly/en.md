---
title: "Xty'iinbek Tsoly"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Xty'iinbek Tsoly** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Xty'iinbek Tsoly.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ut'ohmu Tiika
- Sharknose Goby
- Yak T'el Crab
- Xty'iinbek Sleeper
- Chain Shark
- Stardust Sleeper
