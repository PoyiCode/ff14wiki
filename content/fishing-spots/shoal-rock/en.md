---
title: "Shoal Rock"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Shoal Rock** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Shoal Rock.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grinning Anchovy
- Ruby Coral
- Sapphire Coral
- Bone Coral
- Butterfly Fish
- Harutsuge
- Ruby Laver
- The Winter Queen
