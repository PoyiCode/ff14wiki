---
title: "Crescent Cove"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Crescent Cove** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Crescent Cove.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lominsan Anchovy
- Razor Clam
- Blowfish
- Saber Sardine
- Monkfish
- Spotted Puffer
- Rock Lobster
- Fingers
