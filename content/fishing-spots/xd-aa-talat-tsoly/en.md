---
title: "Xd'aa Talat Tsoly"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Xd'aa Talat Tsoly** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Xd'aa Talat Tsoly.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Shimmering Shadow
- Ut'ohmu Dawnfish
- Belonesox
- Xd'aa Talat Cichlid
- Ilyon Asoh Cichlid
