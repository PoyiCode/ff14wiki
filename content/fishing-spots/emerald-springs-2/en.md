---
title: "Emerald Springs"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Emerald Springs** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Emerald Springs.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Mossy Globule
- Greatfrond Turtle
- Towerling Stemling
- Crenicichla Cosmica
- Timberland Camofin
