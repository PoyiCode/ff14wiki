---
title: "Quickspill Delta"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Quickspill Delta** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Quickspill Delta.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Blueclaw Shrimp
- Bullfrog
- Hedgemole Cricket
- Yalm Lobster
- Orn Butterfly
- Tigerfish
- Gnomefish
- Dravanian Scallop
- Madam Butterfly
