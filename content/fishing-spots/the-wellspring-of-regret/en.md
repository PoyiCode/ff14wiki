**The Wellspring of Regret** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Wellspring of Regret.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Protoflesh
- Katoptron
- Comet Tail
- Aoide
- Starscryer
