---
title: "Crackling Canyons"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Crackling Canyons** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Crackling Canyons.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Electrobetta
- Thundering Redbelly
- Yyasulani Bowfin
- Crackling Flounder
- Thunderous Flounder
- Goldentail
