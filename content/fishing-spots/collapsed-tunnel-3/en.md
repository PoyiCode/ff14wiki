title: "Collapsed Tunnel"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Collapsed Tunnel** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Collapsed Tunnel.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Upside-down Squeaker
- Upside-down Minnow
- Inverted Tilapia
- Oizyshrimp
- Oizys Bullion
