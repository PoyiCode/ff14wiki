title: "Glimmerpond Beta"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Glimmerpond Beta** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Glimmerpond Beta.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lunar Goldfish
- Lunar Minnow
- Gleamingray
- Lunar Butterfly
- Lunar Seagrapes
- Fishingway
