---
title: "The Flagship"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Flagship** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Flagship.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Platinum Fish
- Azysfish
- Oil Eel
- Jeweled Jellyfish
- Barreleye
- Functional Proto-hropken
- Allagan Bladeshark
- Opabinia
