**High Tide Harbor** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: High Tide Harbor.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Reasonscale Silverside
- Floating Fife
- Blue Purse
- Goldfin Cavalli
- King Puffer
- Harbor Cloud
- Purse of Riches
