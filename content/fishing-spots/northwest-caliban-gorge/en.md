title: "Northwest Caliban Gorge"

---

**Northwest Caliban Gorge** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Northwest Caliban Gorge.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Stormfish
- Sycorax
- Caliban
- Hoodwinker
- Seatrap
- Sweetmeat Mussel
- Mora Tecta
- Lancetfish
