title: "Thaliak River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Thaliak River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Thaliak River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Hedgemole Cricket
- Sweetfish
- Philosopher's Stone
- Shipworm
- Autumn Leaf
- Illuminati Perch
- Hinterlands Perch
- Thinker's Coral
- The Ewer King
