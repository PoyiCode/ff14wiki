title: "Opalescent Crossing"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Opalescent Crossing** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Opalescent Crossing.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Pearl Shell
- Cobalt Bijou
- Selenium Herring
- Codmonaut
- Opalite Impesctor
