---
title: "Reaver Hide"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Reaver Hide** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Reaver Hide.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lominsan Anchovy
- Merlthor Goby
- Red Coral
- Wahoo
- Blacklip Oyster
- Rock Lobster
- Dinichthys
- Seahag
