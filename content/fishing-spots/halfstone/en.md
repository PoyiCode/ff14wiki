---
title: "Halfstone"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Halfstone** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Halfstone.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Saber Sardine
- Blue Coral
- Hammerhead Shark
- Sea Pickle
- Whitelip Oyster
- Lavender Remora
- Balloonfish
- The Lone Ripper
