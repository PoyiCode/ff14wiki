---
title: "The Burning Wall"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Burning Wall** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Burning Wall.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Yugr'am Salmon
- Bonytongue
- Discus
- Ignus Snail
- Loyal Pleco
- Knifefish
- Southern Pike
- Gigantpole
- Sundisc
- The Thousand-year Itch
