---
title: "Lower Yat Khaal"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Lower Yat Khaal** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Lower Yat Khaal.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Othardian Trout
- Steppe Skipper
- Dawn Crayfish
- Yat Goby
- Jade Sculpin
- Fragrant Sweetfish
- Yat Khan
