title: "The Moros Well"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Moros Well** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Moros Well.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Moros Scorpion
- Moros Crab
- Inverted Hornhelm
- Stygian Pseudomander
- Ironsand Lizard
- Oddweight Ironsand Lizard
