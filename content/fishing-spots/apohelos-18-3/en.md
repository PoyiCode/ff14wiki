---

**Apohelos 18-α** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Apohelos 18-α.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Superstring
- Dragonscale
- Star Eater
- Ypupîara
- Eehs Forhnesh
- Stargilt Lobster
- Eehs Fan
