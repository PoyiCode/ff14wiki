title: "Swirling Cloudtop"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Swirling Cloudtop** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Swirling Cloudtop.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Scorpionfly
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Whiteloom
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Blue Medusa
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Mosasaur
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Wyvern
