---
title: "The Hundred Throes"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Hundred Throes** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Hundred Throes.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Whilom Catfish
- Pipira Pira
- Blueclaw Shrimp
- Grass Carp
- Shadowhisker
- Dravanian Smelt
- River Shrimp
- Scaleripper
