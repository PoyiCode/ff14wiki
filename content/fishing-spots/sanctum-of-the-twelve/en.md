---
title: "Sanctum of the Twelve"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sanctum of the Twelve** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sanctum of the Twelve.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Crayfish
- River Crab
- Acorn Snail
- Moat Carp
- Tricolored Carp
- Pond Mussel
- Dark Bass
- The Assassin
