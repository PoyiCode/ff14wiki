title: "The Eblan Thaw"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Eblan Thaw** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Eblan Thaw.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Garlean Clam
- Animulus
- Eblan Trout
- Cerule Core
- Icepike
- Rimepike
- Snowy Parexus
