---
title: "The For'ard Cabins"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The For'ard Cabins** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The For'ard Cabins.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Reasonscale Silverside
- Stardust Shrimp
- Shallows Sot
- Ghostfish
- Permit
- Inktopus
- Cabinkeep Permit
