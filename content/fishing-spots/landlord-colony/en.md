---
title: "Landlord Colony"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Landlord Colony** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Landlord Colony.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sky Faerie
- Gloaming Coral
- Cloud Rider
- Blue Medusa
- Letter Puffer
- Dragon's Soul
- Sunsail
- The Soul of the Martyr
