title: "Rhalgr's Reach"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Rhalgr's Reach** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Rhalgr's Reach.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Golden Cichlid
- Temple Carp
- Gyr Abanian Trout
- Monk Betta
- Electric Catfish
- Pagan Pirarucu
- Watcher Catfish
