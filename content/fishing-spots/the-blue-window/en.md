---
title: "The Blue Window"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Blue Window** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Blue Window.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Buoyant Oviform
- Sky Faerie
- Whiteloom
- Storm Core
- Winged Gurnard
- Sky Sweeper
- Tornado Shark
- Cloudweed
- The Second One
