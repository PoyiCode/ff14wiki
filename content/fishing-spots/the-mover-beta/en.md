title: "The Mover Beta"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Mover Beta** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Mover Beta.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Butterfly Fry
- Macrobrachium Lar
- Xenocypris
- Blowgun
- The Fury's Aegis
- Darksteel Knifefish
- Onyx Knifefish
