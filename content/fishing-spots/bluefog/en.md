---
title: "Bluefog"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Bluefog** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Bluefog.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Crayfish
- Silverfish
- Giant Bass
- Golden Loach
- Ignus Snail
- Knifefish
- Common Sculpin
- Garpike
- Alligator Garfish
- Hannibal
