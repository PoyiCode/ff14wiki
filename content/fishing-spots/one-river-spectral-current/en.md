---
title: "One River Spectral Current"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**One River Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: One River Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Gensui Shrimp
- Yato-no-kami
- Heron's Eel
- Crowshadow Mussel
- Yanxian Goby
- Iridescent Trout
- Un-Namazu
- Gakugyo
- Ginrin Goshiki
- Jewel of Plum Spring
