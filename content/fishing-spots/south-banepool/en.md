---
title: "South Banepool"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**South Banepool** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: South Banepool.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Abalathian Smelt
- Icepick
- Ice Faerie
- Lake Urchin
- Ammonite
- Seema Patrician
- Fanged Clam
- Mountain Kraken
- Magic Bucket
- Captain Nemo
