---
title: "Delta Quadrant"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Delta Quadrant** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Delta Quadrant.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Fountfish
- Rockclimber
- Aether Eye
- Yalm Lobster
- Euphotic Pirarucu
- Pickled Pom
- Aphotic Pirarucu
