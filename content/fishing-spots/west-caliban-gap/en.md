---
title: "West Caliban Gap"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**West Caliban Gap** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: West Caliban Gap.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Stormfish
- Sycorax
- Ancient Shrimp
- Yeti Crab
- Aapoak
- Gonzalo's Grace
- Maru Crab
