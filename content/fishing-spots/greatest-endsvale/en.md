---
title: "Greatest Endsvale"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Greatest Endsvale** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Greatest Endsvale.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Star-blue Guppy
- Lunar Cichlid
- Teareye
- Replipirarucu
- Ossicula
- Disappirarucu
