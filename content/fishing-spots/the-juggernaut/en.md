---
title: "The Juggernaut"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Juggernaut** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Juggernaut.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Copperfish
- Mudcrab
- Lamprey
- Grip Killifish
- Silverfish
- Sludgeskipper
- Vampire Lampern
- Mirrorscale
