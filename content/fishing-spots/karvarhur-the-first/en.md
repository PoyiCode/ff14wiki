title: "Karvarhur the First"

---

**Karvarhur the First** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Karvarhur the First.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- First Feastfish
- Urqofrog
- Zeh Tortoh
- Morsel Trout
- Deepwarden
- Qor Pirarucu
- Prime Adjudicator
