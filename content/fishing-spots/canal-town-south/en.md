title: "Canal Town South"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Canal Town South** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Canal Town South.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Canal Bream
- Iris Wrasse
- Canal Drum
- Butterfly Ray
- Hydro Louvar
- Gondola Louvar
