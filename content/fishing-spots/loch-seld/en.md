---

**Loch Seld** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Loch Seld.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Rock Saltfish
- Saltshield Snapper
- Whitehorse
- Pearl-eye
- Sculptor
- Saltmill
- Diamond-eye
- Stethacanthus
