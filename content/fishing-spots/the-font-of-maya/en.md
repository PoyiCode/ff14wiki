---
title: "The Font of Maya"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Font of Maya** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Font of Maya.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Green Swordtail
- Radzbalik
- Handy Hamsa
- Flowerhorn
- Thavnairian Caiman
- Mayashell
- Mayaman
