**Father Collard's Failings** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Father Collard's Failings.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grey Skipper
- Pixie Fish
- Official Ball
- Toadhead
- Celestial
