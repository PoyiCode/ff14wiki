---
title: "Bloodbrine Spectral Current"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Bloodbrine Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Bloodbrine Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Oracular Crab
- Dravanian Bream
- Skaldminni
- Serrated Clam
- Beatific Vision
- Exterminator
- Gory Tuna
- Ticinepomis
- Quartz Hammerhead
- Seafaring Toad
