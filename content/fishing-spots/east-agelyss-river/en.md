---
title: "East Agelyss River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**East Agelyss River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: East Agelyss River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Copperfish
- Lamprey
- Monke Onke
- Mitten Crab
- Discus
- Loyal Pleco
- Archerfish
- Agelyss Carp
- The Drowned Sniper
