title: "Moraby Bay"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Moraby Bay** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Moraby Bay.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Malm Kelp
- Ocean Cloud
- Sea Cucumber
- Vongola Clam
- Moraby Flounder
- Pebble Crab
- Blue Octopus
- Oschon's Print
