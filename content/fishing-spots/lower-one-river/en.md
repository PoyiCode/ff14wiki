**Lower One River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Lower One River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Catching Carp
- Garlean Bluegill
- Yanxian Softshell
- Princess Salmon
- Calligraph
- Singular Shrimp
- Brocade Carp
- Yanxian Sturgeon
- Spectral Kotsu Zetsu
- Fishy Shark
