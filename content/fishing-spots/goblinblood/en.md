---
title: "Goblinblood"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Goblinblood** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Goblinblood.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Mudcrab
- Dark Bass
- Five-ilm Pleco
- Black Ghost
- Yugr'am Salmon
- Bonytongue
- Silverfish
- Goblin Perch
- The Gobfather
