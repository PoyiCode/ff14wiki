**Lower Soda-lime Float** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Lower Soda-lime Float.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Kissing Counterfeit
- Float Cloud
- Navy Coelacanth
- Untitled Work No. 345
- Royal Comet Tail
- Grim Impesctor
