---
title: "Ceruleum Field"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Ceruleum Field** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Ceruleum Field.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grip Killifish
- Silverfish
- Ignus Snail
- Sludgeskipper
- Boltfish
- Ilsabardian Bass
- Goldfish
- Wootz Knifefish
- Wootz Knifefish Zenith
