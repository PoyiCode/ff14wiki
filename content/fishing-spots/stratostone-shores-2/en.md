title: "Stratostone Shores"

---

**Stratostone Shores** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Stratostone Shores.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Skylit Petal
- Indigo Pulpscale
- Sylvan Core
- Fishform: Aquameter
- Bluebloom
- Crabby Indicolite
