---
title: "Hopeseed Pond"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Hopeseed Pond** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Hopeseed Pond.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Mudcrab
- Black Eel
- Black Ghost
- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Mudskipper
- Clown Loach
- Seema
- The Green Jester
