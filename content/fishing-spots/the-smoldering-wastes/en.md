---
title: "The Smoldering Wastes"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Smoldering Wastes** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Smoldering Wastes.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Whilom Catfish
- Rock Mussel
- Blueclaw Shrimp
- Three-lip Carp
- Warmwater Bichir
- Marble Oscar
- The Dreamweaver
