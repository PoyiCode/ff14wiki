---

**North Isle of Endless Summer** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: North Isle of Endless Summer.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Blowfish
- Leafy Seadragon
- Wahoo
- Little Thalaos
- Shall Shell
- Megalodon
- Giant Squid
- Castaway Chocobo Chick
- Little Perykos
