---

**Lower Soot Creek** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Lower Soot Creek.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Crayfish
- Dusk Goby
- Brass Loach
- Maiden Carp
- Dark Bass
- Aegis Shrimp
- Slime King
