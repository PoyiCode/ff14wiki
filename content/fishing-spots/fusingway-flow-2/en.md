**Fusingway Flow** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Fusingway Flow.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Shearclaw
- Bellows Crab
- Crimplouse
- Pipetongue
- Garnet Seahorse
- Vent Impesctor
