---

**Southern Diadem Lake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Southern Diadem Lake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Thunderbolt Sculpin
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Alligator Snapping Turtle
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Bonytongue
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Hermit Goby
