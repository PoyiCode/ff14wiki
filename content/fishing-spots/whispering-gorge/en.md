---
title: "Whispering Gorge"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Whispering Gorge** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Whispering Gorge.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crayfish
- Pipira
- Gudgeon
- Maiden Carp
- Moat Carp
- Raincaller
- Matron Carp
