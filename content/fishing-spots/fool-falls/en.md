title: "Fool Falls"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Fool Falls** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Fool Falls.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crimson Crayfish
- River Crab
- Copperfish
- Bluebell Salmon
- Jade Eel
- Warmwater Trout
- Four-eyed Fish
- Dark Bass
- Fall Jumper
- Joan of Trout
