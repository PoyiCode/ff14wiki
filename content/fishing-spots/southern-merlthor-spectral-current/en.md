**Southern Merlthor Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Southern Merlthor Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sea Nettle
- Great Grandmarlin
- Shipwreck's Sail
- Charlatan Survivor
- Hi-aetherlouse
- Azeyma's Sleeve
- Aetheric Seadragon
- Mythril Sovereign
- Roguesaurus
- Coral Manta
