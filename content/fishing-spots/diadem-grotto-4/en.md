title: "Diadem Grotto"

---

**Diadem Grotto** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Diadem Grotto.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Starflower
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Cyan Crab
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Fickle Krait
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Proto-hropken
