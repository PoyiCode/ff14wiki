---
title: "Dragonhead Latrines"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Dragonhead Latrines** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Dragonhead Latrines.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Striped Goby
- Rainbow Trout
- Abalathian Smelt
- Seema
- Crimson Trout
- Kissing Trout
- Dawn Maiden
