title: "South Bloodshore"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**South Bloodshore** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: South Bloodshore.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Coral Butterfly
- Blue Octopus
- Blue Coral
- Sea Bo
- Bianaq Bream
- Fullmoon Sardine
- Silver Shark
- Gigant Clam
- The Greatest Bream in the World
