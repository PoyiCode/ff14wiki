title: "The Source"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Source** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Source.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Hard Candy
- Clean Saucer
- Rabbit Skipper
- Xanthic Bass
- Albino Rock Crab
- Laxan Inkhorn
- Platinum Seahorse
- The Unforgiven
