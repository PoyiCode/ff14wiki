---
title: "Red Mantis Falls"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Red Mantis Falls** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Red Mantis Falls.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Mitten Crab
- Silverfish
- Clown Loach
- Discus
- Knifefish
- Agelyss Carp
- Goldfish
- Jungle Catfish
- Imperial Goldfish
