---
title: "Vesper Bay"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Vesper Bay** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Vesper Bay.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lominsan Anchovy
- Harbor Herring
- Pebble Crab
- Tiger Cod
- Rothlyt Oyster
- Blue Octopus
- Rivet Oyster
