**Hyperstellar Downconverter** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Hyperstellar Downconverter.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Platinum Fish
- Azysfish
- Oil Eel
- Mucous Minnow
- Oliphant's Trunk
- Ceti
