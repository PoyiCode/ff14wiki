---
title: "The Mourning Widow"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Mourning Widow** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Mourning Widow.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crayfish
- Chub
- Dwarf Catfish
- Princess Trout
- Dusk Goby
- Gudgeon
- Maiden Carp
- Caterwauler
