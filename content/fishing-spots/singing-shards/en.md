---
title: "Singing Shards"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Singing Shards** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Singing Shards.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Mudcrab
- Lamp Marimo
- Bonytongue
- Silverfish
- Giant Bass
- Knifefish
- Garpike
- Ilsabardian Bass
- Blood Red Bonytongue
