title: "The Derelicts"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Derelicts** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Derelicts.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Milky Coral
- Gravel Mussel
- Bonefish
- Crimson Sea Spider
- Eulmore Butterfly
- Red Hammerhead
- Winged Dame
