---
title: "Bronze Lake Shallows"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Bronze Lake Shallows** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Bronze Lake Shallows.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crimson Crayfish
- River Crab
- Faerie Bass
- Jade Eel
- Pond Mussel
- Worm of Nym
