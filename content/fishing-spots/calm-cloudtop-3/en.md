title: "Calm Cloudtop"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Calm Cloudtop** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Calm Cloudtop.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Scorpionfly
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Whiteloom
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' King's Mantle
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Blind Manta
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Cloudshark
