title: "South Drybone"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**South Drybone** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: South Drybone.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Dusk Goby
- Mudskipper
- Faerie Bass
- Acorn Snail
- Mudcrab
- Bone Cleaner
- Mud Pilgrim
