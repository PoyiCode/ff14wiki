title: "Westbound Zorgor"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Westbound Zorgor** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Westbound Zorgor.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Zorgor Core
- Cloudribbon
- Zorgor Scorpion
- Vagrant Cascade
- Zorgor Condor
- Sunray Ray
- Yowekwa Inkhead
- Vagrant Keeper
