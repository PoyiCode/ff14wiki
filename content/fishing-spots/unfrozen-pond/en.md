title: "Unfrozen Pond"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Unfrozen Pond** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Unfrozen Pond.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Abalathian Smelt
- Icepick
- Ice Faerie
- Sorcerer Fish
- Albino Octopus
- Coerthan Clione
- Flarefish
