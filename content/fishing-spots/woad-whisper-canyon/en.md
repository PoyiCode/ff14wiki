---
title: "Woad Whisper Canyon"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Woad Whisper Canyon** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Woad Whisper Canyon.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Dwarf Catfish
- Dusk Goby
- River Crab
- La Noscean Perch
- Copperfish
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Glass Perch
- Crystal Perch
