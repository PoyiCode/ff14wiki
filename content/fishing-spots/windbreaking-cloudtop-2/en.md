**Windbreaking Cloudtop** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Windbreaking Cloudtop.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Ghost Faerie
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Ashfish
- Grade 2 Artisanal Skybuilders' Rhomaleosaurus
- Grade 2 Artisanal Skybuilders' Gobbie Mask
- Grade 2 Artisanal Skybuilders' Cometfish
- Grade 2 Artisanal Skybuilders' Anomalocaris
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Oscar
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Blind Manta
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Archaeopteryx
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Cloudshark
