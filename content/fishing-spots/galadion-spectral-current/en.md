title: "Galadion Spectral Current"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Galadion Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Galadion Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merman's Mane
- Heavenskey
- Ghost Shark
- Fishmonger
- Navigator's Print
- Casket Oyster
- Quicksilver Blade
- Funnel Shark
- Nimble Dancer
- Sothis
