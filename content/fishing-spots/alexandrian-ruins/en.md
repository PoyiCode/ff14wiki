---
title: "Alexandrian Ruins"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Alexandrian Ruins** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Alexandrian Ruins.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Electrobetta
- Thundering Redbelly
- Yyasulani Bowfin
- Goldgrouper
- Heirloom Goldgrouper
