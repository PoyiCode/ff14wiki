---
title: "The Deep Tangle"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Deep Tangle** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Deep Tangle.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Dark Bass
- Mitten Crab
- Giant Bass
- Ignus Snail
- Knifefish
- Boxing Pleco
- Monke Onke
- Ignus Horn
