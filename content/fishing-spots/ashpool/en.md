title: "Ashpool"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Ashpool** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Ashpool.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Abalathian Smelt
- Ice Faerie
- Fanged Clam
- Lake Urchin
- Gigant Grouper
- Battle Galley
- Northern Oyster
- La Reale
