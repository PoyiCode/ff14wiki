---
title: "North Bloodshore"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**North Bloodshore** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: North Bloodshore.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Harbor Herring
- Ogre Barracuda
- Black Sole
- Sea Pickle
- Ash Tuna
- Whitelip Oyster
- Balloonfish
- Red Coral
- Mazlaya Marlin
- Bombardfish
