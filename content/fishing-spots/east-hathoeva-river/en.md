title: "East Hathoeva River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**East Hathoeva River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: East Hathoeva River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Rainbow Trout
- Faerie Bass
- Dark Sleeper
- Copperfish
- Mudcrab
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Black Eel
- Velodyna Carp
- The Grinner
