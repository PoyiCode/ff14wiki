title: "Miyakabek'zoma"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Miyakabek'zoma** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Miyakabek'zoma.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ligaka Guppy
- Dumplingfish
- Banded Candiru
- Oxydoras
- Honeycomb Sponge
- Iron Oxydoras
