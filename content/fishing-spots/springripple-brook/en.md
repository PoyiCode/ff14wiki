---
title: "Springripple Brook"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Springripple Brook** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Springripple Brook.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Brass Loach
- Maiden Carp
- Rainbow Trout
- River Crab
- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Bluebell Salmon
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Moldva
