**Sylvan Soaking Pool** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sylvan Soaking Pool.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Fishform: Alluvimix
- Soaking Betta
- Fishform: Alluvimark
- Indicoresin Trahira
- Turquoise Vine
