---
title: "Rathefrost"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Rathefrost** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Rathefrost.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lamp Marimo
- Silverfish
- Giant Bass
- Ignus Snail
- Knifefish
- Assassin Betta
- Ilsabardian Bass
- Goldfish
- Poxpike
- Void Bass
