**Western Nervure** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Western Nervure.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Perchsplorer
- Nervure Driftwood
- Fishform: Aquacycle
- Ripe Citrusfish
- Fishform: Vegiclean
- Topiary Carp
