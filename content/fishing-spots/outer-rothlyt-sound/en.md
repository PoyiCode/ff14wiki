title: "Outer Rothlyt Sound"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Outer Rothlyt Sound** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Outer Rothlyt Sound.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crow Puffer
- Rothlyt Kelp
- Living Lantern
- Honeycomb Fish
- Godsbed
- Lansquenet
- Thavnairian Shark
- Nephrite Eel
- Spectresaur
- Ginkgo Fin
