---
title: "Proud Creek"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Proud Creek** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Proud Creek.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Pond Mussel
- Mitten Crab
- Seema
- Golden Loach
- Loyal Pleco
- Oakroot
- Southern Pike
- Pirarucu
- Shadowstreak
