title: "Residential Sector"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Residential Sector** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Residential Sector.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Everkeep Yabby
- Stickleback
- Custodian Carp
- Mosaic Loach
- Pixel Loach
