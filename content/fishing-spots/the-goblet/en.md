---
title: "The Goblet"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Goblet** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Goblet.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Brass Loach
- Faerie Bass
- Copperfish
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Dark Bass
- Lamprey
- Silverfish
- Goldfish
- Spearnose
