---
title: "The Iron Feast"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Iron Feast** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Iron Feast.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Pipira Pira
- Blueclaw Shrimp
- Dragonhead
- Caiman
- Thunderbolt Eel
- Amber Salamander
- Thunderscale
