---
title: "Yugr'am River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Yugr'am River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Yugr'am River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Warmwater Trout
- Black Eel
- Dark Bass
- Yugr'am Salmon
- Mitten Crab
- Trader Eel
- The Warden's Wand
