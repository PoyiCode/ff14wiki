title: "Saint Fathric's Temple"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Saint Fathric's Temple** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Saint Fathric's Temple.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grey Skipper
- Cerulean Loach
- Blood-eyed Frog
- Golden Lobster
- Little Bounty
- Saint Fathric's Face
- Gold Hammer
