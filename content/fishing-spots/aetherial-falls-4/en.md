**Aetherial Falls** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Aetherial Falls.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ctenophora Lunaris
- Protomyke #721
- Argonauta Lunaris
- Aetherial Sword
- Macropinna
- Deepmoon Seadragon
