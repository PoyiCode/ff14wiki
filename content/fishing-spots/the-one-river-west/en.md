**The One River (West)** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The One River (West).

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Longhair Catfish
- Doman Trout
- Kotsu Zetsu
- Seraphim
- Hagoromo Bijin
