title: "The Chlorophospond"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Chlorophospond** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Chlorophospond.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Feverfish
- Calicia
- Protomyke #987
- Lunar Deathworm
- Chlorophos Deathworm
- Lopoceras Elegans
