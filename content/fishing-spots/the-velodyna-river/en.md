title: "The Velodyna River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Velodyna River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Velodyna River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Velodyna Grass Carp
- Greenstream Loach
- Bloodsipper
- Black Velodyna Carp
- Velodyna Salmon
- Downstream Loach
