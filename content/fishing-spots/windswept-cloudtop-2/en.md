title: "Windswept Cloudtop"

---

**Windswept Cloudtop** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Windswept Cloudtop.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Scorpionfly
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Whiteloom
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Gurnard
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Storm Chaser
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Helicoprion
