title: "Lower Black Tea Brook"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Lower Black Tea Brook** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Lower Black Tea Brook.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crayfish
- Pipira
- Gudgeon
- Maiden Carp
- Moat Carp
- Bloody Brewer
