---

**Mu Springs Eternal** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Mu Springs Eternal.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Rosebud Frog
- Knight Goby
- Cleyran Carp
- Giant Snakehead
- Gigagiant Snakehead
