title: "The Long Climb"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Long Climb** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Long Climb.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Giant Bass
- Ignus Snail
- Thunderbolt Sculpin
- Warmwater Trout
- Bronze Lake Trout
- Giant Donko
- Thundergut
