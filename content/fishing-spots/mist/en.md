---
title: "Mist"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Mist** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Mist.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Harbor Herring
- Blue Octopus
- Blowfish
- Bianaq Bream
- Indigo Herring
- Ash Tuna
- Fullmoon Sardine
- Nautilus
- Twitchbeard
