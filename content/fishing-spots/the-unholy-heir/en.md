---
title: "The Unholy Heir"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Unholy Heir** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Unholy Heir.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Crayfish
- Maiden Carp
- Mudskipper
- Mudcrab
- Acorn Snail
- Dark Bass
- Aegis Shrimp
- Dark Knight
