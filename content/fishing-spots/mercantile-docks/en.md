---
title: "Mercantile Docks"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Mercantile Docks** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Mercantile Docks.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Doman Trout
- Doman Grass Carp
- Lordly Salmon
- Kotsu Zetsu
- White Clam
- Suiten Ippeki
