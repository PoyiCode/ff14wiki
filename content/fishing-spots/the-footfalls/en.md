---
title: "The Footfalls"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Footfalls** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Footfalls.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crayfish
- Mudskipper
- Faerie Bass
- Mudcrab
- Acorn Snail
- Copperfish
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Mud Golem
