**West Beaconveil** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: West Beaconveil.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Potash Guppish
- Aurora Shell
- Untitled Work No. 66
- Imitation Engraver
- Isosceles Pearl
