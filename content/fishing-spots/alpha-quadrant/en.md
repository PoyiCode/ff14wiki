---

**Alpha Quadrant** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Alpha Quadrant.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Platinum Fish
- Bullwhip
- Blood Skipper
- High Allagan Helmet
- Allagan Hunter
- Augmented High Allagan Helmet
