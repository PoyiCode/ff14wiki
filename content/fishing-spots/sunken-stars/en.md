title: "Sunken Stars"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sunken Stars** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sunken Stars.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sea Alpaca
- Marbled Hatchetfish
- Starsnipper
- Urqotrout
- Yok Huy Stonecutter
- Moongripper
