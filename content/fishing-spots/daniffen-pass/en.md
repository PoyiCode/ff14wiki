---
title: "Daniffen Pass"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Daniffen Pass** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Daniffen Pass.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Striped Goby
- Blindfish
- Lamp Marimo
- Abalathian Smelt
- Golden Loach
- Cave Cherax
- Charon's Lantern
