title: "Longmirror Lake"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Longmirror Lake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Longmirror Lake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grey Skipper
- Cerulean Loach
- Official Ball
- Rebel
- Wild Red Betta
- Fuchsia Bloom
- The Sound of Fury
- Ealad Skaan
