---

**Hidden Falls** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Hidden Falls.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crimson Crayfish
- Dark Sleeper
- Mitten Crab
- Spotted Puffer
- Discus
- Kobold Puffer
- The Salter
