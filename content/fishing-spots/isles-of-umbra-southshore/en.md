title: "Isles of Umbra Southshore"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Isles of Umbra Southshore** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Isles of Umbra Southshore.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Fullmoon Sardine
- Red Coral
- Blacklip Oyster
- Nautilus
- Mummer Wrasse
- Plaice
- Sea Devil
- Coelacanth
- Coelacanthus
- Endoceras
