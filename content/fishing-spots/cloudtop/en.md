---
title: "Cloudtop"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Cloudtop** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Cloudtop.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Starflower
- Buoyant Oviform
- Mahu Wai
- Mitre Slug
- Blue Cloud Coral
- Fleece Stingray
- Cirrostratus
