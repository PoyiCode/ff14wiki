title: "Glimmerpond Alpha"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Glimmerpond Alpha** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Glimmerpond Alpha.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Arsenic Axolotl
- Sunny Jellyfish
- Universal Darkfin
- Glimmerfish
- Lepopredator
- Soliclymenia
