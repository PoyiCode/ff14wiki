---
title: "Miyakabek'zu"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Miyakabek'zu** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Miyakabek'zu.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Kozama'uka Skipper
- Bopo'u Sleeper
- Candiru
- Trahira
- Driftwood Catfish
- Riverlong Candiru
