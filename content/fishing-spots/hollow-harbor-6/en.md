---

**Hollow Harbor** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Hollow Harbor.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Moonrock Candy
- Lunar Platyfish
- Crenicichla Lunaris
- Fullmoon Ray
- Lunar Yellowfin
- Wayeater
