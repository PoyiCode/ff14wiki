title: "Rootslake"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Rootslake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Rootslake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Crayfish
- Climbing Perch
- Root Skipper
- Mitten Crab
- Armored Pleco
- Judgment Staff
- Son of Levin
