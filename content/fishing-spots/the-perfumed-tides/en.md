---
title: "The Perfumed Tides"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Perfumed Tides** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Perfumed Tides.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Fiery Goby
- Puff-paya
- Narunnairian Octopus
- Roosterfish
- Basilosaurus
- Thavnairian Calamari
- Basilosaurus Rex
- Gharlichthys
