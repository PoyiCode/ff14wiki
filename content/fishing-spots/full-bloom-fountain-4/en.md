**Full Bloom Fountain** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Full Bloom Fountain.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Cosmic Hedgemole Cricket
- Fishform: Aquaflow
- Eriocaulon Auxesicum
- Aquagrapes
- Cosmoss
