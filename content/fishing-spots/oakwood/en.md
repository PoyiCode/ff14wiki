---
title: "Oakwood"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Oakwood** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Oakwood.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- River Crab
- Faerie Bass
- Jade Eel
- Pond Mussel
- Warmwater Trout
- Four-eyed Fish
- Dark Bass
- Common Sculpin
- Coeurlfish
- Toramafish
