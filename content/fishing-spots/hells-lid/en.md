---
title: "Hells' Lid"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Hells' Lid** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Hells' Lid.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grinning Anchovy
- Hellyfish
- Ruby Shrimp
- Blockhead
- Liopleurodon
- Ukiki
- Shrieker
- Wicked Wartfish
- The Vegetarian
