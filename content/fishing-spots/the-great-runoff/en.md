---
title: "The Great Runoff"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Great Runoff** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Great Runoff.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Thavnairian Goby
- Qeyiq Sole
- Garjana Wrasse
- Golden Barramundi
- Sarikuyruk
- Vidyutvat Wrasse
