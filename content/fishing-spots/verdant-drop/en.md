---
title: "Verdant Drop"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Verdant Drop** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Verdant Drop.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Rainbow Trout
- River Crab
- Copperfish
- Black Eel
- Climbing Perch
- Yugr'am Salmon
- Spotted Pleco
- Fall Jumper
- Sylphsbane
