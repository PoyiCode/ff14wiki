**The Prismatic Pull** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Prismatic Pull.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Untitled Work No. 288
- Desertingway
- Ammochronologist
- Polarized Eel
- Quicksand Mantaroid
