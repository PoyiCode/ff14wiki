---
title: "Sagolii Desert"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sagolii Desert** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sagolii Desert.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sandfish
- Sand Bream
- Dustfish
- Storm Rider
- Antlion Slug
- Dune Manta
- Sagolii Monkfish
- Magic Carpet
- Helicoprion
