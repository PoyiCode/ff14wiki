---
title: "Bopo'uihih"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Bopo'uihih** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Bopo'uihih.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Kozama'uka Skipper
- Bopo'u Sleeper
- Poison Dyefrog
- Pleated Matamata
- Uyuy Xage
- Oily Ropefish
- Muttering Matamata
