---
title: "Seagazer Shoals"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Seagazer Shoals** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Seagazer Shoals.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- South Kholusian Cod
- Kholusian Flounder
- Blood Cloud
- Shapeshifter
- Minstrelfish
- Clavekeeper
- Shadeshifter
- Aquamaton
