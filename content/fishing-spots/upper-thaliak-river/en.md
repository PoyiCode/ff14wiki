title: "Upper Thaliak River"

---

**Upper Thaliak River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Upper Thaliak River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Hedgemole Cricket
- Scholar Sculpin
- Sweetfish
- Shipworm
- Yalm Lobster
- Loosetongue
- Armored Catfish
- Thavnairian Leaf
- The Speaker
- Armor Fish
