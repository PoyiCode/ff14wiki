title: "Voor Sian Siran"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Voor Sian Siran** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Voor Sian Siran.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Hedgemole Cricket
- Cloudfish
- Bullfrog
- Maiboi
- Bubble Eye
- Caiman
- Cloud Kelp
- Inkfish
