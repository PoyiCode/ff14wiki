title: "South Mjrl's Regret"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**South Mjrl's Regret** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: South Mjrl's Regret.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Clawbow
- Yellow Pipira
- Rak'tika Trout
- Night's Bass
- Robber Crab
- Silver Kitten
- Recordkiller
