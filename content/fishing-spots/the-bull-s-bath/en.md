---

**The Bull's Bath** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Bull's Bath.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Death Loach
- Invisible Crayfish
- Bull's Bite
- Peeping Pisces
- Meditator
- Alligator Snapping Turtle
- Hemon
