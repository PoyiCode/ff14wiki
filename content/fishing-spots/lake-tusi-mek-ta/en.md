---

**Lake Tusi Mek'ta** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Lake Tusi Mek'ta.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Clawbow
- Yellow Pipira
- Robber Crab
- Clown Tetra
- Eryops
- Spangled Pirarucu
- Priest of Yx'Lokwa
- Greater Serpent of Ronka
