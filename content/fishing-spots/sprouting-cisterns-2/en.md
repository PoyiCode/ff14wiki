title: "Sprouting Cisterns"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sprouting Cisterns** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sprouting Cisterns.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Topiary Gibal
- Botanitrout
- Peachpetal Shell
- Chlorotic Pike
- Riotous Dianthus
- Wisteria Jellyfish
