title: "Burnt Lizard Creek"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Burnt Lizard Creek** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Burnt Lizard Creek.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Copperfish
- Warmwater Trout
- Dark Bass
- Lamprey
- Plaguefish
- Old Hollow Eyes
