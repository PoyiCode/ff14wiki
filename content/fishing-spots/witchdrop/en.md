---
title: "Witchdrop"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Witchdrop** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Witchdrop.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Cloud Jellyfish
- Skyfish
- Cloud Cutter
- Blind Manta
- Rift Sailor
- Saucerfish
- Rhamphorhynchus
- Mahar
- Shonisaurus
