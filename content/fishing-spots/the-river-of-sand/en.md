title: "The River of Sand"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The River of Sand** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The River of Sand.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Shadow Crab
- Desert Dustfish
- Sand Egg
- Hornhelm
- Web-footed Sand Gecko
- The Horned King
- Cinder Surprise
