---
title: "The Outer Fist"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Outer Fist** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Outer Fist.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Comet Minnow
- Balloon Frog
- Lantern Marimo
- Bloodsipper
- Gilfish
- Redtail
- Bloodtail Zombie
