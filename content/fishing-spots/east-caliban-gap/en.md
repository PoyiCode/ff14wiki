title: "East Caliban Gap"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**East Caliban Gap** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: East Caliban Gap.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Stormfish
- Sycorax
- Hoodwinker
- Cyan Sea Devil
- Pancake Octopus
- The Mother of All Pancakes
