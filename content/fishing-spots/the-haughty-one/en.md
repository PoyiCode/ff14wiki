title: "The Haughty One"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Haughty One** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Haughty One.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grey Skipper
- Cerulean Loach
- Official Ball
- Blood-eyed Frog
- Spotted Ctenopoma
- Sunken Tome
