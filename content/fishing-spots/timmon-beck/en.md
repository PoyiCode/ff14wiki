---
title: "Timmon Beck"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Timmon Beck** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Timmon Beck.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Steelhead Trout
- Miounnefish
- Balloon Frog
- Death Loach
- Gyr Abanian Chub
- Hardhead Trout
- Xenacanthus
