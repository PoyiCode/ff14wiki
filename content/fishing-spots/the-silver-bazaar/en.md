---
title: "The Silver Bazaar"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Silver Bazaar** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Silver Bazaar.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lominsan Anchovy
- Pebble Crab
- Tiger Cod
- Rothlyt Oyster
- Hammerhead Shark
- Indigo Herring
- Haraldr Haddock
- Rock Lobster
- Dirty Herry
