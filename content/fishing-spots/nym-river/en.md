---
title: "Nym River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Nym River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Nym River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Chub
- Dwarf Catfish
- Princess Trout
- Dusk Goby
- Gudgeon
- Maiden Carp
- La Noscean Perch
- High Perch
