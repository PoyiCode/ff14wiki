---

**Unnamed Margin** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Unnamed Margin.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- First Mate's Finger
- Specterfish
- Rhotano Permit
- Rhotano Dahlia
- Lodesmate's Pen
- Rhotano Roosterfish
- Rhotanosaurus
- Royal Handmaiden
- Spectral Starfish
- Frolicsome Fish
