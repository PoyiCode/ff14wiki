title: "Middle Thaliak River"

---

**Middle Thaliak River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Middle Thaliak River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Philosopher's Stone
- Scholar Sculpin
- Sweetfish
- Hedgemole Cricket
- Bullfrog
- Thaliak Caiman
- Berserker Betta
- Goblin Bass
- Inkshell
- Bobgoblin Bass
