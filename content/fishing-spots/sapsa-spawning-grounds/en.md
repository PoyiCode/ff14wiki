---
title: "Sapsa Spawning Grounds"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sapsa Spawning Grounds** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sapsa Spawning Grounds.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Fullmoon Sardine
- Whitelip Oyster
- Red Coral
- Blacklip Oyster
- Nautilus
- Sea Devil
- The Captain's Chalice
