title: "Cenote Jayunja"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Cenote Jayunja** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Cenote Jayunja.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Mudfish
- Yak T'el Catfish
- Variatus
- Welkin Catfish
- Flawless Saucer
- Moonmarking Saucer
