---
title: "The Pappus Tree"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Pappus Tree** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Pappus Tree.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Fountfish
- Crystalfin
- Aether Eye
- Lungfish
- Tiny Axolotl
- Flintstrike
- Hundred-eyed Axolotl
