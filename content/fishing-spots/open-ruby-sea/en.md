title: "Open Ruby Sea"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Open Ruby Sea** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Open Ruby Sea.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crimson Kelp
- Reef Squid
- Pinebark Flounder
- Mantle Moray
- Barded Lobster
- Sanbaso
- Shisui Goby
- Violet Sentry
- Spectral Snake Eel
- Heavensent Shark
