---
title: "Forgotten Springs"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Forgotten Springs** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Forgotten Springs.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Copperfish
- Warmwater Trout
- Lamprey
- Plaguefish
- Ropefish
- Clown Loach
- Golden Loach
- Iron Noose
