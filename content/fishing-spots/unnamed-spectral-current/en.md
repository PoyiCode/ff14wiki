**Unnamed Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Unnamed Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Captain's Finger
- Bright Red Coral
- Royal Favorite
- Pirate's Purse
- Captain's Pen
- Tylosaurus
- Cieldalaes Roosterfish
- Renegade Rhotanosaurus
- Red Boarfish
- Akupara
