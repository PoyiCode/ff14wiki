title: "Diadem Grotto"

---

**Diadem Grotto** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Diadem Grotto.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Cherax
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Marimo
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Catfish
- Grade 3 Skybuilders' Ricefish
