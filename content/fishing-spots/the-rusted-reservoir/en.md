---
title: "The Rusted Reservoir"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Rusted Reservoir** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Rusted Reservoir.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Hard Candy
- Abyssal Snail
- Water Ball
- Misty Killifish
- Platinum Guppy
- Moonlight Guppy
