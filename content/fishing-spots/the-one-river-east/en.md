---

**The One River (East)** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The One River (East).

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Yanxian Koi
- Longhair Catfish
- Doman Trout
- Yanxian Barramundi
- Kotsu Zetsu
- Othardian Salmon
- Axelrod
