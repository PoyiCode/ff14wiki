**Unmoved Source Alpha** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Unmoved Source Alpha.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Butterfly Fry
- Topminnow
- Tessera
- Giant Aetherlouse
- Fat Snook
- Jumbo Snook
