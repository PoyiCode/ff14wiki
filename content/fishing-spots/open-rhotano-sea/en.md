---

**Open Rhotano Sea** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Open Rhotano Sea.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Rhotano Wahoo
- Rhotano Sardine
- Deep Plaice
- Crimson Monkfish
- Chrome Hammerhead
- Ogre Eel
- Dark Nautilus
- Lampfish
- Spectral Bass
- Sabaton
