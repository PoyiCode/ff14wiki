**Limne 3-α** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Limne 3-α.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Wormhole Worm
- Unidentified Flying Biomass II
- Triaina
- Argonaut
- Echinos
- Space Bishop
- Alyketos
- Argonauta argo
