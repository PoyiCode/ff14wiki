title: "The Stainless Course"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Stainless Course** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Stainless Course.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Garlean Clam
- Dark Crown
- Eblan Trout
- Imperial Pleco
- Bluetail
- Kitty Herring
- Sovereign Shadow
