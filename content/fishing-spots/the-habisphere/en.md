---
title: "The Habisphere"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Habisphere** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Habisphere.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sky Faerie
- Rudderfish
- Unidentified Flying Biomass
- Bomb Puffer
- Sky Sweeper
- Tupuxuara
- Stupendemys
- Cobrafish
- Crystal Pigeon
