---
title: "West Agelyss River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**West Agelyss River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: West Agelyss River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crayfish
- Chub
- Dwarf Catfish
- Princess Trout
- Gudgeon
- La Noscean Perch
- Great Gudgeon
