---
title: "The Driftdowns"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Driftdowns** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Driftdowns.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Heritage Loach
- Outskirts Sniffer
- Moon Croppie
- Driftdowns Trout
- Burst Lungfish
- Thunderswift Trout
