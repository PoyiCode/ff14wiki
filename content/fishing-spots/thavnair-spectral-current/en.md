title: "Thavnair Spectral Current"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Thavnair Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Thavnair Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Great Red Mantis
- Guzel Gwl
- Darpana
- Silverdart
- Satrapsaurus
- Tiger Mantis
- Mehi-mahi
- Junior Jinbei
- Pliosaurus
- Manasvin
