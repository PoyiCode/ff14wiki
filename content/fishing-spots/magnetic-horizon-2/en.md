title: "Magnetic Horizon"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Magnetic Horizon** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Magnetic Horizon.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Inverted Anchovy
- Inverted Barracuda
- Geras Crab
- Pole Shifter
- Iron Longnose
