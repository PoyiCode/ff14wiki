---
title: "Oschon's Torch"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Oschon's Torch** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Oschon's Torch.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sea Cucumber
- Vongola Clam
- Moraby Flounder
- Pebble Crab
- Tiger Cod
- Rothlyt Oyster
- Mahi-mahi
- Silver Sovereign
