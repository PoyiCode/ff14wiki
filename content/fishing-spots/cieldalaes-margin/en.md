---
title: "Cieldalaes Margin"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Cieldalaes Margin** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Cieldalaes Margin.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Tortoiseshell Crab
- Lady's Cameo
- Metallic Boxfish
- Goobbue Ray
- Watermoura
- King Cobrafish
- Mamahi-mahi
- Lavandin Remora
- Spectral Butterfly
- Cieldalaes Geode
