---
title: "Tao Khaal"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Tao Khaal** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Tao Khaal.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Zagas Khaal
- Dry Steppe Skipper
- Dusk Crayfish
- Bowfish
- Tao Bitterling
- Elder Gourami
- The Word of God
