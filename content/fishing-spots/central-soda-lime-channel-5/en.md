---

**Central Soda-lime Channel** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Central Soda-lime Channel.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Glass Dahlia
- Fishy Jellyfish
- Limingway
- Gold Ruby Pansyfin
- Sinking Crystascute
