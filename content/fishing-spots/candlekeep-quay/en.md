---
title: "Candlekeep Quay"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Candlekeep Quay** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Candlekeep Quay.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lominsan Anchovy
- Vongola Clam
- Moraby Flounder
- Pebble Crab
- Tiger Cod
- Rothlyt Oyster
- Shall Shell
- Sabertooth Cod
