**Handmirror Lake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Handmirror Lake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grey Skipper
- Pixie Fish
- Lemonfish
- Wimple Carp
- Faeshine Clam
- Mirror Image
- Leannisg
