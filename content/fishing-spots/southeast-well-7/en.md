---
title: "Southeast Well"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Southeast Well** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Southeast Well.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Star Pleco
- Lunar Grass Carp
- Moonlit Snakehead
- Moonbeam
- Moongate Cod
- Lunar Sturgeon
