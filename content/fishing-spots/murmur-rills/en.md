---
title: "Murmur Rills"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Murmur Rills** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Murmur Rills.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Brass Loach
- Maiden Carp
- Rainbow Trout
- River Crab
- Bluebell Salmon
- Tricolored Carp
- Blue Widow
