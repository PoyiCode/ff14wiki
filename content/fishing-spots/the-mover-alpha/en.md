title: "The Mover Alpha"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Mover Alpha** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Mover Alpha.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Butterfly Fry
- Prochilodus Luminosus
- Xenocypris
- Mesonauta
- Greengill Salmon
- Mossgill Salmon
