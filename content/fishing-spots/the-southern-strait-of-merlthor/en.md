---

**The Southern Strait of Merlthor** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Southern Strait of Merlthor.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ghoul Barracuda
- La Noscean Jelly
- Sunfly
- Marine Bomb
- Shaggy Seadragon
- Momora Mora
- Merlthor Butterfly
- Gladius
- Spectral Discus
- Little Leviathan
