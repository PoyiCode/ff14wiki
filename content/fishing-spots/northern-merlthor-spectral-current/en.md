**Northern Merlthor Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Northern Merlthor Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Hammerclaw
- Coccosteus
- Bartholomew the Chopper
- Prowler
- Mopbeard
- Wild Urchin
- Gugrusaurus
- Coral Seadragon
- The Fallen One
- Elasmosaurus
