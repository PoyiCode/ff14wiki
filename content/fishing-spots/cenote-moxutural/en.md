title: "Cenote Moxutural"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Cenote Moxutural** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Cenote Moxutural.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Mudfish
- Yak T'el Catfish
- Checkered Cichlid
- Moxutural Gar
- Moxutural Greatgar
