---

**Where the Dry Return** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Where the Dry Return.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Stormfish
- Deep Purple Coral
- Caliban
- Stippled Eel
- Ondobane
- The Ondotaker
