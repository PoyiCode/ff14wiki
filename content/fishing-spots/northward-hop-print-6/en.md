title: "Northward Hop-print"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Northward Hop-print** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Northward Hop-print.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Melancholia
- Mooncrystal Perch
- Lunar Prismfish
- Hopping Goby
- Lunar Wolffish
- Platinum Bichir
