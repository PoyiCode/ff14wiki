title: "Lower Hathoeva River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Lower Hathoeva River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Lower Hathoeva River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Rainbow Trout
- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Faerie Bass
- Mudcrab
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Black Eel
- Climbing Perch
- Yugr'am Salmon
- Velodyna Carp
- Floating Boulder
