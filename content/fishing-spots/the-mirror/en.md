---
title: "The Mirror"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Mirror** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Mirror.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Crayfish
- Pipira
- Gudgeon
- Brass Loach
- Maiden Carp
- Moat Carp
- Tricolored Carp
- Ghost Carp
