---
title: "Swiftperch"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Swiftperch** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Swiftperch.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lominsan Anchovy
- White Coral
- Harbor Herring
- Moraby Flounder
- Pebble Crab
- Tiger Cod
- Rothlyt Oyster
- Blue Octopus
- Halibut
- Armorer
