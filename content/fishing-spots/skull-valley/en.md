---
title: "Skull Valley"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Skull Valley** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Skull Valley.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Tiger Cod
- Rothlyt Oyster
- Razor Clam
- Blue Octopus
- Wahoo
- Goosefish
- Junkmonger
