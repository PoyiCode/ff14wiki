---
title: "Everschade"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Everschade** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Everschade.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Black Eel
- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Monke Onke
- Mitten Crab
- Spotted Pleco
- Bonytongue
- Levinlight
