title: "Pavana's Remorse"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Pavana's Remorse** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Pavana's Remorse.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Green Swordtail
- Radzbalik
- Half-moon Betta
- Banana Eel
- Browned Banana Eel
