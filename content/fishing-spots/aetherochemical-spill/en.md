**Aetherochemical Spill** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Aetherochemical Spill.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Platinum Fish
- Bullwhip
- Blood Skipper
- Aetherochemical Compound #123
- Proto-hropken
- Aetherochemical Compound #666
