---
title: "The Lower Hungering Gardens"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Lower Hungering Gardens** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Lower Hungering Gardens.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Isle Skipper
- Dermogenys
- Iribainion
- Colossoma
- Phyllinos
- Gilt Dermogenys
