title: "Southern Diadem Lake"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Southern Diadem Lake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Southern Diadem Lake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 2 Skybuilders' Cloudskipper
- Grade 2 Skybuilders' Meditator
- Grade 2 Skybuilders' Coeurlfish
- Grade 2 Skybuilders' Garpike
