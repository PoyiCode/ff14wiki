---
title: "Kugane Coast"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Kugane Coast** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Kugane Coast.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ruby Herring
- Whirlpool Turban
- Leopard Prawn
- Spear Squid
- Floating Lantern
- Rubescent Tatsunoko
- Hatatate
- Silent Shark
- Spectral Wrasse
- Mizuhiki
