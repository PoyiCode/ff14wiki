title: "Weston Waters"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Weston Waters** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Weston Waters.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Cloudfish
- Dravanian Squeaker
- Weston Bowfin
- Hundred Fin
- Lovers' Clam
- Illuminati Mask
- Thousand Fin
