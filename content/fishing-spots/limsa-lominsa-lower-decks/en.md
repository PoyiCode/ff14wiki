**Limsa Lominsa Lower Decks** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Limsa Lominsa Lower Decks.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Lominsan Anchovy
- Malm Kelp
- Finger Shrimp
- Ocean Cloud
- Harbor Herring
- Coral Butterfly
- Blue Octopus
- Octomammoth
