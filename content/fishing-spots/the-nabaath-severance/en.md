---
title: "The Nabaath Severance"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Nabaath Severance** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Nabaath Severance.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Shadow Crab
- Desert Dustfish
- Amber Lamprey
- Hornhelm
- Desert Saw
- Areng Dire
- Gold Dustfish
- Nabaath Saw
