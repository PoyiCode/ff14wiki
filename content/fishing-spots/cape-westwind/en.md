---
title: "Cape Westwind"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Cape Westwind** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Cape Westwind.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Haraldr Haddock
- Whitelip Oyster
- Silver Shark
- Wahoo
- Blacklip Oyster
- Plaice
- Sea Devil
- Titanic Sawfish
- Pirate's Bane
