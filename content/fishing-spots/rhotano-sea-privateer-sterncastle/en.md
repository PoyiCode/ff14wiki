**Rhotano Sea (Privateer Sterncastle)** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Rhotano Sea (Privateer Sterncastle).

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Blowfish
- Leafy Seadragon
- Rock Lobster
- Wahoo
- Little Thalaos
- Shall Shell
- Megalodon
- Giant Squid
- Nepto Dragon
