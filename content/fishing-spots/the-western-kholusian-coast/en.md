title: "The Western Kholusian Coast"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Western Kholusian Coast** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Western Kholusian Coast.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- South Kholusian Cod
- Kholusian Flounder
- Shellfracture Kelp
- Creamy Oyster
- Rose Shrimp
- Razorfish
- Kholusian King Crab
- Blue Crab
- Steel Razor
