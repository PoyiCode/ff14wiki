title: "The Slow Wash"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Slow Wash** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Slow Wash.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Idle Goby
- Rapids Jumper
- Gyr Abanian Trout
- Heather Charr
- Death Loach
- Invisible Crayfish
- The Undecided
