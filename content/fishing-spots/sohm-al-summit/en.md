title: "Sohm Al Summit"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Sohm Al Summit** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Sohm Al Summit.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sky Faerie
- Gloaming Coral
- Cloud Rider
- Rudderfish
- Gobbie Mask
- Star Faerie
- Pteranodon
- Dimorphodon
