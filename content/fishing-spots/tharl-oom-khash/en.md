---
title: "Tharl Oom Khash"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Tharl Oom Khash** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Tharl Oom Khash.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sky Faerie
- Cloud Rider
- Rudderfish
- Manasail
- Brown Bolo
- Cobrafish
- Storm Chaser
- Nyctosaur
- Bloodchaser
- Raimdellopterus
