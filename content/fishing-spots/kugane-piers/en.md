---
title: "Kugane Piers"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Kugane Piers** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Kugane Piers.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grinning Anchovy
- Ruby Shrimp
- Striped Fugu
- Glass Tuna
- Hanatatsu
- Ribbon Eel
- Rising Dragon
