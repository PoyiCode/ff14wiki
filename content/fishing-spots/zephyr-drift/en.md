title: "Zephyr Drift"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Zephyr Drift** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Zephyr Drift.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Lominsan Anchovy
- Finger Shrimp
- Ocean Cloud
- Sea Cucumber
- Harbor Herring
- Coral Butterfly
- Angelfish
- Blue Octopus
- Zalera
