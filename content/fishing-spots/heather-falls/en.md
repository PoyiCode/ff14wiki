---
title: "Heather Falls"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Heather Falls** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Heather Falls.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Adamantite Bichir
- Death Loach
- Abalathian Salamander
- Steelshark
- Purple Buckler
- The Last Tear
