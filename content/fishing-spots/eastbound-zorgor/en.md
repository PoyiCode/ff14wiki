title: "Eastbound Zorgor"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Eastbound Zorgor** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Eastbound Zorgor.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Zorgor Core
- Cloud Wasp
- Azure Glider
- Hellsnow Shark
- Raging Hanuhead
- Cloudsail
- Azure Diver
