title: "The Ship Graveyard"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Ship Graveyard** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Ship Graveyard.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Red Coral
- Wahoo
- Blacklip Oyster
- Pike Eel
- Mummer Wrasse
- Plaice
- Giant Squid
- Megalodon
- Frilled Shark
