---
title: "The Tangle"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Tangle** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Tangle.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Crayfish
- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Silverfish
- Sludgeskipper
- Assassin Betta
- Boltfish
- Ilsabardian Bass
- Cadaver Carp
- Ninja Betta
