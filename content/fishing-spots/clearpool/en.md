---
title: "Clearpool"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Clearpool** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Clearpool.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Abalathian Smelt
- Ice Faerie
- Coerthan Crab
- Hotrod
- Capelin
- Priestfish
- Ghost Faerie
- Bishopfish
- Charibenet
