title: "The Salt Strand"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Salt Strand** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Salt Strand.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Sea Cucumber
- Pebble Crab
- Helmet Crab
- Razor Clam
- Blowfish
- Sea Bo
- Black Sole
- Tiny Tortoise
- Meteor Survivor
