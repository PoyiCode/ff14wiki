**Limne 18** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Limne 18.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Wormhole Worm
- Unidentified Flying Biomass II
- Sophos Deka-okto
- Class Twenty-four
- Terrifyingway
- Alien Mertone
- Monster Carrot
- Forbiddingway
