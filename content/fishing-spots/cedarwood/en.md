---
title: "Cedarwood"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Cedarwood** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Cedarwood.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Ocean Cloud
- Harbor Herring
- Moraby Flounder
- Pebble Crab
- Tiger Cod
- Blue Octopus
- Leafy Seadragon
- Syldra
