title: "The Spoiled One"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Spoiled One** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Spoiled One.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Brightmirror Clam
- Grey Skipper
- Cerulean Loach
- Cherry Herring
- Dammroen Herring
