---
title: "The Arms of Meed"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Arms of Meed** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Arms of Meed.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Gyr Abanian Trout
- Gigant Bass
- Eastern Pike
- Scimitarfish
- Ala Mhigan Ribbon
- Rockfish
- Lily of the Veil
