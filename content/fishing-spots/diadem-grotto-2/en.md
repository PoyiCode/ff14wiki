title: "Diadem Grotto"

---

**Diadem Grotto** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Diadem Grotto.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 2 Skybuilders' Cloudskipper
- Grade 2 Skybuilders' Meditator
- Grade 2 Skybuilders' Bitterling
- Grade 2 Skybuilders' Caiman
