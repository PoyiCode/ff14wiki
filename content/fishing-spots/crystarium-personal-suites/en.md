title: "Crystarium Personal Suites"

---

**Crystarium Personal Suites** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Crystarium Personal Suites.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Crystal Knife
- Laxan Carp
- Crystarium Tetra
- Skeletonfish
- Loose Pendant
