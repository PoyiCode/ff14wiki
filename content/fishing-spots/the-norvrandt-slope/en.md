---
title: "The Norvrandt Slope"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Norvrandt Slope** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Norvrandt Slope.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Stormfish
- Sycorax
- Sweetflesh Oyster
- Azure Sea Spider
- Rainbow Shrimp
- Predator
- Il Lydha
- Opal Shrimp
