title: "Canal Town North"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Canal Town North** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Canal Town North.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Canal Bream
- Iris Wrasse
- Devotion Clam
- Forgotten One
- Copper Shark
- Shined Copper Shark
