---

**The Northern Strait of Merlthor** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Northern Strait of Merlthor.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Floefish
- Megasquid
- Oschon's Stone
- Tossed Dagger
- Tripod Fish
- Net Crawler
- Merlthor Lobster
- Elder Dinichthys
- Spectral Sea Bo
- Shooting Star
