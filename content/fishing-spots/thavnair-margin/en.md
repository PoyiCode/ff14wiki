title: "Thavnair Margin"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Thavnair Margin** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Thavnair Margin.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Red Mantis
- Datli Gwl
- Agama's Strand
- Palaka's Blade
- Parjanya Wrasse
- Simolestes
- Thavnasaurus
- Junior Jinbei
- Spectral Grouper
- Silkfin
