title: "West Banepool"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**West Banepool** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: West Banepool.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Fanged Clam
- Lake Urchin
- Ice Faerie
- Mercy Staff
- Snowcaller
- Coerthan Oyster
- Warmscale Pleco
- Merciless
