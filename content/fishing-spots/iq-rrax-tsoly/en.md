title: "Iq Rrax Tsoly"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Iq Rrax Tsoly** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Iq Rrax Tsoly.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Tsoly Mu
- Iq Rrax Leaffish
- Archmatron Angelfish
- Yak T'el Salamander
- Shadowtongue
- Iron Shadowtongue
