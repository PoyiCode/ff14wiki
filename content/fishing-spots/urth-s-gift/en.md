---
title: "Urth's Gift"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Urth's Gift** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Urth's Gift.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Monke Onke
- Bonytongue
- Mitten Crab
- Velodyna Carp
- Southern Pike
- Boxing Pleco
- Takitaro
- Giant Takitaro
- Namitaro
