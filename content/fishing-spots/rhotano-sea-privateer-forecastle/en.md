**Rhotano Sea (Privateer Forecastle)** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Rhotano Sea (Privateer Forecastle).

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Finger Shrimp
- Harbor Herring
- Blowfish
- Balloonfish
- Ash Tuna
- Red Coral
- Ogre Barracuda
- Mazlaya Marlin
- Gigant Octopus
- The Old Man in the Sea
