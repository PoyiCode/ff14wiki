title: "Northern Diadem Lake"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Northern Diadem Lake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Northern Diadem Lake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Glacier Core
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Kissing Fish
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Cavalry Catfish
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Manasail
