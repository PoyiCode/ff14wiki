title: "Scholar's Harbor"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Scholar's Harbor** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Scholar's Harbor.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Pecten
- Northern Herring
- Dog-faced Puffer
- Cobalt Chromis
- Guitarfish
- Aetherolectric Guitarfish
