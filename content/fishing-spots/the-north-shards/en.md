---
title: "The North Shards"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The North Shards** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The North Shards.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lamp Marimo
- Silverfish
- Giant Bass
- Ignus Snail
- Assassin Betta
- Ilsabardian Bass
- Goldfish
- Morinabaligi
- Canavan
- Kuno the Killer
