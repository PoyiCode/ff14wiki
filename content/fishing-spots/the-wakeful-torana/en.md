title: "The Wakeful Torana"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Wakeful Torana** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Wakeful Torana.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Green Swordtail
- Ksirapayin
- Wakeful Watcher
- Red Drum
- Wakeful Warden
