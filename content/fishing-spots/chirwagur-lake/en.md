**Chirwagur Lake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Chirwagur Lake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Chirwagur Loach
- Alright Alright Alright
- Zorlortor
- Shooting Starscale
- Ole Ole Ole
