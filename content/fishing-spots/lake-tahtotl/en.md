title: "Lake Tahtotl"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Lake Tahtotl** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Lake Tahtotl.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Mudcrab
- Pond Mussel
- Bonytongue
- Silverfish
- Assassin Betta
- Boxing Pleco
- Goldfish
- Bowfin
- Cornelia
