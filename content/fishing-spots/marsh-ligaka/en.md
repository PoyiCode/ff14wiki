---
title: "Marsh Ligaka"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Marsh Ligaka** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Marsh Ligaka.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ligaka Guppy
- Dumplingfish
- Spotted Stingray
- Yellow Peacock Bass
- Shovelnose Catfish
- Excavator Catfish
