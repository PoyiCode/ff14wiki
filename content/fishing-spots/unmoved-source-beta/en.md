---

**Unmoved Source Beta** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Unmoved Source Beta.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Butterfly Fry
- Raiamas
- Xenocypris
- Red Bowfin
- Swampsucker Bowfin
