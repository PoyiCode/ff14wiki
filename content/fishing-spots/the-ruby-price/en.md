---

**The Ruby Price** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Ruby Price.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grinning Anchovy
- Glass Herring
- Coeurl Snake Eel
- Coral Horse
- Ku'er
- The Ruby Dragon
