---
title: "The Eastern Kholusian Coast"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Eastern Kholusian Coast** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Eastern Kholusian Coast.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- South Kholusian Cod
- Shellfracture Kelp
- Blood Cloud
- Spearhead Squid
- Kholusian Wrasse
- Weedy Seadragon
- Henodus
- Ondo Kelp
- Henodus Grandis
