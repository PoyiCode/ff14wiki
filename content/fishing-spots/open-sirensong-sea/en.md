title: "Open Sirensong Sea"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Open Sirensong Sea** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Open Sirensong Sea.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Deepshade Sardine
- Sirensong Mussel
- Arrowhead
- Pink Shrimp
- Sirensong Mullet
- Selkie Puffer
- Poet's Pipe
- Marine Matanga
- Spectral Coelacanth
- Dusk Shark
