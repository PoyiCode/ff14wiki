**Outskirts Shallows** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Outskirts Shallows.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Heritage Loach
- Outskirts Sniffer
- Pumpkin Perch
- Tiger Muskellunge
- Sprouting Perch
