---
title: "Coerthas River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Coerthas River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Coerthas River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Striped Goby
- Maiden Carp
- Rainbow Trout
- Seema
- Giant Bass
- Crimson Trout
- Kissing Trout
- Daniffen's Mark
