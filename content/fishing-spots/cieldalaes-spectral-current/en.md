title: "Cieldalaes Spectral Current"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Cieldalaes Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Cieldalaes Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Titanshell Crab
- Mythril Boxfish
- Mistbeard's Cup
- Anomalocaris Saron
- Flaming Eel
- Jetborne Manta
- Devil's Sting
- Callichthyid
- Meandering Mora
- Hafgufa
