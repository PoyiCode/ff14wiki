title: "Open Bloodbrine Sea"

---

**Open Bloodbrine Sea** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Open Bloodbrine Sea.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Thaliak Crab
- Star of the Destroyer
- True Scad
- Blooded Wrasse
- Bloodpolish Crab
- Blue Stitcher
- Bloodfresh Tuna
- Sunken Mask
- Spectral Eel
- Bareface
