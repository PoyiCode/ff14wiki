title: "The Hills of Amber"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Hills of Amber** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Hills of Amber.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Garik Crab
- Desert Dustfish
- Web-footed Sand Gecko
- Nabaath Manta
- Amber Monkfish
- Thorned Lizard
- Ambling Caltrop
