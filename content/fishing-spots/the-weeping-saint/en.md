---
title: "The Weeping Saint"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Weeping Saint** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Weeping Saint.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lamp Marimo
- Abalathian Smelt
- Giant Bass
- Crimson Trout
- Common Sculpin
- Northern Pike
- Angry Pike
- Darkstar
