**Buffeted Cloudtop** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Buffeted Cloudtop.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Ghost Faerie
- Grade 4 Skybuilders' Ashfish
- Grade 2 Artisanal Skybuilders' Pterodactyl
- Grade 2 Artisanal Skybuilders' Skyfish
- Grade 2 Artisanal Skybuilders' Rhamphorhynchus
- Grade 2 Artisanal Skybuilders' Dragon's Soul
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Mosasaur
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Storm Chaser
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Wyvern
- Grade 3 Artisanal Skybuilders' Helicoprion
