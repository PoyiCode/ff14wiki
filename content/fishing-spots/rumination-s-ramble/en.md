title: "Rumination's Ramble"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Rumination's Ramble** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Rumination's Ramble.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Isle Skipper
- Albino Loach
- Iribainion
- Golden Shiner
- Mangar
- Planetes
