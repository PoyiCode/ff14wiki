title: "Rhotano Spectral Current"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Rhotano Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Rhotano Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Aronnax
- Sweeper
- Silencer
- Deep-sea Eel
- Executioner
- Floating Saucer
- True Barramundi
- Prodigal Son
- Slipsnail
- Stonescale
