---
title: "The Nail"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Nail** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Nail.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Striped Goby
- Abalathian Smelt
- Giant Bass
- Crimson Trout
- Bronze Lake Trout
- Northern Pike
- Kissing Trout
- Emperor Fish
- Starbright
