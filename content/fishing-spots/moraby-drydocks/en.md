---
title: "Moraby Drydocks"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Moraby Drydocks** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Moraby Drydocks.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Ocean Cloud
- Harbor Herring
- Moraby Flounder
- Pebble Crab
- Tiger Cod
- Rothlyt Oyster
- Navigator's Dagger
- Blue Octopus
- Jacques the Snipper
