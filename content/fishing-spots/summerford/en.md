title: "Summerford"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Summerford** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Summerford.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Merlthor Goby
- Finger Shrimp
- White Coral
- Ocean Cloud
- Harbor Herring
- Vongola Clam
- Blue Octopus
- Ogre Barracuda
- Silver Shark
- Gigantshark
