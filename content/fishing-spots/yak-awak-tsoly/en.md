title: "Yak Awak Tsoly"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Yak Awak Tsoly** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Yak Awak Tsoly.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Shimmering Shadow
- Ut'ohmu Dawnfish
- Apoda
- Mirror Carp
- Yak Awak Core
- Awaksbane Apoda
