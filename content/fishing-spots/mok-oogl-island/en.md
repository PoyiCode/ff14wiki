**Mok Oogl Island** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Mok Oogl Island.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Starflower
- Sky Faerie
- Scorpionfly
- Moogle Spirit
- Vampiric Tapestry
- Heavens Coral
- Basking Shark
- Sea Butterfly
