title: "Upper Watts River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Upper Watts River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Upper Watts River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Skulleater
- Toffee Snail
- Watts Trout
- Hucho Taimen
- Winged Hatchetfish
- Giant Taimen
