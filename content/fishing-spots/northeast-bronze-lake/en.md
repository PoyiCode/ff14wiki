---
title: "Northeast Bronze Lake"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Northeast Bronze Lake** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Northeast Bronze Lake.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Jade Eel
- Pond Mussel
- Four-eyed Fish
- Bonytongue
- Mitten Crab
- Silverfish
- Giant Bass
- Bronze Lake Trout
- Giant Catfish
- King of the Spring
