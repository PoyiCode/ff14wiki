---
title: "The Brewer's Beacon"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Brewer's Beacon** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Brewer's Beacon.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- White Coral
- Harbor Herring
- Helmet Crab
- Rothlyt Oyster
- Navigator's Dagger
- Ogre Barracuda
- Gigant Octopus
- Navigator's Brand
