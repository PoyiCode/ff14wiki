title: "Empty Heart"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Empty Heart** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Empty Heart.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Dusk Goby
- Crimson Crayfish
- Faerie Bass
- Acorn Snail
- Moat Carp
- Copperfish
- Eunuch Crayfish
- Grip Killifish
- Faerie Queen
