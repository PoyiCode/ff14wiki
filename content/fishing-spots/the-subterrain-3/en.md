---
title: "The Subterrain"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Subterrain** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Subterrain.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Burrow Shrimp
- Pseudoturtle
- Upside-down Aether Eye
- Inverted Carp
- Burrow Bolo
- Euhedroturtle
