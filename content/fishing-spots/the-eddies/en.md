---
title: "The Eddies"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Eddies** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Eddies.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Hedgemole Cricket
- Cloudfish
- Bullfrog
- Yalm Lobster
- Vanuhead
- Catkiller
- Red Sky Coral
- Paikiller
