---
title: "The Comet's Tail"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Comet's Tail** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Comet's Tail.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Bone Melter
- Falling Star
- Capsized Squeaker
- Nirvana Crab
- Cardinalfish
- Lake Sphairai
- The Archbishop
