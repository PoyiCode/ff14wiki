---
title: "Upper Yat Khaal"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Upper Yat Khaal** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Upper Yat Khaal.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Tail Mountains Minnow
- Zagas Khaal
- Dry Steppe Skipper
- Skytear
- Dusk Crayfish
- Blade Skipper
