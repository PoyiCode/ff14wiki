title: "The Lavender Beds"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Lavender Beds** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Lavender Beds.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Pipira
- Dark Sleeper
- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Tricolored Carp
- Shadow Catfish
- Spotted Pleco
- Bonytongue
- Monke Onke
- Sweetnewt
