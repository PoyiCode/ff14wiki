---
title: "The Isle of Ken"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Isle of Ken** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Isle of Ken.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Hard Candy
- Clean Saucer
- Albino Garfish
- Vicejaw
- Forgiven Melancholy
- The Jaws of Undeath
- Listracanthus
