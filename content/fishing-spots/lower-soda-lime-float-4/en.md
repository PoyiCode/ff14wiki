---

**Lower Soda-lime Float** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Lower Soda-lime Float.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Kissing Counterfeit
- Soda-lime Sunrise
- Navy Coelacanths
- Filigree Floret
- Soda Mocktail
- Bubbling Impesctor
