---
title: "The Isle of Bekko"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**The Isle of Bekko** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: The Isle of Bekko.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Grinning Anchovy
- Hellyfish
- Ruby Shrimp
- Dafu
- Glass Tuna
- Tawny Wench Shark
- Usuginu Octopus
- Fatty Herring
- Seven Stars
