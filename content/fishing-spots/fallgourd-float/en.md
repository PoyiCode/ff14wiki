title: "Fallgourd Float"

---

**Fallgourd Float** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Fallgourd Float.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Striped Goby
- Brass Loach
- Maiden Carp
- Rainbow Trout
- Ala Mhigan Fighting Fish
- Faerie Bass
- Dark Sleeper
- Shadow Catfish
- Judgeray
