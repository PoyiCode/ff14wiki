title: "Rothlyt Spectral Current"

---

**Rothlyt Spectral Current** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Rothlyt Spectral Current.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Garum Jug
- Smooth Jaguar
- Rothlyt Mussel
- Levi Elver
- Pearl Bombfish
- Trollfish
- Panoptes
- Crepe Sole
- Knifejaw
- Placodus
