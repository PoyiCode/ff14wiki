---
title: "Whilom River"
summary: "A fishing spot in Eorzea."
---

**Whilom River** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Whilom River.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Whilom Catfish
- Rock Mussel
- Pipira Pira
- Three-lip Carp
- Dravanian Bass
- Blueclaw Shrimp
- Twin-tongued Carp
