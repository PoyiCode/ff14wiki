title: "Exploratory Ice Hole"

---

**Exploratory Ice Hole** is a fishing hole in Eorzea where a resident can cast a line. In-game name: Exploratory Ice Hole.

**Fish you can catch here：**

- Lamp Marimo
- Abalathian Smelt
- Northern Pike
- Kissing Trout
- The Matriarch
