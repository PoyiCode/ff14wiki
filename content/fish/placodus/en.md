**Placodus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Placodus.

This scalekin excavates bivalves from the sea floor, before crushing them, shells and all, between its flattened back teeth. The fragmented ruins of hundreds upon hundreds of shellfish litter the shore where it chooses to feed.

Caught at: Rothlyt Spectral Current
