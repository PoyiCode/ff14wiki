**Dusk Goby** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dusk Goby.

A hardy species, the dusk goby can survive not only in freshwater, but also the mouths of rivers, where the tides can bring saltwater malms inland. Depending on the salt content of the water it lives in, the color of its scales can vary from a deep amber to a golden orange.

Caught at: Nym River, Woad Whisper Canyon, The Mourning Widow, Empty Heart, Nophica's Wells, Upper Soot Creek, Lower Soot Creek, North Drybone, South Drybone, The Clutch
