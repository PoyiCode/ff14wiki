**Gudgeon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gudgeon.

Each night, this freshwater fish will use its fins to dig a shallow burrow in river or lake beds, where it will sleep, relatively safe from predators. Because of this characteristic, La Noscean fishers often playfully refer to the wavekin by another name—the “cravenfish.”

Caught at: The Vein, The Mirror, West Agelyss River, Nym River, The Mourning Widow, Nophica's Wells, Whispering Gorge, Jadeite Flood, Lower Black Tea Brook, Upper Black Tea Brook
