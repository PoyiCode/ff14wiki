**Akupara** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Akupara.

These sizable turtles traverse the seas with their shells held above the waves. Thus exposed to the elements, their carapaces become a rich tapestry of plant life, with the oldest specimens playing host to unique, highly complex ecosystems.

Caught at: Unnamed Spectral Current
