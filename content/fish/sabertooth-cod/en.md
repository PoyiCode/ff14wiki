**Sabertooth Cod** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sabertooth Cod.

This tiger cod has developed the wickedly bladelike fangs for which it is named. Would-be catchers are advised to exercise the utmost caution when removing the hook from its snapping maw.

Caught at: Candlekeep Quay
