**Blue Purse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Blue Purse.

The fine, gold-rimmed scales of this wavekin almost look like overlapping coins, bringing to mind a bulging purse filled with riches.

Caught at: High Tide Harbor
