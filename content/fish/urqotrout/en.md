**Urqotrout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Urqotrout.

As it inhabits a highly concentrated brine, this wavekin boasts naturally salty flesh. It is prized by the Yok Huy for being effectively pre-seasoned, and thereby delightfully simple to prepare.

Caught at: Sunken Stars
