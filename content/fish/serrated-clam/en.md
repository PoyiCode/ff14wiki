**Serrated Clam** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Serrated Clam.

Haunted by the fanged clams of Coerthas, a man dreamed the mollusks had migrated to the ocean and developed an insatiable urge to crush all living things betwixt their serrated shells. Some nightmares can be so realistic...

Caught at: Bloodbrine Spectral Current
