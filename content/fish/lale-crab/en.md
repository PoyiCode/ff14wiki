**Lale Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lale Crab.

Among the rosaceous gwl crabs clustered along Thavnair's shores may be found brighter and more keenly shaped specimens—distinctive blooms all their own. So elegant are their wine-colored carapaces and delicate steps that any noblewoman of Radz-at-Han treasures the gift of a lale crab above that of its namesake tulip, integrity of her suitors' digits be damned.
