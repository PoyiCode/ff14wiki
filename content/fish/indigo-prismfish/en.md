**Indigo Prismfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Indigo Prismfish.

The mysterious dark hues of this wavekin are only achieved through the rare mating of a violet and a blue prismfish, making the specimens highly sought-after.

Caught at: Prism Lake
