**Yat Goby** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yat Goby.

The winsome-faced bottom-feeder wields its fin with the dexterity of a finger, delicately digging into the riverbed to dine on only the finest sediment.

Caught at: Lower Yat Khaal
