**Il Lydha** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Il Lydha.

Believed to be a new variation of shellfish created by recent changes to the flow of aether along the ocean floor, this captivating rainbow-colored clam was commemoratively christened by Bismarck.

Caught at: The Norvrandt Slope
