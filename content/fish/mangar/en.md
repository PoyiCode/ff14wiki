title: "Mangar"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Mangar** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mangar.

Stout and thick, this golden wavekin looks more like an ingot with fins than a fish. Residents of Elpis, however, do not ascribe any particular importance to gold, and thus the mangar's extravagance is largely lost on them.

Caught at: Rumination's Ramble
