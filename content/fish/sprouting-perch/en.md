**Sprouting Perch** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sprouting Perch.

On closer inspection, the speckled markings that adorn this wavekin are actually bud-like protrusions. Any fish lured close by the apparition of a sprouting buffet usually find themselves on the menu instead.

Caught at: Outskirts Shallows
