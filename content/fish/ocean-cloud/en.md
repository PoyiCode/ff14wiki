**Ocean Cloud** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ocean Cloud.

A semi-transparent jellyfish commonly found in the Strait of Merlthor, where it goes through nine separate stages of development before reaching adulthood.

Caught at: Limsa Lominsa Lower Decks, Limsa Lominsa Upper Decks, Zephyr Drift, Summerford, Moraby Bay, Cedarwood, Moraby Drydocks, Isles of Umbra Northshore
