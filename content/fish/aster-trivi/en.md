**Aster Trivi** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Aster Trivi.

This ultramarine starfish takes center stage in Crystarium lessons on the night sky, with educators placing it amongst the lesser asteroidea to represent the brightest celestial bodies.

Caught at: The Trivium
