**Mosquito Fish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mosquito Fish.

Historically, this wavekin was introduced to various locales as a means of pest control—a cunning way of leveraging its enduring appetite for infuriating insects. To this day, populations still inhabit the waterways of Tuliyollal itself.

Caught at: Downripple
