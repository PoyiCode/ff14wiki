**Selkie Puffer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Selkie Puffer.

Though this fish supposedly resembles a mythological being known as a selkie, few sailors have actually laid eyes upon such a creature. As such, the comparison may be thoroughly inaccurate, provoking the ire of selkies skulking in seaside taverns with every utterance.

Caught at: Open Sirensong Sea
