**Galadion Goby** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Galadion Goby.

Sail to any part of the high seas and it is almost a given that some species of goby is swimming under the deck, and Galadion waters are no exception. A discerning eye will see that this particular goby has a somewhat wider dorsal fin than its counterpart found in the Strait of Merlthor.

Caught at: Outer Galadion Bay
