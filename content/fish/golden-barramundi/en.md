**Golden Barramundi** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Golden Barramundi.

Alchemists of the Great Work are responsible for this wavekin's golden scales. Upon closer inspection, one may find that some bear symbols or even figures in profile, evidence of alchemical attempts to turn them into coinage.

Caught at: The Great Runoff
