**Mailfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mailfish.

This wavekin's silver scales are reminiscent of chainmail. It swims with its head bowed, and this mannerism has made it popular as a symbol of the Far East's hospitable culture.

Caught at: Kugane Spectral Current
