**Moongripper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Moongripper.

On moonlit nights, this crustacean lifts its mighty pincers aloft, reaching out as if to pluck the pockmarked satellite right out of the sky. According to Yok Huy legend, specimens that successfully grasp the moon's image in their claws grow to be stronger and larger than their counterparts.

Caught at: Sunken Stars
