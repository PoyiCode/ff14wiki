**Bone Cleaner** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bone Cleaner.

A freshwater snail that survives solely on the carrion of animals that get caught and perish in the sucking mud of swamps and marshes.

Caught at: North Drybone, South Drybone
