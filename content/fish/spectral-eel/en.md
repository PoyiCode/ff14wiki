**Spectral Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spectral Eel.

Mistaking its azure glow for a shocking aura, a determined naturalist took hold of one of these eels with a rubber-lined glove. As it happens, she should have been more wary of its teeth in her leg.

Caught at: Open Bloodbrine Sea
