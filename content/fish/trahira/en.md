**Trahira** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Trahira.

This mottled brown wavekin effortlessly blends into its muddy habitat. When hunting, the agile fish will dart out with lethal precision, ensnaring its unlucky prey with razor-sharp fangs.
