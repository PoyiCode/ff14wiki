**Harlequin Queen** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Harlequin Queen.

Routinely observed leading retinues of harlequin lancers with magnanimous poise, this regal wavekin lashes out with its venomous barbels to fell prey with deadly precision.
