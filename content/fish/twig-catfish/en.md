**Twig Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Twig Catfish.

It is said that this thin brown fish evolved to resemble a fallen branch or tree root in order to evade predators. Its algae-based diet helps to “clean up” the water in which it resides.

Caught at: Sapsweet Cenote
