**Shimmering Shadow** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shimmering Shadow.

When the silver scales of this wavekin catch the light, its body appears to shimmer with a faint red glow. This enchanting effect is all the more pronounced when a shoal forms.

Caught at: Xd'aa Talat Tsoly, Iq Br'aax Reservoir, Yak Awak Tsoly
