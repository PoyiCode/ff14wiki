**Net Crawler** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Net Crawler.

The first fishers that spied this wavekin in their nets were astounded by its massive size, but their enthusiasm was soon dampened when they discovered that it had little in the way of edible meat. Although scant, its flavor was appetizing, and culinarians that know how to get the most out of each crab have become prized commodities in Limsa Lominsa.

Caught at: The Northern Strait of Merlthor
