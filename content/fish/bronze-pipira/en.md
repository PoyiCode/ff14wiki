summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Bronze Pipira** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bronze Pipira.

A tutelary divinity to those who work in the quarry, this fish has teeth so strong as to shatter stone. Indeed, it chews rocks into pieces so fine that its body absorbs the particles and adopts their color.

Caught at: Giantsgall Grounds
