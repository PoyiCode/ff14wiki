**Black Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Black Eel.

Black eels reach maturity in the Sea of Jade, at which point they return back up the Velodyna and Hathoeva rivers to spawn. A popular ingredient in pies, these wavekin are best sought after at night, for they hide in holes and crevices during the daylight hours, only emerging to hunt after sunset.

Caught at: Everschade, Hopeseed Pond, Verdant Drop, Upper Hathoeva River, Lower Hathoeva River, East Hathoeva River, Yugr'am River
