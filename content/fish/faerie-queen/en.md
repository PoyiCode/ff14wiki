**Faerie Queen** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Faerie Queen.

Unlike its more placid kin, this royalty among faerie bass shies not from confrontation, and has even been known to best entire schools of dark bass.

Caught at: Empty Heart
