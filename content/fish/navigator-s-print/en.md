**Navigator's Print** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Navigator's Print.

Llymlaen, watcher of the seas and guardian of Limsa Lominsa. A benevolent god, and perhaps a clumsy one if certain legends are to be believed. It is said She is able to restore life to Her children of the sea—a skill She was quick to learn after stepping on said children one too many times, leaving peculiar fish-shaped footprints in Her wake.

Caught at: Galadion Spectral Current
