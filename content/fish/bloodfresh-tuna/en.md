**Bloodfresh Tuna** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bloodfresh Tuna.

This wide-ranging ocean fish is hauled in from the coastal waters of the north seas. When freshly carved, its fillets are praised for their bright, bloody hue and delectable flavor.

Caught at: Open Bloodbrine Sea
