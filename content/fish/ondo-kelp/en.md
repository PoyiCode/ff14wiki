title: "Ondo Kelp"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Ondo Kelp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ondo Kelp.

This substantial and nutrient-rich seaweed was originally cultivated at the bottom of the Tempest, but was introduced to Kholusia when brought to market by the Ondo. As it is now quite popular amongst land-dwellers, experiments to grow it closer to the ocean's surface proceed apace.

Caught at: The Eastern Kholusian Coast
