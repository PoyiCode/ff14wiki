**Deepwarden** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Deepwarden.

This looming, ancient wavekin has circled the depths of Karvarhur the First since time immemorial, watching on as generations of Yok Huy have prospered and fallen.

Caught at: Karvarhur the First
