**Casket Oyster** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Casket Oyster.

Like any casket worth opening, the mollusk locked firmly within this shell is said to be simply to die for. Their valves are often fused to large rocks on the seabed, however, making the task of their harvest quite difficult. Captains often keep caskets of a different sort at the ready in case of emergency.

Caught at: Galadion Spectral Current
