**Leaf Tatsunoko** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Leaf Tatsunoko.

An eccentric protrusion enables this seafarer to evade predators by posing as algae, which it also relies on for cloaking its eggs.

Caught at: Onokoro
