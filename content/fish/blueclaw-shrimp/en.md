**Blueclaw Shrimp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Blueclaw Shrimp.

This freshwater shrimp uses its long, powerful claws to not only tear mussels from the rocks they are attached, but also pry them open and slice the containing flesh clean from the shells.

Caught at: The Hundred Throes, Whilom River, The Smoldering Wastes, The Iron Feast, Quickspill Delta
