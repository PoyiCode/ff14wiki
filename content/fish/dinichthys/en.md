**Dinichthys** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dinichthys.

A legendary shark-eating saltwater fish covered in thick armor and said to have once been worshipped by the Sahagin.

Caught at: Reaver Hide
