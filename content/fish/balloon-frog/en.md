**Balloon Frog** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Balloon Frog.

Named for the cheek-puffing posture it manifests when threatened, which can be seen as either intimidating or adorable, depending on your position in the food chain.

Caught at: The Outer Fist, Timmon Beck
