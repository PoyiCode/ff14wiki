**Welkin Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Welkin Catfish.

According to Yok Huy legend, this species came about when one of their forest-dwelling ancestors sought to capture the night sky.

Caught at: Cenote Jayunja
