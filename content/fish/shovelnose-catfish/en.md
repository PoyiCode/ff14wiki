**Shovelnose Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shovelnose Catfish.

This peculiar catfish has a slender body that tapers towards the mouth. This characteristic, along with its long barbels, helps the wavekin trawl the substrate in search for tasty insects and microbial organisms.

Caught at: Marsh Ligaka
