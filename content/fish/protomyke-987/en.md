**Protomyke #987** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Protomyke #987.

Do not be fooled by its wriggling tentacular mycelium—this tiny organism has no sentience whatsoever, and mimics the movements of creatures it lives amongst without even knowing that it does so. It synthesizes other organic matter equally unconsciously as it develops.

Caught at: The Chlorophospond
