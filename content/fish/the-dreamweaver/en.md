**The Dreamweaver** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: The Dreamweaver.

If the clickings of the Vath braveheart are to be believed, the soul of this elusive warmwater bichir rises each night from the Smoldering Wastes to invade his mind and torment his dreams.

Caught at: The Smoldering Wastes
