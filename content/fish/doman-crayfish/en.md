---

**Doman Crayfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Doman Crayfish.

Head to a countryside creek in Yanxia on a warm summer's day and you'll be sure to see children fishing out these crustacean critters, which are as common as they are harmless.

Caught at: The Heron's Nest, The Heron's Way, Prism Canyon
