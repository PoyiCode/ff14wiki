**Leafy Seadragon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Leafy Seadragon.

Though once found all across the five seas, seadragon numbers are now but a mere fraction of what they once were, due in part to Ishgardian culling parties.

Caught at: Cedarwood, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Sterncastle), North Isle of Endless Summer
