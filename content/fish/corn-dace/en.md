**Corn Dace** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Corn Dace.

This wavekin shares several similarities with the corn grown throughout Tuliyollal: a plump yellow form and prevalence on the dinner table.

Caught at: Ku'uxage
