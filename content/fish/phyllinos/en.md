**Phyllinos** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Phyllinos.

A wavekin created to resemble a piece of leafy vegetation. So successful was this endeavor, the phyllinos was almost registered as a plant species. Until it laid eggs, that is.

Caught at: The Lower Hungering Gardens
