**Muttering Matamata** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Muttering Matamata.

As pleated matamata age, their shells continue to grow indefinitely. According to legend, the largest and most ancient specimens eventually fuse with the landscape, blocking rivers in the process—the once-mighty roar of water reduced to a mere muttering. Though naturalists have yet to observe the phenomena in the wild, this specimen renders the tales believable.

Caught at: Bopo'uihih
