title: "Thorned Lizard"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Thorned Lizard** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Thorned Lizard.

The spiny plates that cover this scalekin from the top of its nose to tip of its tail have led some to nickname it the knight of the desert. It traverses the sands by biting its tail and rolling along like a tumbleweed.

Caught at: The Hills of Amber
