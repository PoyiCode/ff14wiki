**Daio Squid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Daio Squid.

Known to Hingans as the “great king,” this rare kraken maintains a shroud of mystery, only surfacing from deep-sea depths when it truly needs a release.

Caught at: The Isle of Zekki
