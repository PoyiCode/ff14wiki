**Doman Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Doman Trout.

Run-of-the-mill trout found far and wide across Yanxia. Whoever said cats won't touch them has never owned a Doman cat.

Caught at: The Heron's Nest, The Heron's Way, Prism Canyon, Doma Castle, Mercantile Docks, The One River (East), The One River (West), Plum Spring, The Silver Canal, The Doman Enclave
