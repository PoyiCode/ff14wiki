**Morsel Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Morsel Trout.

This fish's name has origins in both the Pelupelu and Yok Huy languages, respectively referred to as “the filling fish” and “the fish of scant flesh.” The modern appellation attempts to strike a balance between the conflicting perspectives.

Caught at: Karvarhur the First
