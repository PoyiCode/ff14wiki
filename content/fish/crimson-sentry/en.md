**Crimson Sentry** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Crimson Sentry.

One of the two guardians of Shisui of the Violet Tides, this sentry has many times stained the sea red with the blood of those foolish enough to encroach upon the sacred halls.

Caught at: Ruby Spectral Current
