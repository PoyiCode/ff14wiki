**Quicksilver Blade** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Quicksilver Blade.

For centuries this wavekin was thought to be the stuff of legends, but recent encounters have shown it to be both very real and quite lethal. Speculation amongst anglers traces its name to accounts of a monster born of alchemy—an abomination of living liquid let loose by careless keepers to stalk the waters of Galadion Bay. Regardless, the danger of the creature itself remains cold, hard fact.

Caught at: Galadion Spectral Current
