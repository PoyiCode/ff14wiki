**Wimple Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Wimple Carp.

This wavekin has a characteristic white hood resembling the headdresses worn by women of a certain religious order. Rumor has it that wimple carp are actually white all over—their black stripes are merely the inky scrawls of tricksy pixies.

Caught at: Handmirror Lake
