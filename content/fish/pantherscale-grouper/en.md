**Pantherscale Grouper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pantherscale Grouper.

Similar to the beast from which its name derives, this wavekin's defining brown spots stretch across the whole of its body. In the grouper's case, however, said spots confuse potential predators by their semblance to eyes rather than acting as camouflage.
