title: "Specterfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Specterfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Specterfish.

Many sailors consider these fish to be an ill omen, likening their pallid, shimmering forms to the souls of those lost at sea. Such superstition is easily derided—until one witnesses the chilling sight of these nigh ethereal creatures undulating beneath the waves.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
