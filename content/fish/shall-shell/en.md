**Shall Shell** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shall Shell.

This enormous bivalve mollusk is easily recognizable by the unique wave pattern found on its outer shell. An old fisher's tale tells of a man who accidentally stepped in the mouth of one during low tide. Unable to move due to the shell's crushing force, the man eventually drowned when the tide came in.

Caught at: Candlekeep Quay, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Sterncastle), North Isle of Endless Summer
