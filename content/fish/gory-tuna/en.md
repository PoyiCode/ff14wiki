**Gory Tuna** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gory Tuna.

Any tuna that can survive and flourish in the cruelest of the Bloodbrine's currents is a rarity to be treasured—a notion reflected in the steep price one of these exceptional specimens commands when brought to auction in the fish markets.

Caught at: Bloodbrine Spectral Current
