**Chirwagur Loach** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Chirwagur Loach.

A slender cylindrical fish covered head to tail with fine grey scales. Already small, this wavekin shrinks even further when cooked whole, often leaving naught but salt-encrusted skin.

Caught at: Chirwagur Lake
