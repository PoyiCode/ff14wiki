**Functional Proto-hropken** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Functional Proto-hropken.

Markedly more developed than the proto-hropken, the slightest hint of intelligence can be sensed when staring into this abomination's eyes.

Caught at: The Flagship
