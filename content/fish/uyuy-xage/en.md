title: "Uyuy Xage"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Uyuy Xage** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Uyuy Xage.

In the Hanuhanu tongue, this serpent's name translates to “large egg”—a reference to the scalekin's ever-watchful state as it protects its eggs during hatching season. The reckless few who have approached a nesting uyuy xage and survived the encounter do not recommend the experience.

Caught at: Bopo'uihih
