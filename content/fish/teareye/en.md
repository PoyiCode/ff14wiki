title: "Teareye"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Teareye** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Teareye.

This wavekin, with its dark, star-shaped eyes, appears forever on the verge of tears. It is said that it weeps for its long-lost brethren, who traveled to Etheirys's surface as meteoric messengers long ago.

Caught at: Greatest Endsvale
