**Hippo Frog** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hippo Frog.

An amphibian with the terrible proclivity to swallow its prey whole—even should that prey be its equal in size. The sight of its wide-open mouth in the water is strikingly similar to that of the hippos known to inhabit the northern region of Thavnair.

Caught at: Giantsgall Grounds
