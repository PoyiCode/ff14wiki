**Hook Fish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hook Fish.

The bones of this wavekin are uncommonly sturdy and sharp, which led to them being used as hooks by the fishers of the Ruby Sea. Legends abound of the first angler whose mettle was a match for this fish, and each ship captain insists that their version of the tale is the truth.

Caught at: Onokoro
