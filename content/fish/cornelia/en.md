**Cornelia** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cornelia.

As pugilists the realm wide exchange tales of the mighty Cornelia—brawler of legend—so, too, do fishers speak of the cornelia—a boxing pleco who will only leave her place among the rocks when all other pleco have been sent down for the count.

Caught at: Lake Tahtotl
