---

**Shuckfin Dace** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shuckfin Dace.

Some scholars have likened this wavekin's form to an ear of corn. Indeed, the sight of these fish basking in the sun with their silk-like tailfins aquiver is akin to that of a golden field, ripe kernels ready to harvest.

Caught at: Ku'uxage
