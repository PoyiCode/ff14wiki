title: "Charibenet"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Charibenet** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Charibenet.

After witnessing the voracity with which it devoured its kin, it almost seemed wrong *not* to name this gluttonous ice faerie after the Holy See's infamous First Inquisitor.

Caught at: Clearpool
