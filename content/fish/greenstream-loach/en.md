**Greenstream Loach** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Greenstream Loach.

There's no prying this intractable flatfish loose once it deploys its ventral fin for suction, and its aptitude for withstanding the most violent of currents has rendered its name synonymous with obstinacy.

Caught at: The Velodyna River
