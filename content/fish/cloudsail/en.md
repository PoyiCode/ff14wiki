**Cloudsail** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cloudsail.

With its oversized dorsal fin and aerodynamic snout, this wavekin can be glimpsed soaring through the mists betwixt Yok and Xak Tural. Much like the clouds that blanket its natural habitat, the flesh of this toothsome fish is light and fluffy, making it a popular ingredient in a variety of local dishes.

Caught at: Eastbound Zorgor
