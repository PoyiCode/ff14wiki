**Yok Huy Stonecutter** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yok Huy Stonecutter.

This bladelike fish is said to resemble the implements used by Yok Huy quarry workers. Individuals with ragged fins are sarcastically likened to old tools sorely in need of a good honing.

Caught at: Sunken Stars
