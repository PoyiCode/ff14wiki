**Pixel Loach** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pixel Loach.

Reminiscent of the display panels utilized throughout Everkeep, the intricate patterns adorning this wavekin shift and shimmer as it swims. When exposed to an electrical current, these markings cycle through an exuberant myriad of colors, much to the delight of any onlookers.

Caught at: Residential Sector
