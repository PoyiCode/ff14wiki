**Hellyfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hellyfish.

Tormented soul that has ventured from the bowels of the underworld through a hellmouth to claim the lives of mortals with its virulent venom. Petting not recommended.

Caught at: Hells' Lid, The Isle of Bekko
