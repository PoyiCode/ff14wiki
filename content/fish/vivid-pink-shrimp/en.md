**Vivid Pink Shrimp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Vivid Pink Shrimp.

Aether-rich spectral currents are believed to be the secret of this shrimp's brilliant pink shade, as well as its exceedingly resilient shell. Indeed, the crustacean's carapace is so strong that shipwrights often fashion its legs and pincers into nails for their vessels.

Caught at: Sirensong Spectral Current
