**Rosebud Frog** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rosebud Frog.

Legend has it that this amphibian first emerged into the world from a red rosebud. Given the species' love of nectar, scholars have surmised that whoever was responsible for the tale simply misinterpreted the sight of a young frog enjoying a fragrant meal.

Caught at: The Knowable, Mu Springs Eternal, Leynode Aero
