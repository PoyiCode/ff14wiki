**River Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: River Crab.

A tiny freshwater crustacean commonly found in rivers throughout Eorzea. Known to feed on dead fish and algae, it is believed that if the river crab were ever to disappear, the realm's waters would quickly become fouled with corruption.

Caught at: Sweetbloom Pier, Verdant Drop, Springripple Brook, Sanctum of the Twelve, Upper Hathoeva River, Murmur Rills, Woad Whisper Canyon, Oakwood, Fool Falls, Bronze Lake Shallows
