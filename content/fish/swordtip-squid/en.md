**Swordtip Squid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Swordtip Squid.

The sleek, clear body of this wavekin has inspired countless swordsmiths during their seaborne journeys. Opinions are divided on whether the dried swordtip squid or dried spear squid is tastier, with one's martial background apparently being the main indicator of preference.

Caught at: Kugane Spectral Current
