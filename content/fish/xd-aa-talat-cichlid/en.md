**Xd'aa Talat Cichlid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Xd'aa Talat Cichlid.

According to Xbr'aal legend, this cichlid—native to Yak T'el—is favored by the spirit of the tsolys, who bestowed the wavekin with their enchanting coloration and graceful demeanor.

Caught at: Xd'aa Talat Tsoly
