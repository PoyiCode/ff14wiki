**Shined Copper Shark** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shined Copper Shark.

This gargantuan shark gleams with the brilliance of burnished copper. Some believe this luster results from a diet rich in Endless and their aether.

Caught at: Canal Town North
