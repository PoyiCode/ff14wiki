**Hellsnow Shark** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hellsnow Shark.

It is said that a traveling Eorzean angler once spied a school of these airborne sharks while crossing Zorgor the Boundless. Deathly afeared of the wavekin's Abalathian cousins, the angler cried, “Oh, *hells* no!”—an utterance rarely used in Tural—and promptly ran the other way. Witnesses to this event assumed he was referring to the sharks, and thus was history written.

Caught at: Eastbound Zorgor
