**Sky Sweeper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sky Sweeper.

Sky sweepers are one of the few creatures living in the Sea of Clouds that do not actually have the capability of flight. These sharklike beings are spawned on the backs of larger flying fish, where they spend their entire lives feeding on vilekin and sloughed-off skin.

Caught at: The Blue Window, The Habisphere
