**Balloonfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Balloonfish.

A saltwater fish covered in scores of sharp spines, discouraging would-be predators from dining on this otherwise defenseless fry. Some fishers will also call them “sea bombs,” though none have ever been known to self-destruct.

Caught at: North Bloodshore, Halfstone, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Forecastle)
