**Crepe Sole** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Crepe Sole.

A royal servant once remarked that this fish was astonishingly similar to an item of footwear, specifically a delicate sandal inadvertently kicked into the ocean by an overly excited sultan of Ul'dah.

Caught at: Rothlyt Spectral Current
