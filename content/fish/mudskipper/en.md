**Mudskipper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mudskipper.

An amphibious fish which can survive for bells out of water as long as its skin remains moist. When on land, the mudskipper uses its pectoral fins to “wander” about in search of shelter. Because of this trait, the fish is sometimes associated with the god Oschon.

Caught at: Hopeseed Pond, The Footfalls, The Unholy Heir, North Drybone, South Drybone, Haukke Manor
