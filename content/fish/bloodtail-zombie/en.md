**Bloodtail Zombie** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bloodtail Zombie.

Local fisher legend has that this catfish died choking on a meal too large to slide down its gullet...only to return to the river of its unholy spawning to feed on the brains of lesser fries.

Caught at: The Outer Fist
