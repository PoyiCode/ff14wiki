**Winged Hatchetfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Winged Hatchetfish.

This wavekin's bloated belly and long tail lend it the silhouette of a hand axe, leading the metal-smelting dwarves of Kholusia to venerate it as a guardian spirit of the toolcrafting industry.

Caught at: Upper Watts River
