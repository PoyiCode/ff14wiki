**Oracular Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Oracular Crab.

A particularly large variant of the bloodpolish crab said to appear with the spectral current. Its habit of cramming the shredded pieces of prey into a chosen hollow has been interpreted by some as a ritual offering to the all-seeing spirits of the deep.

Caught at: Bloodbrine Spectral Current
