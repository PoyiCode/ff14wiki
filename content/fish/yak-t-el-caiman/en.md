**Yak T'el Caiman** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yak T'el Caiman.

It is believed that this sizable beast uses underground waterways to travel here from the ocean. It seizes its prey in its massive jaws and disappears beneath the surface, never to be seen again.

Caught at: Bitterbark Cenote
