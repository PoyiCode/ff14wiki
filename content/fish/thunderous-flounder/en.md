**Thunderous Flounder** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Thunderous Flounder.

When the scales of this flat fish catch the light, they appear to coruscate with electrical energy. This startling effect is all the more pronounced when lightning strikes nearby.

Caught at: Crackling Canyons
