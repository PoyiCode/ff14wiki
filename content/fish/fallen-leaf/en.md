**Fallen Leaf** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Fallen Leaf.

If a tree falls in the woods, and there is no one there to see it, do the leaves that fall in water become possessed by migrant spirits and generate a new species of fish?

Caught at: Dimwold
