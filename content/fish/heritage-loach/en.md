**Heritage Loach** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Heritage Loach.

This lithe, slender wavekin has long been beloved by the inhabitants of Heritage Found. Capable of readily adapting to even the most severe environmental changes, it has survived numerous calamities in which other, less hardy species have perished.

Caught at: Outskirts Shallows, The Driftdowns
