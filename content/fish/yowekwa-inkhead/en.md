**Yowekwa Inkhead** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yowekwa Inkhead.

Squid native to the waters beneath Zorgor the Boundless. While the Tonawawta have used its ink as pigment since days of eld, its flesh is considered inedible by even the most ambitious epicureans.

Caught at: Westbound Zorgor
