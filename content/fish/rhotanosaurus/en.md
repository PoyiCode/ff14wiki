summary: "A fish of Eorzea."

**Rhotanosaurus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rhotanosaurus.

Unlike its mosasaur cousins that stalk the waters of inland Gyr Abania, this scalekin freely roams the realm's oceans. Despite presiding over a much larger habitat, its innate ferocity remains unchanged.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
