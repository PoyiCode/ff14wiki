**Clavekeeper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Clavekeeper.

This singular species of moray dwells primarily on the coast of Kholusia, and is so named for its inexplicable habit of threatening anyone or anything which approaches the Clave. While most consider this behavior passing strange, others regard these self-appointed guardians with a degree of reverence.

Caught at: Seagazer Shoals
