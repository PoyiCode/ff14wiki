**Panoptes** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Panoptes.

The plethora of white spots on this ray's back appear as a host of unblinking eyes—an illusion so beguiling that superstitious sailors once spread rumors of a ghostly, aquatic voidsent.

Caught at: Rothlyt Spectral Current
