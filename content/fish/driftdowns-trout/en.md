**Driftdowns Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Driftdowns Trout.

This trout species somehow survives in the stagnant, waste-filled sediment of the driftdowns. Unsurprisingly, its highly polluted flesh is entirely unsuitable for consumption.

Caught at: The Driftdowns
