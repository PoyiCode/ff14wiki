**Pearl Bombfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pearl Bombfish.

While it floats in the aether-dense waters of the spectral current, the spine-covered belly of this balloonfish glimmers like mother-of-pearl. A return to more mundane seas, however, soon sees that glorious radiance fade to a sickly shade of beige.

Caught at: Rothlyt Spectral Current
