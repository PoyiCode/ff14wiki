**Shirogane Clam** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shirogane Clam.

Not only does this mollusk produce invaluable pearls, but its shells are treasured by goldsmiths across Hingashi for their unmatched luster.

Caught at: Isari
