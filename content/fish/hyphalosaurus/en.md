**Hyphalosaurus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hyphalosaurus.

Aptly named the “submerged lizard,” this lanky specimen utilizes its flat, elongated tail as a fin, allowing it to glide through the freshwater depths with relative ease.

Caught at: Deepmoat
