**King Cobrafish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: King Cobrafish.

Once it has impaled a victim with its long, venom-filled fangs, this underwater potentate will then coil around its hapless prey and settle down for a royal feast. Hail to the king.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Margin
