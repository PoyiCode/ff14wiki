**Bloody Brewer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bloody Brewer.

Whereas the common pipira is satisfied with a bit of skin from your ankles, the bloody brewer will not stop until it paints the waters of Black Tea Brook a bright crimson.

Caught at: Lower Black Tea Brook
