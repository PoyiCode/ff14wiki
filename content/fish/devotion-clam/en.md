**Devotion Clam** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Devotion Clam.

A beige bivalve originally hailing from a region of expansive shallows. This highly nutritious and exquisitely flavorsome creature was once a frequent fixture on dining tables across Alexandria.

Caught at: Canal Town North
