**Spring Urchin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spring Urchin.

Though found solely in the hyperborean reaches of Eorzea, the spring urchin can only survive in the heated waters of geothermal springs. A good number of the shrill howls that fill the Coerthan nights are from yetis who mistakenly step on the urchins' spiked shells when wading through the natural baths.

Caught at: Dragonspit
