**Spectresaur** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spectresaur.

As if a hulking scalekin with a fang-filled maw wasn't terrifying enough, this spectral harbinger appears to be warped by a surfeit of aetheric power.

Caught at: Outer Rothlyt Sound
