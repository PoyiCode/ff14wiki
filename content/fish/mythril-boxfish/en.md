**Mythril Boxfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mythril Boxfish.

The metallic sheen of this fish's scales has been intensified by an inundation of aether. Fisherfolk will sometimes fashion its remains into a box in truth, the containers favored for carrying hooks, pretty seashells, and other such valuables.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
