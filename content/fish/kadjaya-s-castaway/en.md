**Kadjaya's Castaway** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Kadjaya's Castaway.

Found twirling endlessly in the waters of the Giantsgall Grounds, legend has it these wavekin were once dancers who dreamt of performing in Kadjaya's Footsteps before suffering a fall from grace.

Caught at: Giantsgall Grounds
