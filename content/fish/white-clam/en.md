**White Clam** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: White Clam.

In Far Eastern legend, this eye-catching mollusk is fabled to project its aura as a shimmering mirage above the water. Those who procure such wondrous shells are known to treasure them as incense holders, often gilding the interior with gold or other precious metals.

Caught at: Mercantile Docks
