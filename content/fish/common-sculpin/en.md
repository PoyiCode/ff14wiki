**Common Sculpin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Common Sculpin.

Found across Eorzea, this freshwater fish has highly developed fins and spines which not only protect the wavekin, but serve as weapons for hunting its prey which includes insects and other creatures.

Caught at: Sylphlands, The Weeping Saint, Oakwood, Bluefog
