**Oily Ropefish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Oily Ropefish.

This wavekin secretes a coat of oily mucus that allows it to adapt to subtle changes in water temperature, and is prized by artisans for its utility in the production of heavy-duty lubricants.

Caught at: Bopo'uihih
