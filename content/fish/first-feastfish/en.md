**First Feastfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: First Feastfish.

According to legend, a member of this species was the very first morsel consumed by the ancient Yok Huy progenitor. Given this wavekin's high nutritional value, one can commend the giant's decision after emerging—presumably famished—from the primordial lake.

Caught at: Karvarhur the First
