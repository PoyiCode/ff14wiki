**Goldgrouper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Goldgrouper.

A resplendent golden wavekin found amongst the submerged ruins of Alexandria. The excess lightning energy that permeates the area has imbued its scales with metallic strength and a chrome-like sheen.

Caught at: Alexandrian Ruins
