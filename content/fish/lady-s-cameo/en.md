**Lady's Cameo** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lady's Cameo.

So beautiful did folk find the silvery spiral of this conch that they likened it to a shimmering jewel—specifically the priceless one which slipped through the careless fingers of Princess Edvya and bounced into the ocean as she skipped across the deck of the royal galleon.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Margin
