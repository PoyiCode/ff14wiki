**Golden Day** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Golden Day.

This wavekin and its dull golden scales has become a symbol of nostalgic reminiscence—calling to mind the faded glory of bygone days. Despite the somber association, the sight of this charming fish has soothed many an onlooker over the years.

Caught at: Proto Alexandria
