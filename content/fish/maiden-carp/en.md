**Maiden Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Maiden Carp.

Large schools of these dark silver fish can be found in the White Maiden and her tributaries. Their tendency to gather in great numbers makes them quite easy to catch, and as such they have been a staple for those living in the Twelveswood since the time of the first settlers.

Caught at: The Vein, The Mirror, Sweetbloom Pier, Springripple Brook, Murmur Rills, Fallgourd Float, Coerthas River, Nym River, The Mourning Widow, Nophica's Wells, Upper Soot Creek, Lower Soot Creek, The Unholy Heir, Whispering Gorge, Jadeite Flood, Lower Black Tea Brook, The Clutch, Upper Black Tea Brook
