**Canal Drum** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Canal Drum.

In days gone by, the thoroughfares of Canal Town would often be thronged with crowds watching one of the resort's many musical troupes. This rounded wavekin takes its name from the drums used in such performances. Appellation aside, tapping out a triplet or two on its scales is discouraged.

Caught at: Canal Town South
