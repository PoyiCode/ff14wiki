**Glass Tuna** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Glass Tuna.

With a taste as smooth and crystal clear as its name, one bite of this tuna will have you half-full of optimism.

Caught at: Kugane Piers, The Isle of Bekko, Shirogane
