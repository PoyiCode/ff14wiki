**Silkfin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Silkfin.

This subspecies is revered as a sign of good fortune in fishing villages across Thavnair. Its boldly patterned flanks are easily visible from above the water's surface, and sightings are said to precipitate a bumper catch for the day.

Caught at: Thavnair Margin
