title: "Warmwater Trout"

---

**Warmwater Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Warmwater Trout.

This freshwater fish can only survive in tepid waters such as those heated by the flows of lava coursing beneath the mighty volcano O'Ghomoro or the desert sun of Thanalan.

Caught at: Oakwood, Fool Falls, Yugr'am River, Burnt Lizard Creek, Forgotten Springs, The Long Climb
