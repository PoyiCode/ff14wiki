**Marrow Sucker** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Marrow Sucker.

Nature has bestowed this freshwater snail with prodigious size and a powerful suction to match. Any carrion it chances upon is cleaned inside out.

Caught at: North Drybone
