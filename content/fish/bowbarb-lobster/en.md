**Bowbarb Lobster** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bowbarb Lobster.

Characterized by its long whiskers, a male bowbarb lobster placed upon its back and viewed from exactly the right angle resembles a bowman taking aim.

Caught at: Ruby Spectral Current
