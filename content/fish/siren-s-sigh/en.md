**Siren's Sigh** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Siren's Sigh.

Often spotted congregating around wreckage on the ocean floor, these fish were named for the sirens thought to lure ships to their demise.

Caught at: Sirensong Spectral Current
