**Bopo'u Sleeper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bopo'u Sleeper.

Upon encountering this fish submerged in the mud, novice naturalists may mistake its inactivity for drowsiness. In actual fact, this wavekin has perfected the art of remaining perfectly still—waiting for hapless prey to come to *it*.

Caught at: Bopo'uihih, Waters Hanu, Miyakabek'zu
