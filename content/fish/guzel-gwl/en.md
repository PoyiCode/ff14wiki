**Guzel Gwl** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Guzel Gwl.

Exposure to spectral currents seems to have further heightened this crustacean's already exceptional beauty—and taste. This pedigree is unambiguously reflected in its name: “resplendent rose,” as well as the fact it is routinely served at the satrap's palace.

Caught at: Thavnair Spectral Current
