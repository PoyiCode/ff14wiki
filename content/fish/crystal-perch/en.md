**Crystal Perch** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Crystal Perch.

A rare specimen of glass perch that has attained even greater clarity in its transparent body. The play of light it produces when held to sunlight is a marvel to behold.

Caught at: Woad Whisper Canyon
