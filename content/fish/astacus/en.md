**Astacus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Astacus.

Yet another variety of the already ubiquitous crayfish, this wavekin, boiled in salt water, can oft be found upon Sharlayan dining tables in the early days of summer.

Caught at: Oinops, Reflecting Pool
