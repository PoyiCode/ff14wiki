**Marbled Hatchetfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Marbled Hatchetfish.

The Yok Huy tell of a mighty warrior of yore who, beset by grief, cast down their axe. The weapon transformed into a majestic wavekin bearing marbled scales, its intricate patterns a manifestation of the warrior's agony.

Caught at: Sunken Stars
