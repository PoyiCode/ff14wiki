**Cave Killifish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cave Killifish.

Rugged little fry with the wherewithal to thrive in even the dankest, darkest subterranean climes, though it is unclear how they ever got there in the first place.

Caught at: Dimwold
