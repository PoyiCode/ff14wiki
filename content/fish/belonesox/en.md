**Belonesox** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Belonesox.

The dark brown scales adorning this wavekin's back fade to a glistening white towards its belly. Compared to the majority of other killifish, this particular species can grow to an impressive size—as large as a Xbr'aal's hand in some cases.

Caught at: Xd'aa Talat Tsoly
