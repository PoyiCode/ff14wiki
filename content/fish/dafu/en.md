title: "Dafu"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Dafu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dafu.

From an ancient Far Eastern term for “genius” or “master of its trade,” its name speaks to the inspiring artistry on display in this magnificent specimen.

Caught at: The Isle of Bekko
