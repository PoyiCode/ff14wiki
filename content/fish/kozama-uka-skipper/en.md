**Kozama'uka Skipper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Kozama'uka Skipper.

This agile fish leaps between waterways with its broad, sail-like fins, constantly on the hunt for new sources of insect prey—or perhaps a mere change of scenery.

Caught at: Bopo'uihih, Waters Hanu, Miyakabek'zu
