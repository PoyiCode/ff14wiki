**Othardian Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Othardian Trout.

A familiar face anywhere you journey on the subcontinent, its ubiquity has led to innumerable interpretations of a multitude of dishes all across Othard. Indeed nowhere is safe for this fish.

Caught at: Nem Khaal, Azim Khaat, Lower Yat Khaal
