**Faerie Bass** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Faerie Bass.

A common freshwater fish found in rivers and lakes in almost every corner of Eorzea. A staple for many of the realm's smallfolk.

Caught at: Upper Hathoeva River, Lower Hathoeva River, East Hathoeva River, Fallgourd Float, Empty Heart, Oakwood, The Footfalls, North Drybone, South Drybone, Bronze Lake Shallows, The Goblet
