**Ignus Snail** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ignus Snail.

The fiery red shells of this freshwater snail, also known as “Ifrit's claws,” are prized by the Amalj'aa, and are useful when bartering with the beast tribe.

Caught at: North Silvertear, Rathefrost, The Deep Tangle, The Burning Wall, Zahar'ak, Ceruleum Field, Bluefog, The North Shards, Parata's Peace, The Long Climb
