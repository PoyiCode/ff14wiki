**Permit** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Permit.

This cautious wavekin's broad silver body is speckled with inky black spots. The slightest hint of a predator is enough to send this timid specimen darting off to the relative safety of a rocky crevasse or other shaded spot.

Caught at: The For'ard Cabins
