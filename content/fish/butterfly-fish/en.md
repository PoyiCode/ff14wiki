**Butterfly Fish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Butterfly Fish.

Though indigenous to southern waters, now and again a papilliferous wanderer will lose its way and flutter mindlessly into the Ruby Sea.

Caught at: Shoal Rock
