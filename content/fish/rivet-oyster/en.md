**Rivet Oyster** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rivet Oyster.

An enormous oyster so named for the tenacity with which it clings on to rock. Those who desire to catch it must be prepared to pull up entire outcroppings.

Caught at: Vesper Bay
