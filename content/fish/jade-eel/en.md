**Jade Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Jade Eel.

Similar to salmon, this green-grey freshwater eel lives the majority of its life in the salty waters of the sea, returning to inland rivers to only spawn and die.

Caught at: Oakwood, Fool Falls, Northeast Bronze Lake, Bronze Lake Shallows, Northwest Bronze Lake
