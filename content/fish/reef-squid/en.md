**Reef Squid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Reef Squid.

Though these wavekin's migration pattern covers most Far Eastern waters, it is to the Ruby Sea's rocky reefs that they return to deposit their eggs. Relatively average as far as stature is concerned, reef squid typically grow to two fulms at the longest.

Caught at: Open Ruby Sea
