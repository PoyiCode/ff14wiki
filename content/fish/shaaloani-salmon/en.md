**Shaaloani Salmon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shaaloani Salmon.

During spawn season, its skin turns bright red to attract a mate. Jerky made from this salmon has long been prized as a nonperishable staple in Shaaloani.

Caught at: Niikwerepi
