**Clown Loach** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Clown Loach.

Bright patterns, similar to a clown's motley, cover this small freshwater fish from head to tail.

Caught at: Hopeseed Pond, Red Mantis Falls, Zahar'ak, Forgotten Springs
