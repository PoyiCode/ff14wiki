**River Barramundi** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: River Barramundi.

Despite its name, phylogeny suggests that this fish is actually more carp than barramundi, though the herons and other avians that feed off of it don't seem to mind.

Caught at: The Heron's Way
