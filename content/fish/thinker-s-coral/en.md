summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Thinker's Coral** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Thinker's Coral.

In light of its origin, perhaps it is unsurprising that this coral is thought to be imbued with Thaliak's wisdom. Many even believe that giving a chunk of it a good lick will grant the taster a flash of inspiration—a practice that is both highly frowned upon and widely perpetuated in scholarly circles.

Caught at: Thaliak River
