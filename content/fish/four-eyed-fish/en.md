---
title: "Four-eyed Fish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Four-eyed Fish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Four-eyed Fish.

Four-eyed fish do not actually have four eyes, but two eyes that are divided into an upper and lower portion that allow the wavekin to see both above and below the water at the same time. Contrary to popular belief, eating one will not improve or restore eyesight.

Caught at: Oakwood, Fool Falls, Northeast Bronze Lake, Northwest Bronze Lake
