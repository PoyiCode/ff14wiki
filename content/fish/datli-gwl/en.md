**Datli Gwl** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Datli Gwl.

In the old tongue of Thavnair, this handsome crab's name means “sweet rose.” Although naturally poisonous, a judicious boiling will neutralize the toxins, providing for a safe, delectable feast.

Caught at: Thavnair Margin
