**Deep Canopy** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Deep Canopy.

The rhomboid form of this pale yellow fish closely resembles fallen foliage. Indeed, local folklore tells of leaves borne on the wind morphing into wavekin after alighting on the surface of the Iq Br'aax reservoir.

Caught at: Iq Br'aax Reservoir
