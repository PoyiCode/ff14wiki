**Honeycomb Sponge** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Honeycomb Sponge.

This aquatic invertebrate was named for the hexagonal pores in its spongin mesh. Once its living cells have been cruelly stripped away, its skeletal remains can be used to create luxurious lathers with the soap of one's choice.

Caught at: Miyakabek'zoma
