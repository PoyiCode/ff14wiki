**Death Loach** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Death Loach.

This fish gives new meaning to “the sweet kiss of death,” as wars have been waged over its delectable flesh, hence its fateful name.

Caught at: Timmon Beck, Grymm & Enid, The Slow Wash, Heather Falls, The Bull's Bath
