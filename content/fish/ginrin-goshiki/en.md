**Ginrin Goshiki** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ginrin Goshiki.

Boasting scales of deep vermilion and silver, this paragon among carp makes its abode within spectral currents, thus its vivid coloration. From the water's surface, the wavekin reflects light much like a glimmering gold ingot, and as a result, many an unknowing sailor has fallen overboard in pursuit of false riches.

Caught at: One River Spectral Current
