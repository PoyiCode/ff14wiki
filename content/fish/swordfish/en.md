title: "Swordfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Swordfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Swordfish.

Fishermen willing to cross blades with this sharp-nosed billfish are in for a scrumptious treat, should they survive the battle.

Caught at: Onokoro
