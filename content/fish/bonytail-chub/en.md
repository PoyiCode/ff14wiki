**Bonytail Chub** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bonytail Chub.

In contrast with their stout heads and bodies, these fish have extremely thin tails—some little more than skin and bone, hence the name. The caudal fin is forked deeply.

Caught at: Niikwerepi, Lake Toari
