**Starsnipper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Starsnipper.

When night falls, this tiny crustacean scuttles forth, brandishing its pincers, seemingly trying to snip at the stars reflected on the water's surface. One can only begin to fathom what drove the crab to its unceasing battle with the firmament.

Caught at: Sunken Stars
