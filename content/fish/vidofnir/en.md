**Vidofnir** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Vidofnir.

Incomparable size combined with a decided lack of pigment in this noontide oscar's skin invokes comparisons to the great white dragon, Vidofnir.

Caught at: Eil Tohm
