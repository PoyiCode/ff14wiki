**Garjana Wrasse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Garjana Wrasse.

The sensation experienced when touching this wrasse's scales is akin to that of being kissed by lightning, giving rise to its name. No doubt related is how the wavekin causes disturbances to aetherial currents, particularly when gathered with others of its kind.

Caught at: The Great Runoff
