**Senbei Octopus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Senbei Octopus.

When fished from the sea, this octopus folds its body into a shape not unlike the round crackers popular in the Far East. As an inevitable consequence, many a sailor has been caught by surprise when their late-night snack transformed into a ball of writhing tentacles.

Caught at: Ruby Spectral Current
