**Checkered Cichlid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Checkered Cichlid.

Also known as the “wicker fish” due to its alternating dark and light pattern resembling a type of fish basket woven by Mamool Ja crafters.

Caught at: Cenote Moxutural
