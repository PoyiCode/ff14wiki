---
title: "Archerfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Archerfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Archerfish.

When feeding, the archerfish will lift its head ever so slightly above a lake or river's surface and spit a stream of water up to a yalm to strike flies from low-lying branches into the water with remarkable accuracy.

Caught at: East Agelyss River, Raincatcher Gully
