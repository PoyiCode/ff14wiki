**Jetborne Manta** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Jetborne Manta.

The tremendous speed at which this manta glides along can only be explained by arcane enhancement. The prevailing theory suggests some manner of adaptation in its gills which allows the wavekin to convert the aetherial current into a preternatural propulsive force.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
