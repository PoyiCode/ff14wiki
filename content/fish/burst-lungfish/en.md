**Burst Lungfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Burst Lungfish.

This remarkable species breathes by means of lungs, perhaps due to the shoals where it resides. Exhalations are summarily absorbed by the body, which creates enough carbonic acid to trigger a popping noise upon exposure to air.

Caught at: The Driftdowns
