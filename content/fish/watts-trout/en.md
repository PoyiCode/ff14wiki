**Watts Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Watts Trout.

A familiar face to all those living along Watts River, this fish has long been an important source of protein for the dwarves who dwell there. When a fisher catches a Watts trout, it is customary to call out its name—the traditional reply being “You should know, you've just caught one.”

Caught at: Upper Watts River, White Oil Falls, Lower Watts River
