**Gyr Abanian Chub** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gyr Abanian Chub.

When spawning, the head and tail of the Gyr Abanian chub take on a lustrous hue. It is commonly believed that this change in color drastically improves the fish's otherwise mundane flavor.

Caught at: Timmon Beck
