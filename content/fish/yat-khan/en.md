**Yat Khan** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yat Khan.

Is there none who would dispute this goby's claim to the rule of Yat Khaal? If you answered “no,” you would be correct.

Caught at: Lower Yat Khaal
