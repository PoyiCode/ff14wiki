**Pumpkin Perch** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pumpkin Perch.

According to Tonawawtan legend, this almost frighteningly fecund fish was born from a pumpkin seed cast into the river. Attempts to recreate the fable have invariably failed to produce results.

Caught at: Outskirts Shallows
