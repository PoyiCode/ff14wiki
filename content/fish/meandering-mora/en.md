**Meandering Mora** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Meandering Mora.

This enormously rotund variety of sunfish does not swim so much as allow itself to be swept along. Meetings between potential mates occur only at the whim of the currents, leading many to wonder how the species has not already drifted into extinction.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
