**Sea Alpaca** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sea Alpaca.

These wavekin favor the briniest waters, inhabiting inland lakes that were once contiguous with the ocean. Naturalists saw fit to name them for the Pelupelu's popular pack animal—though they are nowhere near as fuzzy as their mammalian counterparts.

Caught at: Sunken Stars
