**Crimson Kelp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Crimson Kelp.

A number of Far Eastern gastronomical experts rate crimson kelp among the most indispensable ingredients in Far Eastern cuisine. Prepared in a multitude of mouthwatering ways, it sees use in stock, seasoning, and as a dish unto itself.

Caught at: Open Ruby Sea
