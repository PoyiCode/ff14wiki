**Butterfly Ray** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Butterfly Ray.

This elongated oval ray looks like an oversized butterfly come to rest with its wings unfurled. Referencing the wavekin's color, some needlessly petty naturalists insist that it actually bears closer resemblance to a moth.

Caught at: Canal Town South
