**Unidentified Flying Biomass II** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Unidentified Flying Biomass II.

Given its high polymer resinoid shell is so dense that it remains nigh impossible to open, some theorize that UFB-II is in fact a ship within which reside countless sentient—though nevertheless delicious—beings.

Caught at: Limne 18, Limne 3-α, Limne 3-β
