**Sea Cucumber** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sea Cucumber.

A purple, spiny, sluglike creature found in the shallows off of the island of Mazlaya, and treasured in the Far East as a delicacy.

Caught at: Zephyr Drift, Moraby Bay, Oschon's Torch, The Salt Strand
