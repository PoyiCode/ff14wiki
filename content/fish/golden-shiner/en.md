**Golden Shiner** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Golden Shiner.

Created strictly for aesthetic purposes, this wavekin's glimmering scales cast a brilliant effect across the water's surface, rendering the fish's own beauty sadly dull by comparison.

Caught at: Rumination's Ramble
