**Zorlortor** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Zorlortor.

Said to have been named after a cowardly Yok Huy, this timid silver amphibian is often seen paddling around saltwater pools. One can only imagine the burning shame Zorlortor must have felt being associated with such a creature.

Caught at: Chirwagur Lake
