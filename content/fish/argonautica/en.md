**Argonautica** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Argonautica.

As most of the sumptuous wavekin are snatched up by Far Eastern fishers, rarely does a snake eel reach the size of this monstrous serpent.

Caught at: Onokoro
