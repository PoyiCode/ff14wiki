**Zeh Tortoh** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Zeh Tortoh.

Roughly translated, the Yok Huy name of this wavekin means “fish of never-ending blooms.” A fitting name for a creature bearing such a close resemblance to a delicate petal.

Caught at: Karvarhur the First
