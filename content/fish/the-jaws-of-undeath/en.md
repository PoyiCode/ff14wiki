**The Jaws of Undeath** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: The Jaws of Undeath.

Researchers have termed their imposing nests “fortresses,” a word that, while perhaps grandiose, befits their makers' position at the top of the food chain. Woe betide any who encroaches upon one of these “fortresses,” for its castellan will defend it as fiercely as would any on land.

Caught at: The Isle of Ken
