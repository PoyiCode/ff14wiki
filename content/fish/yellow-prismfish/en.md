summary: "A fish of Eorzea."

**Yellow Prismfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yellow Prismfish.

Scales that absorb a citrine pigment in the waters of Prism Lake to glimmer like gold. As for its flavor, one is reminded of the age-old adage “All that glitters isn't food.”

Caught at: Prism Lake
