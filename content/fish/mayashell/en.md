**Mayashell** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mayashell.

This ancient turtle has long bathed in the maya of its aquatic habitat, making even a pinch of its powdered shell a surpassing source of alchemical power.

Caught at: The Font of Maya
