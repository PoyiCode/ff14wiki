**Beguiler Chub** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Beguiler Chub.

This rare variety of chub is so named for the peculiar habit of retaining its radiant hues even after the breeding season has ended, leading to many a confounded fish.

Caught at: Rogue River
