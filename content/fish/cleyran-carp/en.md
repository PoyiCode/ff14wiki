**Cleyran Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cleyran Carp.

This jet black carp is one of a few surviving specimens from Cleyra. Sadly, many other wavekin native to the region now exist only as entries in textbooks and scholarly journals.

Caught at: Mu Springs Eternal
