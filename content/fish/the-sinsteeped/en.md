**The Sinsteeped** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: The Sinsteeped.

During spawning season, this wavekin becomes enveloped in milky eggs. A small panic was caused when a citizen of the Crystarium mistook a pregnant fish for a waterborne sin eater, and although the mistake was soon realized, the incident earned the creature this ominous moniker forevermore.

Caught at: The Quadrivium
