**Yak T'el Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yak T'el Catfish.

This wavekin puts the “cat” in “catfish,” as they hold a natural curiosity toward people, and many Mamool Ja like to spoil them with treats.

Caught at: Ankledeep, Cenote Moxutural, Cenote Jayunja, Sapsweet Cenote, Bitterbark Cenote
