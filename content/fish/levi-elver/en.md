title: "Levi Elver"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Levi Elver** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Levi Elver.

These large eels are held sacred by the Sahagin, who believe them to be the children of Leviathan. The same eels are quietly eaten by Rothlyt Sound sailors, who believe them to be delicious (and auspicious for safe voyages).

Caught at: Rothlyt Spectral Current
