**Scarlet Queen** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Scarlet Queen.

This beautiful, selectively bred species was awarded to the royal family upon Queen Sphene's resurrection as an Endless.

Caught at: Proto Alexandria
