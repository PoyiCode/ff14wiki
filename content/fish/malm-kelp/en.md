**Malm Kelp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Malm Kelp.

This dark green variety of seaweed found in all five of Eorzea's seas has been known to grow up to a malm (1760 yalms) in length.

Caught at: Limsa Lominsa Lower Decks, Limsa Lominsa Upper Decks, Moraby Bay
