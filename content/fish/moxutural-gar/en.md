**Moxutural Gar** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Moxutural Gar.

On rare occasions, this species rises to the surface for air. Some have suggested perhaps these sociable creatures have grown lonely in the time since Moxutural Zooj was abandoned.

Caught at: Cenote Moxutural
