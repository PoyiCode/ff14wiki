**Eunuch Crayfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Eunuch Crayfish.

Thought to be born of blood spilled by Azeyma, the Warden, the grip of this tiny freshwater crustacean's claw is so strong, it has been the end of many a Lalafell's fingers...and more. Take care when handling.

Caught at: The Vein, Sweetbloom Pier, Springripple Brook, Upper Hathoeva River, Lower Hathoeva River, East Hathoeva River, Woad Whisper Canyon, Empty Heart, Nophica's Wells, The Footfalls, The Goblet
