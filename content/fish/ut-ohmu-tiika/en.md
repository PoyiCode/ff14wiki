---
title: "Ut'ohmu Tiika"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Ut'ohmu Tiika** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ut'ohmu Tiika.

Its name is a union of two tongues: the Xbr'aal “ut'ohmu” and the Mamool Ja “tiika.” That the Mamool Ja chose this appellation speaks volumes to their yearning for the forest above.

Caught at: Xty'iinbek Tsoly
