**Goldfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Goldfish.

The brilliant luster of this freshwater fish native to the Far East has made it a popular addition to the aquariums and garden ponds of Ul'dahn patricians. Once a rarity, recent overbreeding has seen a sharp drop in the market price for these wavekin.

Caught at: Lake Tahtotl, North Silvertear, Rathefrost, Red Mantis Falls, Ceruleum Field, The North Shards, The Goblet
