title: "Tripod Fish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Tripod Fish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tripod Fish.

The sturdy fins of this fish maintain their firmness even bells after they have been wrested from the seas. In fact, by measuring the distance of each fin from the other, many say you can form a triangle whose proportions are as precise as any an arcanist could devise.

Caught at: The Northern Strait of Merlthor
