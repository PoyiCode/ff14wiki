**Rhalgr's Bolt** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rhalgr's Bolt.

When this electric eel shocks and awes its prey like a god of thunder, the victim rocks and rolls, and the spell it's under slowly robs it of its soul.

Caught at: Mirage Creek
