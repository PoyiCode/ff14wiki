**Dravanian Squeaker** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dravanian Squeaker.

A unique variety of brightly mottled catfish which swims upside down in order to confuse both prey and predators.

Caught at: Weston Waters
