**Ghostfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ghostfish.

The silver scales of this saltwater fish are highly reflective, endowing it with the illusion of transparency. Known to be inquisitive, it is often seen swimming to the surface when people are present.

Caught at: The For'ard Cabins
