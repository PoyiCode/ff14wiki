**Black Jet** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Black Jet.

Said to be stained black by the blood of their slain caretakers, these fish swim with a fervor only those who have fled for their lives know. Just three are enough to turn a river's tide—would that they could turn the tide of battle as well.

Caught at: The Lozatl
