title: "Protoflesh"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Protoflesh** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Protoflesh.

Developed by the Ea to test the viability of returning to flesh-based bodies, this specimen was likely modeled after one of the few organic beings its creators managed to remember.

Caught at: The Wellspring of Regret, Abode of the Ea, Elysian Fields, Elysian Seas α, Elysian Seas β, Elysian Playgrounds α, Elysian Playgrounds β
