**Sunfly** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sunfly.

Schools of these fish are often referred to as swimming flower beds, so named for their vibrant scales and flower petal shape. A decidedly bashful aquatic flower, some might say, for few have ever had the pleasure of beholding their beauty up close.

Caught at: The Southern Strait of Merlthor
