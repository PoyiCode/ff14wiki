**Ttokatoa** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ttokatoa.

The dense black roe laid by this wavekin sink down to the substrate where it spends much of its time. Known in the Hhetsarro tongue as the “swallower of dawn,” to witness this wavekin's gaping maw loom up from the depths of Lake Toari is to keenly understand the origins of its name.

Caught at: Lake Toari
