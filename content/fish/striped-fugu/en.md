**Striped Fugu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Striped Fugu.

Beyond the potentially fatal toxins of its liver and ovaries lies a flavor to die for, as survivors will attest.

Caught at: Kugane Piers, Shirogane
