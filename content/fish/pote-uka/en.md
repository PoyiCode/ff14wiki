title: "Pote'uka"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Pote'uka** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pote'uka.

Named an “envoy of rainbows” in the Hanuhanu tongue, this fish gleams in seven distinct colors. Its scales are as popular as precious stones when it comes to decoration due to their brilliance and luster.

Caught at: Waters Hanu
