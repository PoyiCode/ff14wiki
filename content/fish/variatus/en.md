title: "Variatus"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Variatus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Variatus.

One theory has it that this fish was purposely brought here from the Ut'ohmu Horizon, where other similarly vibrant species have been documented.

Caught at: Cenote Jayunja
