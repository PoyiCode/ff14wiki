**Lavender Remora** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lavender Remora.

A violet-hued fish found in the warmer waters of Eorzea's southern seas. It will attach itself to sharks or whales using suction cups growing from its underbelly, and feed on the scraps left by its host.

Caught at: Costa del Sol, Halfstone, Isles of Umbra Northshore
