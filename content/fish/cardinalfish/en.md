**Cardinalfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cardinalfish.

A freshwater fish, so named for its resemblance to the vestments worn by the cardinals of Ishgard's orthodox faith.

Caught at: The Comet's Tail
