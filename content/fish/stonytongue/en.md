**Stonytongue** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Stonytongue.

This hunter's streamlined brow is tailored to nabbing small fry and insects, and casts a striking contrast against the lofty peaks of Gyr Abania it inhabits.

Caught at: The Ephor
