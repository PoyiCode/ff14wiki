**Spectral Starfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spectral Starfish.

Prolonged exposure to high concentrations of aether has partially crystallized these curious wavekin. They are frequently sighted at the forefront of spectral currents, calling to mind stars streaking across the firmament.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
