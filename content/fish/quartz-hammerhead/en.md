**Quartz Hammerhead** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Quartz Hammerhead.

An overlong stint in the aetherial blue has altered this wavekin's distinctive head into a semi-crystalline state. By peering into its pale, translucent depths, one can observe the pulsing strands of the creature's nervous system.

Caught at: Bloodbrine Spectral Current
