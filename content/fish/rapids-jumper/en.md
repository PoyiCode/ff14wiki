**Rapids Jumper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rapids Jumper.

This species often fails to live up to the fanfare inferred from its fast-paced, go-against-the-flow name, as the only flow it has to go against is the Slow Wash.

Caught at: The Slow Wash
