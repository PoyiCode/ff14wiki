**Everkeep Yabby** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Everkeep Yabby.

This crustacean is found in waterways throughout Everkeep. A culinary favorite, steamed yabby is often paired with a variety of colorful but questionable sauces.

Caught at: Residential Sector
