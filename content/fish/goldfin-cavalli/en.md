**Goldfin Cavalli** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Goldfin Cavalli.

The golden fins of this saltwater fish are often likened to the honed tip of a halberd. Seen as protectors of the capital's harbor, these wavekin are venerated by Tuliyollan sailors.

Caught at: High Tide Harbor
