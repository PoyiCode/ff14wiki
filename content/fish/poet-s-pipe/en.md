**Poet's Pipe** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Poet's Pipe.

A species of saltwater fish that is likened to an ocarina on account of its shape. Legend holds that, enchanted by a siren's song, a bard cast himself into the sea, and from the instrument he was clutching this fish was born.

Caught at: Open Sirensong Sea
