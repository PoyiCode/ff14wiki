**Plattershell** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Plattershell.

This prolific crustacean is a popular food source for many living in Yok Tural, and with good reason. For a crab, it is impressively large and meaty—more than fit for a feast.

Caught at: Waters Hanu
