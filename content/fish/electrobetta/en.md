**Electrobetta** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Electrobetta.

This elegant wavekin is wreathed in an eerie purple glow. Naturalists have observed the species ingesting small waterborne fragments of electrope, and speculate that the resultant buildup of electrical charge is responsible for the luminance.

Caught at: Crackling Canyons, Alexandrian Ruins
