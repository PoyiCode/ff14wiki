**Oschon's Stone** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Oschon's Stone.

If the legends are true, this fish came to be when Oschon, god of wanderers and vagrants, cast a stone into the ocean which then gained sentience. Despite this and the passage of countless centuries, however, the stone has somehow yet to realize it has become a fish.

Caught at: The Northern Strait of Merlthor
