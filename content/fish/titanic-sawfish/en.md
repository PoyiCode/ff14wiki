**Titanic Sawfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Titanic Sawfish.

Feared by sailors and fishers alike, legends say that this gargantuan saltwater fish once ripped a hundred-man galleon in two with its saw-like head.

Caught at: Cape Westwind
