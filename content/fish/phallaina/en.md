**Phallaina** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Phallaina.

This oddly proportioned, sharklike creature sails the wispy aether currents of the sea of stars on wings more expansive than it is long. Despite every appearance to the contrary, its physical composition has more in common with flora than the fauna it resembles.

Caught at: Limne 3-β
