**Lotl-in-waiting** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lotl-in-waiting.

Said to spring forth from sunbeams as radiant droplets, these wavekin are worth their weight in gold, for they have long been considered the ideal gift to ingratiate oneself with those in high places.

Caught at: Xobr'it Tsoly
