**Urqofrog** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Urqofrog.

Endemic to Karvarhur the First, on average, these amphibians grow no larger than a single handspan. Girthier specimens have been observed, however—some bulking up to be wider than the waist of a Pelupelu. Admittedly, this is hardly an awe-inspiring metric.

Caught at: Karvarhur the First
