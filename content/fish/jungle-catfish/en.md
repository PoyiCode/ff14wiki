title: "Jungle Catfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Jungle Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Jungle Catfish.

It is said that jungle catfish can grow to become as large as an aurochs, and while there are sightings aplenty of these massive specimens, one that large has yet to be caught.

Caught at: Red Mantis Falls
