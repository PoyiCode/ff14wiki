**Monk Betta** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Monk Betta.

Exhibiting an austere warrior spirit and fierce territorial aggression, violence among fellow fish of the order is often marked by vicious brutality.

Caught at: Rhalgr's Reach
