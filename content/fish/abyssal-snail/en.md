**Abyssal Snail** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Abyssal Snail.

A purple-shelled snail that feeds on the leaves of Lakeland plants. It is named not for any hellish qualities of its own, but after its shell's resemblance to the horns of a legendary demon from the deep.

Caught at: The Rift of Sighs, The Rusted Reservoir
