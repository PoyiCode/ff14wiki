**Bone Crayfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bone Crayfish.

A white-carapaced freshwater crustacean commonly found living in caves or deep lakes where sunlight is absent. After molting, the new shell is so thin and transparent that one can see the creature's organs.

Caught at: The Mirror, Sweetbloom Pier, Sanctum of the Twelve, Rootslake, The Tangle, Nophica's Wells, Upper Soot Creek, Lower Soot Creek, The Unholy Heir, Bluefog, The Clutch, Blind Iron Mines
