title: "Planetes"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Planetes** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Planetes.

Even more lustrous than one's average mangar, the planetes was so named by a taxonomist of some repute, who marked the transient light it cast up to the surface as it wandered through Rumination's Ramble.

Caught at: Rumination's Ramble
