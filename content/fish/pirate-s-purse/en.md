**Pirate's Purse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pirate's Purse.

Long associated with wealth and fortune, many believe this wavekin's raiment of gold-rimmed scales resembles a clutch of hoarded treasure.

Caught at: Unnamed Spectral Current
