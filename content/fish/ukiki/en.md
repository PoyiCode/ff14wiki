title: "Ukiki"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Ukiki** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ukiki.

This variety of Eastern sunfish makes its home in the Ruby Sea. Local fisherfolk named it for its passing resemblance to a floating log, though outsiders struggle to see the similarities.

Caught at: Hells' Lid
