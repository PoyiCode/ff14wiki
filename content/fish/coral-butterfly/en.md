**Coral Butterfly** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Coral Butterfly.

A colorful saltwater fish commonly found in the warm seas around the island of Bianaq. Not known for its flavor, the wavekin is most often put on display in aquariums.

Caught at: Limsa Lominsa Lower Decks, Limsa Lominsa Upper Decks, Zephyr Drift, South Bloodshore
