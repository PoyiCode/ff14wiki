**Hedgemole Cricket** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hedgemole Cricket.

This tiny vilekin has the capacity to live on both land and in water, using its powerful barbed forelegs to both burrow and to swim. Its reproductive capacity is immense; however, since both fish and birds feed on the cricket, its population remains fairly controlled.

Caught at: Thaliak River, Quickspill Delta, Upper Thaliak River, Middle Thaliak River, Voor Sian Siran, The Eddies
