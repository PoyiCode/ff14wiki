**Driftwood Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Driftwood Catfish.

Thanks to the bark-like pattern along its sides, this fish excels at hiding among driftwood and fallen trees. The Hanuhanu in particular consider the tender, fatty flesh of this fish a delicacy.

Caught at: Miyakabek'zu
