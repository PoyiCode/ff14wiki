**Spear Squid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spear Squid.

These sharp-headed cephalopods are believed to bring good fortune to those who wield the spears they were named for—immediately, if said lancer enjoys dried squid as a snack. This perhaps explains the high demand for spear squid in Ishgard in recent years.

Caught at: Kugane Coast
