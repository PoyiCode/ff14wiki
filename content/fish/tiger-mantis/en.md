**Tiger Mantis** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tiger Mantis.

Although large for mantis shrimp, these tiger-striped crustaceans are extremely timid. They spend much of their time cowering on the seabed, rarely moving unless swept up in a spectral current.

Caught at: Thavnair Spectral Current
