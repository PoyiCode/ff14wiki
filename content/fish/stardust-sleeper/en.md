**Stardust Sleeper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Stardust Sleeper.

Although known to also inhabit the Stardust Sea, this secretive wavekin favors the deep, briny waters of Xty'iinbek Tsoly. As such, sightings are few and far between.

Caught at: Xty'iinbek Tsoly
