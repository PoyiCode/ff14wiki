**Rhotano Roosterfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rhotano Roosterfish.

This exceptionally aggressive wavekin is a common sight in the waters betwixt Thavnair and Eorzea. Some jokingly remark that its temperament must be learned from the pirates who ply their cutthroat trade in the region.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
