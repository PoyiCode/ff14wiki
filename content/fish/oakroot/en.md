**Oakroot** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Oakroot.

A giant freshwater fish found in inland lakes and rivers. Its name is derived from its resemblance to a knotty oaken root.

Caught at: Sylphlands, Proud Creek
