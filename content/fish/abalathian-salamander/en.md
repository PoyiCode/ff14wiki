**Abalathian Salamander** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Abalathian Salamander.

Wavekin that plays the runt among its kith. Its rubbery flesh results in an unappetizing texture when cooked, rendering it for all practical purposes inedible.

Caught at: Heather Falls
