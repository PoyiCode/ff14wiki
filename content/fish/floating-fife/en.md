title: "Floating Fife"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Floating Fife** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Floating Fife.

Occasionally seen swimming with its head pointed toward the seabed, this slender wavekin bears a remarkable resemblance to its namesake. Keen-eyed observers will note that it features on the insignia of the Landsguard fife and drum corps.

Caught at: High Tide Harbor
