**Raimdellopterus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Raimdellopterus.

Also known as the “king of the wyverns,” the raimdellopterus was given its official name in honor of the bicentennial of Frandelont Raimdelle's masterwork, the Raimdelle Codex.

Caught at: Tharl Oom Khash
