title: "Bowfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Bowfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bowfish.

Xaela archers often present offerings of this high-strung arciform fish to their gods before venturing forth on the hunt.

Caught at: Tao Khaal
