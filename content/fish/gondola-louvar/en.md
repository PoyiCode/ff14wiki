**Gondola Louvar** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gondola Louvar.

This saltwater fish can be found languidly roaming the waterways of Canal Town, shoals of lesser wavekin in tow. To exert control over its territory, it plies the same routes back and forth with the consistency of a commercial ferry service.

Caught at: Canal Town South
