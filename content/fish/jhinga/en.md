**Jhinga** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Jhinga.

This plump and delicious prawn is a popular main ingredient on the isle of Thavnair. Baked whole or added to curry, no part of the crustacean is wasted—even a meal's leftover shells find their way into alchemical concoctions.

Caught at: The Thavnairian Coast
