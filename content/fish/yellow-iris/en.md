**Yellow Iris** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yellow Iris.

The delicate yellow scales of this fish belie its invasive nature, and many an ecosystem has been disrupted by the yellow iris's arrival on currents from afar. Sailors know to batten down the hatches upon sighting a school of them, for they invariably carry rough seas in their wake.

Caught at: Ruby Spectral Current
