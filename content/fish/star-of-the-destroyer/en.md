**Star of the Destroyer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Star of the Destroyer.

Some say that when the burning fragments of Rhalgr's beacon plunged into the ocean, the life which pulsed within them could not be extinguished. On moonlit nights they float upon the surface, the star-studded waters becoming as a mirror to the twinkling sky above.

Caught at: Open Bloodbrine Sea
