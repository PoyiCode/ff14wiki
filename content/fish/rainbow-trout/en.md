**Rainbow Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rainbow Trout.

Found in the crystal-clear rivers and lakes of Mor Dhona and Coerthas, this fish's scales reflect all seven colors of the rainbow, making it difficult to locate in the water.

Caught at: Verdant Drop, Springripple Brook, Upper Hathoeva River, Middle Hathoeva River, Lower Hathoeva River, East Hathoeva River, Murmur Rills, Fallgourd Float, Coerthas River, Dragonhead Latrines, Snowcloak
