**Greater Serpent of Ronka** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Greater Serpent of Ronka.

The great serpent's true form is described as a gargantuan scaled monstrosity. Could the appearance of this...somewhat less impressive creature be a sign that the fabled beast's own return to the forest is imminent? Or is it entirely unrelated?

Caught at: Lake Tusi Mek'ta
