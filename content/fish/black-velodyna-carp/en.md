**Black Velodyna Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Black Velodyna Carp.

The rivers of Velodyna and Hathoeva are rife with these hungry natators, whose ebon exterior derives from a dark diet.

Caught at: The Velodyna River
