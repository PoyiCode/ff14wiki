**Tylosaurus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tylosaurus.

Deriving from its protruding cylindrical snout, this ancient scalekin's name roughly translates to “knobbed lizard.” Some specimens grow to such a colossal size that they are mistaken for whales.

Caught at: Unnamed Spectral Current
