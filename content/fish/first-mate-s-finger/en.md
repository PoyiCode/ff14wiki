**First Mate's Finger** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: First Mate's Finger.

Owing to its green-tinged carapace and digit-like shape, Roegadyn sailors who spot one of these creatures floundering on deck will invariably ask their fellow countrymen if anyone is missing a finger. That the joke endures is less a testament to its hilarity, and more an indication that encounters with the crustacean remain a rare novelty.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
