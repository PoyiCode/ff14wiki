**Giant Snakehead** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Giant Snakehead.

Possessing primitive lungs, this sizable fish is often seen surfacing for air. When observing such behavior, even the most experienced naturalists have been known to mistake its blunt head for that of a scalekin.

Caught at: Mu Springs Eternal
