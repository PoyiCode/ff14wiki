title: "Moldva"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Moldva** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Moldva.

This Ala Mhigan fighting fish has never known defeat, and for this remarkable feat folk have named it for the celebrated gladiator.

Caught at: Springripple Brook
