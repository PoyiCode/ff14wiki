**Turali Beaded Lizard** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Turali Beaded Lizard.

As potent as beaded lizard venom is, Mamool Ja are immune to its adverse effects, which would certainly explain why Mamook soldiers of eld coated all their arrows in the stuff.

Caught at: Ankledeep
