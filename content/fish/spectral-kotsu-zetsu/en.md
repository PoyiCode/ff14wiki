**Spectral Kotsu Zetsu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spectral Kotsu Zetsu.

When the spectral currents affect a kotsu zetsu, its bone-like tongue takes on the same glow as its scales. These appendages are highly valued by local artisans, who utilize special tools to clean and carve them into luminous pendants and finery.

Caught at: Lower One River
