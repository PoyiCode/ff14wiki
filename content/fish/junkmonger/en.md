**Junkmonger** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Junkmonger.

No goosefish has ever had such a voracious appetite as the junkmonger, who feeds upon refuse such as bottles and fishing rods with as much relish as it would an Ul'dahn banquet.

Caught at: Skull Valley
