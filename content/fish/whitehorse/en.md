**Whitehorse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Whitehorse.

Wherever there are damselfish in distress, this gallant champion shall gallop heroically to the rescue. Bred in the harshest saltwater climes, this noble steed is up to the challenge come hells or high-saline water.

Caught at: Loch Seld
