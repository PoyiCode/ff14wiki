**Gilt Dermogenys** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gilt Dermogenys.

Perhaps to prove those who doubted their aesthetic sensibilities wrong, this wavekin's creator sought to adjust its symbiotic microbes in pursuit of a specimen that would glitter like the sun that fell upon it. After much trial and error, the star was graced with dermogenys species new and old—the original haloed in silver, its younger cousin, gold.

Caught at: The Lower Hungering Gardens
