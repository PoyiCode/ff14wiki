**Red Mantis** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Red Mantis.

Often mistaken for common shrimp, these red-shelled crustaceans can make deft use of their scythe-like front legs.

Caught at: Thavnair Margin
