**Pipira** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pipira.

A fierce freshwater fish most commonly found in Gridania's Jadeite Flood and Black Tea Brook. Though not large or strong enough to inflict any real damage, pipira will bite the ankles of anyone who steps near them.

Caught at: The Vein, The Mirror, Sweetbloom Pier, Whispering Gorge, Jadeite Flood, Lower Black Tea Brook, Upper Black Tea Brook, The Lavender Beds
