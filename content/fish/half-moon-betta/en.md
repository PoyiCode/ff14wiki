**Half-moon Betta** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Half-moon Betta.

This wavekin's already arresting azure and crimson tail grows even more brilliant when kissed by the nacre light of the moon. As such, it comes as little surprise that this fish is a great favorite in Radz–at–Han's wealthier circles.

Caught at: Pavana's Remorse
