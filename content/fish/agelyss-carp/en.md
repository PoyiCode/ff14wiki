**Agelyss Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Agelyss Carp.

A rare species of carp that only can be found in the Agelyss River—so rare, in fact, that the smallfolk believe that even seeing one can bring seven days of good fortune to one's family.

Caught at: East Agelyss River, Raincatcher Gully, Red Mantis Falls
