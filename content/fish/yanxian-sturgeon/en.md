**Yanxian Sturgeon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yanxian Sturgeon.

Existing since prehistoric times, this fish possesses peculiar butterfly-shaped patterns upon its shark-like body—traits that have earned it the appellation “butterfly shark” in the Far East. Its flavorsome roe is enjoyed as a rare delicacy.

Caught at: Lower One River
