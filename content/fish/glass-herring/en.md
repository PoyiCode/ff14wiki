**Glass Herring** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Glass Herring.

This migratory fish thrives under transparency, unlike its misleading red cousins. Its brine-pickled eggs foreshadow deliciousness.

Caught at: The Ruby Price, Isari
