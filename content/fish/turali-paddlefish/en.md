**Turali Paddlefish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Turali Paddlefish.

The black roe laid by this paddlefish has long been treasured as a delicacy in Shaaloani. Some are even willing to pay for it in gold.

Caught at: Lake Toari
