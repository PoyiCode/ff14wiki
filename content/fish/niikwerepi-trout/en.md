**Niikwerepi Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Niikwerepi Trout.

This trout travels along Niikwerepi from lake to lake across its entire lifespan, from the moment of its hatching to maturity and beyond.

Caught at: Niikwerepi, Lake Toari
