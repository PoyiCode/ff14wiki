**Superstring** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Superstring.

This creature's many waving strands shine with pinpricks of light akin to countless stars. Indeed, any who dares capture it may now hold the fates of entire galaxies in their hands...

Caught at: Apohelos 18-γ, Apohelos 18-β, Apohelos 18-α
