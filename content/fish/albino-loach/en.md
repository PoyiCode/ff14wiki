**Albino Loach** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Albino Loach.

This variety of golden loach was born quite by accident in the Chthonic Horns. Sadly, every attempt at registering its concept has been met with skepticism, as the wavekin is far too susceptible to predation.

Caught at: Rumination's Ramble
