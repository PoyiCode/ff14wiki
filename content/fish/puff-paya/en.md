**Puff-paya** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Puff-paya.

This puffer's resemblance to fruit was not lost on the taxonomist who had the pleasure of naming it. Its similarities to the papaya are more than skin-deep, in fact—for when it is detoxified and sun-dried, it makes for a surprisingly sweet treat.

Caught at: The Perfumed Tides
