**Grey Skipper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Grey Skipper.

The grey skipper provides a rare drop of shade in the Kingdom of Rainbows. It has its origins in the Kingdom of Voeburt and remains staunchly loyal to its former masters, refusing to engage in any way with the pixies' magicks.

Caught at: Handmirror Lake, Longmirror Lake, The Haughty One, The Jealous One, Father Collard's Failings, The Spoiled One, Saint Fathric's Temple
