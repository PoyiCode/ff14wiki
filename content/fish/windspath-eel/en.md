**Windspath Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Windspath Eel.

Readily identified by the faintly unsettling eye-like markings that adorn their tails, wild specimens are usually light brown. In the abnormally pure waters of Windspath Gardens, however, this eel has developed a muted, almost transparent hue.

Caught at: The Knowable
