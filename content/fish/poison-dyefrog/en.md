**Poison Dyefrog** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Poison Dyefrog.

Although the toxins this tiny frog secretes are deadly, the Hanuhanu have used the substance to dye their plumage for generations.

Caught at: Bopo'uihih
