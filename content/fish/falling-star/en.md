**Falling Star** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Falling Star.

Denomination for a faintly luminescent subset of algae, their phosphorescent fires allegedly lit long ago by the burning star cast forth by Rhalgr.

Caught at: The Comet's Tail
