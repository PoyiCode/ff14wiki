**Razor Clam** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Razor Clam.

A long bivalve mollusk most commonly found buried in the beaches of islands situated in the Rhotano Sea. They are known to leap out of their burrows when surprised or agitated.

Caught at: The Salt Strand, Skull Valley, Crescent Cove
