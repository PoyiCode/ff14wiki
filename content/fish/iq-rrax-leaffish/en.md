**Iq Rrax Leaffish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Iq Rrax Leaffish.

Some say this fish struck upon its disguise when a flood of ashen leaves flowed into the waters of Iq Rrax Tsoly from the Xobr'it Cinderfield. Certainly, to the untrained eye, this wavekin is all but indistinguishable from a charred, waterborne leaf.

Caught at: Iq Rrax Tsoly
