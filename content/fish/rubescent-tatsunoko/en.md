**Rubescent Tatsunoko** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rubescent Tatsunoko.

Fortunate enough to share the red-and-white coloration of the leopard prawn, these meek wavekin use the coincidence to their advantage, curling up and blending in with colonies of crustaceans to avoid conflict with other species. They may be biding their time as they grow, knowing they will one day rise upon the waves as Ruby Dragons...or not.

Caught at: Kugane Coast
