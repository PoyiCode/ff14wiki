summary: "A fish of Eorzea."

**Priestfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Priestfish.

The crozier-like bioluminescent proboscis dangling from the priestfish's forehead attracts prey in the murky depths of the pools in which this distant cousin of the monkfish resides.

Caught at: Clearpool
