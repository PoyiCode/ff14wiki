**Prodigal Son** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Prodigal Son.

Despite having grown to a far larger size than considered necessary for his kind, he continues to roam the waters of the world in search of what a fish would consider a good time, rather than return to his spawning grounds to produce offspring.

Caught at: Rhotano Spectral Current
