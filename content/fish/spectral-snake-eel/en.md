**Spectral Snake Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spectral Snake Eel.

While the majority of snake eels tend to lurk under the sands and await the coming of their prey, these creatures—likely realizing that such tactics are not quite as effective when one has sparkling blue scales—have been spotted near the water's surface, blending in with the azure surf to catch their victims unawares.

Caught at: Open Ruby Sea
