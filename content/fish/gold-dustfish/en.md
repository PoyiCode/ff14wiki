**Gold Dustfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gold Dustfish.

Such is the beauty of this creature's aurulent scales that it has been likened to a treasure once lost by the Nabaath royal family to the dunes of Amh Araeng.

Caught at: The Nabaath Severance
