title: "Sycorax"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Sycorax** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sycorax.

The iridescent sheen of the sycorax's shell is the result of the aether incorporated into its shell. This crystallized magick is also the source of its unusual name, which is that of a witch of legend, or so the scholars believe.

Caught at: Northwest Caliban Gorge, West Caliban Gap, East Caliban Gap, Purpure, The Norvrandt Slope
