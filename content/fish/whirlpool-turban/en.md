**Whirlpool Turban** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Whirlpool Turban.

The unique grooves on these wavekin's spiral shells are believed to disrupt ocean currents, creating whirlpools wheresoever they bob in shallow tides. The fishers of Kugane pay little attention to cautionary tales surrounding them, however, as the flesh pairs exceedingly well with soy sauce.

Caught at: Kugane Coast
