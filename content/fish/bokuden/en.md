title: "Bokuden"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Bokuden** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bokuden.

This wavekin was aptly dubbed Bokuden—wandering swordsman of legend—after a territorial scuffle saw three score samurai fish float to the surface of the waters before Doma Castle.

Caught at: Doma Castle
