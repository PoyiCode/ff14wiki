**Astacus Aetherius** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Astacus Aetherius.

A propagated color variant, this wavekin has an exquisite cobalt hue which is thought to be caused by the complex aether flowing both above and below ground in Sharlayan.

Caught at: Deepmoat
