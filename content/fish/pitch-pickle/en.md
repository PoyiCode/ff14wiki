**Pitch Pickle** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pitch Pickle.

This rare species of sea pickle was a favorite of Hingan nobility in generations past. As was the fashion at the time, they preferred to dye their teeth black, and a single pitch pickle was a swift means to a stygian smile.

Caught at: Ruby Spectral Current
