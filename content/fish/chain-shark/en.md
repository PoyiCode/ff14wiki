**Chain Shark** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Chain Shark.

While originally native to the Indigo Deep, a number of these sharks migrated to the cenotes in search of prey and simply never went back.

Caught at: Xty'iinbek Tsoly
