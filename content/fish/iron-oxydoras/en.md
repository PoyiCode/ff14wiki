**Iron Oxydoras** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Iron Oxydoras.

Much like its lesser brethren, this formidable fish has a long history of being brandished as a blunt instrument. In the days of Yok Huy conquest, warriors encountering these wavekin in Kozama'uka would snatch the creatures from the shallows, thereby acquiring slippery—and presumably perturbed—cudgels with which to wage war.

Caught at: Miyakabek'zoma
