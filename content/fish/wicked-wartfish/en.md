**Wicked Wartfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Wicked Wartfish.

Cursed with a face so ugly, it is said demons of the underworld use the wartfish to frighten souls attempting to escape into returning to hell.

Caught at: Hells' Lid
