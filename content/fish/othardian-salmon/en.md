**Othardian Salmon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Othardian Salmon.

The light and flaky flesh of the Othardian salmon pairs well with the aromatic rice wines of Hingashi, making the fish a highly sought-after commodity throughout the realm.

Caught at: The One River (East)
