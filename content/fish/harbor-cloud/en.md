**Harbor Cloud** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Harbor Cloud.

Blooms of these jellyfish can be found off the docks of High Tide Harbor. When dried, their durability and elasticity make them valued packaging amongst traders.

Caught at: High Tide Harbor
