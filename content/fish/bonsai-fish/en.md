**Bonsai Fish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bonsai Fish.

The bonsai fish's resemblance to a leafy tree branch protects the surface-swimming wavekin from hungry birds looking for their next meal.

Caught at: The Silver Canal
