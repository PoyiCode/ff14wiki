**Blind Brotula** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Blind Brotula.

This pale, puny fish may seem helpless at first blush, but its aether-sensitive organs enable it to defend itself with primitive magicks.

Caught at: Bitterbark Cenote
