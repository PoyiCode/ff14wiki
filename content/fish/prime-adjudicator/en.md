**Prime Adjudicator** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Prime Adjudicator.

With a lifespan that exceeds even that of Yok Huy, this enormous wavekin commands the depths of Karvarhur the First while robed in thick swaths of lichen that bespeak its ancient legacy.

Caught at: Karvarhur the First
