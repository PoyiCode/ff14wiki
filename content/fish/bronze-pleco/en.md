**Bronze Pleco** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bronze Pleco.

The coloration of this bottom-feeding fish is akin to a dull metallic patina. Preferring to lurk among rocks and logs, it subsists primarily on a diet of algae.

Caught at: The Dewspun Bank, Peaks Poga
