**Azure Diver** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Azure Diver.

This acrobatic wavekin is often seen leaping skyward before plummeting straight and true into the mists it calls home. On one occasion, a particularly dextrous specimen found itself the target of a mysterious traveler's probing spear tip. Predicting a swift skewering, onlookers were instead treated to a dazzling aerial display as the creature nimbly evaded defeat.

Caught at: Eastbound Zorgor
