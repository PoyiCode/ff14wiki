**Laxan Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Laxan Carp.

These scaly swimmers were once ten a gil in Lakeland, before being driven to extinction by the Flood. Now they exist only in the experimental breeding beds of the Crystarium.

Caught at: The Trivium, The Quadrivium, Crystarium Personal Suites
