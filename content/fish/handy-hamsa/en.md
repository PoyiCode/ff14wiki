**Handy Hamsa** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Handy Hamsa.

With its golden head, mottled scales, and overall rotundity, this wavekin bears something of a resemblance to Thavnair's beloved hamsa. Even the fish's darting, unpredictable movements are reminiscent of its land-dwelling namesake.

Caught at: The Font of Maya
