**Bigcuda** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bigcuda.

Although bicuda are known for their magnitude, the bigcuda dwarfs its kin. It defends its place in its species' size-based hierarchy—and commensurate territory—with a ferocity bordering on the savage, though theories that it has thinned out the competition by taking bites out of its kin remain unsubstantiated.

Caught at: Ksiroda
