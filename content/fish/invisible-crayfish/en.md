**Invisible Crayfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Invisible Crayfish.

This crawdad is best known to Gyr Abanians via the faerie tale in which its brilliant shell fades with each cycle of the Slow Wash, until only an empty white palette remains.

Caught at: The Slow Wash, The Bull's Bath
