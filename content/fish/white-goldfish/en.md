**White Goldfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: White Goldfish.

A life of solitude in the aether-starved depths of Prism Lake has left this miniature carp devoid of both color and aspect.

Caught at: Prism Lake
