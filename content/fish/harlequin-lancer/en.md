**Harlequin Lancer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Harlequin Lancer.

The black and yellow splotches that cover this wavekin's body bring to mind the gaudy costumes of clowns and jesters. Yet far from serving to entertain, these vibrant colors warn predators that the lancer's flesh is filled with deadly toxins.

Caught at: The Knowable
