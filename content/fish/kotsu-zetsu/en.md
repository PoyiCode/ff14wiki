title: "Kotsu Zetsu"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Kotsu Zetsu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Kotsu Zetsu.

A compound of the Hingashi terms “kotsu,” meaning “bone,” and “zetsu,” meaning “tongue,” referring to the oral harpoon this fish uses to spear its prey. Its tail is known to grow scarlet with age and bloodguilt.

Caught at: Mercantile Docks, The One River (East), The One River (West), Plum Spring, The Doman Enclave
