title: "Meditator"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Meditator** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Meditator.

This fish is in the unique habit of entering a catatonic, simulated death state in order to survive the dry season, appearing not unlike an ascetic deep in meditation.

Caught at: The Ephor, The Bull's Bath
