**Yak T'el Salamander** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yak T'el Salamander.

This emerald-tinted giant is said to lurk in the depths with its great maw open, patiently waiting to ambush unsuspecting prey and swallow them whole.
