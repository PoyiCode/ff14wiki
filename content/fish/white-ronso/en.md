title: "White Ronso"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**White Ronso** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: White Ronso.

The black-and-white stripes which adorn its body resemble a rare pattern of Ronso fur. The similarities between the two end there, however, as this fish's glower is known to cause people to soil themselves in laughter rather than terror.

Caught at: Lower Watts River
