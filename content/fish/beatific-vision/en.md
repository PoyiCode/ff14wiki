**Beatific Vision** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Beatific Vision.

A priestfish that has discovered a higher calling. This wavekin has swum in the aether-rich waters of the divine, enrapturing its flock with the deific luminescence of its lure.
