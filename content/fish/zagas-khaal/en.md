**Zagas Khaal** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Zagas Khaal.

Ask a nomad of the Azim Steppe to blurt out the first fish that comes to mind, and without fail you will hear the name of this pervasive species, followed by an awkward silence.

Caught at: Nem Khaal, Hak Khaal, Upper Yat Khaal, Azim Khaat, Tao Khaal
