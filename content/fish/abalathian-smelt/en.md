**Abalathian Smelt** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Abalathian Smelt.

A tiny freshwater fish native to the lakes and rivers fed by runoff from Abalathia's Spine. Too small to fillet, the fish are usually tossed into stewpots and eaten whole.

Caught at: The Nail, The Weeping Saint, Dragonhead Latrines, Daniffen Pass, Exploratory Ice Hole, Riversmeet, Unfrozen Pond, Clearpool, South Banepool, Ashpool
