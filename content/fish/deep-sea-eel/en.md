**Deep-sea Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Deep-sea Eel.

Some fishers claim that the “deep” part of its name in fact refers to its profound understanding of the greater workings of the universe, rather than the depth at which it swims.

Caught at: Rhotano Spectral Current
