**Reasonscale Silverside** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Reasonscale Silverside.

When Tuliyollal's population began to boom, the Head of Reason sought to preempt future shortages by introducing this foreign species as a source of food. Fortunately for the growing city and its hungry residents, the fish soon became established in their new environment.

Caught at: The For'ard Cabins, High Tide Harbor
