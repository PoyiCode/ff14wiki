**Red Drum** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Red Drum.

Due to both its habitat's proximity to the markets and its highly palatable flesh, this large wavekin fills the bellies of many of Radz–at–Han's citizens on a daily basis.

Caught at: The Wakeful Torana
