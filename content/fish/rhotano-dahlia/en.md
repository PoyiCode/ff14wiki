**Rhotano Dahlia** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rhotano Dahlia.

This stinging anemone inhabits the shallows of the Rhotano Sea. Its barbed feelers are laced with a potent toxin that fells prey in an instant and, for some obscure reason that naturalists still struggle to ascertain, tints them a deep green.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
