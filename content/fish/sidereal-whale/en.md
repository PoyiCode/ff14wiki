**Sidereal Whale** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sidereal Whale.

Recent theories on the subject of this cosmic wavekin suggest it to be the inspiration for the lunar whale—the starfaring airship of Mysidian myth.

Caught at: Limne 3-β
