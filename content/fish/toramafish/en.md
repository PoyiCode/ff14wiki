**Toramafish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Toramafish.

Already known for their ferocity, coeurlfish deemed exceptionally violent are given the name of the coeurl's deadly cousin.

Caught at: Oakwood
