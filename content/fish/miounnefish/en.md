**Miounnefish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Miounnefish.

Pipira, indigenous to the Black Shroud, were successfully transplanted into the climes of Gyr Abania, where they bear the name of the guildmaster largely responsible for their change of address.

Caught at: Upper Mirage Creek, Timmon Beck, Dimwold
