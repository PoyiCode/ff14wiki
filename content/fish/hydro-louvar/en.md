**Hydro Louvar** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hydro Louvar.

Favoring warm, temperate waters, this wavekin once roamed oceans far and wide. Sadly, its present habitat rather curtails these migratory tendencies.

Caught at: Canal Town South
