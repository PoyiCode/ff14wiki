---

**Floating Saucer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Floating Saucer.

When viewed from a distance, could be mistaken for an upturned porcelain saucer. Even when observed from close proximity, the faint pattern even resembles the remnants of a picture rendered in gold leaf.

Caught at: Rhotano Spectral Current
