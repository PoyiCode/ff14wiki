**Mermaid Scale** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mermaid Scale.

This mollusk's blue and purple hues are made all the more vibrant by the aether-rich spectral waters it calls home. Though the mermaid supposed to have shed these “scales” invariably eludes them, sailors continue to fancy she is just out of sight whensoever they happen upon one.

Caught at: Sirensong Spectral Current
