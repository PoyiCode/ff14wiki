title: "Moat Carp"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Moat Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Moat Carp.

Historic records state that these carp were originally introduced to the Jadeite Flood to serve as food in the event of a siege. Legend has that a man in a faraway kingdom once caught ten thousand in a single sitting.

Caught at: The Mirror, Sanctum of the Twelve, Empty Heart, Whispering Gorge, Zahar'ak, Jadeite Flood, Lower Black Tea Brook, Upper Black Tea Brook
