**Rothlyt Mussel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rothlyt Mussel.

Prized for their succulent, orange flesh, these bivalve mollusks also fetch a hefty purse from jewelers, who work the shimmering, nacreous layers of the shells into magnificent trinkets.

Caught at: Rothlyt Spectral Current
