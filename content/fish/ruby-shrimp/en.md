**Ruby Shrimp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ruby Shrimp.

For all their differences, the people of Othard and Hingashi have always had one thing in common, as these zesty and succulent prawns are loved on all sides of the Ruby Sea.

Caught at: Kugane Piers, Hells' Lid, The Isle of Bekko, Onokoro, The Isle of Zekki, Shirogane
