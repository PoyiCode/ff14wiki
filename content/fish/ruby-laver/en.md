**Ruby Laver** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ruby Laver.

A long-standing staple amongst the costal villages along the Ruby Sea, this nutrient-rich seaweed finds its way into most every dish, from breakfast to supper. It is even added to rice wine to enhance its flavor.

Caught at: Shoal Rock
