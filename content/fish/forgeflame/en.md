**Forgeflame** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Forgeflame.

Sporting fiery fins, this wavekin is thought to be a wayward ingot sent flying up and out of the Great Work's forge and into the river in which it now calls home.

Caught at: Ksiroda
