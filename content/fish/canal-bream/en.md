title: "Canal Bream"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Canal Bream** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Canal Bream.

This wavekin was a popular source of food in the port settlement Canal Town was based on. It is said that young chefs tasked with filleting the fish were often put off by its large, posthumously watchful eyes.

Caught at: Canal Town South, Canal Town North
