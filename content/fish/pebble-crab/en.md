**Pebble Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pebble Crab.

A small bluish grey crab found in tide pools along the La Noscean coast. Their tendency to cut fishing lines and steal bait from hooks makes these wavekin despised by fishers.

Caught at: Moraby Bay, Cedarwood, Moraby Drydocks, Oschon's Torch, The Salt Strand, Candlekeep Quay, Swiftperch, The Silver Bazaar, Vesper Bay
