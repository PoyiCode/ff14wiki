**Awaksbane Apoda** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Awaksbane Apoda.

Despite being considerably larger than other apoda, this ancient amphibian's chief form of defense remains the same: secreting a highly toxic slime. Unlike its smaller cousins, however, it produces toxins in such quantities as to blight the waters of the tsoly it calls home.

Caught at: Yak Awak Tsoly
