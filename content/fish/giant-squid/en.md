**Giant Squid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Giant Squid.

This rarely seen giant cephalopod makes an appearance in ancient Sea Wolf mythology as the creature on which the legendary whale Bismarck fed.

Caught at: The Ship Graveyard, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Sterncastle), North Isle of Endless Summer
