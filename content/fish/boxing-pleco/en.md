**Boxing Pleco** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Boxing Pleco.

The largest pleco known to naturalists, this variety is known for violently flailing its fins and ramming its head into any unfortunate creature that happens to swim by, especially during the spawning season.

Caught at: Urth's Gift, Lake Tahtotl, The Deep Tangle
