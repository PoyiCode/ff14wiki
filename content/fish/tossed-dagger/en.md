**Tossed Dagger** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tossed Dagger.

In contrast to the soaring arcs of its brethren in other parts of the ocean, this fish leaps through the Strait of Merlthor in short, cutting crescents. However, unlike its namesake, two sailors can toss it back and forth for bells and still have all their extremities intact at the end.

Caught at: The Northern Strait of Merlthor
