**Tsoly Mu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tsoly Mu.

In the Xbr'aal language, “mu” means “sun,” while “tsoly” refers to a cenote. A fitting name for this vibrant red wavekin, which is easily mistaken for a mysterious light source when spotted in the depths.

Caught at: Iq Rrax Tsoly
