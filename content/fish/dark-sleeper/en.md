**Dark Sleeper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dark Sleeper.

A small freshwater fish originally from the eastern continent of Othard. It is said that the first dark sleeper was introduced to Eorzea by an exiled Lalafellin prince who wished to once again experience the luxuries of his homeland.

Caught at: Sweetbloom Pier, Upper Hathoeva River, Middle Hathoeva River, East Hathoeva River, Fallgourd Float, Hidden Falls, The Lavender Beds
