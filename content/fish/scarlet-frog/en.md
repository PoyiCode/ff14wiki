**Scarlet Frog** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Scarlet Frog.

A diet of poisonous mites causes a mild toxin to build up in the secretions of this red-hued frog. It is a simple process to extract the paralytic poison and refine it into a pain-dulling elixir, a practice routinely employed by field chirurgeons.

Caught at: Mirage Creek
