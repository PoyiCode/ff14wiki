**Lansquenet** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lansquenet.

This wavekin is known for roaming great distances on the ocean's currents, often attaching itself to schools of smaller fish in the role of temporary guardian. Such behavior gives the impression of a mercenary protecting a band of travelers, but it is rarely—if ever—paid for its services.

Caught at: Outer Rothlyt Sound
