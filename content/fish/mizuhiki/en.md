title: "Mizuhiki"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Mizuhiki** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mizuhiki.

A fish of white and crimson named for its resemblance to the rice paper cords traditionally used to wrap gifts in Hingashi. Those who manage to hook one of these decorative creatures are said to be blessed with good fortune.

Caught at: Kugane Coast
