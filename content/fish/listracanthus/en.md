**Listracanthus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Listracanthus.

A shark species of primal origin, the serpentine listracanthus glides smoothly through the water, the hair-thin scales upon its back swaying to and fro like an undulating mane. For years untold has it dwelled in the Source, and some consider it the ever-present consort of the Isle of Ken.

Caught at: The Isle of Ken
