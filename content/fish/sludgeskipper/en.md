**Sludgeskipper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sludgeskipper.

Similar to its cousin the mudskipper, the sludgeskipper can also survive outside water for several bells. The sludgeskipper, however, is at home in highly polluted pools where other creatures would likely perish.

Caught at: North Silvertear, The Tangle, The Juggernaut, Ceruleum Field, Parata's Peace
