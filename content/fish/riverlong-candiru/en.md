**Riverlong Candiru** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Riverlong Candiru.

Capable of singlehandedly reducing the largest of predators to naught but a pile of polished bones, this wavekin combines the voraciousness of its kin with an independent tenacity that is chilling to behold.

Caught at: Miyakabek'zu
