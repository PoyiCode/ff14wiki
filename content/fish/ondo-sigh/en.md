**Ondo Sigh** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ondo Sigh.

Yearning for the life his people left behind under the tides, an unnamed Ondo submerged his head beneath the ocean surface and gave cry. His silent lament gave form not to countless bubbles, but to a single fish, its size matching the depth of the Ondo's longing.

Caught at: The Flounders' Floor
