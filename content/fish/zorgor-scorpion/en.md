**Zorgor Scorpion** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Zorgor Scorpion.

On occasion, travelers of Zorgor the Boundless may catch sight of one of these scorpions leaping onto the bridge, a helpless cloudribbon in its pincers. What happens next is generally not a treat for the eyes.

Caught at: Westbound Zorgor
