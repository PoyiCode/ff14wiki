**Lopoceras Elegans** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lopoceras Elegans.

When the Loporrits came across this abnormally winding cephalopod, its beauteous spirals reminded them of a long, floppy ear. Though the lunar residents could not come to a consensus on how such an ear could be so long and floppy, they were nevertheless impressed, and would unanimously agree to name the creature an honorary Loporrit.

Caught at: The Chlorophospond
