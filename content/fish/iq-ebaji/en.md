**Iq Ebaji** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Iq Ebaji.

Exceedingly agile, this shrimp bears a name evocative of rust and deadly shears. Its tight ruddy-brown carapace conceals flesh bountiful in nourishment.

Caught at: Iq Br'aax Reservoir
