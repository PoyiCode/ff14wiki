**Oliphant's Trunk** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Oliphant's Trunk.

Similar to an electric eel, the near-blind oliphant's trunk emits low-frequency electrical pulses into surrounding waters in order to better locate its prey.
