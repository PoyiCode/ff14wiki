**Hells' Claw** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hells' Claw.

This formidable king of the Crab Pots dons an armored shell of volcanic rock to protect itself against would-be predators. While this defense mechanism has proven successful, the sweltering heat of its once-molten carapace would cook the crustacean from the inside out had it not made a habit of launching itself into the cool depths of the Ruby Sea.

Caught at: Ruby Spectral Current
