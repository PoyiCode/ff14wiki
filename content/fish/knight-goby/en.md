**Knight Goby** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Knight Goby.

This valorous wavekin is the national fish of Alexandria. Revered as a symbol of loyalty, its silver scales invite comparisons to the realm's armored knights.

Caught at: The Knowable, Mu Springs Eternal, Leynode Aero
