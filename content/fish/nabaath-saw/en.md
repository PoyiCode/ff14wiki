**Nabaath Saw** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Nabaath Saw.

Eschewing griffins and dragons and other typical icons of might, the Nabaath Areng military based its insignia on the ferocious Nabaath saw. Sneering foes soon learned to respect that symbol, sometimes at the business end of a weapon crafted from said wavekin's lethal dorsal fins.

Caught at: The Nabaath Severance
