**Spotted Stingray** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spotted Stingray.

The white spots on the back of this ray are easily mistaken for eyes. Indeed, this may well be the point—to keep predators and prey alike guessing as to which way the wavekin is *actually* looking.

Caught at: Marsh Ligaka
