**Velodyna Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Velodyna Carp.

This scarlet cousin to the maiden carp can most commonly be found in the Velodyna and Hathoeva rivers. A favorite amongst Wildwood and Duskwight Elezen—amazingly one of the few things the clans actually agree on.

Caught at: Sylphlands, Upper Hathoeva River, Middle Hathoeva River, Lower Hathoeva River, East Hathoeva River, Urth's Gift
