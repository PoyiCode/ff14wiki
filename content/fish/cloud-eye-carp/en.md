**Cloud-eye Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cloud-eye Carp.

A relatively recent discovery in the pantheon of Turali fauna, there is still much to learn about this mysterious wavekin. Lacking eyes altogether, this fish seems to navigate its environs using an array of other senses.

Caught at: Xobr'it Tsoly
