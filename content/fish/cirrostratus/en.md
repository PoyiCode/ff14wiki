title: "Cirrostratus"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Cirrostratus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cirrostratus.

Named for the thin summertime clouds it resembles, the cirrostratus is believed to achieve flight despite its enormous size due to its parchment-like membranous “wings.”

Caught at: Cloudtop
