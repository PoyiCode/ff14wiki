**Iridescent Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Iridescent Trout.

Spectral currents have given this wavekin's scales a faint iridescence. They are highly valued by artisans of the Far East, with the most radiant scales fetching a high price at auction.

Caught at: One River Spectral Current
