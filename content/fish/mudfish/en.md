---
title: "Mudfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Mudfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mudfish.

This wavekin burrows into the mud at the bottom of lakes. They were so commonly discovered by diggers that they were at one point believed to come from the earth itself.

Caught at: Ankledeep, Cenote Moxutural, Cenote Jayunja, Sapsweet Cenote, Bitterbark Cenote
