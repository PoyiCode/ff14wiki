---
title: "Maiboi"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Maiboi** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Maiboi.

Meaning “thin one” in the Vanu tongue, maiboi are a symbol of weakness amongst the tribes of Abalathia, and to be called a maiboi by your kin is often considered the greatest of insults.

Caught at: Voor Sian Siran, Diadem Skysprings, Southern Diadem Lake
