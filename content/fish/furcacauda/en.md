**Furcacauda** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Furcacauda.

A resourceful ancient once coined the concept for a flat-tailed wavekin whose bones could be crafted into fine tableware. Fish bones did not make for aesthetically pleasing saucers, however, and the plan was eventually abandoned.

Caught at: Lethe
