title: "Toadhead"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Toadhead** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Toadhead.

Some scholars say that the large, bubble-like bags under its eyes are this shortsighted wavekin's way to make predators believe it sees more than it does. Others insist that it is merely sleep-deprived.

Caught at: Father Collard's Failings
