**Kissing Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Kissing Trout.

Native to the freshwater rivers of Abalathia's Spine, the kissing trout is famous for its mating “dance” in which a male and female will face each other for several moments before proceeding to couple.

Caught at: Coerthas River, The Nail, Dragonhead Latrines, Exploratory Ice Hole
