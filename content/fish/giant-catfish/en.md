**Giant Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Giant Catfish.

A giant freshwater fish found throughout Aldenard and known for the decidedly feline whiskers which it uses to detect the movement of prey.

Caught at: Northeast Bronze Lake, Northwest Bronze Lake
