**Neo Arowana** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Neo Arowana.

Native to a now-lost realm, this species' survival is credited to Alexandrian hobbyists who bred the fish for its iridescent appearance.
