title: "Flaming Eel"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Flaming Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Flaming Eel.

Should it be exposed to fire, this oily eel will burn quite readily, the flaming pattern on its skin soon replaced by actual flames. It is prized among seafarers as a substitute fuel for cooking, and for the surfeit of fish oil which can be extracted from its flesh.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
