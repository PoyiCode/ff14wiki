**Maru Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Maru Crab.

This bulky ocean crustacean bears the name of a water deity, the tales of which are passed down in the local lore of the Tempest. Highly sensitive to aetheric disturbances, it is often able to gather its fellows and flee before predators or other dangers arrive.

Caught at: West Caliban Gap
