**Iron Shadowtongue** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Iron Shadowtongue.

While the skins of related species can be processed into armor, the sable scales of this primordial wavekin boast even greater toughness, and are sturdy enough to substitute as weaponry without alteration.

Caught at: Iq Rrax Tsoly
