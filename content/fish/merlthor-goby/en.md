**Merlthor Goby** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Merlthor Goby.

A vividly striped saltwater fish commonly found in the Strait of Merlthor. The gobies are situated near the bottom of the food chain and feed a great deal of larger species. This makes the fish an ideal choice for mooching.

Caught at: Limsa Lominsa Lower Decks, Limsa Lominsa Upper Decks, Zephyr Drift, Summerford, Moraby Bay, Cedarwood, South Bloodshore, Skull Valley, Isles of Umbra Northshore, The Ship Graveyard, Cape Westwind, Reaver Hide, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Sterncastle), North Isle of Endless Summer
