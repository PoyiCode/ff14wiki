**The Winter Queen** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: The Winter Queen.

For this ancient harutsuge, spring never comes, and while her brethren follow the warm currents to the Indigo Deep, she remains to rule the Ruby Tide in royal solitude.

Caught at: Shoal Rock
