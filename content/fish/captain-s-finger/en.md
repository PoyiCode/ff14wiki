**Captain's Finger** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Captain's Finger.

Of several species said to resemble a Roegadyn's formidable digits, these specimens are unquestionably the girthiest. Apparently, the creature's appellation is much to the chagrin of delicate-fingered nautical officers across the realm.

Caught at: Unnamed Spectral Current
