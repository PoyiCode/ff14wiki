**Grip Killifish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Grip Killifish.

A rare type of fish found in harsh environments, such as the stagnant rainwater ponds collecting in the basin of Empty Heart on the western finger of the Gods' Grip.

Caught at: Empty Heart, The Juggernaut, Ceruleum Field, Parata's Peace, Moondrip
