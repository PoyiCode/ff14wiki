title: "Oho Hunu"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Oho Hunu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Oho Hunu.

In the Hanuhanu tongue, Oho Hunu means “windflame.” The name refers both to the wavekin's impressive speed and the burning red gradation extending to the tip of its tail fin.

Caught at: The Dewspun Bank, Peaks Poga
