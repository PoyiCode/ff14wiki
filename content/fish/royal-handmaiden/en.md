**Royal Handmaiden** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Royal Handmaiden.

Legend has it that an envoy's attendant fell from their vessel while traversing the seas, whereupon they assumed a new, aquatic form beneath the waves—kimono transforming into scale, stitch by vibrant stitch.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
