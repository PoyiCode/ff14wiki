**Shaggy Seadragon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shaggy Seadragon.

This fish is often dragged out of the ocean along with the morass of seaweed in which it makes its home. The tangled algae is also its main source of sustenance, resulting in short periods of time in which it must swim through dangerous waters bereft of home or nourishment.

Caught at: The Southern Strait of Merlthor
