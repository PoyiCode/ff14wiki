**Red Coral** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Red Coral.

A piece of sharp coral, rarely found in any of Eorzea's five seas. The material is so rare, in fact, that it often fetches prices similar to those asked for garnets or rubies.

Caught at: North Bloodshore, Isles of Umbra Northshore, Isles of Umbra Southshore, The Ship Graveyard, Sapsa Spawning Grounds, Reaver Hide, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Forecastle)
