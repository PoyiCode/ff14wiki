**Hagoromo Bijin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hagoromo Bijin.

The billowing fins of this Far Eastern seraphim are oft compared to a kimono featured in a famous painting of a legendary angelic being.

Caught at: The One River (West)
