**Cloudribbon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cloudribbon.

Peering over the side of Zorgor the Boundless, it might look as though someone's dropped their favorite blue ribbon, but in most cases it's just a cloudfish riding on updrafts using its tiny membranes.

Caught at: Westbound Zorgor
