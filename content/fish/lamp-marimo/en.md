**Lamp Marimo** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lamp Marimo.

A rare type of luminescent moss found on the bottom of lakes and in caves. Slight currents cause the moss to form into a ball as it grows. They are often shunned by uneducated smallfolk who believe them to be water-breathing will-o'-the-wisps.

Caught at: North Silvertear, Rathefrost, The Weeping Saint, Daniffen Pass, Exploratory Ice Hole, Singing Shards, The North Shards, Blind Iron Mines, Eil Tohm
