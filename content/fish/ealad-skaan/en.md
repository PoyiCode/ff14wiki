**Ealad Skaan** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ealad Skaan.

Called “butterfly wings” in the faerie tongue in praise of its elegant fins, this wavekin is said to have been glamoured by a pixie to resemble their beloved, departed Titania.

Caught at: Longmirror Lake
