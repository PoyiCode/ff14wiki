**Moon Croppie** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Moon Croppie.

Distributed widely across Yyasulani, this species is prized as a source of food. When cooked, its white flesh becomes wonderfully plump and tender.

Caught at: The Driftdowns
