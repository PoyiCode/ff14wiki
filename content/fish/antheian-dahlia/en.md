**Antheian Dahlia** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Antheian Dahlia.

The fins of this striking wavekin resemble the delicate petals of the dahlia. With the addition of new antheian varieties, the original creator hoped to turn the waters of Lethe into a shifting field of flowers.

Caught at: Lethe
