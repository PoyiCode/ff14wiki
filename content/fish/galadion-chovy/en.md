**Galadion Chovy** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Galadion Chovy.

While at first glance this anchovy may appear no different from those found in the waters of Limsa Lominsa, one sip of the broth made from its bones will compel even the most captious eater to return to Galadion Bay time and again.

Caught at: Outer Galadion Bay
