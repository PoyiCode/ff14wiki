**Pike Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pike Eel.

When stretched straight from head to tail, the longest of saltwater pike eels can measure more than a full yalm in length—or, the length of an average pike.

Caught at: Isles of Umbra Northshore, The Ship Graveyard
