**Dwarf Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dwarf Catfish.

An undersized variety of catfish found in still, swampy waters across Aldenard. The Padjali name for this fish is “gee-gee,” supposedly taken from the grinding sound created when it moves its fins.

Caught at: Rogue River, West Agelyss River, Nym River, Woad Whisper Canyon, The Mourning Widow
