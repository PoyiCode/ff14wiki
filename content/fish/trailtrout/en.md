---
title: "Trailtrout"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Trailtrout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Trailtrout.

It is said that the Hhetsarro study the fat deposits of the trailtrout to get a sense for the changing seasons and plan their next migration.

Caught at: Lake Toari
