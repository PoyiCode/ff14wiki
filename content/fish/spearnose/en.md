**Spearnose** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spearnose.

The spear-shaped nose of this rare wavekin is not actually a nose, but the creature's mouth—a mouth not actually used to spear prey, but to suck it forth from between rocks.

Caught at: The Goblet
