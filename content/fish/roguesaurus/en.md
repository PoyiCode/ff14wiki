title: "Roguesaurus"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Roguesaurus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Roguesaurus.

Taking its name from the Rogue River, fossil remains indicate that it was once a land-dwelling scalekin that returned to the water. The other inhabitants of the Rogue River were, by all accounts, rather unhappy with this arrangement, as it proceeded to steal the top position on the food chain.

Caught at: Southern Merlthor Spectral Current
