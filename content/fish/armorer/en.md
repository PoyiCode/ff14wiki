**Armorer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Armorer.

This rare shellbound wavekin is named for the metallic scales that protect its one foot from predators.

Caught at: Swiftperch
