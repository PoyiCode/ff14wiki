**Glass Flounder** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Glass Flounder.

Flat sheet of a fish that pairs wonderfully with grog when properly stewed. Makes an easier-to-chew entrée than its name might imply.

Caught at: Onokoro
