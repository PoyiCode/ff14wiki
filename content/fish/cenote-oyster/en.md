**Cenote Oyster** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cenote Oyster.

The thick shell of this wavekin, found only in saltwater cenotes, is pulverized and added to soil to improve its drainage. Historically, this has saved countless structures from damage during heavy rain.

Caught at: Bitterbark Cenote
