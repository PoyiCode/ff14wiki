**Brassfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Brassfish.

Sharing color with the metal alloy of its name, this fish has been chromatically cultivated to achieve a variety of metallic hues, resulting in warehouse-worths of industrial fish.

Caught at: Dotharl Khaa, The Silver Canal
