**Vagrant Cascade** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Vagrant Cascade.

The faint glow of this translucent tube—a result of both fungal and aetheric influences—may lead an unwitting observer to mistake it for a ghostly apparition floating in the mists.

Caught at: Westbound Zorgor
