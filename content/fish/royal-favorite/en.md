**Royal Favorite** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Royal Favorite.

Tales speak of a royal attendant who plunged from their ship, only to transform into a resplendent wavekin in the inky depths. Some say they now ply the spectral currents with grace and determination, hoping to be reunited with their beloved former lord.

Caught at: Unnamed Spectral Current
