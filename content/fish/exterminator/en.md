**Exterminator** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Exterminator.

Armed with heavy pincers and a voracious appetite, this crab is rightly feared by the other denizens of the spectral current for its relentless enthusiasm in dismembering and devouring still-living prey.

Caught at: Bloodbrine Spectral Current
