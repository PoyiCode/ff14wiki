**Archmatron Angelfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Archmatron Angelfish.

With its majestic winglike fins and muted coloration, this wavekin has an almost ethereal presence. While similar species are found across the world, this specimen in particular has persisted unchanged for eons.
