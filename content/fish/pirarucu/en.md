**Pirarucu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pirarucu.

A legendary giant freshwater fish rumored to live in the bottomless swamps hidden deep within the unexplored regions of the Black Shroud. This snakelike wavekin is rumored to grow as long as an Elezen is tall.

Caught at: Proud Creek
