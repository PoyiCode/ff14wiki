**Stardust Shrimp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Stardust Shrimp.

This delicious native of the Stardust Sea is a popular staple. In Tuliyollal, these shrimp are deep-fried before being slathered in all manner of rich, fragrant sauces.

Caught at: The For'ard Cabins
