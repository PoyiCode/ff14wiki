**Moxutural Greatgar** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Moxutural Greatgar.

Presiding over Cenote Moxutural with an apparent sense of ownership, this mighty gar once frolicked with local residents. Since the nearby village fell silent, however, it has kept a lonesome vigil in the depths.

Caught at: Cenote Moxutural
