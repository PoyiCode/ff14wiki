**Downstream Loach** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Downstream Loach.

This giant loach will use its powerful fins to propel itself to the top of low waterfalls where it will lie in wait, fighting against the current, until it spots suitable prey in the pool below. Once its sights have been set, the loach will leap off the edge and subdue its prey from above.

Caught at: The Velodyna River
