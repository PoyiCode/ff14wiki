**Bright Red Coral** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bright Red Coral.

This colony's brilliant red coloration stems from exposure to spectral currents, and sets it apart from other more muted coral species native to La Noscea. Equally brilliant is the price it commands at market stalls—though shoppers seeking trinkets might say “eye-watering” instead.

Caught at: Unnamed Spectral Current
