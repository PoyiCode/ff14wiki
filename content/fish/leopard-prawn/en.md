**Leopard Prawn** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Leopard Prawn.

Cousins to the Yanxian tiger prawn, these crustaceans are scrutinized at market like a cut of expensive beef—per the gastronomically astute, the more vivid and varied the pattern of the carapace, the richer the flesh will be.

Caught at: Kugane Coast
