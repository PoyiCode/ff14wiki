**Great Gudgeon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Great Gudgeon.

If the gudgeon is the cravenfish, then the great gudgeon is the greatest craven of them all. So completely is it able to conceal itself within the sand, most folk are still dubious as to its existence.

Caught at: West Agelyss River
