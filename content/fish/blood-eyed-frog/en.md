**Blood-eyed Frog** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Blood-eyed Frog.

These frogs are fawned over by the Fuath, who think of them as adorable infants. Some are even known to craft miniature top hats for their unwitting charges.

Caught at: The Haughty One, The Jealous One, Saint Fathric's Temple
