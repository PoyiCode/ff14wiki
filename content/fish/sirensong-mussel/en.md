**Sirensong Mussel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sirensong Mussel.

In making its abode on the seabeds and reefs of the Sirensong Sea, this mussel quietly wreaks carnage upon other marine life by creating nigh-unnavigable mazes of razor-sharp shells.

Caught at: Open Sirensong Sea
