**Mirror Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mirror Carp.

This carp is adorned with dull, mirrorlike scales which dimly reflect driftwood and other waterborne detritus, allowing it to better conceal itself. Possessing a very mild temperament, it meanders through the waters of its habitat with an unhurried grace.

Caught at: Yak Awak Tsoly
