---
title: "Mahu Wai"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Mahu Wai** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mahu Wai.

Mahu wai grow from the floating islands of Abalathia's Spine, releasing scores of thin transparent tendrils into the sky to catch their prey and haul it back to their giant maws. The word mahu wai means “bottomless greed” in the Vanu tongue.

Caught at: Cloudtop, Blustery Cloudtop, Calm Cloudtop
