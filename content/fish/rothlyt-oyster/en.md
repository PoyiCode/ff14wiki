**Rothlyt Oyster** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rothlyt Oyster.

Also known as the milk of the sea, these tasty bivalve mollusks can be found on sea rocks all around Eorzea, but it is said the plumpest, most succulent grow in the nutrient-rich waters of the Rothlyt Sound.

Caught at: Moraby Drydocks, Oschon's Torch, Candlekeep Quay, Swiftperch, Skull Valley, The Brewer's Beacon, The Silver Bazaar, Vesper Bay
