**Shin Snuffler** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shin Snuffler.

A species of flat catfish residing in the shallow waters of Ankledeep. Locals wading in are often met with the tickling touch of barbels as the pool's residents greet their shins with cautious interest.

Caught at: Ankledeep
