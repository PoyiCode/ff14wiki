**Ligaka Guppy** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ligaka Guppy.

Lured by the promise of new discoveries, some learned Sharlayans choose to embark upon a scholarly tour of Tural in their twilight years. Clearly, creative naming was not the forte of the aging naturalist who located this species of guppy in Marsh Ligaka.

Caught at: Miyakabek'zoma, Marsh Ligaka
