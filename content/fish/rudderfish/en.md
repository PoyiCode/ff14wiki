title: "Rudderfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Rudderfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rudderfish.

Blindingly fast and possessing almost inexhaustible stamina, the rudderfish is a favorite tool for mother dragons teaching their pups to hunt in the air.

Caught at: Sohm Al Summit, Tharl Oom Khash, The Habisphere
