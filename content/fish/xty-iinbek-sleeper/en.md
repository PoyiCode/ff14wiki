**Xty'iinbek Sleeper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Xty'iinbek Sleeper.

This fish has vivid, colorful scales that scarcely ever see the light of day. It spends most of its adult life in the ocean, then returns to Xty'iinbek Tsoly to spawn.

Caught at: Xty'iinbek Tsoly
