**Yak Awak Core** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yak Awak Core.

Found in the depths of Yak Awak Tsoly, these twinkling spheroid creatures are a valuable (if somewhat dim) light source for the people of the Ut'ohmu Horizon.

Caught at: Yak Awak Tsoly
