**Hardscale** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hardscale.

Coated with manifold sheets of interlocking polyfibrous scales capable of deflecting high-velocity harpoons, this is a fish that lives up to its name.

Caught at: Azim Khaat
