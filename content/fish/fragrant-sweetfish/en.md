**Fragrant Sweetfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Fragrant Sweetfish.

A predilection for feeding on the fragrant grasses which grow along the riverbanks has resulted in a fish so tender and infused with flavor that no additional seasoning is necessary.

Caught at: Lower Yat Khaal
