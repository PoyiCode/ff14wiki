title: "Iribainion"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Iribainion** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Iribainion.

When this multicolored wavekin leaps from the water's surface, its brilliant scales reflect sunlight through the misty spray in its wake, making it appear as if it is climbing a rainbow to the sky.

Caught at: Rumination's Ramble, The Lower Hungering Gardens, Lethe
