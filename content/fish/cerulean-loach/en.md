**Cerulean Loach** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cerulean Loach.

Legend has it that this wavekin is descended from a drop of blue sky that fell from the heavens before they were filled with all-encompassing light. The black star shape around its eye isn't accounted for.

Caught at: Longmirror Lake, The Haughty One, The Jealous One, The Spoiled One, Saint Fathric's Temple
