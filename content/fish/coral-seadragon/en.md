**Coral Seadragon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Coral Seadragon.

Its cunning disguise as a piece of inanimate coral is perfectly complemented by its natural tendency to remain perfectly still unless absolutely necessary. This is, however, credited to sheer laziness rather than an innate desire to survive.

Caught at: Northern Merlthor Spectral Current
