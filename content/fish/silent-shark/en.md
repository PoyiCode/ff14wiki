**Silent Shark** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Silent Shark.

Likened to and named for a mouse in the Far East, this shark is a swift and silent hunter that strikes before its prey is able to mount any resistance. Ranging broadly, it can be found in the Ruby Sea and all along the continental coastline.

Caught at: Kugane Coast
