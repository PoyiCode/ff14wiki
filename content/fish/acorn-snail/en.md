**Acorn Snail** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Acorn Snail.

Believed to have hitched a ride on the hull of a trade ship, the acorn snail is an invasive species originally from the New World, far to the west. As it has few natural predators in Eorzea, its numbers have exploded in recent years.

Caught at: Sanctum of the Twelve, Empty Heart, The Footfalls, The Unholy Heir, North Drybone, South Drybone, Blind Iron Mines
