**Moonmarking Saucer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Moonmarking Saucer.

The flat forms of these wavekin are held to wax and wane in perfect harmony with the moon. In the depths of the Ja Tiika Heartland, where the night sky is shrouded by unbroken canopy, people have long used these creatures as a kind of living calendar.

Caught at: Cenote Jayunja
