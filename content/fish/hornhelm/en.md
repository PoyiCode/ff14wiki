title: "Hornhelm"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Hornhelm** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hornhelm.

The large horn that protrudes from this wavekin's head is in fact a modified dorsal fin, adapted for swimming through sand. In the areas around its nesting grounds, rulers will oft be anointed with a crown of these horns, due to the strength they represent.

Caught at: The River of Sand, The Nabaath Severance
