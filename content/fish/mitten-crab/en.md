**Mitten Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mitten Crab.

The claws of this freshwater crustacean are covered in myriad tiny bristles which provide the illusion that the crab is wearing mittens.

Caught at: Everschade, Middle Hathoeva River, Rootslake, Urth's Gift, Proud Creek, The Deep Tangle, Hidden Falls, East Agelyss River, Raincatcher Gully, Red Mantis Falls, Northeast Bronze Lake, Yugr'am River, Northwest Bronze Lake
