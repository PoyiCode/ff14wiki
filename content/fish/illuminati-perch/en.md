**Illuminati Perch** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Illuminati Perch.

Selective breeding by the goblin scholars known as the Illuminati has created a new variety of perch so foul smelling that only someone wearing a specially crafted gas mask (conveniently such as a gobbie mask) might withstand the noxious vapors long enough to enjoy the rich, buttery flesh.

Caught at: Thaliak River
