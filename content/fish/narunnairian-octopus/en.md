**Narunnairian Octopus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Narunnairian Octopus.

An oversized octopus that keeps to the waters surrounding Narunnair. It is fiercely protective of its territory, and even the most minor disturbance—whether intentional or accidental—will earn the offender and all in its vicinity a face full of ink.

Caught at: The Perfumed Tides
