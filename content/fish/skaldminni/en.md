**Skaldminni** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Skaldminni.

“Strummed he upon a lonely rock, 'midst seas of blood and brine. A poet's love for wind and wave did weave through ev'ry line. Into the surf he cast his lute, a soul too deep bestirred. And lo, beneath the salty foam, his song can yet be heard.” - The Saga of the Skaldminni.

Caught at: Bloodbrine Spectral Current
