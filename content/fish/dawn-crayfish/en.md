**Dawn Crayfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dawn Crayfish.

Devoted disciple of the Dawn Father who battles his sworn enemy for control of the Azim Steppe, and rises as his rival sets.

Caught at: Nem Khaal, Lower Yat Khaal
