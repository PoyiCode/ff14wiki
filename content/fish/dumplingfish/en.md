title: "Dumplingfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Dumplingfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dumplingfish.

This freshwater wavekin plies the waters of Marsh Ligaka with the aid of its large dorsal fin. The Hanuhanu, who use this fish to prepare a variety of delicious dumplings, gave it a fitting (if not slightly on the nose) name, that all who encounter it might learn of its culinary uses.

Caught at: Miyakabek'zoma, Marsh Ligaka
