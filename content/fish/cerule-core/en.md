**Cerule Core** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cerule Core.

The Empire's researchers studied, dissected, and analyzed this frosty organism in excruciating detail due to its fascinating ability to convert the water's ceruleum into energy.

Caught at: The Eblan Thaw
