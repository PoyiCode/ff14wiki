**Palaka's Blade** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Palaka's Blade.

The steely glint of this wavekin's dorsal fins evokes the weapons wielded by Thavnair's temple guardians. Perhaps it too watches over some sacred site in the depths.

Caught at: Thavnair Margin
