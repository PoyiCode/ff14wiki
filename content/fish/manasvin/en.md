**Manasvin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Manasvin.

With an imposing name befitting a serpent possessing such massive saber-like tusks, Manasvin is heralded in the Near East as a warder of evil spirits—who are presumably as fearful of being skewered as any mortal would be.

Caught at: Thavnair Spectral Current
