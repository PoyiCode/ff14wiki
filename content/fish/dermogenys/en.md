**Dermogenys** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dermogenys.

What this wavekin lacks in size is made up for in silvery luster. However, as its shine is the product of the luminescent microbial parasites covering its scales rather than its natural coloration—a dull dirt brown—it is hard to credit the fish's creator for their aesthetic sensibilities.

Caught at: The Lower Hungering Gardens
