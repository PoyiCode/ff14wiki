**Datnioides** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Datnioides.

The young fry of these striped predators are highly susceptible to aetheric fluctuations—a phenomenon which manifests itself in their vibrant scales. As the species matures, this susceptibility wanes, along with the coloration.

Caught at: Leynode Aero
