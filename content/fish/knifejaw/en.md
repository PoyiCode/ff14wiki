**Knifejaw** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Knifejaw.

Eschewing the tedious task of prying barnacles from coral rock, this iron-beaked specimen simply bites down and grinds up entire mouthfuls of gritty matter. Its fangs are said to be as sharp as honed blades, alike in composition to tempered steel.

Caught at: Rothlyt Spectral Current
