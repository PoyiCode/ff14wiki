**Faeshine Clam** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Faeshine Clam.

Although the pearls produced are indescribably beautiful, it is believed that each time one of these glistening orbs is used in the crafting of necklaces, brooches and the like, somebody is spirited away to the land of the faeries, never to be seen again. Some would claim that this is a small price to pay for such exquisite jewelry, however.

Caught at: Handmirror Lake
