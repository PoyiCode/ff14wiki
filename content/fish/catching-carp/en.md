**Catching Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Catching Carp.

Found in freshwater sources across Yanxia, these shy carp prefer to remain in shallow waters for the majority of their lives, hiding under the leaves and roots of the local aquatic plant life. During spawning season, however, they become far more aggressive—if a tranquil pond suddenly comes alive with the sound of splashing, catching carp are likely to blame.

Caught at: Lower One River
