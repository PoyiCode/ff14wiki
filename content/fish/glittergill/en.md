**Glittergill** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Glittergill.

Exposed to the unusual aether of the meteorites, this species has developed a faint, shimmering glow. In Mamook, they are typically given as a wedding gift to young couples.

Caught at: Sapsweet Cenote
