**Ash Tuna** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ash Tuna.

A large saltwater fish known to migrate between the Sea of Jade and the Sea of Ash. In recent years, overfishing by boats from the Far East has led to a significant drop in their numbers.

Caught at: Costa del Sol, North Bloodshore, Mist, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Forecastle)
