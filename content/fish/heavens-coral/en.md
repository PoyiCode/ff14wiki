**Heavens Coral** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Heavens Coral.

Scholars of marine life believe that heavens coral is merely blue coral that, after prolonged exposure to high concentrations of wind-aspected aether, have taken on a lighter blue tint.

Caught at: Mok Oogl Island
