summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Oil Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Oil Eel.

When assaulted by a predator, the oil eel will release copious amounts of a slick, mucus-like substance that prevents adequate purchase, allowing the wavekin to escape. Despite what the name suggests, the secreted substance does not actually contain any oil.

Caught at: Hyperstellar Downconverter, The Flagship
