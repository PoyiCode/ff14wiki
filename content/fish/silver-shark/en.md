**Silver Shark** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Silver Shark.

A large, spindle-shaped shark commonly found in the Sea of Ash and known to attack much larger prey—such as fishers and sailors—without provocation.

Caught at: Summerford, South Bloodshore, Costa del Sol, Isles of Umbra Northshore, Cape Westwind
