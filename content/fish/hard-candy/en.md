**Hard Candy** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hard Candy.

When sea beasts suck these algal organisms into their maws, it looks as if they are sucking on sweets—hence the unusual name. In truth, they are soft, sticky, and taste of seaweed.

Caught at: The Rift of Sighs, The Rusted Reservoir, The Source, Sullen, The Isle of Ken
