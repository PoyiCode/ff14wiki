**The Horned King** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: The Horned King.

This elusive wavekin is prized for the hornlike fins that protrude through the sand. The crown princes of Nabaath Areng once hunted them as part of their passage into manhood, though it is rumored that those who lacked the skill to catch their own bought the horns from enterprising fishers.

Caught at: The River of Sand
