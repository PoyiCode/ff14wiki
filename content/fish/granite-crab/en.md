**Granite Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Granite Crab.

Granite crabs will not eat raw meat and have been witnessed drawing land-dwelling prey into magma before devouring it.

Caught at: Mourn, West Mourn, Anyx Old, Halo
