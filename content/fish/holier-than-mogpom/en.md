**Holier-than Mogpom** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Holier-than Mogpom.

An exceedingly unusual sphere of algae that glitters radiant as a crystal. The belief that pom-poms blessed by copious amounts of aether turn a beautiful shade of violet after death is one commonly held among mooglekind, which raises several questions both related and unrelated to the matter at hand.

Caught at: Greensward
