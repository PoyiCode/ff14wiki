**Dark Knight** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dark Knight.

Despite its ruthless reputation, the dark knight is a sorely misunderstood fish. Ask yourself: How far would you go in order to survive in the forbidding environs of the Unholy Heir?

Caught at: The Unholy Heir
