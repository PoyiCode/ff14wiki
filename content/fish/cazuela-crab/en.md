**Cazuela Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cazuela Crab.

Considered a delicacy by the Hanuhanu, the preferred method of preparing this weighty crustacean is to simply place it atop a boiling cookpot and steam the crab, carapace and all.

Caught at: Waters Hanu
