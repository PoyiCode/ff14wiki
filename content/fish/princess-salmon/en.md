title: "Princess Salmon"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Princess Salmon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Princess Salmon.

Unlike most other salmon species, the princess does not migrate out to sea, and lives out its entire life in familiar freshwater. Its succulent flesh was the favorite of a popular Doman princess generations past, which led to it being given its name.

Caught at: Lower One River
