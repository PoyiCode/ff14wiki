**Ku'uxage Bitterling** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ku'uxage Bitterling.

While bitterlings are commonly found throughout the waterways of Yok Tural, those in Ku'uxage possess a unique hunting habit. The wavekin often leap through the foamy spray of waterfalls, catching any insects in their path.

Caught at: Ku'uxage
