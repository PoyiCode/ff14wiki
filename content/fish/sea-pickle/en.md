**Sea Pickle** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sea Pickle.

A blue, spiny, sluglike creature found in the shallows off of Vylbrand, and considered a delicacy by Sea Wolves.

Caught at: North Bloodshore, Halfstone
