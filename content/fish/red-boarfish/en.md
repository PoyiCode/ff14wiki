**Red Boarfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Red Boarfish.

This wavekin's red, seedlike form conjures thoughts of abundance and plenty. For this reason, it is prized as a symbol of good fortune among spearfishers and divers alike.

Caught at: Unnamed Spectral Current
