**Ruby Coral** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ruby Coral.

More commonly known as “Reef Ruby” or “Peasant Ruby,” you'll find no shortage of this ostensible gemstone polished and set in the trinkets of Hingan traders.

Caught at: Shoal Rock
