title: "Stormfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Stormfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Stormfish.

The flesh of this migratory wavekin is a staple food of the Ondo. Stormfish travel in schools many thousands strong, and when they swarm in circles, they can create great whirlpools upon the surface of the sea. Superstitious sailors refuse to sup upon their meat.

Caught at: The Flounders' Floor, Where the Dry Return, Northwest Caliban Gorge, West Caliban Gap, East Caliban Gap, Purpure, The Norvrandt Slope
