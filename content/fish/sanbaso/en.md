title: "Sanbaso"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Sanbaso** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sanbaso.

This knifejaw's stripes create a striking resemblance to the patterned hat donned during the ceremonial sanbaso dance. Much like these performances, the fish are believed to herald peace between nations and bountiful harvests in days to come.

Caught at: Open Ruby Sea
