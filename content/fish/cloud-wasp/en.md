**Cloud Wasp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cloud Wasp.

Colonies of cloud wasps nest in the trestles of Zorgor the Boundless, serving and protecting their queen in much the same fashion as honeybees.

Caught at: Eastbound Zorgor
