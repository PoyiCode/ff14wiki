**Horned Frog** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Horned Frog.

This creature's bulbous brownish body helps it blend among fallen leaves and other dead vegetation. Readily identified by the protrusions above its eyes, it is said to detest having its horns mocked. Alas, no horned frog has ever learned the common tongue to corroborate this belief.

Caught at: Xobr'it Tsoly
