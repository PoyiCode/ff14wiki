**Spangled Pirarucu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spangled Pirarucu.

The Ronkans once wore clothing sewn with coins, and the glimmering scales of the spangled pirarucu appear to be keeping that ancient fashion trend alive. Catching one of these sparkling specimens has been likened to reeling in a wriggling treasure trove.

Caught at: Lake Tusi Mek'ta
