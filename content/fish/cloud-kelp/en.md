**Cloud Kelp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cloud Kelp.

Once pulled from the water, the spongy leaves of this kelp can be squeezed to produce a prodigious amount of liquid. The plant is possessed of natural sterilization properties, and any water thus filtered through its fronds is purified of any harmful contaminants.

Caught at: Voor Sian Siran
