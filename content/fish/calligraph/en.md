**Calligraph** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Calligraph.

This subspecies of lightscale is unique for having scales that look as though they had ink dripped upon them. According to Far Eastern legend, it came into being when a monk skilled at calligraphy held a brush over the One River and spilled a few drops of ink into the water.

Caught at: Lower One River
