**Rhotano Permit** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rhotano Permit.

One of the many permit species that thrive in waters from Eorzea to Tural. This particular variety is notably itinerant, and is known for migrating in accordance with seasonal currents.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
