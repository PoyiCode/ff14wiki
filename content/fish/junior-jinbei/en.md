**Junior Jinbei** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Junior Jinbei.

The calmest, most carefree inhabitant of Thavnairian coastal waters, this wavekin bears the markings of its approximate Hingan namesake: a breezy patterned garment traditionally worn during the warm summer months.

Caught at: Thavnair Margin, Thavnair Spectral Current
