**Forgotten One** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Forgotten One.

The Meso Terminal contains nary a record on this fish, nor the circumstances surrounding its introduction to the resort. The whys, hows, and wheres of its existence are a mystery that will likely persist forevermore.

Caught at: Canal Town North
