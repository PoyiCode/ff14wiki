**Dark Bass** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dark Bass.

This freshwater fish, known for its voracious appetite, is originally native to the southern continent of Meracydia. As its only natural predator is man, dark bass numbers have risen greatly in recent years, especially due to the increasing popularity of the faerie bass.

Caught at: Sanctum of the Twelve, Goblinblood, The Deep Tangle, Oakwood, Fool Falls, Upper Soot Creek, Lower Soot Creek, The Unholy Heir, Yugr'am River, Burnt Lizard Creek, The Clutch, The Goblet
