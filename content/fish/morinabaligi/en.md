**Morinabaligi** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Morinabaligi.

A giant freshwater fish so shrouded in mystery, that naught is known of it...except that it may take as many as five score years for the morinabaligi to mature...and that its eggs are delicious...and that those eggs are poisonous...and that this fact still does not prevent many adventuring elite from supping on the rare delicacy.

Caught at: The North Shards
