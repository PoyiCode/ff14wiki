---
title: "Lancetfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Lancetfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lancetfish.

The lancetfish fills its own body and bones with water to control its buoyancy in the depths of the ocean, leading some to believe it is an incarnation of the deep-sea currents themselves.

Caught at: Northwest Caliban Gorge
