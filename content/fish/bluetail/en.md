**Bluetail** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bluetail.

This translucent, cerulean catfish generally keeps to deeper waters, but will occasionally rise to the surface. Much more consistent is the alarmed reaction of the capital's citizens when one is sighted in the Stainless Course, as the wavekin bears every resemblance to an ice floe.

Caught at: The Stainless Course
