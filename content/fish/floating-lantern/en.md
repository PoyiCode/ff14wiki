**Floating Lantern** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Floating Lantern.

Weaving through the tides like a paper lantern lost in a soft summer breeze, this glowing invertebrate was once thought by local fishers to be a malevolent spirit known as a “bake-chochin,” playing tricks on unknowing passersby with its alluring lamplight. The discovery of its true nature was embarrassing for all present, save the jellyfish, which appeared unperturbed.

Caught at: Kugane Coast
