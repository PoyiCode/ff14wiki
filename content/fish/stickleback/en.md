**Stickleback** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Stickleback.

The row of pointed spines that adorn this wavekin's back act as a deterrent to predators. Incidentally, the appellation “stickleback” is used to deride the startling fashion choices made by wayward youths.

Caught at: Residential Sector
