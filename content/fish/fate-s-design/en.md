**Fate's Design** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Fate's Design.

The dazzling colors of this wavekin's scales oft inspire comparison to Thavnair's vibrant silks. In Thavnair's fishing hamlets, a bride will weave the pattern of a fate's design caught by her betrothed into garments for the ceremony.

Caught at: The Thavnairian Coast
