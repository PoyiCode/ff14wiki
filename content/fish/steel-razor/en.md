**Steel Razor** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Steel Razor.

Cutting vertically through the ocean like the blade it so resembles, this steel-hued wavekin can—once its stiffened husk has been dried and sharpened—be put to actual use as a surprisingly effective razor.

Caught at: The Western Kholusian Coast
