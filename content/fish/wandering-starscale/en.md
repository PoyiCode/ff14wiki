**Wandering Starscale** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Wandering Starscale.

As a satellite forever orbits its star, so too does this wavekin follow an elliptical trail around and around a pole perceptible only to its species. Should the fish veer too far from its orbit, it would surely perish.

Caught at: Abode of the Ea, Elysian Playgrounds α, Elysian Playgrounds β
