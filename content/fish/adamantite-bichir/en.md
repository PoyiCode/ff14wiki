**Adamantite Bichir** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Adamantite Bichir.

Clad in hard, interlocking links that resemble a suit of mail more than they do scales, this fish is known to leave a trail of broken muud suud teeth in its wake.

Caught at: Heather Falls
