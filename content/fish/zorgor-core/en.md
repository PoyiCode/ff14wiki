**Zorgor Core** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Zorgor Core.

One theory has suggested that these creatures are born from dense pockets of ambient aether, but the truth of it has yet to be ascertained.

Caught at: Westbound Zorgor, Eastbound Zorgor
