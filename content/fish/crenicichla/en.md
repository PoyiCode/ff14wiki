**Crenicichla** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Crenicichla.

This Turali cichlid's delicate fins are a sight to behold in motion—if one can find a pristine specimen. Crenicichla are possessed of a rather aggressive demeanor, and their fins often end up ragged and torn from frequent bouts of intraspecies sparring.

Caught at: The Dewspun Bank
