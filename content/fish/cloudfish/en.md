---
title: "Cloudfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Cloudfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cloudfish.

The Vanu believe that these fish are born of the fine rainless clouds that fill the sky in the summer months. While living, their bodies remain translucent. When taken from the water, however, the translucence is lost and the cloudfish's skin turns pure white.

Caught at: Eil Tohm, Weston Waters, Voor Sian Siran, The Eddies, Diadem Skysprings, Southern Diadem Lake, Northern Diadem Lake
