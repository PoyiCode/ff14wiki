**Platinum Seahorse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Platinum Seahorse.

Smitten with its precious platinum gleam, Lakeland nobles will pay top coin to have this rare wavekin for their private aquariums. Apothecaries, too, prize its dried remains as an ingredient for various alchemical mixtures.
