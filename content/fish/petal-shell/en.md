**Petal Shell** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Petal Shell.

Aptly named for their resemblance to plum blossom petals, these vividly colored bivalves have long been used not only for decoration, but also in the construction of various tools.

Caught at: Plum Spring
