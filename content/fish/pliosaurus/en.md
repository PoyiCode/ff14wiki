**Pliosaurus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pliosaurus.

This venerable scalekin is endowed with rows of saw-like teeth, which it uses to terrifying effect in dispatching hapless prey. Mere mention of its name—and the image of those dreadful fangs that inevitably follows—is enough to strike fear into the hearts of veteran fishers, and shred any confidence that mere ilms of timber might keep one safe from harm.

Caught at: Thavnair Spectral Current
