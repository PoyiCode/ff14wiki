**Opabinia** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Opabinia.

One can only imagine the manner of twisted carnal desires which drove the Allagan bioengineer who created this five-eyed, tentacled monstrosity.
