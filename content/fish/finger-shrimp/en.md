**Finger Shrimp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Finger Shrimp.

A common saltwater shrimp found in the plankton-rich waters of the Sea of Jade. Boiled in seawater and topped with a squeeze of fresh Thanalan sun lemon is the preferred method of preparation.

Caught at: Limsa Lominsa Lower Decks, Limsa Lominsa Upper Decks, Zephyr Drift, Summerford, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Forecastle)
