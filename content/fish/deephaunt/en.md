**Deephaunt** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Deephaunt.

Found in the brackish waters which collect in the caverns of Woven Oath, these fish instinctively sink down to the deepest, darkest part of their stygian homes. Over the years, their eyes have devolved into blind orbs, this lack of vision causing them to swim a wandering, erratic course in the search for food.

Caught at: Woven Oath
