title: "Wraithfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Wraithfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Wraithfish.

Devastated by her husband's infidelities, the fisherman's wife hanged herself one night in the woods, and her visage would come to haunt him each day, upon the face of every fish he caught.

Caught at: Dimwold
