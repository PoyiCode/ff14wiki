**Wahoo** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Wahoo.

A razor-fanged saltwater fish known to migrate throughout the warm waters of the Sea of Ash, following the schools of shrimp on which it feeds. While not considered a delicacy, the wahoo is treasured amongst alchemists who employ the bones and scales in several different medicines.

Caught at: Skull Valley, Isles of Umbra Northshore, The Ship Graveyard, Cape Westwind, Reaver Hide, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Sterncastle), North Isle of Endless Summer
