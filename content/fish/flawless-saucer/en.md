**Flawless Saucer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Flawless Saucer.

Mathematically, this creature's body is a near-perfect circle. The Mamool Ja have used them as ritual platters since time immemorial.

Caught at: Cenote Jayunja
