**Scimitarfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Scimitarfish.

This crescent-bodied fish may resemble a Gyr Abanian blade in shape, but as a weapon is better suited to slapping than slashing.

Caught at: The Arms of Meed
