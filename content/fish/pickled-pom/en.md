**Pickled Pom** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pickled Pom.

While it may look as though it would be at home attached to a moogle's head and smell like it has been soaked in vinegar, the question of what it actually is remains unanswered.

Caught at: Delta Quadrant
