**Oschon's Print** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Oschon's Print.

Never has a Moraby flounder lived so long and grown so large, fisherfolk believe this specimen to be the very fish trod upon by the Wanderer Himself.

Caught at: Moraby Bay
