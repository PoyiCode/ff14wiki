**Bitterbark Caiman** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bitterbark Caiman.

These hulking scalekin spend much of their lives in murky brackish waters, flesh slowly steeping in salt. So delicious a prize are these pre-seasoned creatures, hunters will gladly risk life and limb to bring one down.

Caught at: Bitterbark Cenote
