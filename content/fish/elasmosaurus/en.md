**Elasmosaurus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Elasmosaurus.

Once thought to be extinct, legends tell of this enormous creature being used to tow pirate ships in the seas around Aerslaent. They are said to form close bonds with those worthy of their respect, even going so far as to sacrifice themselves for the good of their masters.

Caught at: Northern Merlthor Spectral Current
