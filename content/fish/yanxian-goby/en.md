**Yanxian Goby** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yanxian Goby.

This species of goby has migrated south from the Azim Steppe. The aether-rich spectral currents are their domain, and they swim through the turbulent waters without challenge.

Caught at: One River Spectral Current
