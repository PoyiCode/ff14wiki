**Ponderer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ponderer.

This slight, thin-finned wavekin is known for its docility and diffidence, and can oft be found tarrying in the water's darker corners, as if lost in deep contemplation.

Caught at: Reflecting Pool
