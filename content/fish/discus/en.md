**Discus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Discus.

A colorful freshwater fish that can only survive in rivers located in more temperate climes. Though there is no proof, most believe that the fish was introduced to Eorzean waters by foreign traders from the south.

Caught at: Hidden Falls, East Agelyss River, Raincatcher Gully, Red Mantis Falls, The Burning Wall, Zahar'ak
