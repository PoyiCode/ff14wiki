**Peeping Pisces** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Peeping Pisces.

The bulging eyes that endow this fish with expanded peripheral vision also give it its voyeuristic moniker and scandalous reputation.

Caught at: The Bull's Bath
