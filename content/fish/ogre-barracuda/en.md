**Ogre Barracuda** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ogre Barracuda.

A large saltwater wavekin that roams the sea in small schools, preying on smaller fish such as anchovies or sardines. Its name comes from fishers who claim that landing an ogre barracuda is more difficult than slaying an actual ogre.

Caught at: Summerford, North Bloodshore, The Brewer's Beacon, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Forecastle)
