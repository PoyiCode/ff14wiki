**Darpana** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Darpana.

The distinct pattern adorning the scales of this flat fish resembles the glinting eye of some deep-sea behemoth. Indeed, the illusion is enough to send even wavekin much larger than darpana fleeing in terror.

Caught at: Thavnair Spectral Current
