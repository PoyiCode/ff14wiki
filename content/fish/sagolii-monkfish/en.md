**Sagolii Monkfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sagolii Monkfish.

When hunting, the Sagolii monkfish uses a method similar to its sea-bound cousin—attracting potential prey to its location with a glowing appendage situated directly above its gaping maw.

Caught at: Sagolii Desert
