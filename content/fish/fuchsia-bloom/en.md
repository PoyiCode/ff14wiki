**Fuchsia Bloom** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Fuchsia Bloom.

This fish is said to have come into being when a pixie, having grown tired of playing princess, dropped a wreath of flower petals into a nearby pond. Any who have seen a school of these wavekin up close will attest that the legend must be true, for the vibrancy of their coloring rivals that of any flower bed.

Caught at: Longmirror Lake
