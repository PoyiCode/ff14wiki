**Gilfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gilfish.

Glamorous per its name, one glint of this fish's ostentatious scales is often enough to invite avaricious wish-chasers to a watery grave.

Caught at: The Outer Fist
