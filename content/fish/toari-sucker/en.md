**Toari Sucker** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Toari Sucker.

The Tonawawta traditional swimming style is said to have taken inspiration from the strong and steady movements of the Toari sucker.

Caught at: Lake Toari
