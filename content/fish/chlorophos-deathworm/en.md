**Chlorophos Deathworm** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Chlorophos Deathworm.

Per Loporrit sources, little can compare to the experience of gazing into silent subterranean waters to see this massive, writhing worm casting a sickly glow. They likely have the sunken spores of proximal fungi to thank for the unique experience, though the scientific community has been slow to verify this hypothesis.

Caught at: The Chlorophospond
