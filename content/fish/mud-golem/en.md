**Mud Golem** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mud Golem.

This mudcrab has grown to such impossible proportions as to put people in mind of a golem when it scuttles through murky waters.

Caught at: The Footfalls
