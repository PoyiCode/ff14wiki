**Velodyna Salmon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Velodyna Salmon.

This species of salmon swims in the Velodyna and Hathoeva rivers. The people of Gyr Abania mark its journey upstream as a sure sign that the seasons have turned.

Caught at: The Velodyna River
