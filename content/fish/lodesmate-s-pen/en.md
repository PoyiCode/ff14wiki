**Lodesmate's Pen** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lodesmate's Pen.

This specimen's name derives from its slender, cylindrical form, as well as several recorded instances of lodesmen losing their quills overboard and resorting to questionable measures to replace them. One has to pity the luckless wavekin hauled from the depths and subjected to multiple snout-first inkwell dippings.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
