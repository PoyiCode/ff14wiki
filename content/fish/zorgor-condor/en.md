**Zorgor Condor** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Zorgor Condor.

This carnivorous predator primarily targets cloudfish, but may sometimes dive at pedestrians crossing Zorgor the Boundless, especially if they appear to be carrying a tasty snack.

Caught at: Westbound Zorgor
