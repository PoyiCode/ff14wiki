**Hunmu Ob** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hunmu Ob.

The distinctive striped pattern of this wavekin functions as camouflage when swimming through sun-dappled waters. It is little wonder, then, that the Xbr'aal name for this fish means “hidden in white.”

Caught at: Iq Br'aax Reservoir
