**Dark Ambusher** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dark Ambusher.

A dark sleeper that has grown to immense proportions. Having the appearance of a boulder, it lies completely still in wait for prey, and strikes with an alacrity that belies its girth.

Caught at: Sweetbloom Pier
