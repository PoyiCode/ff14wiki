**Aquamaton** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Aquamaton.

This metallic, mechanical marvel was forged by the dwarves as a means to excavate underwater resources. The moment it entered the ocean, however, it jetted away into the depths like an excited pup, never to return...

Caught at: Seagazer Shoals
