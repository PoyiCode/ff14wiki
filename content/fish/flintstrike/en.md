**Flintstrike** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Flintstrike.

This wavekin has developed its glowing orange fins in order to attract a mate. This also has the unwanted side effect of drawing the attention of every predator within a square malm, though this is perhaps a small price to pay to find one's true love.

Caught at: The Pappus Tree
