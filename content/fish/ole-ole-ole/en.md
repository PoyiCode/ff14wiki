title: "Ole Ole Ole"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."

**Ole Ole Ole** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ole Ole Ole.

Though the name may suggest to Eorzean speakers some association with the leftward direction, rest assured that, unlike its smaller kin, this specimen can only swim toward its right fin. Its appellation instead refers to a chant beloved by fans of a local ball-based sport—who must resist the temptation to send it soaring aloft.

Caught at: Chirwagur Lake
