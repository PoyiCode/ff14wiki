**Frozen Regotoise** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Frozen Regotoise.

This creature is a particularly long-lived specimen of the regotoise family. In keeping with a popular fiction they derived from gods know where, the Loporrits insist that the Frozen Fissure is, in fact, just one of the grooves in the massive moon tortoise's craggy shell.

Caught at: The Frozen Fissure
