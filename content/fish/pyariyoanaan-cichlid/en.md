title: "Pyariyoanaan Cichlid"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Pyariyoanaan Cichlid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pyariyoanaan Cichlid.

This midsized freshwater fish is said to change the color of its scales based on weather and water currents. As the fisherfolk say: “When the cichlid is brown, the sun sticks around; when the cichlid is blue, rain will pass through.”

Caught at: Niikwerepi
