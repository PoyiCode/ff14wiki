**Sunray Ray** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sunray Ray.

This carefree ray lives happily amongst the clouds, guided only by the wind and its own sense of adventure. Not even capture can unsettle its insouciance, as it tends to bond with the fisher that reels it in.

Caught at: Westbound Zorgor
