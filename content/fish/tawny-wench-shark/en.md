**Tawny Wench Shark** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tawny Wench Shark.

To spear this shark with a single harpoon is a Kojin rite of passage, a custom lending itself to easy innuendo and foisting an undeservedly tawdry reputation upon the shark.

Caught at: The Isle of Bekko
