**Judgment Staff** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Judgment Staff.

Named after the primal Ramuh's signature weapon, this strange freshwater fish will only feed when the weather is rainy.
