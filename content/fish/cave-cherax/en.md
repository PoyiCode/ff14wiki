**Cave Cherax** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cave Cherax.

It is said that not even a fishing line made from adamantite wire can withstand the cutting force of this legendary giant crustacean's claws.

Caught at: Daniffen Pass
