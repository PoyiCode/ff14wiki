**Aronnax** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Aronnax.

Possesses incredible speed, though unfortunately only when swimming backwards. This oceanic oddity wishes it had eyes in the back of its head.

Caught at: Rhotano Spectral Current
