**Ut'ohmu Dawnfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ut'ohmu Dawnfish.

An undeniably beautiful wavekin that has inspired reams of sometimes questionable poetry over the years. One renowned bard had this to say of the fish: “o'er the Horizon, the first blush of dawn, drawn down to the depths by the sea's mournful call.”

Caught at: Xd'aa Talat Tsoly, Iq Br'aax Reservoir, Yak Awak Tsoly
