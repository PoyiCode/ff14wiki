**Hafgufa** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hafgufa.

Thought to be a scion of the abomination which dwells beneath Hullbreaker Isle, this betentacled fiend harbors a hatred of Lominsans, seeming to seek out and entangle their vessels with single-minded purpose.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
