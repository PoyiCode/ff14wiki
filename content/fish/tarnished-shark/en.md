**Tarnished Shark** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tarnished Shark.

A distant relative of the silver shark, its tarnished, dark grey skin is attributed to the warmer climate of its home near Galadion Bay.

Caught at: Outer Galadion Bay
