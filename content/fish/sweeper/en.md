summary: "A fish of Eorzea."

**Sweeper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sweeper.

Unlike its close cousin, the ferocious hammerhead, this particular species is content to forgo larger prey in favor of scouring the ocean floor for smaller morsels. Given the methodical manner in which it feeds, many believe it is merely trying to keep its habitat free from detritus.

Caught at: Rhotano Spectral Current
