**Crystal Knife** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Crystal Knife.

This wavekin is named for its resemblance to a blade of sharp, thin crystal—so much so that rumor has it the Crystal Exarch once mistakenly attempted to open his correspondence with one.

Caught at: The Trivium, The Quadrivium, Crystarium Personal Suites
