**Titanshell Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Titanshell Crab.

This crab is prized for the cloud-soft whiteness and delicacy of its meat. Unscrupulous fishmongers are known to boil cheaper specimens to simulate the crimson hue of its carapace, but a canny customer always checks the thickness of the shell.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
