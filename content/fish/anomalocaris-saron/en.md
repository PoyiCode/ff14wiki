**Anomalocaris Saron** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Anomalocaris Saron.

Propelling itself through the water with sinuous undulations, this primitive wavekin falls upon its prey with surprising swiftness. Its similarities to the anomalocaris in the Sea of Clouds suggest an evolutionary divergence in the distant past.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
