**Ilsabardian Bass** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ilsabardian Bass.

Unlike its Eorzean cousins, the Ilsabardian bass is mean-tempered and quick to bite, attacking other species sharing the same lake until there are none left but its own. Ill luck is said to come to all who gaze into its deep-red eyes.

Caught at: North Silvertear, Rathefrost, The Tangle, Ceruleum Field, Singing Shards, The North Shards, Parata's Peace
