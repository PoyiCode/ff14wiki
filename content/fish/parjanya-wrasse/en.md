**Parjanya Wrasse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Parjanya Wrasse.

Long seen as harbingers of inclement weather, this wavekin's name approximates to “rain cloud” in the common tongue. While these fish do indeed tend to appear before storms hit, naturalists posit they are simply fleeing as fast as their little fins will carry them.

Caught at: Thavnair Margin
