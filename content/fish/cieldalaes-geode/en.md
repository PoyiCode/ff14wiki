**Cieldalaes Geode** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Cieldalaes Geode.

In contrast to its dull, rubble-like exterior, the inside of this wavekin's maw rivals the rainbow beauty of a crystal prism. Those braving a closer look should also be prepared to admire its razor-sharp teeth.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Margin
