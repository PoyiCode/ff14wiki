**Thavnairian Goby** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Thavnairian Goby.

Small, freshwater wavekin whose territory stretches from coral to coast in southern Thavnair. They are oft touted as a local delicacy by the fisherfolk.

Caught at: Yedlihmad, The Thavnairian Coast, The Great Runoff
