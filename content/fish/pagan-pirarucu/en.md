**Pagan Pirarucu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pagan Pirarucu.

An unorthodox variety of catfish upon which Rhalgr did not see fit to bestow His gift of thunder, hence it has little more than its biting wit with which to fend for itself.

Caught at: Rhalgr's Reach
