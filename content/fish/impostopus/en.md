title: "Impostopus"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Impostopus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Impostopus.

By making full use of their tentacles, these cephalopods are said to be capable of impersonating any living creature. While they clearly understand the composition of their subjects, impostopodes seem to enjoy adding additional eyes to these imitations by way of their suction cups.

Caught at: Sirensong Spectral Current
