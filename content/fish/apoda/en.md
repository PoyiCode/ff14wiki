title: "Apoda"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Apoda** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Apoda.

Its rather repulsive form is somewhere between a worm and a snake in appearance. When threatened, this ancient amphibian secretes a toxic slime to deter would-be predators.

Caught at: Yak Awak Tsoly
