**Five-ilm Pleco** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Five-ilm Pleco.

This freshwater fish commonly found in the rivers of the Black Shroud will often grow no larger than a mere five ilms.

Caught at: Middle Hathoeva River, Goblinblood
