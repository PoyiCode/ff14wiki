**Twin-tongued Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Twin-tongued Carp.

With three lips and two tongues, this rare carp has enough internal and external appendages to render any respectable ichthyologist flummoxed.

Caught at: Whilom River
