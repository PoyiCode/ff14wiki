**Yellow Peacock Bass** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yellow Peacock Bass.

When viewed on the peacock bass spectrum, this species is on the gaudier end of the scale. Despite its brazen coloring, however, this wavekin is adept at concealing itself amongst the reeds while on the hunt for prey.

Caught at: Marsh Ligaka
