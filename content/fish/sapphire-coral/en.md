**Sapphire Coral** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sapphire Coral.

Luckily for the survival of the species, this sapphiric simulant has failed to come in vogue with the Hingan fashion-conscious as has its equally alluring ruby cousin.

Caught at: Shoal Rock
