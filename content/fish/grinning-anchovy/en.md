**Grinning Anchovy** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Grinning Anchovy.

This fowl-mouthed forage fish often plumbs the depths of Hingan cauldrons, its juices boiled out to form the base of beloved broths.

Caught at: Kugane Piers, The Ruby Price, Hells' Lid, The Isle of Bekko, Shoal Rock, Shirogane
