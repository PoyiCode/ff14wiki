**Autarch's Supper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Autarch's Supper.

Revered for their role in maintaining the cleanliness of water sources, these slender wavekin are considered symbols of good fortune in Mamook. Such is the reverence for these fortuitous fish that they are traditionally served at the weaning ceremonies of two-headed Mamool Ja infants.

Caught at: Sapsweet Cenote
