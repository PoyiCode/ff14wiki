---
title: "Sea Devil"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Sea Devil** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sea Devil.

Legends tell of giant sea devils emerging from the sea to wrap their many arms around ships and drag them to the abyss. This seems impossible today, as the largest catches in recent memory are little more than a yalm across.

Caught at: Isles of Umbra Southshore, Cape Westwind, Sapsa Spawning Grounds
