**Golden Pipira** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Golden Pipira.

Armed with a paralyzing toxin even more potent than its smaller cousin, this pipira need only dart in and nip its prey once. The bitten victim is robbed of voluntary movement almost immediately, the slight twitching ignored by the voracious wavekin as it feeds on its helpless—and very much still alive—meal.

Caught at: Mjrl's Tears
