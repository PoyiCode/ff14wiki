**Silver Sovereign** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Silver Sovereign.

This primordial wavekin can grow to a length of three yalms, or half again taller than a Roegadyn. Few fish afford the catcher greater bragging rights than the silver sovereign.

Caught at: Oschon's Torch
