**The Ewer King** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: The Ewer King.

While the Thaliak River with its majestic waterfall the Tipped Ewer is home to countless creatures large and small, only one has the size, strength, and cunning to be crowned “king.”

Caught at: Thaliak River
