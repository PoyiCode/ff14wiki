**Mythril Sovereign** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mythril Sovereign.

An elusive fish known to travel the seas along spectral currents. The faint glow of their scales from absorbing the currents' aether makes them a spectacular catch.

Caught at: Southern Merlthor Spectral Current
