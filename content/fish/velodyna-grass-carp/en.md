**Velodyna Grass Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Velodyna Grass Carp.

Midsized, yet critical, link in the food chain of the river valley from which it borrows its name.

Caught at: The Velodyna River
