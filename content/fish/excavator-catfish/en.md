**Excavator Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Excavator Catfish.

This large freshwater fish uses its tapered, shovel-like snout to probe the riverbed, hunting for tasty morsels amidst the mud.

Caught at: Marsh Ligaka
