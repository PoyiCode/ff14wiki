**Gharlichthys** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gharlichthys.

Professor Gharl—a Studium scholar of both Near and Far Eastern ancestry—happened upon this whalelike wavekin while conducting a survey of marine life populations in Thavnair. Overjoyed to discover such an ancient species so close to home, the Auri academic proudly began to refer to the creature as “Gharl's fish.”

Caught at: The Perfumed Tides
