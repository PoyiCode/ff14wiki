**Kholusian King Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Kholusian King Crab.

Until recent years, it was believed that these ungainly wavekin were swallowed by the Flood of Light, unable to escape due to the unbelievably slow pace at which they move. Whether they survived thanks to their natural resilience or through sheer luck remains a mystery, though many were glad to see these edible crustaceans return in great numbers to the waters of Kholusia.

Caught at: The Western Kholusian Coast
