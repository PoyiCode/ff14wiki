**Giant Trahira** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Giant Trahira.

This devious wavekin is known to lurk on the riverbeds of Ihuykatumu, disguising itself as a rock and devouring any overly curious small fry that cross its path.

Caught at: Peaks Poga
