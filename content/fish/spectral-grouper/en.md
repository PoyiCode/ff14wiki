**Spectral Grouper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spectral Grouper.

The semi-crystalline flesh of this grouper results from absorbing a surfeit of aether over its lifespan—most of which it spends swimming at the vanguard of spectral currents.

Caught at: Thavnair Margin
