**Yak T'el Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yak T'el Crab.

Xbr'aal folklore describes this crab as a messenger of the underworld. The Mamool Ja, on the other hand, consider it a herald of the heavens above.

Caught at: Xty'iinbek Tsoly
