**Ruby Herring** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ruby Herring.

This variety of herring is a common catch when trawling the seas surrounding Kugane. Its varied culinary uses see that demand is always of a pace with supply, and thus has the Ruby herring filled the coinpurses and stomachs of the local smallfolk for generations.

Caught at: Kugane Coast
