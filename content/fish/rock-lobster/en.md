**Rock Lobster** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Rock Lobster.

A large crimson crustacean commonly found hiding in the rocks off the shores of islands. The rich, flavorful tails are regarded as a delicacy across Eorzea.

Caught at: The Silver Bazaar, Crescent Cove, Reaver Hide, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Sterncastle)
