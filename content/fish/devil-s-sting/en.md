**Devil's Sting** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Devil's Sting.

Sailors speak grimly of a devilish fish which rides the spectral current. They claim this wavekin takes a dark delight in chasing down and impaling prey with its highly toxic spines, only to wait and watch as its victim writhes in protracted agony.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
