---

**Tonguesnatcher** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tonguesnatcher.

According to tales from the golden age of Yok Huy society, this ancient crustacean is said to have cut out the tongues of perjurers. One look at the creature's bladelike pincers is enough to convince even the most skeptical scholars of the fables' veracity.

Caught at: Ku'uxage
