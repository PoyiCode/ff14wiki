title: "Sunfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Sunfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sunfish.

Legends say this giant saltwater fish will help those stranded in the water by allowing them to ride it back to shore.

Caught at: Isles of Umbra Northshore
