title: "Sculptor"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Sculptor** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sculptor.

With its sawlike upper jaw this aquatic artist chisels, scrapes, and carves away at rock salt deposits for days on end, tinkering away at a mysterious form that often bears a curious resemblance to the phoebad.

Caught at: Loch Seld
