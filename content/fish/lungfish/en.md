title: "Lungfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Lungfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lungfish.

Unlike gigantoads, newts, or nankas which, by exhibiting properties of both wave- and scalekin, are able to survive in and out of water, the lungfish is a rare example of a wavekin that, despite being void of scalekin properties, can still survive for extended periods on dry land—sometimes for several summers.

Caught at: The Pappus Tree
