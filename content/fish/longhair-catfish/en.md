**Longhair Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Longhair Catfish.

Meandering purposefully along the bed of the One River, this fish's signature whiskers exude an air of solitary sagacity befitting its unofficial “hermitfish” appellation.

Caught at: The One River (East), The One River (West)
