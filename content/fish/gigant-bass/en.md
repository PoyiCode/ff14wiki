**Gigant Bass** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gigant Bass.

Fish known to grow as long as a boastful Gyr Abanian fisherman's arms can stretch.

Caught at: The Arms of Meed
