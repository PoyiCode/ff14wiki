---
title: "Kissing Fish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Kissing Fish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Kissing Fish.

To the layman's eye, the repeated pressing of perpetually puckered mouths by two of these wavekin appears to be an act of affection, when in fact, it is heated territorial dispute.

Caught at: Eil Tohm
