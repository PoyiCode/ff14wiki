**Qeyiq Sole** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Qeyiq Sole.

These flatfish bear the name of “boat” in a language spoken by migrants from the continent, likely because they resemble a passing ship's shadow. They can oft be seen slinking along the sandy bottoms of shoals in search of prey.

Caught at: Yedlihmad, The Thavnairian Coast, The Great Runoff
