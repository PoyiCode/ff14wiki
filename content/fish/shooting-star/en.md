**Shooting Star** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shooting Star.

These fish were once a common sight in Eorzean waters, but are now on the brink of extinction in the wake of the Seventh Umbral Calamity. Originally named by the Sahagin, their scarcity now lends itself to this moniker now widely used in the fishing community.

Caught at: The Northern Strait of Merlthor
