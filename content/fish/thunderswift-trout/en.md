**Thunderswift Trout** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Thunderswift Trout.

Deftly navigating the detritus-strewn waters at impossible speeds, there is nary an ilm of the driftdowns safe from this fearsome fish.

Caught at: The Driftdowns
