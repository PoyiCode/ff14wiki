**Crimson Monkfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Crimson Monkfish.

Inhabiting the turbulent currents of the Rhotano Sea, the dull crimson of this fish's scales is made even more menacing when the proboscis upon its head shines red. Many a treasure-seeking fisher has been drawn in by its ruby glow, but few have returned with anything save open wounds.

Caught at: Open Rhotano Sea
