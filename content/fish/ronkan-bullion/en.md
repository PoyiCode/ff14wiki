**Ronkan Bullion** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ronkan Bullion.

Some assert that this hulking fish is one of the lost treasures of Ronka, transformed into a living creature by ancient magicks. Regardless of the tale's veracity, it is unlikely that this wavekin could return to its original form by the hands of an amateur—only descendants of Ronkan priests can perform the necessary rites to do so.

Caught at: Mjrl's Tears
