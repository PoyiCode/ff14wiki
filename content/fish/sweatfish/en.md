---
title: "Sweatfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Sweatfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sweatfish.

The arduous sister of the seraphim shines the glorious sunlit streaks of her Dawn Father against the ominous night sky of her Dusk Mother, and is said to be birthed from the very beads off their battle-wet brows.

Caught at: Azim Khaat
