**Ankledeep Catfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ankledeep Catfish.

This catfish is so flat, it can easily conceal itself in the shallows. Those who decide to wade out into the cenote are encouraged to watch their step.

Caught at: Ankledeep
