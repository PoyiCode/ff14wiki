**Goobbue Ray** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Goobbue Ray.

Most assume this manta was so named for its great width and distinctive coloring, but some choose to believe the rather more whimsical tale of its origin—that a pack of goobbues, wading in the shallows, grew so enamored of the ocean that they assumed forms which would let them frolic in the waves forevermore.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Margin
