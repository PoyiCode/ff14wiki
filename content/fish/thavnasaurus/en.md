**Thavnasaurus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Thavnasaurus.

This ancient reptile inhabits the seas near Thavnair, where it enjoys enduring favor amongst the populace, appearing in folklore and myriad local tales. For despite its glistening maw of razor-sharp teeth, the Thavnasaurus has a remarkably gentle temperament.

Caught at: Thavnair Margin
