**Giant Aetherlouse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Giant Aetherlouse.

A freshwater crustacean with a moss-green carapace. Its habit of ingesting aether makes it a crude-yet-effective means for measuring the aetheric concentration of any water source.

Caught at: Unmoved Source Alpha
