**Captain's Pen** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Captain's Pen.

Belonging to a species of wavekin that have the occasional misfortune of being used as writing implements, these specimens are noted for their impressive size. Presumably their indignation when being brandished as a pen is all the greater for this.

Caught at: Unnamed Spectral Current
