**Tiger Muskellunge** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tiger Muskellunge.

This wavekin is a hybrid between two species native to Alexandria and Yyasulani respectively. Although remarkably hardy, it is unable to produce offspring.

Caught at: Outskirts Shallows
