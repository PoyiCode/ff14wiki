**Elder Gourami** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Elder Gourami.

Myriad nomad tribes of the Azim Steppe have taken to calling this otherwise unremarkable member of the gourami family “elder” for its twin beard-like ventral fins.

Caught at: Tao Khaal
