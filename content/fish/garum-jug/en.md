**Garum Jug** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Garum Jug.

Pufferfish are often dried and crafted into some manner of serviceable container—in this case, a jug for holding fish sauce. The wavekin itself produces a defensive liquid much like a squid, which is said to have a flavor similar to the fermented condiment stored in its remains.

Caught at: Rothlyt Spectral Current
