**Mudcrab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mudcrab.

As its name implies, the mudcrab spends most of its life in the muddy freshwater shallows of swamps and moors. There are still some superstitious smallfolk who believe that the wavekin are actually the spawn of golems, and will go out of their way to crush them under rocks upon finding one.

Caught at: Hopeseed Pond, Lower Hathoeva River, East Hathoeva River, Goblinblood, Lake Tahtotl, The Juggernaut, The Footfalls, The Unholy Heir, North Drybone, South Drybone, Zahar'ak, Haukke Manor, Singing Shards
