**Shadowtongue** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shadowtongue.

This ancient wavekin is wreathed in lustrous metallic scales. It is said that in the age of conflict between the Mamool Ja and Xbr'aal, these sturdy scales were used in the making of plate armor and other protective gear.

Caught at: Iq Rrax Tsoly
