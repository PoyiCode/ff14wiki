**Lordly Salmon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lordly Salmon.

A favorite of Ganen, the first Doman king. Per the local jus primae piscis practice, the first-caught fish of each season would be the lord's to dine on that night.

Caught at: Mercantile Docks
