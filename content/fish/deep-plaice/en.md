**Deep Plaice** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Deep Plaice.

Normally found inhabiting relatively shallow seafloors, this species is said to be seen in waters as deep as 200 yalms. Researchers speculate a proliferation of these fish in recent years has forced them to seek deeper waters where they can more freely feed.

Caught at: Open Rhotano Sea
