**Spectral Butterfly** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spectral Butterfly.

Does the swishing of this aquatic butterfly's fins summon an aetheric storm in Sharlayan? Whatever the cause—or effect—of its crystalline form, none can question its haunting beauty.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Margin
