**Fae Rainbow** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Fae Rainbow.

Legend tells of an overcurious pixie who tread too close to Phisor Lran, but escaped their terrible fate by transforming into a fish. However, whether it be with fang and claw or with hook, line, and sinker, fate has a way of catching those who run from it.

Caught at: The Jealous One
