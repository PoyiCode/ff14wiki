**Frolicsome Fish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Frolicsome Fish.

When these vibrantly striped wavekin were first spotted frolicking offshore by a group of island mammets, they bequeathed it with an intuitive—if not particularly inventive—name.

Caught at: Unnamed Margin
