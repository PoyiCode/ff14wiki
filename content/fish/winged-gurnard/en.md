**Winged Gurnard** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Winged Gurnard.

The wings of this flying fish are almost completely made up of concentrated aether—aether that dissipates once the fish has been caught, leaving naught but a skeletal frame.

Caught at: The Blue Window
