**Curtain Pleco** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Curtain Pleco.

Migratory fish with a giant curtain-like dorsal fin, which it lifts to move along until the day it bows out in its final act.

Caught at: Hak Khaal
