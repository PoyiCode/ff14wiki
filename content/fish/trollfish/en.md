**Trollfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Trollfish.

Solitary, unsightly, and extremely hostile. Do not approach unless you enjoy being whipped to within an ilm of your life by a cranial crest that hits like a steel war fan.

Caught at: Rothlyt Spectral Current
