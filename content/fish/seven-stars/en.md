**Seven Stars** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Seven Stars.

Like stars in the night sky, the maw of this tawny wench shark is pocked with scars left by the myriad attempts of lesser fishers to claim this lofty prize.

Caught at: The Isle of Bekko
