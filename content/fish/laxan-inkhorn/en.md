**Laxan Inkhorn** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Laxan Inkhorn.

Since ancient times, scholars have coveted this species of gastropod, not as a source of nutrition, but for the purple ink-like liquid that seeps out when it is disturbed. So common a sight were the discarded shells in the educational institutions of Lakeland that the creature came to be seen as a symbol of man's desire to pass on his knowledge to future generations.
