**Oxydoras** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Oxydoras.

The flanks of this large wavekin are covered with a row of spines, leading some to consider its viability as a kind of scaly, living cudgel. Some ideas are best nipped in the bud before foolhardy hands are ripped to shreds by indignant catfish.

Caught at: Miyakabek'zoma
