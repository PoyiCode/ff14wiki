---

**Ka Puyhu** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ka Puyhu.

The Hanuhanu word “ka” means “rainbow,” while “puhyu” describes the state of floating. A fitting name for such a graceful, iridescent wavekin. For their part, the Hanuhanu appreciate the flavor and texture of this fish as much as its beauty—dining upon it during festivals and other worthy occasions.

Caught at: Ku'uxage
