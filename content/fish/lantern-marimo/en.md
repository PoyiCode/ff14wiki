**Lantern Marimo** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Lantern Marimo.

Many a monk has reached enlightenment meditating before the faint aura emitted by this illuminating breed.

Caught at: The Outer Fist
