title: "Suiten Ippeki"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Suiten Ippeki** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Suiten Ippeki.

Meaning the “deep blue of sea and sky,” its name was coined by a poet who once viewed the brilliant salmon swimming the crystal-clear waters of the One River and claimed it was as if the very heavens had descended from above.

Caught at: Mercantile Docks
