**Goldenfin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Goldenfin.

So much alike gold is its color, this rare variety of coral butterfly has sent many a greedy pirate to a watery demise. For this it has come to be known also as the “fool's fish.”

Caught at: Limsa Lominsa Upper Decks
