**Bishopfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bishopfish.

The divine radiance emitted by the bishopfish's cranial proboscis is like to dazzle many a smaller fry into literally “giving its life” for the cloth.

Caught at: Clearpool
