**Mud Pilgrim** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mud Pilgrim.

No mudskipper has journeyed further from water in search of the promised land than the fabled mud pilgrim.

Caught at: South Drybone
