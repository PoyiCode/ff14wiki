**Callichthyid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Callichthyid.

A species of catfish armored in rows of bony plates. Territorial rivals will ram each other with shocking ferocity, the victor decided when the osseous scales of the weaker specimen crack and break away.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
