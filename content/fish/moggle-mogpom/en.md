**Moggle Mogpom** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Moggle Mogpom.

Considerably larger than most mogpoms, the moggle mogpom is believed not only to be the discarded pom of a fallen comrade, but *the* pom of legendary Moghome chieftain, Moggle Mog.

Caught at: Greensward
