**Ultimoat Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ultimoat Carp.

This large wavekin was once a domineering presence in Alexandria's castle moat. When the realm was reborn as Proto Alexandria, the few remaining carp also found themselves enjoying a new lease on life.

Caught at: Proto Alexandria
