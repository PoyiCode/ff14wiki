**Assassin Betta** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Assassin Betta.

A violent species of freshwater fish from central Abalathia's Spine that will attack other fish if they draw too near while feeding.

Caught at: Lake Tahtotl, North Silvertear, Rathefrost, The Tangle, The North Shards, Moondrip
