**Saltshield Snapper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Saltshield Snapper.

Where freshwater leaves this soft-shelled chelonian exposed, the briny waters of its natural habitat serve to calcify its carapace into an impregnable underwater fortress.

Caught at: Loch Seld
