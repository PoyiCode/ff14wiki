**Hookstealer** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Hookstealer.

Countless are the fishers who have cast their lines hoping to be the first to land this elusive prize—their hooks worn by the wavekin in defiance.

Caught at: Upper Mirage Creek
