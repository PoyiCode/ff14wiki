**Silverdart** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Silverdart.

When dried, these slender, sharp-nosed fish make for excellent projectiles. Some Thavnairian merchants have even taken to launching them at wall-mounted maps, planning their next excursion based on where the silver snout pierces the parchment. One wonders how many businesses have floundered as the result of an errant throw.

Caught at: Thavnair Spectral Current
