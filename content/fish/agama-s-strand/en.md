**Agama's Strand** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Agama's Strand.

Said to have started life as a fallen strand of Saint Agama's hair, the dried forms of these wavekin are often seen adorning worshipers' garments like parched little talismans.

Caught at: Thavnair Margin
