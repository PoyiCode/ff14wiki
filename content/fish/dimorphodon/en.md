**Dimorphodon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dimorphodon.

Do not be fooled by its size. The dimorphodon may be five to seven times smaller than its wyvern cousins, but its temperament is twice as foul, and its appetite four times as large.* *All values listed here have no basis in scientific fact.

Caught at: Sohm Al Summit
