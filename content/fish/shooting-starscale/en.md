**Shooting Starscale** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shooting Starscale.

Folklore describes this wavekin as the remnant of a fallen star come to life, from an era in which the skies above Tural blazed with meteor showers. Indeed, when seen glinting in the water on a moonlit night, it is easily confused for the reflection of a comet arcing across the heavens.

Caught at: Chirwagur Lake
