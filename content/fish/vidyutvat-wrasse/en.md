**Vidyutvat Wrasse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Vidyutvat Wrasse.

A tropical fish whose Hannish name means “thundercloud.” It can often be found swimming dead center in a school of garjana wrasse, every ilm the reigning deity of its electrifying subjects.

Caught at: The Great Runoff
