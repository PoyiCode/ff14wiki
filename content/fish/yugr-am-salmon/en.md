**Yugr'am Salmon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yugr'am Salmon.

This freshwater fish matures in the salty waters of the Bloodbrine Sea, only to return up the Yugr'am River where it was born to spawn. Many beastkin are known to prey on the salmon that return to the rivers weakened by their long journey inland.

Caught at: Verdant Drop, Middle Hathoeva River, Lower Hathoeva River, Goblinblood, Yugr'am River, The Burning Wall
