**Tortoiseshell Crab** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tortoiseshell Crab.

The carapace of this crab is unusually thick and durable, as if it had, indeed, traded shells with an amenable, hard-backed reptile. Such natural defenses, however, avail it little against a sudden influx of crab boats, seeking to capitalize on the growing demand for this Cieldalaes delicacy.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Margin
