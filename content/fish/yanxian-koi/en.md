title: "Yanxian Koi"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Yanxian Koi** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yanxian Koi.

The One River is flooded with this species, which is surprisingly nutritious if you can look past its muddy stink and abundance of small sharp bones. Locals are wont to drench it in liquor and sugar and let soak until palatable.

Caught at: The One River (East)
