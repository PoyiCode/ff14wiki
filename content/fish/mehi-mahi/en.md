**Mehi-mahi** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mehi-mahi.

This mahi-mahi subspecies moves at an infuriatingly ponderous pace. Seeking sport, some Thavnairian fishers will attempt to harry the creature along, though seldom to any avail. Apparently, even the sight of a fishing boat's prow bearing down upon it isn't enough to rouse a mehi-mahi from its leisurely locomotion.

Caught at: Thavnair Spectral Current
