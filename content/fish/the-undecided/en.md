**The Undecided** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: The Undecided.

Despite extended research into the behavior, scholars have been unable to determine exactly why this peculiar variety of rapids jumper feels inclined to constantly travel up- and downstream, the prevailing hypothesis currently being “just because.”

Caught at: The Slow Wash
