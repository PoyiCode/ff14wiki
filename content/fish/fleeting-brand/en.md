**Fleeting Brand** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Fleeting Brand.

A parasitic wavekin found lurking in the crevices of the regotoise's large shell. Its resemblance to the pillars of Zodiark's imprisonment that lie scattered across the moon's surface gave rise to its needlessly grandiose name.

Caught at: The Frozen Fissure
