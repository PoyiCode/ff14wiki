title: "White Oil Perch"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**White Oil Perch** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: White Oil Perch.

Fishers are often amused by the nonchalant expression on the faces of these wavekin, even when dangling from a line with a hook in their mouth. Their sedate manner is also seen in the way they slowly drift around the pool at the bottom of White Oil Falls, which is said to make their meat wonderfully tender, whether fried or left in the sun to dry.

Caught at: White Oil Falls
