**Shallows Sot** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shallows Sot.

Named after its alleged resemblance to the red face of an inveterate drunkard, the colloquialism has all but superseded the wavekin's original Yok Huy name. The fish itself seems unperturbed by the unflattering comparisons.

Caught at: The For'ard Cabins
