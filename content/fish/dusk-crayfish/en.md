**Dusk Crayfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dusk Crayfish.

Devoted disciple of the Dusk Mother who battles her sworn enemy for control of the Azim Steppe, and waxes as her nemesis wanes.

Caught at: Hak Khaal, Upper Yat Khaal, Tao Khaal
