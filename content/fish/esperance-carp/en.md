title: "Esperance Carp"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Esperance Carp** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Esperance Carp.

These large and lively specimens are descended from the wavekin that once dwelled in Alexandria's castle moat. Eager to see the population flourish, naturalists chose to enshrine their hope in the naming of the fish.

Caught at: Proto Alexandria
