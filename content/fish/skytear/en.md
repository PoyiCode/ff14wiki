**Skytear** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Skytear.

This fish receives its melancholy moniker from the Xaela, its azure hue conjuring the lachrymose image of a heavenly drop into a pool of sorrow.

Caught at: Upper Yat Khaal
