**Jade Sculpin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Jade Sculpin.

This celadon swimmer sports a semi-precious shade of green more befitting an amphibian.

Caught at: Lower Yat Khaal
