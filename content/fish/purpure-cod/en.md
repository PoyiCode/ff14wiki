**Purpure Cod** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Purpure Cod.

It is said that an Ondo youth first discovered this reclusive fish living in the depths of Purpure and found it so delectable that they secretly feasted upon it for moons, unwilling to share their bounty.

Caught at: Purpure
