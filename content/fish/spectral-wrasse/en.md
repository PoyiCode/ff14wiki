**Spectral Wrasse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spectral Wrasse.

Ichthyologists have spent many sleepless nights studying the behaviors of the spectral wrasse, unable to understand what drives them to chase spectral currents—if, indeed, extant specimens did not end up there by accident. The single paper published on the subject, entitled “No Wrasse for the Wicked,” has been responsible for several leaving the profession entirely.

Caught at: Kugane Coast
