**Vagrant Keeper** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Vagrant Keeper.

Appearing out of nowhere, borne on sudden, ever-shifting air currents, these tunicates are widely feared as spectral messengers from the dark depths of Zorgor—not to mention their penchant for grabbing the ankles of unwary passersby.

Caught at: Westbound Zorgor
