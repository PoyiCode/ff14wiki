**Spectral Megalodon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Spectral Megalodon.

As if being made of pure muscle and armed with rows of razor-sharp teeth was not imposing enough, this apex predator is blessed with an ominous, spectral glow. It seems unconcerned by the fact this alerts prey of its approach, as attempts to flee are almost guaranteed to end in failure. Perhaps this is nature's way of giving its next meal a sporting chance.

Caught at: Outer Galadion Bay
