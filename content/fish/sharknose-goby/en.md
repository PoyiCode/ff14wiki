**Sharknose Goby** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sharknose Goby.

The blue stripes on its back and golden tinge of its cranial scales serve to further highlight its decidedly sharklike snout.
