**Yanxian Softshell** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yanxian Softshell.

Yanxians prize the nutrient-rich meat of softshell turtles, historically imbibing their blood as a cure-all. Judging this to be “savage,” the Empire prohibited the practice upon the region's annexation, and it is yet unclear how this will affect Yanxian tradition long-term.

Caught at: Lower One River
