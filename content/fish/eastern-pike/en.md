title: "Eastern Pike"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Eastern Pike** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Eastern Pike.

This eastern relative of the northern pike demonstrates the ranged-attack prowess of an Ala Mhigan pikeman upon his prey, when not himself impaled upon a fisher's spear.

Caught at: The Arms of Meed
