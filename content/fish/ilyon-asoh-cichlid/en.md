**Ilyon Asoh Cichlid** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ilyon Asoh Cichlid.

This beautiful, vibrant wavekin is beloved by the Xbr'aal, and, in times past, was enshrined as a deity in Ilyon Asoh. So profound was the villagers' reverence that they saw fit to ban fishing for the divine creature on all but a handful of days each year.

Caught at: Xd'aa Talat Tsoly
