**Gravel Gudgeon** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gravel Gudgeon.

Succulent fish whose only refuge from the talons of griffins lies nestled under the riverbed. Seldom, if ever, has one been spotted with scales intact.

Caught at: Mirage Creek
