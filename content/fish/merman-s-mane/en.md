**Merman's Mane** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Merman's Mane.

The merman's mane is wont to appear mainly at night, the sight of it often likened to a man—or at least, the head of a man—amidst the waves. It is said to spy this wavekin on your journey is an ill omen, though none have lived to tell if their luck did indeed take a turn for the worse.

Caught at: Galadion Spectral Current
