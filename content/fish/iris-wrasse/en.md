**Iris Wrasse** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Iris Wrasse.

In the past, Endless would flock to the balustrades for a glimpse of these vibrant shoaling fish in the canals, likening their flowing form to a wildflower meadow astir in the wind.

Caught at: Canal Town South, Canal Town North
