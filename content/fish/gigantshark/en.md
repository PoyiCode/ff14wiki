**Gigantshark** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Gigantshark.

Once an ordinary silver shark, legend holds that the gigantshark grew strong through its eternal rivalry with the dreadful octomammoth. By landing this monstrosity, you have saved many a life.

Caught at: Summerford
