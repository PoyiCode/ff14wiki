**Bowfin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Bowfin.

Records of the bowfin being caught in the Black Shroud date back to the time of the first Elezen settlers, and according to the Seedseers of Stillglade Fane, the elementals tell us that the elongated wavekin was one of the first of the Twelve's creations.

Caught at: Lake Tahtotl
