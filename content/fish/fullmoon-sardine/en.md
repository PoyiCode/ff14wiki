**Fullmoon Sardine** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Fullmoon Sardine.

A tiny saltwater fish native to the waters of Vylbrand. Unlike other wavekin, they feed only at night, their activity increasing as Menphina waxes.

Caught at: South Bloodshore, Costa del Sol, Isles of Umbra Northshore, Isles of Umbra Southshore, Sapsa Spawning Grounds, Mist
