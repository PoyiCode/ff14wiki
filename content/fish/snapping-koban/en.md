**Snapping Koban** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Snapping Koban.

Spectral currents make the blue skin of this wavekin almost transparent, causing its yellow fins and mouth to appear as coins floating just under the ocean's surface. Many a greedy sailor has fallen overboard after reaching out a little too far over the bulwark.

Caught at: Kugane Spectral Current
