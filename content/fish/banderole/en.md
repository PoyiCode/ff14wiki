**Banderole** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Banderole.

The banderole earns its name from its pinion-like dorsal fin that is so large it is oft mistaken for a Xaelan standard fallen into the khaal.

Caught at: Hak Khaal
