**Shadeshifter** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Shadeshifter.

So proficient is this wavekin's mimicry that it can live entirely unnoticed in the shadow of other species. Some suspect its ability to preemptively imitate the behavior of other creatures is a limited form of precognition.

Caught at: Seagazer Shoals
