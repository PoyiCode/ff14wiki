**Dravanian Scallop** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dravanian Scallop.

Very little of this oft-overlooked mollusk is actually edible; its colors drab and unsuited for ornamentation. When ground into a powder, however, its shell is highly sought after as an ingredient for various alchemical creations...a fact which likely comes as small solace to the scallop.

Caught at: Quickspill Delta
