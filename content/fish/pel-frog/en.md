**Pel Frog** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Pel Frog.

Easily mistaken for golden ornaments, these dainty amphibians are revered by the Pelupelu as harbingers of financial success. Indeed, some devotees keep the frogs as pets in small pots, hoping to profit from their alleged powers.

Caught at: Downripple
