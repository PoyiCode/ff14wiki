**La Noscean Jelly** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: La Noscean Jelly.

The Seventh Umbral Calamity brought violent upheavals to Eorzea's flora and fauna, not the least of which was an explosion of La Noscea's jellyfish population. The fact that they nearly choke the shores of the region is made only slightly bearable by their relatively harmless sting.

Caught at: The Southern Strait of Merlthor
