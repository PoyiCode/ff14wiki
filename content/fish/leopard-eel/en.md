**Leopard Eel** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Leopard Eel.

Perhaps because there are too many in the sea to give a unique name to, it is not uncommon for a fish to be called after something on land to which it bears a strong resemblance. Or, in this case, after something to which it bears a scant resemblance.

Caught at: Outer Galadion Bay
