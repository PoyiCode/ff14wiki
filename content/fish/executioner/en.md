title: "Executioner"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Executioner** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Executioner.

Rumor has it that one member of this particular species once carved a large hole in the hull of a pirate ship, and thereby drowning all on board. Fishers often go to great lengths to hunt these wavekin, not for their meat, but to stop them before they can cause significant damage.

Caught at: Rhotano Spectral Current
