**Seafaring Toad** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Seafaring Toad.

To all intents and purposes, this appears to be an ordinary toad...swimming in the middle of the ocean. One wonders if life on the high seas was more preferable to being an experimental subject for the inventive minds of Idyllshire.

Caught at: Bloodbrine Spectral Current
