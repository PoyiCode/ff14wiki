**Sky Faerie** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Sky Faerie.

While many an Ishgardian lay has been sung about the beauty of these ephemeral creatures, sky faeries are detested by the Vanu tribes of Abalathia who find them to be grotesque and misshapen monstrosities.

Caught at: Landlord Colony, Sohm Al Summit, Tharl Oom Khash, The Blue Window, Mok Oogl Island, The Habisphere
