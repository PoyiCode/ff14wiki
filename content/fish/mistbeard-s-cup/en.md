**Mistbeard's Cup** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Mistbeard's Cup.

A legendary pirate needs a legendary vessel, and not just the sailing kind. Tavern keepers claim that Mistbeard would come in with the shell of a gargantuan nautiloid, and ask to fill it with a barrel's worth of grog.

Caught at: Cieldalaes Spectral Current
