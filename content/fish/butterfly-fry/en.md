**Butterfly Fry** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Butterfly Fry.

A unique variety of wavekin bred in the Dravanian colony. The exodus saw its return to Old Sharlayan, where the most beautiful specimens are selectively bred and propagated.

Caught at: Unmoved Source Alpha, The Mover Alpha, Unmoved Source Beta, The Mover Beta, Deepmoat
