**Silkweft Prawn** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Silkweft Prawn.

These wavekin are named for the fine material of the nets used to catch them. Connoisseurs insist that any other netting would ruin the prawn's delicate flesh, while others claim it is merely a pretext to sell the crustacean at an inflated price.

Caught at: Kugane Spectral Current
