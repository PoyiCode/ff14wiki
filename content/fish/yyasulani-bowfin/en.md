**Yyasulani Bowfin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Yyasulani Bowfin.

Hailing from a time long before Yyasulani was blighted by the dome's presence, this well-camouflaged wavekin can be found lurking in waters with a potent lightning charge.

Caught at: Crackling Canyons, Alexandrian Ruins
