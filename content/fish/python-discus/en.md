**Python Discus** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Python Discus.

The mottled, python-like pattern of this fish is prized by artisans of all kinds. Rumor has it that one Eulmoran fop employed as many as fifty fisherfolk to trawl the seas in order to have an entire suit fashioned from the elusive wavekin's scales.

Caught at: Sullen
