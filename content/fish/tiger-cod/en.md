**Tiger Cod** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Tiger Cod.

A striped saltwater fish indigenous to the cold northern waters of the Bloodbrine Sea. The name “tiger” is said to have been taken from a similarly striped beast native to the faraway jungles of Othard.

Caught at: Cedarwood, Moraby Drydocks, Oschon's Torch, Candlekeep Quay, Swiftperch, Skull Valley, The Silver Bazaar, Vesper Bay
