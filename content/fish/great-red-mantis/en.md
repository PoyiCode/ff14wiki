**Great Red Mantis** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Great Red Mantis.

This variety of red mantis displays even more exuberant coloration than its cousins. Though this vivid hue dulls to an ash grey after cooking, the flesh remains simply scrumptious with a pinch of salt.

Caught at: Thavnair Spectral Current
