**Blowfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Blowfish.

A saltwater fish that, when provoked, blows up to three times its normal size to intimidate the would-be attacker. It is also known to have an organ which contains a potent silencing poison.

Caught at: The Salt Strand, Costa del Sol, Crescent Cove, Mist, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Forecastle), Rhotano Sea (Privateer Sterncastle), North Isle of Endless Summer
