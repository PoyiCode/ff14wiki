**Flowerhorn** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Flowerhorn.

The impossible-to-ignore cranial protrusion that is this wavekin's claim to fame—rather than being congenital—grows as the fish matures. According to locals, flowerhorns with exceptionally large bumps were anointed in the Font of Maya and thus portend fair tidings.

Caught at: The Font of Maya
