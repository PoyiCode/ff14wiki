---
title: "Dustfish"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Dustfish** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Dustfish.

When threatened, this sand-dwelling fish will beat the ground with its fins, kicking up a cloud of dust and allowing it the opportunity to escape unscathed.

Caught at: Sagolii Desert, Sagolii Dunes
