**Ginkgo Fin** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Ginkgo Fin.

When a school of these emerald-finned wavekin dive and dart in concert, one might almost believe a tree dense with fan-shaped leaves had taken to swimming in the ocean.

Caught at: Outer Rothlyt Sound
