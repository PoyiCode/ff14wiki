title: "Storm Rider"
summary: "A fish of Eorzea."
---

**Storm Rider** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Storm Rider.

These sand-dwelling fish will skip across the desert until they are fortunate enough to stumble across a sandstorm, in which case they will spread their fins and ride the storm great distances.

Caught at: Sagolii Desert, Sagolii Dunes
