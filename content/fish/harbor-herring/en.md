title: "Harbor Herring"

---

**Harbor Herring** is a fish found in the waters of Eorzea — the kind of catch a resident might land while fishing or chat about by the water. In-game name: Harbor Herring.

This particular variety of herring will gather in busy harbors such as Limsa Lominsa's Galadion Bay to feed on the small sea creatures which consume the waste dumped from ships.

Caught at: Limsa Lominsa Lower Decks, Limsa Lominsa Upper Decks, Zephyr Drift, Summerford, Cedarwood, Moraby Drydocks, North Bloodshore, Swiftperch, The Brewer's Beacon, Vesper Bay, Mist, Rhotano Sea (Privateer Forecastle)
