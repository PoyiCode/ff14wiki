**Yok Huy Lantern** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Yok Huy Lantern.

A solemn stone lantern similar to those found flanking the steps of the Breathless Altar at Zirgorteh the Open-armed.
