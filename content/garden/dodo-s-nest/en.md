**Dodo's Nest** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Dodo's Nest.

Though their beady eyes and awkward walk give them an air of unintelligence, dodos are actually quite clever creatures. A mother dodo will, when creating a nest, first dry grass in the sun for several days before adding it to the pile, so as to prevent the growth of molds harmful to her offspring.
