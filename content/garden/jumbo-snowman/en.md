**Jumbo Snowman** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Jumbo Snowman.

Garlean scholars have conducted extensive studies on the subject of why Eorzean snowmen are constructed of only two balls of snow rather than three—the number of choice throughout Ilsabard. The prevailing theory is that extensive studies should not have been conducted on said subject. The difference in size with a normal snowman is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
