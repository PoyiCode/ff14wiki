**Limited Edition Firefly Lamp** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Limited Edition Firefly Lamp.

A wooden cage weaved in a Far Eastern style. The fireflies within provide very little light, but are pleasing to gaze upon. Available for a short time only!
