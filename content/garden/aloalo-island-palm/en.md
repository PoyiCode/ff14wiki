**Aloalo Island Palm** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Aloalo Island Palm.

This tree was imported from the balmy climes of Aloalo Island to brighten your abode with tropical whimsy. Pay no heed to the leaves it drops in homesickness.
