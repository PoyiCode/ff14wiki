**Deluxe Ornamental Bamboo** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Deluxe Ornamental Bamboo.

People hailing from the Far East claim that wishes written on small slips of parchment and tied to the bamboo will come true. (Magical wish parchment sold separately.) Completely different than last year's bamboo, we promise.
