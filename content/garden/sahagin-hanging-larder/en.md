title: "Sahagin Hanging Larder"

---

**Sahagin Hanging Larder** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Sahagin Hanging Larder.

The Sahagin typically hang their foodstuff on this treelike coral formation, the better to prevent its being swept away by the sea.
