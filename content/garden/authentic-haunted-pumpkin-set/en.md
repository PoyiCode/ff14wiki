**Authentic Haunted Pumpkin Set** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Authentic Haunted Pumpkin Set.

Quite why ghosts would wish to haunt an assortment of authentic prize gourds is anyone's guess. Perhaps they are the vengeful spirits of runners-up in a vegetable-growing contest?
