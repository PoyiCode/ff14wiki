**Traverse Clocktower** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Traverse Clocktower.

Crafted to resemble the clocktower awaiting adventurers who tread the furthest reaches of Pilgrim's Traverse, this timepiece makes an imposing, somewhat hypnotic addition to any garden.
