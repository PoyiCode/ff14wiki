**Ja Ja Gural Tree** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Ja Ja Gural Tree.

Originating in the depths of Cenote Ja Ja Gural, this luminescent tree lights up your lawn quite vividly.
