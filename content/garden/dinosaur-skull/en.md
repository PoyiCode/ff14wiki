**Dinosaur Skull** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Dinosaur Skull.

The crown of an ancient beast. While one may find it comical to stick his or her head in its mouth, collectors warn that its teeth are still razor-sharp and there have been unfortunate accidents.
