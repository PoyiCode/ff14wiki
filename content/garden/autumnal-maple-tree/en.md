**Autumnal Maple Tree** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Autumnal Maple Tree.

A young maple tree transplanted at the moment its leaves began turning red, and magicked so that it might remain in this state for all eternity (or until the spell weakens).
