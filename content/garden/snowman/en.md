**Snowman** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Snowman.

Garlean scholars have conducted extensive studies on the subject of why Eorzean snowmen are constructed of only two balls of snow rather than three—the number of choice throughout Ilsabard. The prevailing theory is that extensive studies should not have been conducted on said subject.
