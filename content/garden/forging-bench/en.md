**Forging Bench** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Forging Bench.

An outdoor furnishing perfect for those who prefer to do their smithing out in the open. After all, attempting to do so in an enclosed space does come with the rather bothersome side effects brought on by smoke inhalation.
