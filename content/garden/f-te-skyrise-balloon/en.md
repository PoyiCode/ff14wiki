**Fête Skyrise Balloon** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Fête Skyrise Balloon.

Seen adorning the Firmament during the Skyrise Celebration, this oversized balloon set upon an elegant mount brings a touch of sophistication to any festive occasion.
