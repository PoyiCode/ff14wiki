**Stargazing Deck** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Stargazing Deck.

Replete with seating, this elevated wooden platform affords an unparalleled view of the firmament. For the astrologer who prizes comfort as much as the pursuit of heavenly knowledge.
