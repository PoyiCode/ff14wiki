**Laundry Pole** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Laundry Pole.

A long pole upon which wet laundry is hung to dry in the sun. Take care when drying smallclothes, for the realm is rife with codpiece thieves.
