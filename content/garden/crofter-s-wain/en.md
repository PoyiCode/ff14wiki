**Crofter's Wain** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Crofter's Wain.

A trusty four-wheeled wain crafted from ash, worn from generations of use. Legend goes that its former owner died pulling it through the snow-blasted mountain paths of Coerthas.
