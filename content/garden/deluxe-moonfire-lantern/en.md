**Deluxe Moonfire Lantern** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Deluxe Moonfire Lantern.

An outdoor lantern traditionally placed outside one's home for the duration of the Moonfire Faire. Completely different than last year's lantern, we promise.
