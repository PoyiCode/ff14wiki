**Ultimate Kettle Nexus** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Ultimate Kettle Nexus.

A true kettle of legend that shines with such brilliance that it is said even gods would beg for a sip of its contents. It can boil water for a premium blend of tea in a count of five.
