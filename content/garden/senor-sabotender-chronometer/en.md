**Senor Sabotender Chronometer** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Senor Sabotender Chronometer.

The ruthless progression of time cannot be altered—but its cruel effects on one's countenance can be mollified somewhat by the charms of Senor Sabotender.
