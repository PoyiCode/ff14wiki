**Jumbo Kadomatsu** is an outdoor furnishing residents place to decorate their home exterior and garden. In-game name: Jumbo Kadomatsu.

A mini-tower of sliced bamboo and pine branches—the kadomatsu is a Far Eastern oddity traditionally displayed at homes and in temples for the duration of Heavensturn. The difference in size with a normal kadomatsu is indiscernible to all but the most observant.
