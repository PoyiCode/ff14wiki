**Snowcloak** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Coerthas Central Highlands.

> "As I stood there, my thoughts turned to those left behind in the west. The sun shone strong, yet it could not keep my tears from turning to ice on my cheeks." Such were the researcher's words, and I would see as she saw.

To take it in, go to **Coerthas Central Highlands**, between **08:00–11:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
