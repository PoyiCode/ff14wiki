**The Burning Wall** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Eastern Thanalan.

> Overheard in an alehouse: "Standing atop the crystal affords you the best view of the place, if you're brave enough to make the climb..."

To take it in, go to **Eastern Thanalan**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
