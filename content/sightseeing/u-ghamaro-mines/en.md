**U'Ghamaro Mines** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Outer La Noscea.

> "Reeks of sulphur and slumgullion, for sure, but lookin' down from that furnace, I could swear there was somethin' almost beautiful about the place. Either that or the fumes have gotten to me head..." Such were the soldier's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Outer La Noscea**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
