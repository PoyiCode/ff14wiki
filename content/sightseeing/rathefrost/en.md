**Rathefrost** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Mor Dhona.

> "In fields of crystal, I stood tall atop one of the stones and took in the view in all its shimmering desolation." Such were the adventurer's words, and I would see what he saw.

To take it in, go to **Mor Dhona**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
