**Raubahn's Push** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Northern Thanalan.

> "Even in the wind and swirling snow, the talons are unmistakable, clutching toward the skies. Don't ask what compelled me to climb to the very tip of one, but I can tell you the sight was something to behold." Such were the sentry's words, and I would see what he saw.

To take it in, go to **Northern Thanalan**, between **08:00–11:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clouds**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
