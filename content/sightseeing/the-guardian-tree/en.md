**The Guardian Tree** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Central Shroud.

> "When I feel I have lost my way, I look down upon the ancient arbor under the shining sun. I look down, and I listen." Such were the conjurer's words, and I would see as she saw.

To take it in, go to **Central Shroud**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
