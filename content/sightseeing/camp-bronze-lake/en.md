**Camp Bronze Lake** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Upper La Noscea.

> Overheard at a tavern: "Nothing like feeling the cool breeze on your skin after a hot bath. The view's nothin' to sneeze at, either..."

To take it in, go to **Upper La Noscea**, between **17:00–17:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
