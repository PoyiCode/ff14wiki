**The Fury's Gaze** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Coerthas Central Highlands.

> "Dragons, heretics—I've stared into the eye of many a foe. Stone has no eyes, yet no other gaze can pierce me to the very depths of my soul." Such were the knight's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Coerthas Central Highlands**, between **18:00–04:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Blizzards**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
