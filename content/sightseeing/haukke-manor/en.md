**Haukke Manor** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Central Shroud.

> "I fled as fast as my legs could carry me, till I stood atop massive tree roots with nowhere to run. The glow from the windows, so beautiful, yet so terrible..." Such were the madman's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Central Shroud**, between **18:00–04:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
