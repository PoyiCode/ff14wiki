**The Steel Vigil** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Coerthas Central Highlands.

> Overheard in a tavern: "...Bollocks, I say! Ye haven't truly seen the Vigil until ye've seen it from that very spot. Ye'll be wantin' to tread carefully, mind ye."

To take it in, go to **Coerthas Central Highlands**, between **05:00–07:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
