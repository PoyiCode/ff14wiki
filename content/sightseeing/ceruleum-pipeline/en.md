**Ceruleum Pipeline** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Northern Thanalan.

> "My duties include keeping the portcullis well oiled. A right fine view on a clear day, but don't lose your balance—it's a long way down." Such were the soldier's words, and I would see what he saw.

To take it in, go to **Northern Thanalan**, between **18:00–04:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
