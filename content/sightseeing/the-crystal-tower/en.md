**The Crystal Tower** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Mor Dhona.

> "It was a scene not of this world: a forest of crystal, and in the distance a spire that stretched so high it could have touched the heavens..." Such were the adventurer's words, and I would see as she saw.

To take it in, go to **Mor Dhona**, between **18:00–04:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
