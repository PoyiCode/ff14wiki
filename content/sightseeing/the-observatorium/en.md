**The Observatorium** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Coerthas Central Highlands.

To take it in, go to **Coerthas Central Highlands**, between **18:00–04:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fog**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
