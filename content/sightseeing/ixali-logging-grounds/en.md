**Ixali Logging Grounds** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at North Shroud.

> "I stood there atop the butchered arbor, my fist trembling in anger. Those who would harm our forest home must be shown no quarter." Such were the sentry's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **North Shroud**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
