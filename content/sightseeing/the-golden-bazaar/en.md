**The Golden Bazaar** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Eastern Thanalan.

> "A ghost town? Hah! Mark my words, I'll make my fortune there. When the sun is high, I climb atop the fencing and survey the land for untapped veins of ore." Such were the miner's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Eastern Thanalan**, between **08:00–11:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
