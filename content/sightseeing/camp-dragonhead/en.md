**Camp Dragonhead** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Coerthas Central Highlands.

> "Clearing the parapets after a snowfall is arduous work, but gazing out over the hoar-kissed peaks, a man might forget his aches and pains for a while." So were the knight's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Coerthas Central Highlands**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
