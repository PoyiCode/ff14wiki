**The Sagolii Desert** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Southern Thanalan.

> "After a fortnight of wandering, I found myself under the searing sun, looking out upon the ruins of Allag. It was then I swore to myself: I will not succumb—not this day." Such were the wayfarer's words, and I would see what he saw.

To take it in, go to **Southern Thanalan**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Heat Waves**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
