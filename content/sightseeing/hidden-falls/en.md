**Hidden Falls** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Eastern La Noscea.

> "The ogling from the men as they recline burns brighter than the summer sun. I don't mind much, provided they're handsome...and wealthy doesn't hurt either." Such were the young lady's words, and I would see as she saw.

To take it in, go to **Eastern La Noscea**, between **08:00–11:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/groundsit` emote.
