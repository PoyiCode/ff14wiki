**Zahar'ak** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Southern Thanalan.

> "Some call the sight bleak, but to me it's a thing of beauty. The winds pound away at the hills, but strong we stand—our gaze locked on our foe." Such were the sentry's words, and I would see what he saw.

To take it in, go to **Southern Thanalan**, between **05:00–07:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
