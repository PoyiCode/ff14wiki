**The Nail** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Coerthas Central Highlands.

> "I suppose the view is impressive, yes, but it's rare that I have a moment to appreciate it. Too much work to be done before the sun goes down." Such were the draughtsman's words, and I would see what he saw.

To take it in, go to **Coerthas Central Highlands**, between **17:00–17:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
