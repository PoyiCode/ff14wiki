**Urth's Gift** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at South Shroud.

> "Shrouded in mist, bathed in ephemeral light. I felt as if I'd left the forest behind and come to another world..." Such were the conjurer's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **South Shroud**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fog**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
