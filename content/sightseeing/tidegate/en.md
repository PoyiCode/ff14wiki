**Tidegate** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Western La Noscea.

> Overheard at an alehouse: "When I've misplaced my goods, I climb atop the tents and scour the ground below. Offers a clear view, and you'd be surprised at how sturdy they are..."

To take it in, go to **Western La Noscea**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
