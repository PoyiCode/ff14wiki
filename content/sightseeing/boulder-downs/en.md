**Boulder Downs** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Coerthas Central Highlands.

> "In the shadow of those unholy rocks, the mightiest giant will look as a dwarf. Yet from the right vantage point, even one of humble stature can look upon them all." Such were the knight's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Coerthas Central Highlands**, between **17:00–17:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
