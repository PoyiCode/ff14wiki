**Castrum Marinum** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Western Thanalan.

> "Say what you will about our foe, you can't deny that the architecture of their outposts has a certain ghastly charm. I would have enjoyed the sunset a while longer, but the cohort doesn't take kindly to tourists." Such were the scout's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Western Thanalan**, between **17:00–17:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
