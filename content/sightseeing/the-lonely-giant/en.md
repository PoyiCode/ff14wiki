**The Lonely Giant** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Eastern Thanalan.

> "Merciful are the rains that fall, for they give succor to the thirsting land, and comfort the old wounds of one who journeyed too far from home." Such were the botanist's words, and I would see as she saw.

To take it in, go to **Eastern Thanalan**, between **17:00–17:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Rain**, then use the `/comfort` emote.
