**The Carline Canopy** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Gridania.

> Overheard about town: "There's nothing quite like standing beside the mighty waterwheel, watching brave men and women as they come and go."

To take it in, go to **Gridania**, between **08:00–11:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
