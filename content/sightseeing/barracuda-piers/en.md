**Barracuda Piers** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Limsa Lominsa.

> "Under the azure, I gazed down upon warships three, their billowing sails of crimson leaving no doubt as to who truly rules the seas." Such were the merchant's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Limsa Lominsa**, between **08:00–11:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
