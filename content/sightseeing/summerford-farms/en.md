**Summerford Farms** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Middle La Noscea.

> Overheard at a tavern: "Erstwhile pirates taken to land, drunk on ale and tales of wenches and plunder, climb atop lamps as if they were manning the crow's nest of a great vessel..."

To take it in, go to **Middle La Noscea**, between **05:00–07:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clear Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
