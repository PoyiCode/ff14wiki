**Jijiroon's Trading Post** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Upper La Noscea.

> Overheard at a tavern: "Spent the night on that ledge, I did—anything to escape those shelled monstrosities. Wasn't a bad sight, though I can't say I enjoyed the trip down..."

To take it in, go to **Upper La Noscea**, between **18:00–04:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Thunderstorms**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
