**Woad Whisper Canyon** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Middle La Noscea.

> "Don't tell Baderon, but if ye ask me, ye'll find no better place to down a flagon of ale than where the water dances, keepin' ye cool on even the 'ottest summer's day." Such were the laborer's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Middle La Noscea**, between **12:00–16:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
