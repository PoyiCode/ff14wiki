**Alder Springs** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at North Shroud.

> "So there I was, starin' out over those naked tree limbs. Was feelin' brave, so I thought I'd see just how far I could go. As it turns out? Pretty far." Such were the mountaineer's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **North Shroud**, between **08:00–11:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Clouds**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
