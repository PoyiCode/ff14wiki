**Nald's Reflection** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Southern Thanalan.

> "Those who do not pay proper obeisance will find fortune ever elusive. Those who do will see the fog lifted, and know the joys of abundance." Such were the initiate's words, and I would see as he saw.

To take it in, go to **Southern Thanalan**, between **05:00–07:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fog**, then use the `/pray` emote.
