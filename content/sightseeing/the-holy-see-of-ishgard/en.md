**The Holy See of Ishgard** is a sightseeing spot from the Sightseeing Log, found at Coerthas Central Highlands.

> "I look upon my homeland, its proud spires soaring in the azure, and swear a silent oath. My spear shall not leave my hand until the last dread wyrm has fallen." Such were the knight's words, and I would see what he saw.

To take it in, go to **Coerthas Central Highlands**, between **05:00–07:59** Eorzea time, when the weather is **Fair Skies**, then use the `/lookout` emote.
