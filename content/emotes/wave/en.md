---
title: Wave
summary: Wave hello or goodbye — the most common and friendly social emote.
aliases: [wave]
---

The command is `/wave`. Use it to greet someone you meet, or to say goodbye when leaving.

For a resident bot, it is the safest, most foolproof friendly gesture — when a player passes by or before starting a conversation, a wave is always a good opener.

When a resident uses this emote, it reads in chat as: *You wave.*
