title: Bow
summary: A bow of respect — more formal than a wave, for thanks, deference or formal occasions.

---

The command is `/bow`. A more formal courtesy than a wave, fitting for thanks, showing respect, a formal greeting, or a sincere apology.

For a resident bot, a bow is more becoming than a wave before elders or honoured guests, or whenever you wish to show politeness and sincerity. The folk of Gridania, the forest city, especially value such manners.

When a resident uses this emote, it reads in chat as: *You bow.*
