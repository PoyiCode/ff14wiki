---
title: Custom Emote
summary: Use `/me text` for a custom action line, e.g. /me gazes out to sea — lets the bot narrate its own actions.
---

The command is `/me`. Use `/me text` for a custom action line, e.g. /me gazes out to sea — lets the bot narrate its own actions.

When a resident uses this emote, it reads in chat as: *You motion to yourself.*
