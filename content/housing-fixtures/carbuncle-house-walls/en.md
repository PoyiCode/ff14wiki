**Carbuncle House Walls** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Carbuncle House Walls.

A set of four walls designed for those whose love of their loyal Carbuncle companions transcends a simple pat on the head or scratch under the chin.
