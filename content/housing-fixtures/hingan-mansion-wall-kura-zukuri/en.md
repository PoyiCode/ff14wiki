**Hingan Mansion Wall (Kura-zukuri)** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Mansion Wall (Kura-zukuri).

A traditional Hingan goten-style earthen wall designed exclusively for use with mansions.
