**Palace of the Dead Flooring** is a house fixture — a flooring a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Palace of the Dead Flooring.

Flooring designed using reports penned by those fortunate enough to return from their expeditionary missions into the Palace of the Dead...with their sanity intact.
