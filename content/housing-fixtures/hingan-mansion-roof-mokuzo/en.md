**Hingan Mansion Roof (Mokuzo)** is a house fixture — a roof a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Mansion Roof (Mokuzo).

A wooden Hingan goten-style tiled roof designed exclusively for use with mansions.
