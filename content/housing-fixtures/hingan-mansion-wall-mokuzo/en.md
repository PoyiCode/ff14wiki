**Hingan Mansion Wall (Mokuzo)** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Mansion Wall (Mokuzo).

A wooden Hingan goten-style simple wall designed exclusively for use with mansions.
