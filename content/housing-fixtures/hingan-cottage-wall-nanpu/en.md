**Hingan Cottage Wall (Nanpu)** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Cottage Wall (Nanpu).

A southern Hingan hiraya-style painted wall designed exclusively for use with cottages.
