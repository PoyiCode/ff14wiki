**Hingan House Roof (Kura-zukuri)** is a house fixture — a roof a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan House Roof (Kura-zukuri).

A traditional Hingan yashiki-style tiled roof designed exclusively for use with houses.
