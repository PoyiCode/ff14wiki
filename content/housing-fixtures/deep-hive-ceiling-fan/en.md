**Deep Hive Ceiling Fan** is a house fixture — a ceiling light a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Deep Hive Ceiling Fan.

A ceiling-mounted fan created with the wings of the Gnathic primal Ravana. It literally hums with the Lord of the Hive's untamed power.
