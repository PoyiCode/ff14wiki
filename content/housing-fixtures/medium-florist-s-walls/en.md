**Medium Florist's Walls** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Medium Florist's Walls.

A set of four walls designed for those who wish to bedeck their home with an abundance of flowers—and be forever dusting the pollen from their deck chairs.
