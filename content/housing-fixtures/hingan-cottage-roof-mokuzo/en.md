**Hingan Cottage Roof (Mokuzo)** is a house fixture — a roof a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Cottage Roof (Mokuzo).

A wooden Hingan hiraya-style tiled roof designed exclusively for use with cottages.
