**Leveilleur Estate Chandelier** is a house fixture — a ceiling light a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Leveilleur Estate Chandelier.

A light fixture crafted to resemble the opulent chandeliers of the Leveilleur estate. Not an actual chandelier from the Leveilleur estate.
