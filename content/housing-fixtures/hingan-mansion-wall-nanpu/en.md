**Hingan Mansion Wall (Nanpu)** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Mansion Wall (Nanpu).

A southern Hingan goten-style painted wall designed exclusively for use with mansions.
