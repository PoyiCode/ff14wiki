**Deluxe Starlight Interior Wall** is a house fixture — a interior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Deluxe Starlight Interior Wall.

This wall bears a festive design certain to bring joy and cheer to all...until you finally get around to taking it down six moons later. Decidedly more stellar than last year's walls in ways far too profound to explain here.
