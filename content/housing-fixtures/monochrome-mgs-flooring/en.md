**Monochrome MGS Flooring** is a house fixture — a flooring a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Monochrome MGS Flooring.

Black-and-white floorboards and carpeting of the same design as those used to furnish the Manderville Gold Saucer.
