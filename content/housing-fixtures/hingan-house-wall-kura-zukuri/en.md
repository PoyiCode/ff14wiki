**Hingan House Wall (Kura-zukuri)** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan House Wall (Kura-zukuri).

A traditional Hingan yashiki-style earthen wall designed exclusively for use with houses.
