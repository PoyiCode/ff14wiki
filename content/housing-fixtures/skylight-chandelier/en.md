**Skylight Chandelier** is a house fixture — a ceiling light a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Skylight Chandelier.

This exquisite piece utilizes natural sunlight to provide illumination. How it continues to work at night, however, is a closely guarded secret.
