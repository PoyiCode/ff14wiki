**Sukiya-zukuri House Walls** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Sukiya-zukuri House Walls.

A set of four walls designed for those who wish to transform their estate into a stylish Far Eastern residence with attached onsen.
