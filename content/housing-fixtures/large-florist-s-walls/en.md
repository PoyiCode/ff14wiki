**Large Florist's Walls** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Large Florist's Walls.

A set of four walls designed for those who wish to bedeck their home with an abundance of flowers. Note that certain flora are known to attract murderbees.
