**Hingan House Wall (Mokuzo)** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan House Wall (Mokuzo).

A wooden Hingan yashiki-style simple wall designed exclusively for use with houses.
