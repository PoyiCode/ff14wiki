**Hingan Watchtower (Mokuzo)** is a house fixture — a roof decoration a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Watchtower (Mokuzo).

A wooden tower known in the Far East as a “hinomi yagura.” Used traditionally to maintain a watch for fires.
