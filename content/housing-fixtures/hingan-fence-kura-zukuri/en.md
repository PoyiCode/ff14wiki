**Hingan Fence (Kura-zukuri)** is a house fixture — a fence a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Fence (Kura-zukuri).

A traditional Hingan stone fence designed to add a touch of Far Eastern charm to residences.
