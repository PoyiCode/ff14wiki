**Deluxe Bombard Lamp** is a house fixture — a ceiling light a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Deluxe Bombard Lamp.

A massive lamp designed to resemble the villain of the Moonfire Faire—the Bombard. Completely different than last year's lamp, we promise.
