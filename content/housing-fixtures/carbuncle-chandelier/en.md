**Carbuncle Chandelier** is a house fixture — a ceiling light a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Carbuncle Chandelier.

Because who doesn't want to be welcomed home from a hard day's adventuring by a few Carbuncles dangling from their ceiling?
