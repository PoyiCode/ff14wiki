**Palace of the Dead Interior Wall** is a house fixture — a interior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Palace of the Dead Interior Wall.

An interior wall designed using reports penned by those fortunate enough to return from their expeditionary missions into the Palace of the Dead...with their sanity intact.
