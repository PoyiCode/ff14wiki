**Hingan Watchtower (Nanpu)** is a house fixture — a roof decoration a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Watchtower (Nanpu).

A painted tower known in the southern islands of Hingashi as a “hinomi yagura.” Used traditionally to maintain a watch for fires.
