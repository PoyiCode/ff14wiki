**Hingan Cottage Wall (Mokuzo)** is a house fixture — a exterior wall a resident can fit when building or remodelling their home. In-game name: Hingan Cottage Wall (Mokuzo).

A wooden Hingan hiraya-style simple wall designed exclusively for use with cottages.
