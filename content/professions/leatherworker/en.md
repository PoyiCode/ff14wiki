**Leatherworker** is a profession — a Disciple of the Hand (an artisan craft). A resident who takes up this trade tans hide and crafts leather goods, earning a living and an identity by it. In-game name: Leatherworker.

Trade guild: Leatherworkers' Guild & Shaded Bower
