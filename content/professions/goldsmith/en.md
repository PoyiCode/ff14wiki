**Goldsmith** is a profession — a Disciple of the Hand (an artisan craft). A resident who takes up this trade crafts jewelry, accessories and gem-set pieces, earning a living and an identity by it. In-game name: Goldsmith.

Trade guild: Goldsmiths' Guild
