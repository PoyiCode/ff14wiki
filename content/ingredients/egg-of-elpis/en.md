**Egg of Elpis** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Egg of Elpis.

Birthed by a bird of Elpis, the hope within this egg seems unwilling to come out...

Used in dishes: Baked Alien Soup, Banana Ponzecake, Carrot Pudding, Caviar Sandwich, Clam Cake, Creamy Alpaca Pasta, Gateau au Chocolat, Giant Popoto Pancakes, King Urchin Loaf, Mate Cookie, Melon Pie, Nasi Goreng, Omelette Rice, Paes de Queijo, Peach Tart
