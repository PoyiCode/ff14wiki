**Dodo Tenderloin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dodo Tenderloin.

A lean piece of meat taken from under the short ribs of a dodo.

Used in dishes: Grilled Dodo, Meat Miq'abob, Pea Soup
