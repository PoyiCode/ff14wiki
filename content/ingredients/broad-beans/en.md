**Broad Beans** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Broad Beans.

These large, spring green beans are easy to cultivate, and have served as a staple food for millennia.

Used in dishes: Broad Bean Curry, Broad Bean Salad, Broad Bean Soup, Zurek
