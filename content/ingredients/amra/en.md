---
title: "Amra"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Amra** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Amra.

The signature fruit of Thavnair, the amra is often spotted clutched in the tail of a vajralangula.

Used in dishes: Amra Lassi, Amra Salad
