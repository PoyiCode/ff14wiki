---
title: "Mugwort"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Mugwort** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mugwort.

The young leaves of the mugwort plant are used not only as a spice in cooking, but also as a curative agent in various unguents.

Used in dishes: Mugwort Carp
