**Ovibos Milk** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Ovibos Milk.

Possesses a unique taste one might not normally associate with milk.

Used in dishes: Amra Lassi, Archon Burger, Beef Stroganoff, Bubble Tea, Caramel Popcorn, Carrot Pudding, Cloudsail Meuniere, Coffee Kukuru Rusks, Creamy Hot Chocolate, Dragonfruit Blend, Fruit and Aloe Jelly, Gyros, Omelette Rice, Paes de Queijo, Peach Tart
