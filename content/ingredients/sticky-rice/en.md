title: "Sticky Rice"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Sticky Rice** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sticky Rice.

This grain is imported from a land far to the east. When cooked, it has a consistency slightly more glutinous than normal rice.

Used in dishes: Black Truffle Risotto, Cheese Risotto
