---
title: "Ama Nori"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Ama Nori** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Ama Nori.

This edible seaweed can be consumed raw, or dried into paper-like squares and wrapped around sticky rice.

Used in dishes: Popoto Soba
