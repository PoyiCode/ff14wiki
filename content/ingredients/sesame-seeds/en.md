**Sesame Seeds** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sesame Seeds.

A tiny seed rich in aromatic oil, sesame is native to Meracydia, and thought to have first been brought to Eorzea by migrating Miqo'te.

Used in dishes: Kaiser Roll, Morel Salad, Sesame Cookie
