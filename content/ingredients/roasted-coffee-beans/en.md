title: "Roasted Coffee Beans"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Roasted Coffee Beans** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Roasted Coffee Beans.

Seeds harvested from the coffee plants which grow in volcanic mountainous soils, and then slow-roasted over an indirect flame for several bells to remove bitterness and bring out flavor.

Used in dishes: Triple Cream Coffee
