**Chicken Stock** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Chicken Stock.

A clear golden broth made by boiling chicken bones.

Used in dishes: Baked Alien Soup, Baked Onion Soup, Cawl Cennin, Orobon Stew, Stuffed Cabbage Rolls
