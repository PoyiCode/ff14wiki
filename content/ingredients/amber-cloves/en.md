**Amber Cloves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Amber Cloves.

This sweet spice made from the dried floral buds is used both as flavoring and in essential oils.

Used in dishes: Golden Pineapple Juice, Masala Chai, Mushroom Skewer
