**Cottage Cheese** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cottage Cheese.

A creamy mixture of unripened cheese curds.

Used in dishes: Black Truffle Risotto, Cheese Risotto, Mashed Popotoes, Scrambled Eggs
