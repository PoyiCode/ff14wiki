**Mole Meat** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mole Meat.

A fist-sized chunk of lean, bloody meat cleanly sliced from the carcass of a pesky mole.

Used in dishes: Mole Loaf
