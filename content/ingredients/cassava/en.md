title: "Cassava"

---

**Cassava** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cassava.

A root vegetable cultivated across Tural and staple of myriad different local cuisines.

Used in dishes: Bubble Tea, Paes de Queijo
