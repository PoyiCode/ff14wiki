title: "Wild Coffee Beans"

---

**Wild Coffee Beans** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Wild Coffee Beans.

Slowly and carefully grown at mountainous altitudes, these fresh coffee beans are known to produce more complex flavors than most.

Used in dishes: Cinnamon Cream Coffee, Coffee Kukuru Rusks
