**Wizard Eggplant** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Wizard Eggplant.

A firm purple-skinned vegetable indigenous to the mountains of the Near East. A common wives' tale claims if you eat too many of this vegetable, you will turn into one.

Used in dishes: Boiled Bream, Pasta Ortolano, Ratatouille
