**Secret Recipe Broth** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Secret Recipe Broth.

A clear, briny broth made by boiling down fish. There really is no secret.

Used in dishes: Chawan-mushi, Laghman, Oden, Tako-yaki
