**Prime Loaghtan Rump** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Prime Loaghtan Rump.

A sumptuous slice of meat cut from a loved and nurtured loaghtan.

Used in dishes: Gyros, Loaghtan Rump Steak
