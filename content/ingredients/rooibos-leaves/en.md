**Rooibos Leaves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Rooibos Leaves.

Red bush leaves whose prickly texture belies the marvelously velvety brew they can be steeped to create.

Used in dishes: Rooibos Tea
