**Ramhorn Zucchini** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Ramhorn Zucchini.

This long, thin variety of squash earns its name from its resemblance to the horn of a mountain ram.

Used in dishes: Dhalmel Gratin, Jhammel Moussaka, Mushroom Skewer, Pasta Ortolano, Popoto Salad, Ratatouille, Twilight Popoto Salad
