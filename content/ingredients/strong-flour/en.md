title: "Strong Flour"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Strong Flour** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Strong Flour.

While there may be nothing particularly strong about it in its present state, the difference soon becomes clear when using it to bake bread.

Used in dishes: Honey Croissant, Lemon Waffle
