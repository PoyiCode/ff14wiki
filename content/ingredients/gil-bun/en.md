**Gil Bun** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Gil Bun.

This tiny round mushroom has a cap which resembles a coin, and is known for its rich flavor and pungent aroma.

Used in dishes: Baked Sole, Chicken and Mushrooms, Raptor Stew
