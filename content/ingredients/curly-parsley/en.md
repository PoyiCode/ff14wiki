**Curly Parsley** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Curly Parsley.

This fancifully frilly parsley has an unobtrusive flavor that makes it perfect for use in otherwise bland dishes or as a garnish.

Used in dishes: Broad Bean Salad, Chicken Fettuccine, Farmer's Breakfast, Kalamarakia Tiganita, Mejillones al Ajillo, Peppered Popotoes, Pickled Herring, Risotto al Nero
