---
title: "Stone Cheese"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Stone Cheese** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Stone Cheese.

Gas which forms during the fermentation process creates hollow bubbles throughout this otherwise hard, flavorful cheese.

Used in dishes: Loaghtan Cordon Bleu, Priestly Omelette, Spaghetti Carbonara
