**Sagolii Sage** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sagolii Sage.

A savory herb often found growing in parched desert environments. It is used in cooking and various medicinal ointments and unguents.

Used in dishes: Button Mushroom Saute, Jerked Beef, Sauteed Porcini
