**Hornbill Tenderloin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Hornbill Tenderloin.

A lean piece of meat taken from under the short ribs of an Othardian hornbill.

Used in dishes: Gameni
