title: "Nutmeg"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Nutmeg** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Nutmeg.

A sweet-smelling seed that is ground into a powder and used to flavor pastries and tea. It is also widely used in Far Eastern medicines.

Used in dishes: Dzemael Gratin, Fish Soup, Heavenly Eggnog, Jerked Beef, Persimmon Pudding, Popoto Pancakes, Salt Cod Puffs, Silkie Pudding, Spinach Quiche, Trapper's Quiche
