**Sour Cream** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sour Cream.

A thick cream created by fermenting yak milk.

Used in dishes: Beet Soup, Caramels, Dhalmel Fricassee, Dhalmel Gratin, Espresso con Panna, King Urchin Loaf, Lemon Curd Sachertorte, Papanasi, Pixieberry Cheesecake, Spicy Stellar Soup, Urchin Loaf
