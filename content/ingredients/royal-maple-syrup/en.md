**Royal Maple Syrup** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Royal Maple Syrup.

A viscous, sugary reduction of royal maple sap.

Used in dishes: Banana Ponzecake, Fruit and Aloe Jelly, Maple Teriyaki, Navel Orange Cookies, Pineapple Orange Jelly, Prune Ponzecake, Royal Toast, Sweet Maple Mate, Sweetmuffin
