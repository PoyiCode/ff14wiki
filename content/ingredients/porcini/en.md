**Porcini** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Porcini.

A large fragrant mushroom popular in Coerthan cuisine.

Used in dishes: Baked Pipira Pira, Boscaiola, Sauteed Porcini
