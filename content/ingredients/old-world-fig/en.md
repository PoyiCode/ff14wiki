**Old World Fig** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Old World Fig.

A symbol of long life and femininity, this autumn fruit was originally brought from their homeland to Eorzea by the Sharlayans.

Used in dishes: Fig Bavarois, Frozen Spirits
