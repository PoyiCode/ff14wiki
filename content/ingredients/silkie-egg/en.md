title: "Silkie Egg"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Silkie Egg** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Silkie Egg.

The breed of chicken that lays these eggs is known for its incredibly voluminous plumage. The eggs, on the other hand, are entirely indistinguishable from that of any other chicken.

Used in dishes: Farmer's Breakfast, Silkie Pudding
