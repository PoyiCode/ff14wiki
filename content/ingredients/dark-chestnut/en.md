**Dark Chestnut** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dark Chestnut.

The spine-encased fruit of the dark chestnut tree.

Used in dishes: Marron Glace, Sohm Al Tart, Steppe Tea
