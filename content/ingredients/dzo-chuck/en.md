**Dzo Chuck** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dzo Chuck.

The unbearably tough shoulder meat of a steppe dzo.

Used in dishes: Laghman, Nomad Meat Pie, Shorlog, Warrior's Stew
