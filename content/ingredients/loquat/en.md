**Loquat** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Loquat.

A sweet yet slightly tangy fruit with a thin skin and a fleshy orange interior. Can be found in the warmer valleys of Gyr Abania.

Used in dishes: Crimson Cider, Jellied Compote, Loquat Juice
