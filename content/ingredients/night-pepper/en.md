**Night Pepper** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Night Pepper.

A dark aromatic spice used in cooking as well as pickling.

Used in dishes: Creamy Salmon Pasta, Exquisite Beef Stew, Giant Haddock Dip, Glory Be Soup, King Salmon Meuniere, Loaghtan Rump Steak, Mist Spinach Saute, Peppered Popotoes, Popoto Salad, Popotoes au Gratin, Rare Roast Beef, Roast Ovim, Sausage Links, Smoked Chicken, The Noodles of Elpis
