**Mist Spinach** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mist Spinach.

A highly nutritious, deep-green vegetable.

Used in dishes: Archon Loaf, Broccoli and Spinach Saute, Creamy Salmon Pasta, Herring Pie, Mist Spinach Quiche, Mist Spinach Saute, Robe Lettuce Salad, Stuffed Highland Cabbage
