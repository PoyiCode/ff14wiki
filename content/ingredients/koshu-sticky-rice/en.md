**Koshu Sticky Rice** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Koshu Sticky Rice.

Unlike the drier varieties common in Aldenard and Ilsabard, this particular strand of Far Eastern rice becomes extremely sticky when cooked.

Used in dishes: Banh Xeo, Chirashi-zushi, Persimmon Leaf Sushi
