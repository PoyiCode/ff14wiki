title: "Cooking Sake"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Cooking Sake** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cooking Sake.

Far Eastern rice wine prepared with other ingredients for use in cooking. Often added to seafood dishes to remove unpleasant aromas.

Used in dishes: Grilled Turban, Miso Dengaku, Oden, Onigara-yaki, Pork Kakuni, Steamed Grouper
