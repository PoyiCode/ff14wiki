---
title: "Flint Corn"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Flint Corn** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Flint Corn.

The flint-like kernels of this rare purple corn burst open when heated.

Used in dishes: Caramel Popcorn, Nachos
