**Vermicelli** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Vermicelli.

Thin noodles made with highland flour, eggs, water, and salt. These have been dried and will keep indefinitely.

Used in dishes: Boscaiola, Chicken Fettuccine, Chicken Pasta, Creamy Salmon Pasta, Pasta Ortolano, Peperoncino, Spaghetti al Nero, Spaghetti Carbonara, Spaghetti Pescatore, The Noodles of Elpis, Urchin Pasta
