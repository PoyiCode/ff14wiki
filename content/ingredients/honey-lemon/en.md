**Honey Lemon** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Honey Lemon.

This rare type of lemon native to the continent of Othard is so sweet, it can be eaten raw.

Used in dishes: Haddock Dip, Rolanberry Cheesecake
