**Gyr Abanian Flour** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Gyr Abanian Flour.

Finely milled Gyr Abanian wheat.

Used in dishes: Baklava, Crab Croquette, Jhammel Moussaka, Laghman, Nomad Meat Pie, Papanasi, Persimmon Pudding, Sermon-worthy Meuniere, Sweet and Sour Frogs' Legs, Tako-yaki, Tempura Platter
