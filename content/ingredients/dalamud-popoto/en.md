---
title: "Dalamud Popoto"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Dalamud Popoto** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dalamud Popoto.

Hailing also from the western continent, this cousin to the common popoto is lurid red in color.

Used in dishes: Deep-fried Okeanis, Haddock Dip, Popoto Pancakes
