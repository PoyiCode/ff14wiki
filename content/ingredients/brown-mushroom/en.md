**Brown Mushroom** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Brown Mushroom.

An earthen fungus that can commonly be found sprouting beneath trees growing in the Churning Mists.

Used in dishes: Priestly Omelette, Wildwood Scrambled Eggs
