**Giant Popoto** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Giant Popoto.

An alarmingly overgrown popoto suitable for raising hearty Lalafells. (Any Lalafells reading this may rest assured that this does not qualify as cannibalism.)

Used in dishes: All i Pebre, Alpaca Stew, Baked Eggplant, Beef Stroganoff, Giant Haddock Dip, Giant Popoto Pancakes, Goldentail Pie, Lomo Saltado, Mesquite Soup, Popoto Potage, Quahog Chowder, Vegetable Soup, Yakow Moussaka
