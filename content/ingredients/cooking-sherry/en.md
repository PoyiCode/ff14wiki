**Cooking Sherry** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cooking Sherry.

Fine Lominsan white wine fortified with grape spirit to increase alcohol content and improve its aroma, making it perfect for a variety of culinary dishes.

Used in dishes: Dzo Steak, Loaghtan Steak, Marron Glace, Sauteed Coeurl
