title: "Cloud Banana"

---

**Cloud Banana** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cloud Banana.

Though it resembles a tree, cloud bananas actually grow from an oversized herb. That does not, however, make them any less sweet.

Used in dishes: Dragonfruit Blend, Frozen Spirits
