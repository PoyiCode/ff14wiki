**Sweet Cream Milk** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sweet Cream Milk.

Fat-rich milk taken from buffaloes raised in a controlled, stress-free environment.

Used in dishes: Flaugnarde, Heavenly Eggnog, Wildwood Scrambled Eggs
