title: "Heavensegg"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Heavensegg** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Heavensegg.

A premium chicken's egg, named for its distinguishable shell tinged blue as the heavens.

Used in dishes: Flaugnarde, Heavenly Eggnog, Heavensegg Soup, Priestly Omelette, Spaghetti Carbonara, Wildwood Scrambled Eggs
