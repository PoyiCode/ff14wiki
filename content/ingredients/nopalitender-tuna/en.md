**Nopalitender Tuna** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Nopalitender Tuna.

Harvested from the body of a nopalitender. Its flesh is watery, yet sweet.

Used in dishes: Mesquite Juice, Tart Turali Tart, Tender Shortcake
