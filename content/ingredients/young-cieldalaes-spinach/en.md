**Young Cieldalaes Spinach** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Young Cieldalaes Spinach.

This bunch of Cieldalaes spinach has been picked young to reduce bitterness.

Used in dishes: Morel Salad, Sermon-worthy Meuniere, Spinach Quiche
