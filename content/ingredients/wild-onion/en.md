**Wild Onion** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Wild Onion.

A pungent bulb vegetable native to the Near East.

Used in dishes: Antelope Stew, Beef Stew, Black Truffle Risotto, Boiled Bream, Cawl Cennin, Cheese Risotto, Fish Soup, Landtrap Salad, Lava Toad Legs, Marmot Steak, Mole Loaf, Mutton Stew, Pea Soup, Rabbit Pie, Shepherd's Pie
