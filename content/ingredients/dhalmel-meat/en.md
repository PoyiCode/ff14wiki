title: "Dhalmel Meat"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Dhalmel Meat** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dhalmel Meat.

A gristle-filled slice of meat that will require extensive simmering for it to be rendered palatable.

Used in dishes: Dhalmel Fricassee, Dhalmel Gratin
