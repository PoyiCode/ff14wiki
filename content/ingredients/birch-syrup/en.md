**Birch Syrup** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Birch Syrup.

Birch sap boiled down to a thick, sweet syrup.

Used in dishes: Almond Cream Croissant, Apple Strudel, Creme Brulee, Flaugnarde, Heavenly Eggnog, Hot Chocolate, Kingcake, Marron Glace, Mors, Snurbleberry Tart, Sweet Gnomefish
