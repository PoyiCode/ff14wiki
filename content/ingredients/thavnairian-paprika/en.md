**Thavnairian Paprika** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Thavnairian Paprika.

A brightly colored pepper produced on the island of Thavnair.

Used in dishes: All i Pebre, Amra Salad, Baba Ghanoush, Ceviche, Coconut Cod Chowder, Island Miq'abob, Jhinga Biryani, Karniyarik, Loaghtan Rump Steak, Marinated Broccoflower, Piennolo Tomato Salad, Scallop Salad, Spicy Shakshouka, Sykon Salad, Tacos de Carne Asada
