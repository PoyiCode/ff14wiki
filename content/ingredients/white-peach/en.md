**White Peach** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: White Peach.

A plump white peach highly sought after for its sweet aroma and rich flavor.

Used in dishes: Peach Juice, Peach Tart
