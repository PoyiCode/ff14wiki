title: "Green Leek"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Green Leek** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Green Leek.

Similar to green onions in flavor, the leeks of Gyr Abania are several times thicker, and far more piquant.

Used in dishes: Banh Xeo, Fish Stew, Miso Soup with Tofu, Sauteed Green Leeks, Steamed Grouper, Tako-yaki
