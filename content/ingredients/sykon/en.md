---
title: "Sykon"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Sykon** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sykon.

A fruit known as a symbol of vitality and the lushness of nature.

Used in dishes: Peach Juice, Sykon Bavarois, Sykon Compote, Sykon Cookie, Sykon Salad
