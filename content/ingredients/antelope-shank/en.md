**Antelope Shank** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Antelope Shank.

A tough piece of meat taken from the upper leg of an antelope.

Used in dishes: Antelope Steak, Antelope Stew, Shepherd's Pie
