**Table Salt** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Table Salt.

Finely ground salt used not only for flavoring foods, but for pickling and preserving as well.

Used in dishes: Acorn Cookie, Aldgoat Steak, Alligator Salad, Antelope Steak, Apkallu Omelette, Baked Sole, Battered Fish, Blood Tomato Salad, Boiled Crayfish, Braised Pipira, Button Mushroom Saute, Buttons in a Blanket, Chicken and Mushrooms, Chilled Popoto Soup, Crumpet
