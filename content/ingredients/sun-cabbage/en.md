**Sun Cabbage** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sun Cabbage.

The sweet, tightly packed leaves of Othardian sun cabbages are deceptively sweet, making them perfect in salads or stews.

Used in dishes: Nomad Meat Pie, Stewed River Bream, Warrior's Stew
