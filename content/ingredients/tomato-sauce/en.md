**Tomato Sauce** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Tomato Sauce.

A tangy sauce made from crushed tomatoes.

Used in dishes: Boscaiola, Buttons in a Blanket, Cockatrice Meatballs, Dodo Omelette, Mole Loaf, Pasta Ortolano, Priestly Omelette, Stuffed Cabbage Rolls
