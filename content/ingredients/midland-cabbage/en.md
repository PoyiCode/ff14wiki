**Midland Cabbage** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Midland Cabbage.

A round leafy vegetable which prefers the steady climate of the Black Shroud.

Used in dishes: Boiled Bream, Buttons in a Blanket, Sauerkraut, Stuffed Cabbage
