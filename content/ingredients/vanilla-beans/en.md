title: "Vanilla Beans"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Vanilla Beans** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Vanilla Beans.

A sweet-smelling spice made from the dried pod of the rare vanilla plant. It is often used in the making of confectionaries as well as medicines.

Used in dishes: Apple Strudel, Creme Brulee, Spiced Cider
