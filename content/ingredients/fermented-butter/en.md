**Fermented Butter** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Fermented Butter.

Slightly pungent butter allowed to ferment for several days after churning.

Used in dishes: Almond Cream Croissant, Apple Strudel, Baked Megapiranha, Baked Pipira Pira, Broad Bean Curry, Caramel Popcorn, Caramels, Gateau au Chocolat, Hamsa Curry, King Salmon Meuniere, Lemon Curd Sachertorte, Lemon Muffin, Lemon Waffle, Loaghtan Steak, Lobster Bisque
