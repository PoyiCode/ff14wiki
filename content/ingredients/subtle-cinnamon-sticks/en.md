**Subtle Cinnamon Sticks** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Subtle Cinnamon Sticks.

Finely stripped sticks of cinnamon with a complex flavor detectable to well-developed palates.

Used in dishes: Sykon Cookie, Thavnairian Chai
