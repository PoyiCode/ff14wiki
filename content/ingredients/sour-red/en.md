title: "Sour Red"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Sour Red** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sour Red.

Made from lowland grapes grown on arid soil, this vinegary wine is more suited to cooking than it is to drinking (though that does little to stop most Lominsan pirates from drowning their sorrows in the phenolic seepage).

Used in dishes: Beef Stew, Eft Steak, Lentils and Chestnuts, Steamed Catfish, Sykon Compote
