title: "Salt Leek"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Salt Leek** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Salt Leek.

A long, pungent, leafy vegetable that lacks a bulb like that of its cousin, the onion.

Used in dishes: Cawl Cennin, Dagger Soup, Stuffed Cabbage
