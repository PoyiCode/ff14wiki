title: "Yak Milk"

---

**Yak Milk** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Yak Milk.

The unique flavor of yak milk can be somewhat off-putting to those raised on cow or aldgoat milk.

Used in dishes: Angler Stew, Creme Brulee, Fig Bavarois, Frozen Spirits, Golden Pineapple Juice, Honey Croissant, Hot Chocolate, Ishgardian Muffin, Ishgardian Tea, Kaiser Roll, Kingcake, Kukuru Rusk, Lemon Muffin, Nut Bake, Ovim Meatballs
