**Mature Olive Oil** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mature Olive Oil.

A rich-tasting oil produced with pressed olives at the peak of ripeness.

Used in dishes: Boscaiola, Loaghtan Cordon Bleu, Oyster Confit, Peperoncino, Spaghetti Pescatore
