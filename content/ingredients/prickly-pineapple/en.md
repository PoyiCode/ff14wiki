**Prickly Pineapple** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Prickly Pineapple.

A sweet and tangy fruit covered in a thick, spike-laden skin.

Used in dishes: Pineapple Juice, Pineapple Ponzecake
