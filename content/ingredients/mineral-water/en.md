**Mineral Water** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mineral Water.

Clear water taken from a mountain spring near Ishgard.

Used in dishes: Acorn Cookie, Apkallu Omelette, Bacon Bread, Beef Stew, Boiled Crayfish, Boiled Egg, Cawl Cennin, Chamomile Tea, Cornbread, Dagger Soup, Dark Pretzel, Flatbread, Fried Egg, Honey Muffin, Jack-o'-lantern
