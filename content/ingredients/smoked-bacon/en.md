**Smoked Bacon** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Smoked Bacon.

A large side of boar, cured and then smoked. It is widely accepted across Eorzea that “everything goes better” with this flavorful meat.

Used in dishes: Bacon Bread, Bacon Broth, Heavensegg Soup, Loaghtan Cordon Bleu, Spaghetti Carbonara
