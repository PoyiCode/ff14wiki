---
title: "Robe Lettuce"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Robe Lettuce** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Robe Lettuce.

So named for its supposed resemblance to a faerie tale sorcerer's robes, this variety of lettuce grows at altitude and is known for its crisp leaves.

Used in dishes: Popoto Salad, Robe Lettuce Salad
