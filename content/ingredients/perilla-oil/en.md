**Perilla Oil** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Perilla Oil.

A cooking oil made from Thavnairian perilla leaves.

Used in dishes: All i Pebre, Baba Ghanoush, Beef Stroganoff, Calamari Ripieni, Giant Haddock Dip, Glory Be Soup, Ground Woolback Steak, Island Miq'abob, Jhinga Biryani, Jhinga Curry, Kalamarakia Tiganita, Karniyarik, King Crab Cake, Loaghtan Rump Steak, Marinated Broccoflower
