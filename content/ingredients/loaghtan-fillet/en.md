title: "Loaghtan Fillet"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Loaghtan Fillet** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Loaghtan Fillet.

Taken from the upper loin of a domesticated loaghtan, this meat is tender enough that it does not require any preparation before cooking.

Used in dishes: Loaghtan Cordon Bleu, Loaghtan Steak
