title: "Dragon Pepper"

**Dragon Pepper** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dragon Pepper.

A red-hot spice known to cause severe burns in the mouths of those who consume too many.

Used in dishes: Ema Datshi, Jerked Jhammel, Lava Toad Legs, Peperoncino, Rroneek Steak, Sauerkraut, Shakshouka, Spaghetti al Olio e Peperoncino
