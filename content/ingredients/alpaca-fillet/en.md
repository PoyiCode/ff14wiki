**Alpaca Fillet** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Alpaca Fillet.

A choice cut of alpaca meat commonly reserved for celebratory banquets.

Used in dishes: Alpaca Stew, Boiled Alpaca Steak, Creamy Alpaca Pasta, Stuffed Peppers, Tacos Al Pastor
