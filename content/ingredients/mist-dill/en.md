**Mist Dill** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mist Dill.

A fragrant herb characterized by its thin leaves. It is used in both medicines and culinary dishes.

Used in dishes: Beet Soup, Kasha
