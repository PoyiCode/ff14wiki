**Ovim Meat** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Ovim Meat.

A large, marbled slab of meat cut from the loin of a mountain-dwelling ovim.

Used in dishes: Farmer's Breakfast, Ovim Cordon Bleu, Ovim Meatballs, Roast Ovim, Sausage and Sauerkraut, Sausage Links, Stuffed Highland Cabbage
