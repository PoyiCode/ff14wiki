**Solstice Garlic** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Solstice Garlic.

Favored by chefs for leaving one's breath scant of odor after a meal, this zesty garlic is often used in gourmet dishes.

Used in dishes: Angler Stew, Giant Haddock Dip, Grilled Rail, Pasta Ortolano, Peperoncino, Sauteed Green Leeks, Spaghetti Pescatore, Ukha
