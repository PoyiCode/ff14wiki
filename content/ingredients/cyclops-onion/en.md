**Cyclops Onion** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cyclops Onion.

A giant yellow onion often found growing on Abalathia's Spine.

Used in dishes: Angler Stew, Baked Onion Soup, Blood Bouillabaisse, Blood Tomato Salad, Boscaiola, Carrot Nibbles, Cockatrice Meatballs, Crab Cakes, Crab Croquette, Dhalmel Fricassee, Dhalmel Gratin, Ema Datshi, Farmer's Breakfast, Heavensegg Soup, Imam Bayildi
