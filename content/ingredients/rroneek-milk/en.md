title: "Rroneek Milk"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Rroneek Milk** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Rroneek Milk.

As rroneek only produce small quantities of milk, this creamy, nourishing liquid is highly prized.

Used in dishes: Popoto Potage, Rroneek au Gratin
