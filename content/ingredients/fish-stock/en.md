**Fish Stock** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Fish Stock.

Broth created from boiling down the flesh and bones of various types of seafood.

Used in dishes: Egg Foo Young, Miso Soup with Tofu, Popoto Soba, Pork Stew
