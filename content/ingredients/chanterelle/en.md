title: "Chanterelle"

---

**Chanterelle** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Chanterelle.

An edible mushroom known for its leaf-like appearance as well as its fruit-like fragrance.

Used in dishes: Braised Pipira, Chanterelle Saute, Raptor Stew
