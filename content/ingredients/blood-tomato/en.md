**Blood Tomato** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Blood Tomato.

The slight acidity of this bright tomato is guaranteed to whet one's appetite, making it a long-standing favorite of tavern owners throughout Norvrandt.

Used in dishes: Amra Salad, Baked Eggplant, Blood Bouillabaisse, Blood Tomato Juice, Blood Tomato Salad, Calamari Ripieni, Caviar Canapes, Chili Crab, Coconut Cod Chowder, Elpis Deipnon, Jhinga Curry, Karniyarik, Mushroom Skewer, Pineapple Salad, Pizza
