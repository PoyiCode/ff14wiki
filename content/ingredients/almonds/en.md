**Almonds** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Almonds.

A tear-shaped nut which can be used to season exotic sweets, or pressed for its fragrant oil.

Used in dishes: Baklava, Crowned Pie, Nut Bake, Pastry Fish
