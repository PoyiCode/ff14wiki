**Iceberg Lettuce** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Iceberg Lettuce.

A leafy, moisture-rich vegetable cultivated in Labyrinthos.

Used in dishes: Amra Salad, Archon Burger, Beef Stroganoff, Borscht, Gyros, Happiness Juice, Philosopher's Sandwich, Piennolo Tomato Salad, Scallop Salad, Sykon Salad, Zurek
