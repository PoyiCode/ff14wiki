title: "Giant Pumpkin"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Giant Pumpkin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Giant Pumpkin.

This gourd has been selectively cultivated and grown to a massive size—though many a La Noscean field hand would tell you they've seen bigger.

Used in dishes: Pumpkin Potage, Pumpkin Ratatouille
