**Pearl Sprouts** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Pearl Sprouts.

Tiny cabbage-like vegetables native to the highlands of Coerthas.

Used in dishes: Beet Soup, Stuffed Cabbage Rolls
