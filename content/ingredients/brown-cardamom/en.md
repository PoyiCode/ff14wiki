title: "Brown Cardamom"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Brown Cardamom** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Brown Cardamom.

These earthy spice pods bear a smoky-yet-sophisticated fragrance coveted by many a culinarian.

Used in dishes: Churrasco, Moqueca
