**Rolanberry** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Rolanberry.

A tart fruit—one of few still found growing in the devastated region of Mor Dhona.

Used in dishes: Papanasi, Rolanberry Lassi, Rolanberry Shaved Ice
