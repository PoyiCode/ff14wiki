**Cieldalaes Spinach** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cieldalaes Spinach.

An iron-rich leafy vegetable native to the Cieldalaes.

Used in dishes: Baked Sole, Parsnip Salad, Salmon Muffin, Spinach Saute
