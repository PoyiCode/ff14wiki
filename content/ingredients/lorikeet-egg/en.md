**Lorikeet Egg** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Lorikeet Egg.

The delicate egg of the rainbow lorikeet.

Used in dishes: Crab Cakes, King Crab Cake, Lemon Muffin, Lemon Waffle, Mist Spinach Quiche, Nut Bake, Popoto Salad, Robe Lettuce Salad, Twilight Popoto Salad, Zefir
