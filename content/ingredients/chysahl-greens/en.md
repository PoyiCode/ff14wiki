title: "Chysahl Greens"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Chysahl Greens** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Chysahl Greens.

Though nearly identical in shape and color to gysahl greens, chysahl greens have a deeper, earthier flavor that chocobos (and man) seem to prefer.

Used in dishes: Stuffed Chysahl
