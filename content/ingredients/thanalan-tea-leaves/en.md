title: "Thanalan Tea Leaves"

**Thanalan Tea Leaves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Thanalan Tea Leaves.

These leaves, when dried and steeped in hot water, create a sweet-smelling tea known for its curative properties.

Used in dishes: Buckwheat Tea, Mulled Tea
