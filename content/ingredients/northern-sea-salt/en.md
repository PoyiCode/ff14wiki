**Northern Sea Salt** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Northern Sea Salt.

A mineral-rich condiment made from Sharlayan rock salt.

Used in dishes: Baked Alien Soup, Boiled Alpaca Steak, Boiled Lobster, Borscht, Broccoflower Stew, Caviar Sandwich, Ceviche, Elpis Deipnon, Giant Popoto Pancakes, Gyros, Jhinga Biryani, Pumpkin Potage, Salted Thavnairian Cod, Spicy Stellar Soup, Tandoori Raptor
