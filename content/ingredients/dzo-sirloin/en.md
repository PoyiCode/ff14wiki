**Dzo Sirloin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dzo Sirloin.

A remarkably lean slice of red meat from the back of a steppe-grazing dzo.

Used in dishes: Dzo Steak
