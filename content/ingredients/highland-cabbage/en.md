---
title: "Highland Cabbage"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Highland Cabbage** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Highland Cabbage.

Some particularly hardy Highlanders have been seen to grasp a cabbage in one hand and take great bites from it, though this is not recommended.

Used in dishes: Sausage and Sauerkraut, Stuffed Highland Cabbage
