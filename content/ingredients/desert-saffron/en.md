title: "Desert Saffron"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Desert Saffron** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Desert Saffron.

This spice made from the dried styles of the desert saffron flower gives a bright-yellow color to dishes.

Used in dishes: Blood Bouillabaisse, Bouillabaisse, Buttons in a Blanket, Pastry Fish
