**Olive Oil** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Olive Oil.

A fragrant light-green oil extracted from pressed olives.

Used in dishes: Alligator Salad, Battered Fish, Boiled Bream, Bouillabaisse, Button Mushroom Saute, Cornbread, Deep-fried Okeanis, Ginger Salad, Haddock Dip, La Noscean Toast, Landtrap Salad, Lentils and Chestnuts, Morel Salad, Pan-fried Mahi-mahi, Parsnip Salad
