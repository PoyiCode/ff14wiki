**Cloves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cloves.

A sweet-smelling spice made from the dried buds of the clove tree. It is used to flavor confectionaries, and as an essential oil.

Used in dishes: Eel Pie, Mulled Tea, Spiced Cider, Tailor-made Eel Pie
