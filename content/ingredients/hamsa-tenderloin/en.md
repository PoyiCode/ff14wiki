**Hamsa Tenderloin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Hamsa Tenderloin.

Tender breast meat that is hopefully not poisonous.

Used in dishes: Hamsa Curry, Island Miq'abob, Karniyarik
