**Woolback Loin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Woolback Loin.

A prime cut of meat sourced from a woolback native to Yyasulani.

Used in dishes: Ground Woolback Steak, Woolback Steak
