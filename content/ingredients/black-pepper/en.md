**Black Pepper** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Black Pepper.

An aromatic spice used in cooking as well as pickling.

Used in dishes: Bacon Broth, Beef Stew, Dzemael Gratin, Eel Pie, Eft Steak, Forest Miq'abob, Haddock Dip, Jerked Beef, Jerked Jhammel, Lentils and Chestnuts, Mole Loaf, Orobon Stew, Rabbit Pie, Sausage and Sauerkraut, Shepherd's Pie
