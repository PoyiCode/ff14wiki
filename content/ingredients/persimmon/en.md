**Persimmon** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Persimmon.

A hard, orange fruit common in eastern Othard. It is well known that seven summers must pass before a newly planted persimmon tree will bear fruit.

Used in dishes: Doman Tea, Jellied Compote, Persimmon Pudding
