**Thyme** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Thyme.

A fragrant herb used to flavor various meat and poultry dishes.

Used in dishes: Loaghtan Steak, Salt Cod Puffs, Shakshouka
