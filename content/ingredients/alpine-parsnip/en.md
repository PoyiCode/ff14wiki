**Alpine Parsnip** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Alpine Parsnip.

A long white root vegetable indigenous to the mountainous region of Coerthas.

Used in dishes: Cheese Risotto, Parsnip Salad
