**Coconut Milk** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Coconut Milk.

A liquid made from boiling the fruit's flesh and juice, then straining.

Used in dishes: Coconut Cod Chowder, Dragonfruit Blend, Hamsa Curry, Jhinga Curry, King Urchin Loaf, Lentil Curry, Moqueca, Scallop Curry, Sweet Maple Mate, Urchin Pasta
