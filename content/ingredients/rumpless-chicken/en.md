**Rumpless Chicken** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Rumpless Chicken.

It has no rump. It has some grace. This chicken has a gamy taste.

Used in dishes: Chicken Pasta, Roast Chicken
