**Paprika** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Paprika.

A pepper which can be dried and ground to make a mild red spice. It is often used in medicines, as well as in cooking.

Used in dishes: Alligator Salad, Arros Negre, Deviled Eggs, Meat Miq'abob, Paella, Pickled Herring, Shorlog
