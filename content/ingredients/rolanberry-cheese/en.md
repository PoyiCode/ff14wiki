**Rolanberry Cheese** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Rolanberry Cheese.

Cream cheese blended with tart dried rolanberries.

Used in dishes: Rolanberry Cheesecake, Tomato Pie
