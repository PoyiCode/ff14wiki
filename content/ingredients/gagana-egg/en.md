**Gagana Egg** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Gagana Egg.

The massive egg of a gagana.

Used in dishes: Baklava, Chawan-mushi, Chirashi-zushi, Egg Foo Young, Futo-maki Roll, Kasha, Oden, Papanasi, Persimmon Pudding, Popoto Soba, Shakshouka, Tako-yaki, Tempura Platter, Tofu Pancakes
