**Pearl Ginger** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Pearl Ginger.

An exotic root vegetable native to the Far East that is often dried, ground, and used in cooking as well as in the creation of medicines.

Used in dishes: Ginger Cookie, Mulled Tea, Shark Fin Soup, Spiced Cider
