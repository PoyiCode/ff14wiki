**Broccoflower** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Broccoflower.

A unique vegetable boasting a fractal form that has baffled botanists for generations. Though tempting, it is best not to stare directly at its illusory buds.

Used in dishes: Broccoflower Stew, Marinated Broccoflower
