**Twilight Apple** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Twilight Apple.

A sweet-sour variety of apple named for the gentle gleam of its peel.

Used in dishes: Twilight Popoto Salad, Zefir
