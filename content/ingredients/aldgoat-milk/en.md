**Aldgoat Milk** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Aldgoat Milk.

The thick, creamy milk of an aldgoat nanny.

Used in dishes: Dodo Omelette, Frumenty, Honey Muffin, Mint Lassi, Pearl Chocolate
