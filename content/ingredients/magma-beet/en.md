title: "Magma Beet"

**Magma Beet** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Magma Beet.

A sweet, easy-to-grow tuber native to Abalathia's Spine, these deep-red beets are a staple in Ishgardian culinary culture.

Used in dishes: Beet Soup
