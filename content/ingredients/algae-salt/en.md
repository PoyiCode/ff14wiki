**Algae Salt** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Algae Salt.

A briny salt harvested by sun-drying then burning sea algae.

Used in dishes: Baba Ghanoush, Charred Charr, Dzo Steak, Fish Stew, Nomad Meat Pie, Shorlog, Steppe Salad, Warrior's Stew
