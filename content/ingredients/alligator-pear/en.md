**Alligator Pear** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Alligator Pear.

A soft green fruit, also known as “Shroud butter.” Though not thought to be indigenous to Eorzea, it is not certain how the plant first came to the realm.

Used in dishes: Alligator Salad, Caviar Sandwich, Salmon Muffin
