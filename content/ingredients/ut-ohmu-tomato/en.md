**Ut'ohmu Tomato** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Ut'ohmu Tomato.

Cultivated under the harsh sun of the Ut'ohmu Horizon, these ruby-red tomatoes are prized for their sweetness.

Used in dishes: Acqua Pazza, Lobster Bisque, Mollete, Nachos, Solterito
