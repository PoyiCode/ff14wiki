**Lalafellin Lentil** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Lalafellin Lentil.

Thin round beans resembling tiny convex lenses. Thought to have been introduced to Eorzea by the realm's first Lalafellin settlers.

Used in dishes: Lentils and Chestnuts
