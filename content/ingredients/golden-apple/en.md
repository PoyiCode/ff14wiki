---

**Golden Apple** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Golden Apple.

A mildly sweet apple with a tart aftertaste, this rare fruit is highly sought after amongst bakers.

Used in dishes: Apple Strudel, Flaugnarde, Spiced Cider
