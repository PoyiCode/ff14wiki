title: "Mountain Popoto"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Mountain Popoto** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mountain Popoto.

Crisp mountain popotoes contain high quantities of starch that create a viscous paste when the tubers are ground. This paste can be eaten raw, fried into cakes, or added to stews for thickening.

Used in dishes: Crab Croquette, Fish Stew, Jhammel Moussaka, Oden, Popoto Soba, Warrior's Stew
