title: "White Truffle"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**White Truffle** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: White Truffle.

A rare white mushroom that grows in the soil, rather than above it, making the specimen almost impossible to locate. It is treasured as a delicacy for its unique aroma.

Used in dishes: Priestly Omelette, Scrambled Eggs
