title: "Pixieberry"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Pixieberry** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Pixieberry.

A dainty, capricious variety of fruit—one can never quite be sure whether any given berry will be mild, sweet, or tart.

Used in dishes: Pixieberry Cheesecake, Pixieberry Tea
