**Loaghtan Chump** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Loaghtan Chump.

A marbled piece of meat taken from the rump of a loaghtan where the leg meets the loin.

Used in dishes: Beet Soup, Stuffed Cabbage Rolls
