**Crimson Pepper** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Crimson Pepper.

A pungent spice with just a hint of bitterness behind its bite.

Used in dishes: Crimson Cider, Ema Datshi, Ginger Salad, Imam Bayildi, Sauteed Green Leeks, Sermon-worthy Meuniere
