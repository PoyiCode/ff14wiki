**Jhammel Haunch** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Jhammel Haunch.

Heavy salting and spicing are methods commonly employed to remove the oft overpowering gaminess of this beastkin flesh.

Used in dishes: Jerked Jhammel, Jhammel Moussaka
