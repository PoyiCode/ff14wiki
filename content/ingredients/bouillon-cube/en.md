**Bouillon Cube** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Bouillon Cube.

Animal broth that has been reduced down to a paste and hardened into cubes which will keep for an extended duration.

Used in dishes: Clam Chowder, Dhalmel Fricassee, Emerald Soup, Heavensegg Soup, Kasha
