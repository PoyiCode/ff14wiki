**Buffalo Milk** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Buffalo Milk.

The smooth, light milk of a buffalo cow.

Used in dishes: Baklava, Bubble Chocolate, Cornbread, Crumpet, La Noscean Toast, Rolanberry Shaved Ice, Walnut Bread
