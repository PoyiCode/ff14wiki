title: "Shiitake Mushroom"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Shiitake Mushroom** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Shiitake Mushroom.

These fungi native to the Far East are rarely eaten raw, the majority of those harvested dried, then used to make soup stock.

Used in dishes: Chawan-mushi, Chirashi-zushi, Egg Foo Young, Futo-maki Roll, Gameni
