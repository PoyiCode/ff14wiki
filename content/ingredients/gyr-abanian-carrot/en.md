**Gyr Abanian Carrot** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Gyr Abanian Carrot.

Gyr Abanian carrots are not only longer, but a far deeper orange—nay, crimson—than those grown in central and western Eorzea.

Used in dishes: Exquisite Beef Stew, Gameni, Imam Bayildi, Laghman, Pork Stew, Rare Roast Beef, Steppe Salad, Warrior's Stew
