**Russet Popoto** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Russet Popoto.

An easy-to-peel variety of popoto with thin skin and a high starch content.

Used in dishes: Exquisite Beef Stew, Farmer's Breakfast, Ovim Meatballs, Peppered Popotoes, Popoto Salad, Popotoes au Gratin, Roast Ovim, Twilight Popoto Salad, Ukha, Wine-dark Soup
