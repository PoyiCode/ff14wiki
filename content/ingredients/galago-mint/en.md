**Galago Mint** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Galago Mint.

A leafy herb with a crisp, fragrant aroma. It is often used in cooking and in teas, as well as for its essential oil.

Used in dishes: Boiled Alpaca Steak, Lentils and Chestnuts, Mint Lassi, Mors
