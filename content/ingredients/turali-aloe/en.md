**Turali Aloe** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Turali Aloe.

Distantly related to the strain that grows in Meracydia, this hardy succulent stores water in its leaves to survive in dry climates.

Used in dishes: Banana Ponzecake, Fruit and Aloe Jelly, Tart Turali Tart
