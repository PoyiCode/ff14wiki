**Garlean Cheese** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Garlean Cheese.

A fresh cheese known for both its rich yet refreshingly tangy flavor, as well as for the strict measures enacted to ensure its quality.

Used in dishes: Baked Alien Soup, Baked Eggplant, Borscht, Calamari Ripieni, Caviar Canapes, Chicken Pasta, Garlean Pizza, Mollete, Nachos, Paes de Queijo, The Noodles of Elpis, Turali Pineapple Ponzecake, Wine-dark Soup
