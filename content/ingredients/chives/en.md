**Chives** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Chives.

A thin green onion used in many Ishgardian dishes.

Used in dishes: Angler Stew, Clam Chowder, Emerald Soup, Stuffed Chysahl
