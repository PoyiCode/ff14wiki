title: "Potent Spice"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Potent Spice** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Potent Spice.

Not for the weak of heart or palate, this powdered mixture of natural spices gives any dish a flavorsome kick.

Used in dishes: Archon Burger, Beef Stroganoff, Peach Juice, Peach Tart, Pumpkin Potage, Pumpkin Ratatouille, Scallop Curry, Scallop Salad, Sykon Cookie, Thavnairian Chai
