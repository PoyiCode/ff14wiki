**Buffalo Beans** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Buffalo Beans.

A common variety of green bean recognizable by its long thin pods. Buffalo are said to be fond of the beans, and have been known to eat entire crops if left unchecked.

Used in dishes: Broad Bean Soup, Emerald Soup, Parsnip Salad
