title: "Oriental Soy Sauce"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Oriental Soy Sauce** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Oriental Soy Sauce.

Similar to Mun-Tuy sauce, this Far Eastern seasoning is made by fermenting soybeans in salt and then pressing the beans to collect the remaining savory liquid.

Used in dishes: Boiled Amberjack Head, Gameni, Grilled Turban, Nomad Meat Pie, Onigara-yaki, Popoto Soba, Pork Kakuni, Steamed Grouper
