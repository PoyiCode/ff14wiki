**Rice Vinegar** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Rice Vinegar.

One of Far Eastern cuisine's five main seasonings, rice vinegar is slightly more sweet and mild than Aldenard's vinegars of popoto or apple.

Used in dishes: Chirashi-zushi, Futo-maki Roll, Persimmon Leaf Sushi, Steppe Salad, Sweet and Sour Frogs' Legs
