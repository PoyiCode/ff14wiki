**Queso Fresco** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Queso Fresco.

This fresh cheese is beloved by countless Turali for its mild, creamy flavor.

Used in dishes: Mollete, Solterito
