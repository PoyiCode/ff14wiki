title: "Bamboo Shoot"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Bamboo Shoot** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Bamboo Shoot.

If removed from the ground when less than a fulm in length, bamboo shoots can be peeled and the fleshy interior eaten. A popular ingredient in Far Eastern stews and stir-fries.

Used in dishes: Egg Foo Young, Gameni
