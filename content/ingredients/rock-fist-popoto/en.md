**Rock-fist Popoto** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Rock-fist Popoto.

A popoto variety that makes up for its lack of moisture with a glutinous abundance of starch.

Used in dishes: Rock-fisted Popoto Salad, Rock-fisted Popoto Stew
