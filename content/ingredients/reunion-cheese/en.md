**Reunion Cheese** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Reunion Cheese.

A washed-rind cheese made possible by the fortuitous meeting of a Steppe cheesemaker and an itinerant winemaker. It possesses a creamy texture and nutty flavor—though one would be forgiven for being skeptical, given the smell.

Used in dishes: Papanasi, Salmon Muffin
