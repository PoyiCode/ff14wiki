**Carrot of Happiness** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Carrot of Happiness.

A nutrient-rich vegetable beloved by Loporrits. Just one nibble is enough to inspire feelings of euphoria.

Used in dishes: Broccoflower Stew, Carrot Nibbles, Coconut Cod Chowder, Hamsa Curry, Happiness Juice, Lentil Curry, Lobster Bisque, Rock-fisted Popoto Salad, Spicy Stellar Soup, Vegetable Soup
