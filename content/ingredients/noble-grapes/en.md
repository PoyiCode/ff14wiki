title: "Noble Grapes"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Noble Grapes** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Noble Grapes.

Lowland grapes crossbred over generations to produce maximum sweetness. Their bitter skin makes them unsuitable for eating off the vine, and they are better consumed as juice or wine.

Used in dishes: Grape Juice
