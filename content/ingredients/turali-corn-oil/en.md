**Turali Corn Oil** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Turali Corn Oil.

Extracted from the dried germ of Turali corn, this oil is perfect for frying.

Used in dishes: Broccoli and Spinach Saute, Caramel Popcorn, Ceviche, Clam Cake, Cloudsail Meuniere, Lomo Saltado, Mesquite Soup, Moqueca, Roast Rroneek, Rock-fisted Popoto Salad, Rroneek Steak, Spaghetti al Olio e Peperoncino, Stuffed Peppers
