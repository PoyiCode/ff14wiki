**Palm Sugar** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Palm Sugar.

A sweetener made from boiling down palm syrup.

Used in dishes: Amra Lassi, Bubble Tea, Caramel Popcorn, Carrot Pudding, Cinnamon Cream Coffee, Creamy Hot Chocolate, Gateau au Chocolat, Honeyed Dragonfruit, Melon Juice, Mesquite Juice, Peach Juice, Sideritis Cookie, Skyr, Sykon Bavarois, Sykon Compote
