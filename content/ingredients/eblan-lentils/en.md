**Eblan Lentils** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Eblan Lentils.

Legumes that grow well in cold climates.

Used in dishes: Glory Be Soup, Lentil Curry, Peach Juice, Solterito
