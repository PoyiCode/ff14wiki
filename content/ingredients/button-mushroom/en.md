**Button Mushroom** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Button Mushroom.

A small white mushroom commonly found in moist areas across Eorzea.

Used in dishes: Button Mushroom Saute, Buttons in a Blanket, Forest Miq'abob, Spinach Saute
