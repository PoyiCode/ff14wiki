title: "Dragonfruit"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Dragonfruit** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dragonfruit.

This milky white fruit is shielded by scaly skin not entirely unlike that of dragonkind, though whether any member of the race approves of this comparison to vividly pink produce remains an open question.

Used in dishes: Dragonfruit Blend, Honeyed Dragonfruit
