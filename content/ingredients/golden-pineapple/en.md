**Golden Pineapple** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Golden Pineapple.

A golden-hued variety of pineapple touted for its tart flavor.

Used in dishes: Golden Pineapple Juice, Pineapple Salad
