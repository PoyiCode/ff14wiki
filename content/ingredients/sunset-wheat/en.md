**Sunset Wheat** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sunset Wheat.

A common variety of golden brown wheat eaten as a staple in many of Eorzea's city–states.

Used in dishes: Bacon Bread, Frumenty
