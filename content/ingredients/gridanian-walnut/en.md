**Gridanian Walnut** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Gridanian Walnut.

The seed of a walnut tree, this fragrant nut is covered by a thin, fleshy outer shell and a hard inner shell.

Used in dishes: Baklava, Nut Bake, Walnut Bread
