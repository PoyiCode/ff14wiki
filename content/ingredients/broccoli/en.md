**Broccoli** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Broccoli.

A cruciferous vegetable whose many florets form a distinct tree-like shape.

Used in dishes: Broccoli and Spinach Saute, Creamy Alpaca Pasta, Vegetable Soup
