**Turali Plum** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Turali Plum.

A somewhat bitter fruit native to the shores of Tural.

Used in dishes: Prune-packed Fruitcake, Prune Ponzecake
