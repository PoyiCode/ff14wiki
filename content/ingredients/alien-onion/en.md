**Alien Onion** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Alien Onion.

A bizarre bulb that isn't actually an onion but looks vaguely reminiscent of one...

Used in dishes: Archon Burger, Baked Alien Soup, Calamari Ripieni, Caviar Canapes, Clam Cake, Elpis Deipnon, Garlean Pizza, Ground Woolback Steak, Marinated Broccoflower, Nachos, Philosopher's Sandwich, Popoto Potage, Pumpkin Potage, Rroneek au Gratin, Scallop Salad
