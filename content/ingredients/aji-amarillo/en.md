**Aji Amarillo** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Aji Amarillo.

A vibrant and versatile orange pepper. Equally delicious eaten raw, ground into a paste, or pickled with salt.

Used in dishes: Alpaca Stew, Lomo Saltado
