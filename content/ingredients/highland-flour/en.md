**Highland Flour** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Highland Flour.

Finely milled highland wheat. It has a rich, earthy aroma.

Used in dishes: Almond Cream Croissant, Angler Stew, Apple Strudel, Deep-fried Okeanis, Dhalmel Gratin, Flaugnarde, Kaiser Roll, Kingcake, Loaghtan Cordon Bleu, Sesame Cookie, Sohm Al Tart
