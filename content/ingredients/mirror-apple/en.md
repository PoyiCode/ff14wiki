**Mirror Apple** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mirror Apple.

A sweet yet sour variety of apples native to Coerthas. When polished, the skins become so shiny it is said one can see their own reflection in them.

Used in dishes: Apple Juice, Crowned Pie, Pastry Fish, Popoto Pancakes
