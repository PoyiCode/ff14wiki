**Branchbearer Fruit** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Branchbearer Fruit.

Harvested from the body of a branchbearer. The green flesh is exceedingly sour.

Used in dishes: Fruit and Aloe Jelly, Tart Turali Tart, Wild Banana Blend
