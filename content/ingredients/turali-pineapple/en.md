title: "Turali Pineapple"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."

**Turali Pineapple** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Turali Pineapple.

This sweet and tangy Turali fruit is protected by a tough, spiky rind.

Used in dishes: Mesquite Juice, Pineapple Orange Jelly, Tacos Al Pastor, Tart Turali Tart, Turali Pineapple Ponzecake
