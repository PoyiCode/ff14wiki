---
title: "Honeydew Honey"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Honeydew Honey** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Honeydew Honey.

Instead of from collecting pollen, this deep, flavorful variety of honey is made from bees collecting honeydew excreted by the aphids that gather on the flowers.

Used in dishes: Konpeito, Tofu Pancakes
