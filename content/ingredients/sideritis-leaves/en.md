**Sideritis Leaves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sideritis Leaves.

The tea produced from this plant is somewhat bitter but possesses incredible medicinal properties.

Used in dishes: Bubble Tea, Sideritis Cookie, Thavnairian Chai, Tsai tou Vounou
