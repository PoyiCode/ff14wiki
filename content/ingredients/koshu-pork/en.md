**Koshu Pork** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Koshu Pork.

A dark-haired variety of swine bred on Hingashi's Koshu island. Its carefully selected diet is believed to render its meat tender and its fat flavorful.

Used in dishes: Banh Xeo, Pork Kakuni, Pork Stew, Shakshouka
