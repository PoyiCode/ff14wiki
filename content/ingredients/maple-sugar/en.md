**Maple Sugar** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Maple Sugar.

Crystallized sugar made from boiling down maple syrup.

Used in dishes: Apple Tart, Blood Currant Tart, Bubble Chocolate, Crowned Pie, Dark Pretzel, Fig Bavarois, Ginger Cookie, Ishgardian Tea, Pearl Chocolate, Sohm Al Tart, Steamed Catfish, Triple Cream Coffee
