**Chicken Egg** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Chicken Egg.

The egg of a domesticated chicken.

Used in dishes: Apple Tart, Boiled Egg, Cornbread, Dark Pretzel, Ginger Cookie, Honey Muffin, Knight's Bread, Mole Loaf
