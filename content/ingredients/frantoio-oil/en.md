title: "Frantoio Oil"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Frantoio Oil** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Frantoio Oil.

Oil extracted from frantoio olives, suitable for cooking and certain medicinal concoctions.

Used in dishes: Acqua Pazza, Baked Eggplant, Blood Bouillabaisse, Broad Bean Salad, Chicken Fettuccine, Chili Crab, Churrasco, Creamy Alpaca Pasta, Creamy Salmon Pasta, Farmer's Breakfast, Grilled Rail, Mejillones al Ajillo, Mesquite Soup, Mushroom Saute, Ovim Cordon Bleu
