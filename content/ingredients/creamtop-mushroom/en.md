**Creamtop Mushroom** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Creamtop Mushroom.

A bulbous white mushroom that grows wild in forests.

Used in dishes: Baked Megapiranha, Broccoli and Spinach Saute, Driftwood Catfish Pie, Herring Pie, Island Miq'abob, King Urchin Loaf, Mushroom Saute, Mushroom Skewer, Smoked Chicken
