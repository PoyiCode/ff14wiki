---
title: "Gelatin"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Gelatin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Gelatin.

A clear gelling agent extracted from animal skin and fish bones.

Used in dishes: Blood Currant Tart, Fig Bavarois, Fruit and Aloe Jelly, Jellied Compote, Jellied Harcot, Pineapple Orange Jelly, Pixieberry Cheesecake, Rolanberry Cheesecake, Snurbleberry Tart, Stuffed Cabbage, Sykon Bavarois, Zefir
