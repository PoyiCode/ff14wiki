**Heavenly Kukuru Powder** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Heavenly Kukuru Powder.

A sweet, fragrant powder ground from high-quality kukuru beans imported from exotic lands at a great price. A delicacy only available to the Holy See's most privileged.

Used in dishes: Hot Chocolate
