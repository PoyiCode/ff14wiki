**Swampmonk Thigh** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Swampmonk Thigh.

A moist, tender slice of meat taken from the leg of a swampmonk.

Used in dishes: Mezcal-marinated Swampmonk, Moqueca
