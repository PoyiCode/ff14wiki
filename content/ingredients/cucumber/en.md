---
title: "Cucumber"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Cucumber** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cucumber.

Garleans will often pickle this vine-grown fruit and then drink the juice as a hangover cure.

Used in dishes: Carrot Nibbles, Philosopher's Sandwich, Pumpkin Ratatouille, Sunset Carrot Nibbles, Sykon Salad, Yakow Moussaka
