title: "Cottonseed Oil"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Cottonseed Oil** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cottonseed Oil.

Cooking oil pressed from ruby cotton seeds. Its high flash point and lack of odor make it ideal for deep-frying.

Used in dishes: Crab Croquette, Dzo Steak, Lemon Waffle, Papanasi, Sauteed Green Leeks, Steamed Grouper, Steppe Salad, Stewed River Bream, Sweet and Sour Frogs' Legs, Tako-yaki, Tempura Platter
