**Garden Beet Sugar** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Garden Beet Sugar.

A sweetener made by boiling down the juice extracted from garden beets.

Used in dishes: Caramels, Carrot Pudding, Coffee Biscuit, Dragonfruit Blend, Espresso con Panna, Jellied Harcot, Lemon Waffle, Melon Juice, Mors, Pixieberry Cheesecake, Pixieberry Tea, Sausage Links, Silkie Pudding, Zefir
