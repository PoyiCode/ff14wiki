**Doman Rice** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Doman Rice.

A hardy hybrid of sweet, sticky rice perfected by Far Eastern farmers after countless generations of cross-pollination. Known in Yanxia as “Doman Sunrise.”

Used in dishes: Futo-maki Roll
