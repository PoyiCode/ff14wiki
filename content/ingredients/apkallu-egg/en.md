**Apkallu Egg** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Apkallu Egg.

The large egg of a wild apkallu.

Used in dishes: Apkallu Omelette, Deviled Eggs, Finger Sandwich, La Noscean Toast, Pineapple Ponzecake, Popoto Pancakes, Salt Cod Puffs, Spinach Quiche, Trapper's Quiche
