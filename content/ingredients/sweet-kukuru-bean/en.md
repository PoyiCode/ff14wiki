**Sweet Kukuru Bean** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sweet Kukuru Bean.

An overpoweringly sweet variety of kukuru bean indigenous to Tural.

Used in dishes: Coffee Kukuru Rusks, Creamy Hot Chocolate, Gateau au Chocolat, Sweetmuffin
