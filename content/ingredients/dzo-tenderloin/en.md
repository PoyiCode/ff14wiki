**Dzo Tenderloin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dzo Tenderloin.

A superior carving of meat from a carefully raised domestic dzo.

Used in dishes: Exquisite Beef Stew, Rare Roast Beef
