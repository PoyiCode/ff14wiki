**Gridanian Chestnut** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Gridanian Chestnut.

The seed of a chestnut tree, this savory nut is covered in a coat of prickly spikes.

Used in dishes: Glory Be Soup, Lentils and Chestnuts
