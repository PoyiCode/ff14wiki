**Night Milk** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Night Milk.

Rich and creamy buffalo milk harvested immediately before the beast falls asleep.

Used in dishes: Bacon Bread, Cheese Souffle, Pineapple Ponzecake, Rolanberry Lassi
