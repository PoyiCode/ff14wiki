**Beet** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Beet.

A Garlean root vegetable that boasts a deep red color from its stem to its bulb.

Used in dishes: Borscht, Wine-dark Soup
