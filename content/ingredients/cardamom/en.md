title: "Cardamom"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Cardamom** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cardamom.

Boasting a unique fragrance, these seeds are the secret spice in myriad recipes.

Used in dishes: Broad Bean Curry, Lemon Muffin
