**Royal Kukuru Bean** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Royal Kukuru Bean.

A king among kukuru bean varieties, prized for its high nutritional value.

Used in dishes: Lemon Curd Sachertorte, Sachertorte
