**Jhammel Ginger** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Jhammel Ginger.

Jhammel ginger's bulbous, aromatic roots can be sliced fresh and added to stews and seafood dishes to remove gaminess, ground and steeped in hot water to cure head colds, or dried and ground into a powder to use as a spice for breads and cookies.

Used in dishes: Boiled Amberjack Head, Chili Crab, Crimson Cider, Doman Tea, Ginger Salad, Pork Kakuni, Steamed Grouper
