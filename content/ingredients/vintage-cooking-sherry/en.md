**Vintage Cooking Sherry** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Vintage Cooking Sherry.

Fine Lominsan white wine fortified with grape spirit and allowed to mature to the point of perfection, though the flavor may be too strong for weaker palates.

Used in dishes: Tailor-made Eel Pie
