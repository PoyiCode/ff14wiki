---
title: "Heavens Lemon"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Heavens Lemon** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Heavens Lemon.

Possibly due to the increased sunlight it receives growing above the clouds, heavens lemons lack the acidity of normal sun lemons.

Used in dishes: Blood Tomato Juice, Loquat Juice, Paella, Sermon-worthy Meuniere
