**Yakow Chuck** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Yakow Chuck.

A bit on the sinewy side, but highly nutritious.

Used in dishes: Borscht, Spicy Shakshouka, The Noodles of Elpis, Yakow Moussaka
