**La Noscean Orange** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: La Noscean Orange.

A sweet citrus fruit known to thrive in the temperate climate of lower La Noscea.

Used in dishes: Orange Juice, Roast Canard
