title: "Maiden Artichoke"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Maiden Artichoke** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Maiden Artichoke.

The leaf-like bud of an artichoke plant. Due to the roughness of its petals, only the fleshy lower portions can be consumed, as well as the center of the bud, commonly known as the “heart.”

Used in dishes: Stuffed Artichoke
