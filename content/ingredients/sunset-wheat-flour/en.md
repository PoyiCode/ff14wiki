**Sunset Wheat Flour** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sunset Wheat Flour.

Finely milled sunset wheat.

Used in dishes: Antelope Stew, Beef Stew, Cornbread, Crumpet, Dark Pretzel, Ginger Cookie, Honey Muffin, Ishgardian Muffin, Lava Toad Legs, Mutton Stew, Orobon Stew, Pan-fried Mahi-mahi, Pineapple Ponzecake, Popoto Pancakes, Raptor Stew
