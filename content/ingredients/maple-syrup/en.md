title: "Maple Syrup"

---

**Maple Syrup** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Maple Syrup.

A sweet, dark liquid made by boiling down maple sap.

Used in dishes: Cheese Souffle, Crumpet, Jack-o'-lantern, La Noscean Toast, Pineapple Ponzecake, Rolanberry Shaved Ice
