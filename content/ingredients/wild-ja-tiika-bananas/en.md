**Wild Ja Tiika Bananas** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Wild Ja Tiika Bananas.

These luminescent blue bananas are the direct predecessor of the selectively crossbred strain now grown in Golmajiik Grove. Under the peel, the fruit is mild in color and flavor both.

Used in dishes: Banana Ponzecake, Mesquite Juice, Wild Banana Blend
