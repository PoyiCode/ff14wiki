---
title: "Golden Honey"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Golden Honey** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Golden Honey.

This shimmering, pure-gold honey comes courtesy of the giant honey bee.

Used in dishes: Blood Tomato Juice, Giant Popoto Pancakes, Golden Pineapple Juice, Honey Croissant, Honeyed Dragonfruit, Lemonade, Nut Bake, Pixie Apple Pie
