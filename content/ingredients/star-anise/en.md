title: "Star Anise"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Star Anise** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Star Anise.

Originating from the Far East, this fragrant spice is shaped like eight-pointed stars.

Used in dishes: Shark Fin Soup, Steamed Catfish, Steamed Staff
