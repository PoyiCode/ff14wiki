---
title: "Fennel"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Fennel** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Fennel.

With an aroma similar to licorice, both the stalks and the seeds of this Othardian herb can be used to enhance the flavor of exotic Far Eastern dishes.

Used in dishes: Banh Xeo, Pork Kakuni, Rare Roast Beef, Robe Lettuce Salad, Warrior's Stew
