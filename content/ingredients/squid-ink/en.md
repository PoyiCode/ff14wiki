**Squid Ink** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Squid Ink.

This sticky, stygian liquid—decidedly an acquired taste—was harvested from a spearhead squid.

Used in dishes: Arros Negre, Risotto al Nero, Spaghetti al Nero
