title: "Doman Eggplant"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Doman Eggplant** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Doman Eggplant.

While just as brilliantly purple, this Far Eastern cousin of the wizard eggplant is noticeably thinner, and thus requires less time to cook.

Used in dishes: Imam Bayildi, Jhammel Moussaka, Karniyarik, Miso Dengaku, Stewed River Bream, Yakow Moussaka
