**Buffalo Sirloin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Buffalo Sirloin.

A cut of tender, lean meat taken from the rear back of a buffalo.

Used in dishes: Beef Stew, Jerked Beef
