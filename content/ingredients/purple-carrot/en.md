**Purple Carrot** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Purple Carrot.

A dark purple root vegetable with a reddish stalk, differing somewhat in vitamin content from the orange carrot.

Used in dishes: Archon Loaf, Broad Bean Salad, Pineapple Salad, Purple Carrot Juice, Twilight Popoto Salad
