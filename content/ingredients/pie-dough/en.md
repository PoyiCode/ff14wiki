**Pie Dough** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Pie Dough.

Simple pie dough made of flour and butter.

Used in dishes: Apple Tart, Blood Currant Tart, Crowned Pie, Eel Pie, Pastry Fish, Rabbit Pie, Snurbleberry Tart, Spinach Quiche, Tailor-made Eel Pie, Tomato Pie, Trapper's Quiche
