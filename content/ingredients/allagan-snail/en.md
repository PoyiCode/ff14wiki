**Allagan Snail** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Allagan Snail.

A giant snail believed to have been bred for consumption during the reign of the Great Allagan Empire.

Used in dishes: Lava Toad Legs, Tree Toad Legs
