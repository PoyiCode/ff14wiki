**Piennolo Tomato** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Piennolo Tomato.

A juicy, thick-skinned tomato brimming with essential minerals.

Used in dishes: Garlean Pizza, Piennolo Tomato Salad
