**Dravanian Paprika** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dravanian Paprika.

A rare pepper that is more sweet than it is spicy.

Used in dishes: Loaghtan Steak, Pasta Ortolano, Seafood Stew, Shakshouka
