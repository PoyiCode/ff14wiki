**Coerthan Tea Leaves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Coerthan Tea Leaves.

Considerably smaller than Thanalan tea leaves, more than double the amount of Ishgardian tea leaves are required to brew a single cup of tea.

Used in dishes: Ishgardian Tea, Masala Chai, Pixieberry Tea
