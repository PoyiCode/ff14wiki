**Mountain Salt** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mountain Salt.

Fine grains of “divine blessing” mined from the mountains of Urqopacha.

Used in dishes: All i Pebre, Boiled Lobster, Broccoli and Spinach Saute, Coffee Kukuru Rusks, Driftwood Catfish Pie, Grilled Urqotrout, Maple Teriyaki, Mezcal-marinated Swampmonk, Paes de Queijo, Popoto Potage, Quahog Chowder, Roast Chicken, Rock-fisted Popoto Stew, Rroneek au Gratin, Rroneek Steak
