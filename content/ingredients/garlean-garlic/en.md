**Garlean Garlic** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Garlean Garlic.

A pungent spice used to flavor many different varieties of dishes. It is also thought to ward off vampiric bats, but there is no evidence to substantiate that claim.

Used in dishes: Aldgoat Steak, Antelope Steak, Baba Ghanoush, Bacon Broth, Boiled Bream, Button Mushroom Saute, Dzemael Gratin, Eft Steak, Grilled Dodo, Grilled Warmwater Trout, Haddock Dip, Marmot Steak, Mashed Popotoes, Mejillones al Ajillo, Pumpkin Ratatouille
