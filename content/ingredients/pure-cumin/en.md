title: "Pure Cumin"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Pure Cumin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Pure Cumin.

This bright, earthy spice is prized for its versatility both culinary and otherwise, appearing in all manner of medicinal tinctures and beauty treatments.

Used in dishes: Spicy Stellar Soup, Tandoori Raptor
