**Lingonberry** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Lingonberry.

Plump, ripe, and bursting with flavor. Perfect for jams and sweet treats.

Used in dishes: Mors, Ovim Meatballs
