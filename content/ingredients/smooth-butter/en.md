**Smooth Butter** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Smooth Butter.

A smooth, fatty cream made by churning buffalo milk.

Used in dishes: Apkallu Omelette, Apple Tart, Blood Currant Tart, Braised Pipira, Cawl Cennin, Chanterelle Saute, Chicken and Mushrooms, Chilled Popoto Soup, Crumpet, Dark Pretzel, Dodo Omelette, Eel Pie, Finger Sandwich, Fish Soup, Fried Egg
