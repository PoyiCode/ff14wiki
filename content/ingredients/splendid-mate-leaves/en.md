title: "Splendid Mate Leaves"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Splendid Mate Leaves** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Splendid Mate Leaves.

Grown only in a few select regions, these premium leaves are brewed into an invigorating beverage which many believe has innate magical properties.

Used in dishes: Mate Cookie, Sweet Maple Mate
