**Night Vinegar** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Night Vinegar.

A high-quality distilled vinegar made from royal grapes.

Used in dishes: Blood Tomato Salad, Carrot Nibbles, Loaghtan Rump Steak, Lomo Saltado, Marinated Broccoflower, Mist Spinach Saute, Piennolo Tomato Salad, Roast Ovim, Roast Rroneek, Rock-fisted Popoto Salad, Rroneek Steak, Sausage and Sauerkraut, Sunset Carrot Nibbles
