---
title: "Navel Orange"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Navel Orange** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Navel Orange.

Renowned for its natural sweetness and tender, aromatic flesh, this bright citrus is easily recognized by the small hollow in its skin.

Used in dishes: Navel Orange Cookies, Pineapple Orange Jelly
