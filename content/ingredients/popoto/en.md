**Popoto** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Popoto.

A starchy tuberous vegetable originally introduced to Eorzea by explorers from the distant western continent.

Used in dishes: Antelope Stew, Battered Fish, Chilled Popoto Soup, Dhalmel Gratin, Dzemael Gratin, Eel Pie, Mashed Popotoes, Roast Canard, Shepherd's Pie, Steamed Catfish, Stone Soup
