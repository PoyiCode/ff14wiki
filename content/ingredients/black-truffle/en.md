title: "Black Truffle"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Black Truffle** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Black Truffle.

A rare black mushroom that grows in the soil, rather than above it, making the specimen almost impossible to locate. It is treasured as a delicacy for its unique aroma.

Used in dishes: Apkallu Omelette, Black Truffle Risotto
