**Okeanis Tail** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Okeanis Tail.

When skinned, this slice of serpent-like meat almost appears edible.

Used in dishes: Deep-fried Okeanis, Stuffed Chysahl
