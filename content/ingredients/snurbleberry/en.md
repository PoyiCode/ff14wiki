**Snurbleberry** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Snurbleberry.

This sweet, yet tart red berry is covered in tiny soft hairs that can be removed by lightly rubbing the fruit between one's palms.

Used in dishes: Snurbleberry Tart
