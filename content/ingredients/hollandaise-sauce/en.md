**Hollandaise Sauce** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Hollandaise Sauce.

A rich sauce created with butter, egg yolks, and a hint of lemon.

Used in dishes: Royal Eggs, Stuffed Chysahl
