**Cream Cheese** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cream Cheese.

A smooth, buttery cheese made from cultured buffalo milk.

Used in dishes: Baked Onion Soup, Cheese Souffle, Ema Datshi, Liver-cheese Sandwich, Ovim Cordon Bleu, Popotoes au Gratin, Spinach Quiche, Stuffed Chysahl, Trapper's Quiche
