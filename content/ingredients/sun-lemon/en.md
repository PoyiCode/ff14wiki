**Sun Lemon** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sun Lemon.

A sour citrus fruit indigenous to the Near East.

Used in dishes: Cheese Souffle, Deviled Eggs, Raw Oyster, Salmon Meuniere, Smoked Raptor, Tuna Miq'abob
