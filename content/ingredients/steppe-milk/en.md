title: "Steppe Milk"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Steppe Milk** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Steppe Milk.

Compared to that of an aldgoat, marid, or deathmouse, the milk of a steppe dzo is high in fat and flavor.

Used in dishes: Jhammel Moussaka, Masala Chai, Persimmon Pudding, Popotoes au Gratin, Steppe Tea, Tofu Pancakes
