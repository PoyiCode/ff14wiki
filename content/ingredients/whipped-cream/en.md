**Whipped Cream** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Whipped Cream.

A fluffy and faintly sweet plant-based cream made with oilseed extract.

Used in dishes: Bubble Tea, Cinnamon Cream Coffee, Creamy Alpaca Pasta, Creamy Hot Chocolate, Gateau au Chocolat, Mate Cookie, Tender Shortcake, Turali Pineapple Ponzecake
