title: "Mesquite Beans"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Mesquite Beans** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Mesquite Beans.

Prized for their adaptability, these beans can be planted even in the most arid of environs.

Used in dishes: Fruit and Aloe Jelly, Mesquite Juice, Mesquite Soup, Navel Orange Cookies
