**Oriental Miso Paste** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Oriental Miso Paste.

Similar to Mun-Tuy paste, this Far Eastern seasoning is made by fermenting soybeans in salt.

Used in dishes: Miso Dengaku, Miso Soup with Tofu, Onigara-yaki, Pork Stew
