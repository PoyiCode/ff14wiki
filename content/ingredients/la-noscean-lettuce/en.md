**La Noscean Lettuce** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: La Noscean Lettuce.

A crisp leafy vegetable native to the plains of La Noscea.

Used in dishes: Blood Tomato Salad, Broad Bean Salad, Finger Sandwich, Ginger Salad, Landtrap Salad, Morel Salad, Parsnip Salad, Pineapple Salad
