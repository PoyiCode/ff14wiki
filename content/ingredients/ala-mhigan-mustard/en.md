**Ala Mhigan Mustard** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Ala Mhigan Mustard.

When dried and ground into a fine powder, this yellow-tinged spice can add quite the kick to any dish.

Used in dishes: Deviled Eggs, Mustard Eggs
