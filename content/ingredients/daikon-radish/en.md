**Daikon Radish** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Daikon Radish.

The spicy daikon radishes of Othard are known to grow as long and thick as a grown Hyur's forearm.

Used in dishes: Boiled Amberjack Head, Ginger Salad, Miso Dengaku, Oden, Pork Stew, Steppe Salad
