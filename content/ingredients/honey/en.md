---
title: "Honey"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Honey** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Honey.

Bees make this sweet golden honey from the nectar of flowers.

Used in dishes: Baklava, Chamomile Tea, Frumenty, Honey Muffin, Knight's Bread, Loquat Juice, Roast Canard, Sesame Cookie
