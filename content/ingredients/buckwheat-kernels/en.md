**Buckwheat Kernels** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Buckwheat Kernels.

A nutty grain that can be substituted for flour in breads and noodles. Found across the Three Great Continents, but grown in abundance in the heart of Gyr Abania.

Used in dishes: Buckwheat Tea, Kasha, Popoto Soba
