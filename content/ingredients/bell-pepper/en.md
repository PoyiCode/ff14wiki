title: "Bell Pepper"

---

**Bell Pepper** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Bell Pepper.

This faintly bitter Turali vegetable is also sometimes used in medicines and perfumes.

Used in dishes: Broccoli and Spinach Saute, Mollete, Stuffed Peppers
