title: "Bomba Rice"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Bomba Rice** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Bomba Rice.

This short-grain rice is more absorbent than other varieties and does not clump together when cooked.

Used in dishes: Arros Negre, Broad Bean Curry, Jhinga Biryani, Lentil Curry, Nasi Goreng, Omelette Rice, Paella, Risotto al Nero, Scallop Curry
