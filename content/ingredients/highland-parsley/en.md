---

**Highland Parsley** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Highland Parsley.

Oft used as a garnish in other dishes, this pungent vegetable grows in abundance in the mountainous region of upper La Noscea.

Used in dishes: Chilled Popoto Soup, Deviled Eggs, Dodo Omelette, Fish Soup, Imam Bayildi, Oyster Confit, Peperoncino, Priestly Omelette, Salmon Meuniere
