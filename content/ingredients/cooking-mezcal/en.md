**Cooking Mezcal** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cooking Mezcal.

Distilled from the fermented pulp of megamaguey pineapples.

Used in dishes: Acqua Pazza, Alpaca Stew, Boiled Alpaca Steak, Chicken Pasta, Churrasco, Lobster Bisque, Mezcal-marinated Swampmonk, Prune-packed Fruitcake, Salmon Jerky, Shrimp Cocktail, Solterito, Woolback Steak
