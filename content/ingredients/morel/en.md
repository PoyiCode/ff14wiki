title: "Morel"

---

**Morel** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Morel.

This rare mushroom with an oblong webbed cap is highly sought after by culinarians the realm over.

Used in dishes: Boscaiola, Morel Salad, Urchin Loaf
