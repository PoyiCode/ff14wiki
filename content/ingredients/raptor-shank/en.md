**Raptor Shank** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Raptor Shank.

An extra-lean, tough piece of meat taken from the leg of a raptor.

Used in dishes: Raptor Stew, Smoked Raptor, Tandoori Raptor
