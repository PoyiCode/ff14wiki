**Sheep Casings** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sheep Casings.

Intestine casings for use in cooking.

Used in dishes: Sausage and Sauerkraut, Sausage Links
