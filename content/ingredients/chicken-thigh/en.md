**Chicken Thigh** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Chicken Thigh.

The thigh of a breed of chicken known for the superior flavor and texture of its flesh.

Used in dishes: Chicken Fettuccine, Smoked Chicken
