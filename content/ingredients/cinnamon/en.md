**Cinnamon** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cinnamon.

The sweet-smelling bark of the cinnamon tree is often dried and ground into a spice used to add flavor to confectionaries, though it is also commonly used in medicines.

Used in dishes: Apple Strudel, Apple Tart, Cinnamon Cream Coffee, Crowned Pie, Doman Tea, Espresso con Panna, Frumenty, Heavenly Eggnog, Masala Chai, Mate Cookie, Mulled Tea, Pixie Apple Pie, Spiced Cider, Triple Cream Coffee, Tsai tou Vounou
