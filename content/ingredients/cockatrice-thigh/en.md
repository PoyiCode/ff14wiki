**Cockatrice Thigh** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Cockatrice Thigh.

A lean slice of cockatrice meat good for flavorful broths.

Used in dishes: Cockatrice Meatballs, Liver-cheese Sandwich
