**Bird of Elpis Breast** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Bird of Elpis Breast.

The most tender meat this bird has to offer.

Used in dishes: Broccoflower Stew, Elpis Deipnon, Maple Teriyaki
