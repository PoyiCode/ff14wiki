**Sweet Cream** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Sweet Cream.

The butterfat-rich layer skimmed off of buffalo milk. Widely used in confectionaries.

Used in dishes: Apkallu Omelette, Cawl Cennin, Chicken Fettuccine, Chilled Popoto Soup, Creamy Salmon Pasta, Dzemael Gratin, Flaugnarde, Heavenly Eggnog, Mashed Popotoes, Orobon Stew, Rolanberry Cheesecake, Sachertorte, Sohm Al Tart, Spaghetti Carbonara, Stuffed Artichoke
