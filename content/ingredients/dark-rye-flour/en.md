**Dark Rye Flour** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Dark Rye Flour.

A fine flour made from dark rye.

Used in dishes: Archon Burger, Archon Loaf, Creamy Alpaca Pasta, Elpis Deipnon, Kalamarakia Tiganita, Maple Teriyaki, Mate Cookie, Melon Pie, Peach Tart, Philosopher's Sandwich, Prune-packed Fruitcake, Sideritis Cookie, Sweetmuffin, Sykon Cookie, Tacos Al Pastor
