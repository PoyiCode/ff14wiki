**Yyasulani Garlic** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Yyasulani Garlic.

What this Tural-grown garlic lacks in size, it makes up for with richness of aroma and nutrients.

Used in dishes: Chicken Pasta, Driftwood Catfish Pie, Ground Woolback Steak, Moqueca, Roast Chicken, Roast Rroneek, Rroneek au Gratin, Rroneek Steak, Tacos de Carne Asada, Vegetable Soup
