---
title: "Thundermelon"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Thundermelon** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Thundermelon.

This fruit is sweet, but its rich and electrifying acidity numbs the tongue.

Used in dishes: Melon Juice, Melon Pie
