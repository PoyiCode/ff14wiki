**Peppermint** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Peppermint.

This refreshing herb is suitable for use in cooking, essential oils, and tea.

Used in dishes: Amra Lassi, Chicken Fettuccine, Glory Be Soup, Happiness Juice, Honey Croissant, Peach Juice, Pixieberry Tea, Pizza, Rare Roast Beef, Sausage and Sauerkraut, Skyr, Thavnairian Chai, Wine-dark Soup, Woolback Steak, Zefir
