**Ogre Pumpkin** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Ogre Pumpkin.

A bright-orange squash which sweetens in flavor when cooked.

Used in dishes: Heavensegg Soup, Jack-o'-lantern, Rock-fisted Popoto Stew
