**Upland Wheat Flour** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Upland Wheat Flour.

Finely milled upland wheat.

Used in dishes: Alpaca Stew, Baguette, Baked Alien Soup, Banana Ponzecake, Broad Bean Curry, Broccoflower Stew, Caviar Canapes, Caviar Sandwich, Clam Cake, Cloudsail Meuniere, Coffee Biscuit, Crab Cakes, Driftwood Catfish Pie, Elpis Deipnon, Exquisite Beef Stew
