title: "Highland Oregano"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Highland Oregano** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Highland Oregano.

Due to its higher oil content, highland oregano is preferred over other breeds of oregano by apothecaries and perfumeries across Eorzea.

Used in dishes: Jerked Jhammel
