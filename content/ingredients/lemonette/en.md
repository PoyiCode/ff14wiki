**Lemonette** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Lemonette.

A tiny lemon replete with sour juice.

Used in dishes: Boiled Alpaca Steak, Boiled Lobster, Ceviche, Cloudsail Meuniere, Giant Haddock Dip, Golden Pineapple Juice, Grilled Urqotrout, Honeyed Dragonfruit, Jellied Harcot, Kalamarakia Tiganita, King Salmon Meuniere, Lemon Curd Sachertorte, Lemon Muffin, Lemon Waffle, Lemonade
