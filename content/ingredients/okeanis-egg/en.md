**Okeanis Egg** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Okeanis Egg.

The soft-shelled, slime-covered, ever-so-slightly warm egg of an okeanis.

Used in dishes: Creme Brulee, Kingcake, Royal Eggs, Urchin Loaf
