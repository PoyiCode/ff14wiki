**Elder Nutmeg Seeds** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Elder Nutmeg Seeds.

An ancient spice that emits a sweet aroma. Used in cooking as well as in medicine.

Used in dishes: Giant Popoto Pancakes, Hamsa Curry, Jhinga Curry, Karniyarik, Lentil Curry, Melon Juice, Scallop Curry, Spicy Shakshouka
