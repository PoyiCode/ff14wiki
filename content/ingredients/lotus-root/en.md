---
title: "Lotus Root"
summary: "A cooking ingredient."
---

**Lotus Root** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Lotus Root.

Though they appear solid from the outside, the cylindrical root nodules of the lotus flower are riddled with holes, possibly to accommodate for the waterlogged environments in which the plant grows.

Used in dishes: Chirashi-zushi, Gameni, Steppe Salad, Tempura Platter
