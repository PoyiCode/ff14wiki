**Emerald Beans** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Emerald Beans.

A deep-green legume recently discovered growing on the floating islets above the Sea of Clouds.

Used in dishes: Emerald Soup, Morel Salad
