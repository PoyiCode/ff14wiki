**Coerthan Carrot** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Coerthan Carrot.

A long orange root vegetable commonly grown in the rocky alpine soil of the Coerthas region.

Used in dishes: Antelope Stew, Mutton Stew, Orobon Stew, Rabbit Pie, Shepherd's Pie, Stone Soup, Stuffed Cabbage
