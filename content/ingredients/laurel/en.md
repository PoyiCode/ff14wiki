**Laurel** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Laurel.

A savory spice made from the dried leaves of the laurel bush, or as it is known by some, the yanny bush.

Used in dishes: Dhalmel Fricassee, Eft Steak, Heavensegg Soup, Laghman, Oyster Confit, Ratatouille, Sauerkraut
