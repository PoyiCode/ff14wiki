**Eft Tail** is a foodstuff residents cook with and buy at market — a raw ingredient that goes into Eorzean dishes. In-game name: Eft Tail.

Sinewy meat taken from the tail of an eft.

Used in dishes: Dzemael Gratin, Eft Steak, Trapper's Quiche
