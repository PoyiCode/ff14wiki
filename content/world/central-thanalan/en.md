**Central Thanalan** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Thanalan** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Central Thanalan.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（40%）, Fair Skies（20%）, Dust Storms（15%）, Clouds（10%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（5%）

Landmarks here: Fesca's Wash, Fesca's Watch, Gate of Nald, Gate of Thal, Lost Hope, Nanawa Mines, Royal Plantations, Sagolii Gate, Sil'dih Excavation Site, Stonesthrow, Sultantree, The Bonfire, The Coffer & Coffin, The Quiveron Manse, The Rat's Nest, The Unholy Heir, Ul'dah Dispatch Yard

Aetherytes here: Black Brush Station, Gate of Nald (Central Thanalan), Gate of Thal (Central Thanalan)
