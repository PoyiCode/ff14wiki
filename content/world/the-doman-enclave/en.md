**The Doman Enclave** is an aetheryte in **Othard**. Teleporting here is how a resident usually reaches this area. In-game name: The Doman Enclave.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clear Skies（20%）, Clouds（15%）, Rain（10%）, Fog（10%）, Showers（5%）

Landmarks here: Kienkan, Rissai-juku, Shazenkai, Tenkonto, The Enclave Barracks, The Enclave Demesne, The One Garden, The Ten Thousand Stalls, The Watchtower, The Yard
