**The Lochs** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Gyr Abania** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Lochs.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clear Skies（20%）, Clouds（20%）, Fog（10%）, Thunderstorms（10%）

Landmarks here: Aenadem Ei, Aenadem Ol, Bloodhowe, Cotter Tor, Demilune, Gylbarde's Gate, Lochwatch, Sali Monastery, Sothwatch, Sweetwater, The Dark of Lasting Cumber, The Destroyer, The Divine Audience, The Firkins, The Fourth Royal Casern, The Monstrance, The Pauper's Lode, The Royal Hunting Grounds, The Ruiner, The Saltery, The Song of Kings, The Third Royal Casern, The Tomb of King Manfred, The Tomb of the Errant Sword, The White Aisle

Aetherytes here: Porta Praetoria, The Ala Mhigan Quarter
