**Kholusia** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Norvrandt** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Kholusia.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clouds（15%）, Clear Skies（15%）, Gales（10%）, Rain（10%）, Fog（10%）

Landmarks here: Amity, Barrow Isle, Boiled Owl Bridge, Bottom Rung, Cracked Shell Beach, Dwarven Hollows, Flue Bridge, Gatetown, Governor's Row, Greely Bridge, Greely Sties, Komra, Longbeard Council, Open Arms, Pit 8, Seagazer, Southern Crossing, Stonegazer, The Chisel, The Clave, The Codger's Crook, The Duergar's Tewel, The Ladder, The Lawns, The Leaky Keel, The Split Hull, The Wet Whistle, Top Rung, Venmont Yards, Wax Seal Mills, Whisperwind Cove, White Oil Falls

Aetherytes here: Stilltide, The Path to Glory (Kholusia), Tomra, Wright
