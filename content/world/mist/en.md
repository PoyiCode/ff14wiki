---
title: "Mist"
summary: "A residential district in La Noscea."
---

**Mist** is one of Eorzea's residential districts in **La Noscea**, where residents own houses and apartments and make their homes. A peaceful neighbourhood to live, garden and host friends. In-game name: Mist.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（30%）, Fair Skies（30%）, Clouds（20%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（10%）

Landmarks here: Mistgate Square, Mistgate Square Subdivision, Seagaze Markets, Seagaze Markets Subdivision, The Admiral's Waterspire, The Admiral's Waterspire Subdivision
