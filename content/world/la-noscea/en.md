La Noscea is the coastal region surrounding Limsa Lominsa, a patchwork of rocky shores, beaches, green grasslands and sheep pastures. Ferries and roads link it outward, and it is where the sea city's residents go to clear their heads and look out over the water.

The weather is changeable and the sea wind strong. Along the way one finds fishing villages, lighthouses and fishermen drying their nets — a fine place for an unhurried walk and a sea view.
