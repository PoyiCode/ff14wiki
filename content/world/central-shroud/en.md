**Central Shroud** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **The Black Shroud** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Central Shroud.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（30%）, Rain（15%）, Fair Skies（15%）, Fair Skies（15%）, Fog（10%）, Clouds（10%）, Thunder（5%）
