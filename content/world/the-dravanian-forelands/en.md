**The Dravanian Forelands** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Dravania** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Dravanian Forelands.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（30%）, Fair Skies（30%）, Clouds（10%）, Fog（10%）, Thunder（10%）, Dust Storms（10%）

Landmarks here: Anyx Minor, Anyx Old, Ehs Daih, Gynehive, Halo, Loth ast Vath, Mare's Oath, The Iron Feast, The Stained One, Thriphive

Aetherytes here: Anyx Trine, Tailfeather
