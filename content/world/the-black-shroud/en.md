title: The Black Shroud
summary: The vast forest around Gridania — a sacred land guarded by elementals, deep and hushed.
aliases: [The Black Shroud]
---

The Black Shroud is the great woodland that blankets the Gridania region, its ancient trees towering and tangled with vines, watched over by the forest's elementals. Within are streams, clearings and small hamlets; light filters through the canopy, and the mood is quiet and sacred.

To venture deep or harm the forest without leave is taboo. For a resident, it is a good place to gather mushrooms, listen to birdsong and rest by a stream — but always with respect for the woods.

Zones in this region: Central Shroud, East Shroud, South Shroud, North Shroud, The Lavender Beds, New Gridania, Old Gridania

Landmarks here: Central Shroud, East Shroud, North Shroud, Old Gridania, South Shroud, The Lavender Beds

Aetherytes here: New Gridania
