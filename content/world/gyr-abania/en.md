**Gyr Abania** is one of the world's regions. Aetherytes here: Ala Gannha, Ala Ghiri, Castrum Oriens, Porta Praetoria, Rhalgr's Reach, The Ala Mhigan Quarter, The Peering Stones.

Zones in this region: The Fringes, The Peaks, The Lochs, Rhalgr's Reach

Landmarks here: Rhalgr's Reach, The Fringes, The Lochs, The Peaks
