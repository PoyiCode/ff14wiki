**The Tempest** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Norvrandt** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Tempest.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（60%）, Clouds（20%）, Clear Skies（20%）

Landmarks here: Achora Heights, Bureau of the Administrator, Bureau of the Architect, Bureau of the Secretariat, Purpure, The Caliban Gap, The Capitol, The Crock of Pearls, The Flounders' Floor, The Hall of Rhetoric, The Polyleritae District, The Swallowed Spire, The Trinculo Shelf, The Workbench, Walls of the Forgotten, Where the Dry Return

Aetherytes here: The Macarenses Angle, The Ondo Cups
