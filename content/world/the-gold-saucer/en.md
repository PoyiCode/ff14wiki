**The Gold Saucer** is an aetheryte in **Thanalan**. Teleporting here is how a resident usually reaches this area. In-game name: The Gold Saucer.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（100%）

Landmarks here: Cactpot Board, Chocobo Lift, El Coloso, Lord of Verminion Counter, Main Counter, Main Stage, Mt. Corel, Race Counter, The Manderville Lounge, The Manderville Tables
