**Middle La Noscea** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **La Noscea** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Middle La Noscea.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（30%）, Clouds（20%）, Fair Skies（20%）, Wind（10%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（10%）

Landmarks here: De Nevelle Checkpoint, Foremast, La Thagran Checkpoint, Seasong Grotto, Skylift, The Cookpot, Tiller's Rest, Woad Whisper Canyon, Zephyr Gate

Aetherytes here: Summerford Farms
