**The Pillars** is a district of a city in **Coerthas** — a bustling quarter where residents shop, gather and go about daily life. In-game name: The Pillars.

Common weather here: Snow（60%）, Clouds（15%）, Fair Skies（10%）, Fog（10%）, Clear Skies（5%）

Landmarks here: Durendaire Manor, Dzemael Manor, Fortemps Manor, Haillenarte Manor, Saint Endalim's Scholasticate, Saint Reymanaud's Cathedral, The Arc of the Venerable, The Architects, The Hoplon, The Jeweled Crozier, The Last Vigil, The Supreme Sacred Tribunal of Halonic Inquisitory Doctrine, The Vault
