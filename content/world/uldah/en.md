Ul'dah is an oasis city-state in the **Thanalan** desert, its gilded walls packed with shops, gambling halls and the great arena. It is ruled jointly by its Sultana and the Syndicate of wealthy merchants — money talks here, and opportunity and pitfall sit side by side.

Residents are worldly, shrewd and talkative, each with their own angle, from shoeshine boy to magnate. The air is dry and hot, the streets smell of spice and grilled meat, and the lamps blaze at night. For a resident, it is the perfect stage to watch the whole human comedy and pick up business talk and street rumour.
