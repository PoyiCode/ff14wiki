---

**Foundation** is an aetheryte in **Coerthas**. Teleporting here is how a resident usually reaches this area. In-game name: Foundation.

Common weather here: Snow（60%）, Clouds（15%）, Fair Skies（10%）, Fog（10%）, Clear Skies（5%）

Landmarks here: Congregation of Our Knights Most Heavenly, Saint Reinette's Forum, Saint Valeroyant's Forum, The Arc of the Humble, The Arc of the Worthy, The Forgotten Knight, The Fury's Mercy, The Holy Stables, The Lightfeather Proving Grounds
