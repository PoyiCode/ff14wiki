**Eastern Thanalan** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Thanalan** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Eastern Thanalan.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（40%）, Fair Skies（20%）, Showers（15%）, Clouds（10%）, Fog（10%）, Rain（5%）

Landmarks here: Amalj'aa Encampment , Burgundy Falls, Church of Saint Adama Landama, Final Prayer, Highbridge, Thal's Respite, The Golden Bazaar, The Invisible City

Aetherytes here: Camp Drybone
