**Yok Tural** is one of the world's regions. Aetherytes here: Dock Poga, Earthenshire, Iq Br'aax, Mamook, Many Fires, Ok'hanu, Tuliyollal, Wachunpelo, Worlar's Echo.

Zones in this region: Urqopacha, Kozama'uka, Yak T'el, Tuliyollal
