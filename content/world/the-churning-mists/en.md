**The Churning Mists** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **Dravania** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: The Churning Mists.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（30%）, Fair Skies（30%）, Umbral Static（20%）, Clouds（10%）, Gales（10%）

Landmarks here: Asah, Bahrr Lehs, Easton Eyes, Monsterie, Mother of the Sheave, Sothton Walls, Tharl Oom Khash, The House of Letters, The Lost Landlord, The Rookery, Weston Waters

Aetherytes here: Moghome, Zenith
