**Mare Lamentorum** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **The Sea of Stars** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Mare Lamentorum.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（70%）, Umbral Wind（15%）, Moon Dust（15%）

Landmarks here: Heimdall's Last Sight, Hoper's Hold, Kydonia Knolls, Lovingway's Darlings, The Biting Brand, The Chlorophosgrot, The Cradle of Darkness, The Crushing Brand, The Drowning Brand, The Numbing Brand, The Searing Brand, The Striking Brand, The Watcher's Palace, Vallis Vulneris

Aetherytes here: Bestways Burrow, Sinus Lacrimarum
