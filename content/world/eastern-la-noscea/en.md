**Eastern La Noscea** is an explorable field zone of Eorzea, part of the wider region of **La Noscea** — an area a resident can travel through on their journeys. In-game name: Eastern La Noscea.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（45%）, Fair Skies（30%）, Clouds（10%）, Fog（5%）, Rain（5%）, Showers（5%）

Landmarks here: Castrum Occidens, Gullperch Tower, Hidden Falls, Isle of Endless Summer, Red Mantis Falls, The Garlok's Lair, The Severed String

Aetherytes here: Costa del Sol, Wineport
