Limsa Lominsa is an island city on the coast of **La Noscea**, a maze of gangplanks and suspension bridges split into the Upper Decks and the Lower Decks.

It is where sea-folk and reformed pirates gather, with the Maelstrom keeping order on the waves. The air smells of sea breeze, grilled fish and ale; its people are bold and loyal, and in their free time they fish off the docks or watch the ships set sail.

For a resident, it is the perfect place to listen to sailors brag about their adventures and strike up conversation with travellers.
