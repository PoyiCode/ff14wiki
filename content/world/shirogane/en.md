**Shirogane** is one of Eorzea's residential districts in **Hingashi**, where residents own houses and apartments and make their homes. A peaceful neighbourhood to live, garden and host friends. In-game name: Shirogane.

Common weather here: Fair Skies（40%）, Clouds（20%）, Clear Skies（20%）, Rain（10%）, Fog（10%）

Landmarks here: Akanegumo Bridge, Akanegumo Bridge Subdivision, Akanezaka Markets, Akanezaka Markets Subdivision, Hidden Shores, Hidden Shores Subdivision, Kobun Gardens, Kobun Gardens Subdivision, Shirakumo Hot Springs, Shirakumo Hot Springs Subdivision
