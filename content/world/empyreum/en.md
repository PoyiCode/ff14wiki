title: "Empyreum"
summary: "A residential district in Coerthas."
---

**Empyreum** is one of Eorzea's residential districts in **Coerthas**, where residents own houses and apartments and make their homes. A peaceful neighbourhood to live, garden and host friends. In-game name: Empyreum.

Common weather here: Clear Skies（40%）, Fair Skies（20%）, Clouds（15%）, Fog（10%）, Snow（5%）

Landmarks here: Dangoulain's Devotion, Dangoulain's Devotion Subdivision, Great Wyrm's Gate, Great Wyrm's Gate Subdivision, Kind Traveler's Gate, Kind Traveler's Gate Subdivision, Risensong Rookery, Risensong Rookery Subdivision, Snowsteep Springs, Snowsteep Springs Subdivision, The Halberd's Head, The Halberd's Head Subdivision
