**Wine-dark Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Wine-dark Soup.

A creamy variety of beet soup—though no self-respecting Sharlayan would ever utilize such vulgar nomenclature. *Ever.*

Ingredients used: Beet, Garlean Cheese, Ovibos Milk, Peppermint, Russet Popoto
