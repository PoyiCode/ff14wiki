**Grilled Turban** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Grilled Turban.

A live horned turban cooked in its shell with a splash of soy sauce and rice wine.

Ingredients used: Cooking Sake, Oriental Soy Sauce
