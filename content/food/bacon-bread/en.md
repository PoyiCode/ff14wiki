**Bacon Bread** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Bacon Bread.

Soft dough mixed with thick-sliced bacon and twisted into a pattern resembling a sheaf of wheat.

Ingredients used: Mineral Water, Night Milk, Smoked Bacon, Sunset Wheat
