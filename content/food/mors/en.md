**Mors** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mors.

A traditional beverage brewed from the extract of fermented lingonberries.

Ingredients used: Birch Syrup, Galago Mint, Garden Beet Sugar, Lemonette, Lingonberry
