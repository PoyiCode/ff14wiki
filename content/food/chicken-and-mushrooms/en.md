**Chicken and Mushrooms** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Chicken and Mushrooms.

Simple, home-cooked fare consisting of chicken sautéed with gil buns.

Ingredients used: Chicken Breast, Gil Bun, Smooth Butter, Table Salt
