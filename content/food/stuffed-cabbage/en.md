**Stuffed Cabbage** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Stuffed Cabbage.

A traditional Sea Wolf dish consisting of pike wrapped in cabbage and steamed until soft.

Ingredients used: Clove Oil, Coerthan Carrot, Gelatin, Midland Cabbage, Salt Leek
