**Ovim Cordon Bleu** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Ovim Cordon Bleu.

An indulgent meal of tender ovim meat wrapped around gooey cheese, battered and panfried in oil to give it its crispy, golden brown shell.

Ingredients used: Cream Cheese, Frantoio Oil, Lemonette, Ovim Meat, Upland Wheat Flour
