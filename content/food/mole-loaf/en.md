**Mole Loaf** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mole Loaf.

A moist, juicy loaf of ground mole meat and onions baked to a golden brown.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Chicken Egg, Mole Meat, Tomato Sauce, Wild Onion
