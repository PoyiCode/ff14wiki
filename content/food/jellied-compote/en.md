**Jellied Compote** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Jellied Compote.

Stewed fruit rendered firm with gelatin, then chilled and cut into bite-sized squares.

Ingredients used: Beet Sugar, Gelatin, Loquat, Othardian Plum, Persimmon
