**Sausage and Sauerkraut** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sausage and Sauerkraut.

Aromatic, herb-seasoned meat and pickled cabbage come together in a mouthwatering combination.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Highland Cabbage, Night Vinegar, Ovim Meat, Peppermint, Sheep Casings
