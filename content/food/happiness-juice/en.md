**Happiness Juice** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Happiness Juice.

This uniquely sweet drink consists of an entire squeezed carrot, along with an assortment of other scrumptious additions to give it a kick.

Ingredients used: Carrot of Happiness, Iceberg Lettuce, Peppermint
