**Beef Stroganoff** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Beef Stroganoff.

Choice cuts of meat are simmered in a savory sauce to create this Garlean specialty, the ideal supplement to one's squat regime.

Ingredients used: Butterbeef, Giant Popoto, Iceberg Lettuce, Ovibos Milk, Perilla Oil, Potent Spice
