**Alpaca Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Alpaca Stew.

A hearty stew of alpaca tenderloin and chunks of popoto gently simmered in mezcal.

Ingredients used: Aji Amarillo, Alpaca Fillet, Cooking Mezcal, Giant Popoto, Upland Wheat Flour
