**Sweet and Sour Frogs' Legs** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sweet and Sour Frogs' Legs.

Chunks of meaty steppe bullfrog legs, breaded and deep-fried, then tossed in a sweet and tangy vinegar-based glaze with fresh colorful vegetables.
