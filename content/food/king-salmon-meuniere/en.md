**King Salmon Meuniere** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: King Salmon Meuniere.

A fillet of soft, delectable salmon coated in flour and fried in rich butter comprises this simple-yet-indulgent dish.

Ingredients used: Fermented Butter, Lemonette, Night Pepper, Upland Wheat Flour
