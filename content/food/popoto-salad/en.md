**Popoto Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Popoto Salad.

A creamy side dish of eggs, colorful vegetables, and hearty russet popotoes.

Ingredients used: Frantoio Oil, Lorikeet Egg, Night Pepper, Ramhorn Zucchini, Robe Lettuce, Russet Popoto
