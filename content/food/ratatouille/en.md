**Ratatouille** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Ratatouille.

A nutritious soup made from a variety of vegetables sautéed in lavender oil.

Ingredients used: Garlean Garlic, Laurel, Lavender Oil, Ramhorn Zucchini, Ruby Tomato, Wizard Eggplant
