**Trapper's Quiche** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Trapper's Quiche.

Eggs, cheese, and chopped eft blended together and baked in a flaky crust. A favorite amongst Ishgardian nobles.
