title: "Chirashi-zushi"
summary: "A dish of Eorzea."
---

**Chirashi-zushi** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Chirashi-zushi.

One of many varieties of the Hingan dish sushi, this iteration consists of a full bowl of sweet vinegared rice topped with various items such as thinly sliced omelet, salted fish eggs, dried gourd, stewed mushrooms, and other local fare. Commonly eaten at celebratory events.
