**Jerked Jhammel** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Jerked Jhammel.

Thin slices of tough jhammel meat salted and dried to preserve for long journeys.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Cumin Seeds, Dragon Pepper, Highland Oregano, Jhammel Haunch
