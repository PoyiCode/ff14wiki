**Coffee Biscuit** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Coffee Biscuit.

A buttery biscuit with a subtle coffee flavor.

Ingredients used: Coffee Beans, Garden Beet Sugar, Upland Wheat Flour
