**Beef Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Beef Stew.

A sumptuous stew made by slowly cooking buffalo meat in sour red wine for several hours until it falls off the bone.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Buffalo Sirloin, Mineral Water, Sour Red, Sunset Wheat Flour, Wild Onion
