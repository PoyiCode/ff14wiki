**Tree Toad Legs** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Tree Toad Legs.

A generous portion of battered and fried tree toad legs served with a side of snails.

Ingredients used: Allagan Snail, Garlean Garlic, Smooth Butter, Sunset Wheat Flour, Tree Toad
