**Creme Brulee** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Creme Brulee.

A vanilla-flavored egg custard drenched in a sweet syrup that is then heated with a fire crystal until a crisp brown shell is created.

Ingredients used: Birch Syrup, Okeanis Egg, Vanilla Beans, Yak Milk
