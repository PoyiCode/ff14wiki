**Angler Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Angler Stew.

A thick, creamy stew brimming with tender chunks of fish, onions, and chives.

Ingredients used: Chives, Cyclops Onion, Highland Flour, Solstice Garlic, Yak Milk
