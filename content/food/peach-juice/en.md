**Peach Juice** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Peach Juice.

A generous serving of juice, freshly squeezed from sweet peaches.

Ingredients used: Eblan Lentils, Palm Sugar, Peppermint, Potent Spice, Sykon, White Peach
