---
title: "Pineapple Ponzecake"
summary: "A dish of Eorzea."
---

**Pineapple Ponzecake** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pineapple Ponzecake.

The ponzecake earns its name from the fact that the total weight of the dry ingredients (before pineapples) equals a single ponze.
