**Pumpkin Ratatouille** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pumpkin Ratatouille.

This colorful dish consists of a variety of vegetables, sautéed in perilla oil and then simmered to enrich their combined flavor.

Ingredients used: Blood Tomato, Cucumber, Garlean Garlic, Giant Pumpkin, Perilla Oil, Potent Spice
