**Cheese Souffle** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Cheese Souffle.

A light, fluffy egg dish flavored with only the finest fragrant cheeses.

Ingredients used: Cream Cheese, Dodo Egg, Maple Syrup, Night Milk, Sun Lemon
