**Spaghetti Pescatore** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Spaghetti Pescatore.

Common fare of fisherfolk across Eorzea's southwestern coasts, the sauce used in this pasta dish consists of a wide variety of fresh local seafood—from fish to mollusk.
