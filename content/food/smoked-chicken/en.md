**Smoked Chicken** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Smoked Chicken.

A leg of chicken patiently smoked to perfection.

Ingredients used: Chicken Thigh, Creamtop Mushroom, Frantoio Oil, Lemonette, Night Pepper
