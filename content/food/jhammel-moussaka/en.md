**Jhammel Moussaka** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Jhammel Moussaka.

A Near Eastern casserole made from a spiced jhammel and eggplant mincemeat topped with creamy mashed popotoes.
