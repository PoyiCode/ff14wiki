---

**Pixie Apple Pie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pixie Apple Pie.

A flaky, buttery crust surrounds a sweet compote of stewed pixie apples flavored with a hint of cinnamon.
