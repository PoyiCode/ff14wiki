**Liver-cheese Sandwich** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Liver-cheese Sandwich.

Several slices of a ground liver loaf sandwiched between two halves of a kaiser roll.

Ingredients used: Cockatrice Thigh, Cream Cheese
