**Pork Kakuni** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pork Kakuni.

Thick slices of succulent Koshu pork slow cooked in a sweet, savory sauce. If prepared properly, the meat literally melts in your mouth.
