summary: "A dish of Eorzea."

**Steamed Staff** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Steamed Staff.

Not actually a staff, but a dish made from steaming a freshwater fish known as a retribution staff—a long, thin wavekin believed to be the cause of storms.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Star Anise
