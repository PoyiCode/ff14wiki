**Orobon Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Orobon Stew.

A hearty soup made from the liver of an orobon.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Chicken Stock, Coerthan Carrot, Orobon Liver, Sunset Wheat Flour, Sweet Cream
