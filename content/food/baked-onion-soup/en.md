**Baked Onion Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Baked Onion Soup.

A hearty bouillon soup filled with sweet onions, covered with shredded cheese and baked in an oven until golden brown.

Ingredients used: Chicken Stock, Cream Cheese, Cyclops Onion
