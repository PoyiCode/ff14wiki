**Sparkling Citrus Refreshment** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sparkling Citrus Refreshment.

Dehydrated adventurers, rejoice—this crisp, bubbly beverage boasts an invigorating citrus kick that will thoroughly quench one's thirst on the road. Guaranteed to make even the most arduous trials feel like a tropical vacation.
