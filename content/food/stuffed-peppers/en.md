**Stuffed Peppers** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Stuffed Peppers.

Each half of the bell pepper is packed with finely shredded alpaca meat and other fillings, then roasted until tender and lightly browned.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Alpaca Fillet, Bell Pepper, Turali Corn Oil, Ut'ohmu Chili Sauce
