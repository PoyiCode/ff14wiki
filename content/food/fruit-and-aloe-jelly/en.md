**Fruit and Aloe Jelly** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Fruit and Aloe Jelly.

This gelatinous dessert is flavored with the mouthwatering sour kick of branchbearer fruits.

Ingredients used: Branchbearer Fruit, Gelatin, Mesquite Beans, Ovibos Milk, Royal Maple Syrup, Turali Aloe
