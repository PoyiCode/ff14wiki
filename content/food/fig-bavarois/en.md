**Fig Bavarois** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Fig Bavarois.

A ripe Old World fig encased in a lightly sweetened jelly.

Ingredients used: Gelatin, Maple Sugar, Old World Fig, Yak Milk
