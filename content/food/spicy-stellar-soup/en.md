**Spicy Stellar Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Spicy Stellar Soup.

Simmered with diced alien onions and carrots of happiness aplenty, this stellar soup's flavor is quite literally out of this world.
