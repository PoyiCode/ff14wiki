**Cornbread** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Cornbread.

A fluffy yellow bread made from cornmeal. A specialty at the Drowning Wench in Limsa Lominsa.

Ingredients used: Buffalo Milk, Chicken Egg, Cornmeal, Mineral Water, Olive Oil, Sunset Wheat Flour
