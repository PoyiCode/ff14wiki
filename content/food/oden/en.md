**Oden** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Oden.

A veritable hodgepodge of vegetables, meats, and seafood simmered in a light fish broth for literally days upon end.

Ingredients used: Cooking Sake, Daikon Radish, Gagana Egg, Mountain Popoto, Ruby Tide Kelp, Secret Recipe Broth
