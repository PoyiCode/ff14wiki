**Pea Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pea Soup.

An emerald green soup made from mashed jade peas.

Ingredients used: Dodo Tenderloin, Jade Peas, Mineral Water, Table Salt, Wild Onion
