**Broccoli and Spinach Saute** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Broccoli and Spinach Saute.

A medley of healthy vegetables, stir-fried in corn oil until soft. May be served on its own or atop a bowl of rice.
