**Caviar Canapes** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Caviar Canapes.

Perennial party favorites, these hors d'oeuvres feature delightfully bite-sized bread topped with cheese, vegetables, and salted caviar sturgeon eggs.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Blood Tomato, Garlean Cheese, Upland Wheat Flour
