**Mugwort Carp** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mugwort Carp.

This Velodyna carp has been covered with a copious amount of fragrant mugwort before being roasted over an open flame.

Ingredients used: Mugwort, Table Salt
