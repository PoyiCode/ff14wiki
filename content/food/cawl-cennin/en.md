**Cawl Cennin** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Cawl Cennin.

Soup made from leeks and onions sautéed in butter and mixed with chicken stock.

Ingredients used: Chicken Stock, Mineral Water, Salt Leek, Smooth Butter, Sweet Cream, Wild Onion
