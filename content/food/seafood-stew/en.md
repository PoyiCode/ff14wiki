**Seafood Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Seafood Stew.

A simple stew made from available seafood boiled with tomatoes and spices.

Ingredients used: Dravanian Paprika, Dzemael Tomato
