title: "Dzemael Gratin"

**Dzemael Gratin** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Dzemael Gratin.

Passed down through generations by the Dzemael family, this dish consists of thinly sliced popotoes and eft tails baked in a creamy white sauce.
