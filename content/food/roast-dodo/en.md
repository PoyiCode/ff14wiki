**Roast Dodo** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Roast Dodo.

A whole dodo slow-roasted over an open fire and stuffed with a savory blend of bread and nuts. Often used in year-end ceremonies as an offering to the Twelve.
