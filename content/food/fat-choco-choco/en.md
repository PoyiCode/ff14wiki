title: "Fat Choco Choco"
summary: "A dish of Eorzea."
---

**Fat Choco Choco** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Fat Choco Choco.

The name of this popular sweet is short for either “Fat Chocobo Chocolate” or “Fat Chocolate Chocobo”—no one quite remembers which.
