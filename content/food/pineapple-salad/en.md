**Pineapple Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pineapple Salad.

A salad featuring chunks of golden pineapple tossed with a flavorsome frantoio oil dressing.

Ingredients used: Blood Tomato, Frantoio Oil, Golden Pineapple, La Noscean Lettuce, Lemonette, Purple Carrot
