---

**Blood Tomato Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Blood Tomato Salad.

The dark vinegar topping this salad draws out the flavors of each fresh vegetable without overpowering them. Unless you have poured too much on, that is.
