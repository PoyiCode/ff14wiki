**Morel Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Morel Salad.

A fresh green salad topped with sautéed morels.

Ingredients used: Emerald Beans, La Noscean Lettuce, Morel, Olive Oil, Sesame Seeds, Young Cieldalaes Spinach
