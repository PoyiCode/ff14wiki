**Skyr** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Skyr.

A dairy dish created through a special Sharlayan fermentation process passed down for generations.

Ingredients used: Ovibos Milk, Palm Sugar, Peppermint
