**Mashed Popotoes** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mashed Popotoes.

Boiled popotoes, mashed into a smooth, creamy paste. This dish, originating in the New World, is best with a generous dollop of salty butter.

Ingredients used: Cottage Cheese, Garlean Garlic, Popoto, Smooth Butter, Sweet Cream
