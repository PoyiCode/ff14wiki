**Solterito** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Solterito.

A satisfyingly simple salad of soft cheese and tender beans.

Ingredients used: Cooking Mezcal, Eblan Lentils, Frantoio Oil, Queso Fresco, Ut'ohmu Tomato
