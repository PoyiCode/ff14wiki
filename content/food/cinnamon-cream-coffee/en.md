**Cinnamon Cream Coffee** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Cinnamon Cream Coffee.

The bittersweet flavor of this coffee is complemented by a cinnamon aroma and creamy topping.

Ingredients used: Cinnamon, Palm Sugar, Whipped Cream, Wild Coffee Beans
