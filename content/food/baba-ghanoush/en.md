**Baba Ghanoush** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Baba Ghanoush.

A traditional Thavnairian appetizer of finely chopped dark eggplant spiced with ground cumin and a tantalizing hint of garlic.

Ingredients used: Algae Salt, Dark Eggplant, Garlean Garlic, Perilla Oil, Thavnairian Paprika
