**Salmon Jerky** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Salmon Jerky.

Filleted salmon submerged in brine, marinated in smoky mezcal, then dried for several hours until chewy.

Ingredients used: Cooking Mezcal, Mountain Salt
