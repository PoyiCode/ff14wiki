**Dodo Omelette** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Dodo Omelette.

A traditional Plainsfolk dish made from deftly whisked dodo eggs fried in butter and expertly formed into a soft, fluffy mountain.

Ingredients used: Aldgoat Milk, Dodo Egg, Highland Parsley, Smooth Butter, Table Salt, Tomato Sauce
