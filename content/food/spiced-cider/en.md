**Spiced Cider** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Spiced Cider.

A warmed jug filled with piquant apple juice mulled with zested ginger and cloves, complete with a stick of cinnamon.

Ingredients used: Cinnamon, Cloves, Golden Apple, Pearl Ginger, Vanilla Beans
