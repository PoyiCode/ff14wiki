**Cheese Risotto** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Cheese Risotto.

Sticky rice doused in a rich cheese sauce and simmered over low heat until it reaches just the right consistency.

Ingredients used: Alpine Parsnip, Cottage Cheese, Sticky Rice, Wild Onion
