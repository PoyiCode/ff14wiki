**Rroneek Steak** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Rroneek Steak.

A large slab of lightly marinated rroneek chuck, pan-seared to perfection.

Ingredients used: Dragon Pepper, Mountain Salt, Night Vinegar, Rroneek Chuck, Turali Corn Oil, Yyasulani Garlic
