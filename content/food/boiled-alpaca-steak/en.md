**Boiled Alpaca Steak** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Boiled Alpaca Steak.

Premium alpaca meat stewed to perfection in a tangy mezcal and lemon sauce.

Ingredients used: Alpaca Fillet, Cooking Mezcal, Galago Mint, Lemonette, Northern Sea Salt
