**Salted Thavnairian Cod** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Salted Thavnairian Cod.

A whole shallows cod, broiled and sprinkled with salt to bring out its fishy flavor.

Ingredients used: Lemonette, Northern Sea Salt
