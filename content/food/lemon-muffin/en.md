**Lemon Muffin** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Lemon Muffin.

A sweet and zesty muffin made by mixing lemonette rind and juice into the dough.

Ingredients used: Cardamom, Fermented Butter, Lemonette, Lorikeet Egg, Upland Wheat Flour, Yak Milk
