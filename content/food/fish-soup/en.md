**Fish Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Fish Soup.

A light soup of butter-sautéed whitefish and vegetables.

Ingredients used: Highland Parsley, Nutmeg, Ruby Tomato, Smooth Butter, Wild Onion
