**Priestly Omelette** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Priestly Omelette.

While similar to a regular omelette, this recipe perfected in the Vault's kitchens calls for a fistful of shredded cheese and mushrooms to further enhance the dish's richness.

Ingredients used: Brown Mushroom, Heavensegg, Highland Parsley, Stone Cheese, Tomato Sauce, White Truffle
