**Sermon-worthy Meuniere** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sermon-worthy Meuniere.

A thick fillet of salmon breaded with flour and fried in rich butter. So succulent, it is said a certain Othardian church has deemed it holy. Which church, no one seems to recall.

Ingredients used: Crimson Pepper, Fermented Butter, Gyr Abanian Flour, Heavens Lemon, Young Cieldalaes Spinach
