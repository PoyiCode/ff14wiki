**Stuffed Highland Cabbage** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Stuffed Highland Cabbage.

This traditional dish of minced meat seasoned and packed inside leafy highland cabbage, then stewed in a light broth, is sure to warm you up on the coldest of nights.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Blood Tomato, Frantoio Oil, Highland Cabbage, Mist Spinach, Ovim Meat
