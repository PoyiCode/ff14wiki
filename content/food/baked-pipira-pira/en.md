**Baked Pipira Pira** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Baked Pipira Pira.

A plump pipira pira wrapped with butter and porcini mushrooms and baked in an oven, the steam trapped in the leaf slowly cooking the fish to the perfect flakiness.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Fermented Butter, Porcini
