**Tuna Miq'abob** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Tuna Miq'abob.

A traditional Seeker of the Sun dish consisting of light, flaky tuna and ripe peppers on a stick, roasted to perfection.

Ingredients used: Midland Basil, Olive Oil, Sun Lemon, Table Salt
