**Peach Tart** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Peach Tart.

A round pastry filled with sweet peaches and fragrant spices.

Ingredients used: Dark Rye Flour, Egg of Elpis, Ovibos Milk, Potent Spice, White Peach
