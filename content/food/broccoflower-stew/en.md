**Broccoflower Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Broccoflower Stew.

Broccoflower slices boiled in a savory broth alongside tender poultry and root vegetables.

Ingredients used: Bird of Elpis Breast, Broccoflower, Carrot of Happiness, Northern Sea Salt, Upland Wheat Flour
