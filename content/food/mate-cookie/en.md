**Mate Cookie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mate Cookie.

Splendid mate leaves are kneaded into the dough before baking to create these wonderfully fragrant cookies.

Ingredients used: Cinnamon, Dark Rye Flour, Egg of Elpis, Splendid Mate Leaves, Whipped Cream
