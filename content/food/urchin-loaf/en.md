**Urchin Loaf** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Urchin Loaf.

Sea urchin corals mixed with mushrooms and herbs into a paste that is then placed back into the urchin shell and baked until hard.

Ingredients used: Morel, Okeanis Egg, Sour Cream
