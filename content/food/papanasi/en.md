**Papanasi** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Papanasi.

Thick dough kneaded with soft cheese and fried into a hearty donut, slathered with yet more creamy cheese and a sweet-and-tart berry sauce.

Ingredients used: Cottonseed Oil, Gagana Egg, Gyr Abanian Flour, Reunion Cheese, Rolanberry, Sour Cream
