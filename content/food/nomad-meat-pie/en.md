**Nomad Meat Pie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Nomad Meat Pie.

Ground dzo chuck and various steppe vegetables wrapped in buttery dough and deep-fried in oil.

Ingredients used: Algae Salt, Cyclops Onion, Dzo Chuck, Gyr Abanian Flour, Oriental Soy Sauce, Sun Cabbage
