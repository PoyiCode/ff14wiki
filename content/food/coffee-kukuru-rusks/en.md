**Coffee Kukuru Rusks** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Coffee Kukuru Rusks.

These delectable biscuits are made by soaking kukuru chocolate dough in coffee, then sprinkling it with sugar and baking it.

Ingredients used: Dark Rye, Mountain Salt, Ovibos Milk, Sweet Kukuru Bean, Wild Coffee Beans
