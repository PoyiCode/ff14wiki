**Chawan-mushi** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Chawan-mushi.

Whisked egg mixed with fish broth, meats, and seasonal vegetables, then steamed until hardened into a savory pudding.

Ingredients used: Gagana Egg, Secret Recipe Broth, Shiitake Mushroom
