**Aldgoat Steak** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Aldgoat Steak.

A traditional dish of the Highland Hyur consisting of a thick cut of aldgoat chuck charred over an open flame.

Ingredients used: Aldgoat Chuck, Garlean Garlic, Table Salt
