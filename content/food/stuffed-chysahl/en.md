**Stuffed Chysahl** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Stuffed Chysahl.

Ground okeanis stuffed into a large chysahl head and sprinkled with fresh chives.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Chives, Chysahl Greens, Cream Cheese, Hollandaise Sauce, Okeanis Tail
