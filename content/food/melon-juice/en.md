**Melon Juice** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Melon Juice.

Paralyzingly delicious juice freshly squeezed from thundermelons.

Ingredients used: Elder Nutmeg Seeds, Garden Beet Sugar, Palm Sugar, Thundermelon
