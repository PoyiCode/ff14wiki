**Tandoori Raptor** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Tandoori Raptor.

A Near Eastern staple consisting of tender chunks of baked raptor meat marinated in mouthwatering spices and creamy yoghurt.
