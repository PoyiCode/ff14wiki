**Farmer's Breakfast** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Farmer's Breakfast.

This simple-yet-delightful dish of popotoes and lightly scrambled eggs will fill you up before a day of tilling the fields.
