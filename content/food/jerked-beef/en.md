**Jerked Beef** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Jerked Beef.

A thick strip of lean buffalo meat dried in the sun.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Buffalo Sirloin, Nutmeg, Sagolii Sage, Table Salt
