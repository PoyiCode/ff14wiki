**Shrimp Cocktail** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Shrimp Cocktail.

Deveined shrimp poached in court bouillon broth and served with cocktail sauce for dipping. The tartness of the lemon helps to balance the spice.

Ingredients used: Cooking Mezcal, Lemonette, Ut'ohmu Chili Sauce
