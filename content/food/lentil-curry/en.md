**Lentil Curry** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Lentil Curry.

A dish that combines lentils with a sumptuous blend of spices in a silky coconut milk stew.

Ingredients used: Bomba Rice, Carrot of Happiness, Coconut Milk, Eblan Lentils, Elder Nutmeg Seeds, Upland Wheat Flour
