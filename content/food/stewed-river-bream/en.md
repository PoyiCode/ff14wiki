**Stewed River Bream** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Stewed River Bream.

River bream topped in a sauce made by thickening the broth in which the fish was stewed.

Ingredients used: Cottonseed Oil, Doman Eggplant, Kudzu Root, Sun Cabbage
