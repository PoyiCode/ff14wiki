**Broad Bean Curry** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Broad Bean Curry.

Broad beans stewed with fragrant spices, served with a side of steamed rice.

Ingredients used: Bomba Rice, Broad Beans, Cardamom, Fermented Butter, Upland Wheat Flour
