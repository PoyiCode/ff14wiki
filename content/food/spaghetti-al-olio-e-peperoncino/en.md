**Spaghetti al Olio e Peperoncino** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Spaghetti al Olio e Peperoncino.

This classic pasta dish uses two types of oil to bring out the spice of the dragon peppers.

Ingredients used: Dragon Pepper, Frantoio Oil, Turali Corn Oil, Upland Wheat Flour
