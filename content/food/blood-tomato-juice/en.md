**Blood Tomato Juice** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Blood Tomato Juice.

Ripe, pulpy tomatoes have been crushed and mixed with fresh fruit juice to create this blood red beverage.

Ingredients used: Blood Tomato, Golden Honey, Heavens Lemon
