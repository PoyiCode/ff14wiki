**Heavenly Eggnog** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Heavenly Eggnog.

A decadently creamy concoction crafted from raw eggs, thick cream, honey, and—if you are feeling adventurous—a healthy splash of a dark spiced rum. A favorite in Ishgard's Vault.

Ingredients used: Birch Syrup, Cinnamon, Heavensegg, Nutmeg, Sweet Cream, Sweet Cream Milk
