**Apkallu Omelette** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Apkallu Omelette.

Apkallu eggs beaten and then cooked in rich butter until fluffy and light. A favorite in Vylbrand's coastal hamlets.
