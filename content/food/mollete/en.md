**Mollete** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mollete.

Slices of crispy bread topped with a generous heap of fresh tomatoes and molten cheese.

Ingredients used: Bell Pepper, Garlean Cheese, Queso Fresco, Ut'ohmu Chili Sauce, Ut'ohmu Tomato
