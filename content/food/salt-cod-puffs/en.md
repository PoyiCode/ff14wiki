**Salt Cod Puffs** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Salt Cod Puffs.

Balls of ground salt cod dipped in batter and fried in oil.

Ingredients used: Apkallu Egg, Clove Oil, Nutmeg, Sunset Wheat Flour, Thyme
