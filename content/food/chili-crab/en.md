**Chili Crab** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Chili Crab.

A whole blue crab braised in its shell in a piquant sauce, which takes on a rich shellfish flavor in the course of cooking.

Ingredients used: Blood Tomato, Frantoio Oil, Jhammel Ginger
