**Turali Pineapple Ponzecake** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Turali Pineapple Ponzecake.

The centerpiece of any Yok Tural festival is this striking ponzecake decorated with glazed pineapple rings to symbolize the sun.
