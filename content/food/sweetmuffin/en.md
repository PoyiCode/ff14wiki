**Sweetmuffin** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sweetmuffin.

Kukuru bean powder has been mixed into the batter for even more sugary sweetness.

Ingredients used: Dark Rye Flour, Egg of Elpis, Ovibos Milk, Royal Maple Syrup, Sweet Kukuru Bean
