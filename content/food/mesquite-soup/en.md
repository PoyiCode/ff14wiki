**Mesquite Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mesquite Soup.

An especially nutrient-rich soup, chock-full of diced popoto and smoky mesquite beans.

Ingredients used: Frantoio Oil, Giant Popoto, Mesquite Beans, Turali Corn Oil, White Pepper
