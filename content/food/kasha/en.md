**Kasha** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Kasha.

Buckwheat kernels boiled in bouillon to form a soft, hearty porridge.

Ingredients used: Bouillon Cube, Buckwheat Kernels, Cyclops Onion, Gagana Egg, Mist Dill
