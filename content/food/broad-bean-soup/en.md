**Broad Bean Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Broad Bean Soup.

A rich, flavorful soup of stewed broad beans and juicy chunks of rail meat.

Ingredients used: Broad Beans, Buffalo Beans, Rail Tenderloin
