**Piennolo Tomato Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Piennolo Tomato Salad.

A bright tomato salad lightly tossed in perilla oil.

Ingredients used: Iceberg Lettuce, Night Vinegar, Perilla Oil, Piennolo Tomato, Thavnairian Paprika
