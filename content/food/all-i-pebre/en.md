**All i Pebre** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: All i Pebre.

Bite-sized pieces of eel and popoto stewed in a spiced sauce of Thavnairian paprika.

Ingredients used: Giant Popoto, Mountain Salt, Perilla Oil, Thavnairian Paprika
