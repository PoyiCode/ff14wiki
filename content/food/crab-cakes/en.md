**Crab Cakes** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Crab Cakes.

To preserve the toothsome texture of blue crab, chunks of flesh are gently folded into flour and molded into cakes, which are panfried to golden perfection.

Ingredients used: Cyclops Onion, Lorikeet Egg, Upland Wheat Flour
