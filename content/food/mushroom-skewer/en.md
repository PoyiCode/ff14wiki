**Mushroom Skewer** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mushroom Skewer.

The creamtop mushrooms take center stage in this simple yet filling dish, though the blood tomatoes and ramhorn zucchinis perform admirably in supporting roles.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Amber Cloves, Blood Tomato, Creamtop Mushroom, Ramhorn Zucchini
