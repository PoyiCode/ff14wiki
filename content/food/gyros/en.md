**Gyros** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Gyros.

A slice of slow-cooked loaghtan meat wrapped in leafy greens and tucked into a pocket of freshly baked bread.

Ingredients used: Iceberg Lettuce, Northern Sea Salt, Ovibos Milk, Prime Loaghtan Rump, Upland Wheat Flour
