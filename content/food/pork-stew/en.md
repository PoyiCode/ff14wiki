**Pork Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pork Stew.

This Far Eastern take on a campsite mainstay features thinly sliced pork belly and various root vegetables in a piping hot miso-based soup.

Ingredients used: Daikon Radish, Fish Stock, Gyr Abanian Carrot, Koshu Pork, Oriental Miso Paste
