**Rabbit Pie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Rabbit Pie.

A traditional pastry dish of the Wildwood Elezen formed to resemble a rabbit, but not necessarily containing any meat of leporine origin.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Coerthan Carrot, Marmot Meat, Pie Dough, Wild Onion
