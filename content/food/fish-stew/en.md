**Fish Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Fish Stew.

A salty broth containing balls of finely ground fish paste. A common dish in Far Eastern coastal settlements.

Ingredients used: Algae Salt, Green Leek, Mountain Popoto
