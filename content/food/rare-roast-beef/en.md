**Rare Roast Beef** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Rare Roast Beef.

The finest quality dzo, seared lightly to add a note of smokiness to the still-bleeding meat's naturally savory flavor, and topped with a drizzle of salty au jus.

Ingredients used: Dzo Tenderloin, Fennel, Frantoio Oil, Gyr Abanian Carrot, Night Pepper, Peppermint
