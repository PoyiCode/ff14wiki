**Giant Popoto Pancakes** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Giant Popoto Pancakes.

Giant popotoes are mashed, mixed with eggs, then cooked to create these light and fluffy treats.

Ingredients used: Egg of Elpis, Elder Nutmeg Seeds, Giant Popoto, Golden Honey, Northern Sea Salt, Upland Wheat Flour
