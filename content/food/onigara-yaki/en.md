**Onigara-yaki** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Onigara-yaki.

A lobster split in half lengthwise, slathered with a heavily seasoned sauce, and grilled in its shell over an open flame.

Ingredients used: Cooking Sake, Oriental Miso Paste, Oriental Soy Sauce
