**Deep-fried Okeanis** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Deep-fried Okeanis.

Thinly sliced okeanis fried in oil.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Dalamud Popoto, Highland Flour, Okeanis Tail, Olive Oil
