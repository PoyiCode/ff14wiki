**Mesquite Juice** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mesquite Juice.

The juice of mesquite beans, blended with local fruits for a health drink that tastes great.

Ingredients used: Mesquite Beans, Nopalitender Tuna, Palm Sugar, Turali Pineapple, Wild Ja Tiika Bananas
