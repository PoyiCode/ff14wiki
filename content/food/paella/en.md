**Paella** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Paella.

A hearty dish of seafood and rice infused with spices and enriched by a hint of wood smoke from the open fire over which it was cooked.

Ingredients used: Bomba Rice, Frantoio Oil, Heavens Lemon, Paprika
