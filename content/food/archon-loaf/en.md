---

**Archon Loaf** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Archon Loaf.

Baked with either fish or vegetable flour, this fluffy loaf of rye bread is—from a nutritional standpoint—wholesome indeed.
