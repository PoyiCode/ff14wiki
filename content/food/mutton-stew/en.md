**Mutton Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mutton Stew.

A hearty stew made from lean mutton loin and a selection of freshly picked vegetables.

Ingredients used: Coerthan Carrot, Mineral Water, Mutton Loin, Sunset Wheat Flour, Wild Onion
