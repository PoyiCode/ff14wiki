**Sweet Gnomefish** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sweet Gnomefish.

A gnomefish boiled in sweet birch syrup. The glaze preserves the fish for days after cooking.

Ingredients used: Birch Syrup
