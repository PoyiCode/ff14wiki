**Chilled Popoto Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Chilled Popoto Soup.

A refreshing soup made by blending popoto, leek, and sweet cream to a smooth consistency.

Ingredients used: Highland Parsley, La Noscean Leek, Popoto, Smooth Butter, Sweet Cream, Table Salt
