**Vegetable Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Vegetable Soup.

Packed with tender veggies, this soup is comforting and nutritious in equal measure.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Broccoli, Carrot of Happiness, Giant Popoto, Mountain Salt, Yyasulani Garlic
