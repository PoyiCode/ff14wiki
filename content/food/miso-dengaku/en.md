**Miso Dengaku** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Miso Dengaku.

Stiff tofu squares and vegetables impaled on a stick and slathered in a sweet miso sauce then roasted over hot coals.

Ingredients used: Beet Sugar, Cooking Sake, Daikon Radish, Doman Eggplant, Firm Tofu, Oriental Miso Paste
