**Button Mushroom Saute** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Button Mushroom Saute.

Freshly picked button mushrooms sautéed in olive oil and garlic.

Ingredients used: Button Mushroom, Garlean Garlic, Olive Oil, Sagolii Sage, Table Salt
