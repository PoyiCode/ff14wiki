**Caviar Sandwich** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Caviar Sandwich.

Its every bite extraordinary enough to render one speechless, this sandwich incorporates eggs from the caviar sturgeon and bird of Elpis both. Given the further inclusion of alligator pear slices, one cannot help but wonder at its inventor's obvious preference for ovoid foodstuffs.

Ingredients used: Alligator Pear, Egg of Elpis, Northern Sea Salt, Upland Wheat Flour
