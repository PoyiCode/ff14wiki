**Kingcake** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Kingcake.

A festive ring-shaped cake baked from brioche dough.

Ingredients used: Birch Syrup, Highland Flour, Okeanis Egg, Yak Milk
