**Caramel Popcorn** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Caramel Popcorn.

A moreish corn treat slathered in rich caramel sauce. Made by heating corn kernels until they expand, young and old delight in the pop and crackle made by the blooming kernels.

Ingredients used: Fermented Butter, Flint Corn, Ovibos Milk, Palm Sugar, Turali Corn Oil
