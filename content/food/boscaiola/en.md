**Boscaiola** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Boscaiola.

A hearty dish of chewy noodles, heaped with a generous portion of seasonal mushrooms and drowned in tomato sauce.

Ingredients used: Cyclops Onion, Mature Olive Oil, Morel, Porcini, Tomato Sauce, Vermicelli
