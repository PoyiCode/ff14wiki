**Triple Cream Coffee** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Triple Cream Coffee.

Freshly roasted ground coffee beans percolated to perfection and mixed with an equal amount of thick dairy cream.

Ingredients used: Cinnamon, Maple Sugar, Mineral Water, Roasted Coffee Beans, Sweet Cream
