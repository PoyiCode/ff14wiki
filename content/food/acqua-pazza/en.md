**Acqua Pazza** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Acqua Pazza.

Fresh fish and mollusks sautéed in frantoio oil, then simmered in a mezcal and tomato broth. Originally invented by fisherfolk who would come home from the markets and make soup using whatever remained of their haul.

Ingredients used: Cooking Mezcal, Frantoio Oil, Ut'ohmu Tomato
