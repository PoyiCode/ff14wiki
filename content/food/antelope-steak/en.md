**Antelope Steak** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Antelope Steak.

A slice of lean antelope meat charred on all sides.

Ingredients used: Antelope Shank, Garlean Garlic, Table Salt
