**Creamy Salmon Pasta** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Creamy Salmon Pasta.

Filling homemade pasta topped with tender, flaky salmon and fresh spinach, all smothered in a rich, creamy sauce.

Ingredients used: Frantoio Oil, Mist Spinach, Night Pepper, Sweet Cream, Vermicelli
