**Boiled Lobster** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Boiled Lobster.

This dish is made by giving the lobster a nice, long, scalding bath and then garnishing it with lemon and salt. A simple process that brings out the flavor of the main ingredient.

Ingredients used: Lemonette, Mountain Salt, Northern Sea Salt
