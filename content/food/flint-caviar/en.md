**Flint Caviar** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Flint Caviar.

Salted eggs of the rare emperor fish. Often referred to as black diamonds, the eggs are a delicacy that can only be afforded by the wealthiest of connoisseurs.
