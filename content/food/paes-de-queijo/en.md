**Paes de Queijo** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Paes de Queijo.

A handful of sumptuously soft cheese-filled bread rolls.

Ingredients used: Cassava, Egg of Elpis, Garlean Cheese, Mountain Salt, Ovibos Milk
