**Parsnip Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Parsnip Salad.

A salad made with fried parsnip slices and garnished with a drizzle of fragrant olive oil.

Ingredients used: Alpine Parsnip, Buffalo Beans, Cieldalaes Spinach, La Noscean Lettuce, Mineral Water, Olive Oil
