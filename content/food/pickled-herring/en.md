**Pickled Herring** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pickled Herring.

Succulent chunks of platinum herring marinated in vinegar and topped with finely sliced lemonette to give it a hint of tang.

Ingredients used: Curly Parsley, Cyclops Onion, Frantoio Oil, Lemonette, Paprika
