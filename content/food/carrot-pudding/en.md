**Carrot Pudding** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Carrot Pudding.

To freeze, shred, steam, and bake carrots into this smooth pudding requires both time and skill, but the mouthwatering results are well worth the effort.

Ingredients used: Egg of Elpis, Garden Beet Sugar, Ovibos Milk, Palm Sugar, Sunset Carrot
