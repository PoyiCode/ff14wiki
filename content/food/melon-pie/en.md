summary: "A dish of Eorzea."
---

**Melon Pie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Melon Pie.

The flaky crust serves as a perfect complement to the thundermelon's sweet-yet-electrifying flesh.

Ingredients used: Dark Rye Flour, Egg of Elpis, Fermented Butter, Lemonette, Thundermelon
