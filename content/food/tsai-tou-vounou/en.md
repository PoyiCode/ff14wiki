**Tsai tou Vounou** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Tsai tou Vounou.

A herbal drink with impressive medicinal properties. Sometimes called “mountain tea.”

Ingredients used: Cinnamon, Lemonette, Palm Sugar, Sideritis Leaves
