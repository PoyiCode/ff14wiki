**Ovim Meatballs** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Ovim Meatballs.

Savory, round chunks of minced ovim meat accented with a lingonberry sauce that's sweet yet tart.

Ingredients used: Frantoio Oil, Lingonberry, Ovim Meat, Russet Popoto, Yak Milk
