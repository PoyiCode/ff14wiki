**Amra Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Amra Salad.

This salad, tossed with light dressing and filled with chopped amra fruit, is a sweet and savory treat.

Ingredients used: Amra, Blood Tomato, Iceberg Lettuce, Thavnairian Paprika
