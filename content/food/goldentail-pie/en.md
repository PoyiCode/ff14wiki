**Goldentail Pie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Goldentail Pie.

Shockingly delicious eel baked in a flaky crust.

Ingredients used: Giant Popoto, Upland Wheat Flour, Ut'ohmu Chili Sauce, White Pepper
