**Pixieberry Cheesecake** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pixieberry Cheesecake.

Rich, indulgent cheesecake swirled through with a ribbon of sweet pixieberry sauce.

Ingredients used: Garden Beet Sugar, Gelatin, Lemonette, Pixieberry, Sour Cream, Yak Milk
