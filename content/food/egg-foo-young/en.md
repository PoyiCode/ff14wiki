**Egg Foo Young** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Egg Foo Young.

Succulent crab legs folded into a fluffy omelette and topped with a thick, savory sauce.

Ingredients used: Bamboo Shoot, Fish Stock, Gagana Egg, Kudzu Root, Shiitake Mushroom
