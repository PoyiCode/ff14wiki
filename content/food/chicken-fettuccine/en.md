**Chicken Fettuccine** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Chicken Fettuccine.

Flat, thick pasta smothered in a rich cream sauce with tender slices of chicken thigh.

Ingredients used: Chicken Thigh, Curly Parsley, Frantoio Oil, Peppermint, Sweet Cream, Vermicelli
