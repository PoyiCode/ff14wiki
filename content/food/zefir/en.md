**Zefir** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Zefir.

An irresistibly soft and airy confectionary made from whipped twilight apple purée.

Ingredients used: Garden Beet Sugar, Gelatin, Harcot, Lorikeet Egg, Peppermint, Twilight Apple
