**Frozen Spirits** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Frozen Spirits.

A cool concoction made by fermenting cloud bananas and Old World figs, freezing them with ice crystals, then mashing and mixing the fruit into a slush-like consistency.
