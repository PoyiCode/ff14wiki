**Almond Cream Croissant** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Almond Cream Croissant.

A flaky crescent-shaped pastry sliced in two and buttered with a sweet creamy paste made from almonds.

Ingredients used: Birch Syrup, Fermented Butter, Highland Flour, Honeydew Almonds
