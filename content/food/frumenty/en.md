**Frumenty** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Frumenty.

A tasty gruel of cracked wheat, raisins, and spices boiled in aldgoat milk.

Ingredients used: Aldgoat Milk, Cinnamon, Honey, Sunset Wheat
