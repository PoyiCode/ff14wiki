**Baked Sole** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Baked Sole.

A thin slice of oven-baked black sole smothered in a gil bun sauce.

Ingredients used: Cieldalaes Spinach, Gil Bun, Table Salt
