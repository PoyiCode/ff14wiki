**Spinach Saute** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Spinach Saute.

A simple dish consisting of Cieldalaes spinach sautéed in rich salted butter.

Ingredients used: Button Mushroom, Cieldalaes Spinach, Mineral Water, Smooth Butter, Table Salt
