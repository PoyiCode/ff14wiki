**Mist Spinach Saute** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mist Spinach Saute.

Fresh, flavorful mist spinach tossed in creamy butter until tender, topped with a drizzle of vinegar.

Ingredients used: Fermented Butter, Mist Spinach, Night Pepper, Night Vinegar
