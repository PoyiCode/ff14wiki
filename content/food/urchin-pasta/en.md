**Urchin Pasta** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Urchin Pasta.

Pasta simmered in cream sauce and topped with a generous portion of king urchin.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Coconut Milk, Garlean Garlic, Vermicelli
