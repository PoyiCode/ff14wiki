**Carrot Nibbles** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Carrot Nibbles.

Ribbons of thinly sliced carrots of happiness dressed with vinegar make for a healthful and blissfully refreshing dish.

Ingredients used: Carrot of Happiness, Cucumber, Cyclops Onion, Night Vinegar
