**Nut Bake** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Nut Bake.

A veritable sackful of luscious seeds and nuts went into this sweet and tasty pastry.

Ingredients used: Almonds, Golden Honey, Gridanian Walnut, Lorikeet Egg, Upland Wheat Flour, Yak Milk
