**Royal Eggs** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Royal Eggs.

A breakfast dish consisting of an Ishgardian muffin sliced into halves and topped with salmon, a poached egg, and a creamy yellow sauce made from butter and egg yolks.

Ingredients used: Hollandaise Sauce, Okeanis Egg
