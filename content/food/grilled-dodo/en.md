**Grilled Dodo** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Grilled Dodo.

A lean dodo tenderloin grilled over an open flame.

Ingredients used: Dodo Tenderloin, Garlean Garlic, Table Salt
