**Wildwood Scrambled Eggs** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Wildwood Scrambled Eggs.

Fragrant mushrooms sautéed in a generous pat of creamery butter and served upon a bed of fluffy eggs, scrambled to perfection.
