**Popoto Potage** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Popoto Potage.

A thick, creamy soup made with fresh rroneek milk and plenty of popotoes.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Frantoio Oil, Giant Popoto, Mountain Salt, Rroneek Milk
