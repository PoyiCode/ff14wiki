**Mejillones al Ajillo** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mejillones al Ajillo.

A sumptuous serving of delectable mussels smothered in an oily garlic sauce, each one a perfectly bite-sized burst of flavor.
