**Tacos Al Pastor** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Tacos Al Pastor.

Handmade flour tortillas filled with sautéed alpaca meat and garnished with a spicy pineapple salsa.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Alpaca Fillet, Dark Rye Flour, Lemonette, Turali Pineapple, Ut'ohmu Chili Sauce
