**Crumpet** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Crumpet.

A savory type of leavened bread, often doused in butter and honey and eaten with a cup of piping hot tea. A specialty at the Quicksand in Ul'dah.
