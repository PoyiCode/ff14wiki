**Broad Bean Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Broad Bean Salad.

This nourishing salad of broad beans, purple carrots, and lettuce is dressed lightly with frantoio oil and parsley.

Ingredients used: Broad Beans, Curly Parsley, Frantoio Oil, La Noscean Lettuce, Purple Carrot
