**Baked Alien Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Baked Alien Soup.

Alien onions are sautéed, simmered in a savory bouillon, then topped with crispy cheese for a simple yet satisfying meal.
