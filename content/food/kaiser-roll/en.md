**Kaiser Roll** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Kaiser Roll.

A round Ishgardian bread first made by Halonic monastics who, when brewing ale, would add barm and leftover malt to their daily loaves.

Ingredients used: Highland Flour, Sesame Seeds, Yak Milk
