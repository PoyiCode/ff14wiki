**Philosopher's Sandwich** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Philosopher's Sandwich.

Perfectly nutritionally balanced, this handheld dish is a busy Sharlayan researcher's best friend.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Cucumber, Dark Rye Flour, Egg of Elpis, Iceberg Lettuce
