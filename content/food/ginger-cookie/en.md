**Ginger Cookie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Ginger Cookie.

A sweet and spicy cookie cut into the shape of a legendary black mage said to have recovered MP by eating the crispy treats.

Ingredients used: Chicken Egg, Maple Sugar, Pearl Ginger, Smooth Butter, Sunset Wheat Flour
