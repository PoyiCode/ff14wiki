**Bacon Broth** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Bacon Broth.

Though technically a soup, this Ixali dish consists mainly of large slabs of smoked bacon boiled with several other lesser ingredients.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Garlean Garlic, La Noscean Leek, Smoked Bacon
