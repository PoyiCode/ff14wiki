**Chicken Pasta** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Chicken Pasta.

A hearty pasta dish topped with finely chopped chicken and melting cheese.

Ingredients used: Cooking Mezcal, Garlean Cheese, Rumpless Chicken, Vermicelli, Yyasulani Garlic
