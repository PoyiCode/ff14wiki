**Popotoes au Gratin** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Popotoes au Gratin.

Though the wonderfully flavorsome russet popotoes are undoubtedly the star attraction, it is arguably the cream cheese that steals the show with its distinctive flavor.

Ingredients used: Cream Cheese, Cyclops Onion, Night Pepper, Russet Popoto, Steppe Milk, Upland Wheat Flour
