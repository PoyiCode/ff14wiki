**Eft Steak** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Eft Steak.

A thick slab of gristly eft meat charred to an abyssal black.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Cider Vinegar, Eft Tail, Garlean Garlic, Laurel, Sour Red
