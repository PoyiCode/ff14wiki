**Baked Eggplant** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Baked Eggplant.

Topped with sliced vegetables and a healthy coating of melted Garlean cheese, these rich eggplants have been roasted to tender perfection.
