**Deviled Eggs** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Deviled Eggs.

Boiled eggs stuffed with creamed egg yolk and fish paste.

Ingredients used: Ala Mhigan Mustard, Apkallu Egg, Highland Parsley, Paprika, Sun Lemon
