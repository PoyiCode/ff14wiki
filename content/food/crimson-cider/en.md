**Crimson Cider** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Crimson Cider.

Official beverage of both the Ala Mhigan Resistance and the Doman Liberation Front, or so cider brewers would have you believe.

Ingredients used: Crimson Pepper, Cumin Seeds, Jhammel Ginger, Kudzu Root, Loquat
