**Loaghtan Cordon Bleu** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Loaghtan Cordon Bleu.

Lean loaghtan chops sliced horizontally, stuffed with a thick slice of cheese, breaded, and fried to a golden brown.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Highland Flour, Loaghtan Fillet, Mature Olive Oil, Smoked Bacon, Stone Cheese
