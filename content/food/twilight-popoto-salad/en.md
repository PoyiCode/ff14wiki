**Twilight Popoto Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Twilight Popoto Salad.

Shredded twilight apples give this popoto salad a crunchy texture and fruity tang.

Ingredients used: Frantoio Oil, Lorikeet Egg, Purple Carrot, Ramhorn Zucchini, Russet Popoto, Twilight Apple
