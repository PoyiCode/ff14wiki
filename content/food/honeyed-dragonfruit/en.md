**Honeyed Dragonfruit** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Honeyed Dragonfruit.

A generous serving of dragonfruit slathered with a silky sweet layer of golden honey and a few sour drops of lemonette juice.
