**Stuffed Artichoke** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Stuffed Artichoke.

A roasted artichoke stuffed with a savory blend of pungent cheese and succulent crabmeat.

Ingredients used: Black Pepper, Blue Cheese, Maiden Artichoke, Megalocrab Leg, Sweet Cream
