summary: "A dish of Eorzea."
---

**Emerald Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Emerald Soup.

A bright-green soup made by cooking down emerald beans until they break down into a ghastly paste. Despised by children both high- and lowborn.
