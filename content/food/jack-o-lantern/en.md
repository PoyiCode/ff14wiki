**Jack-o'-lantern** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Jack-o'-lantern.

An ogre pumpkin carved into a grinning face and boiled in a sweet sauce.

Ingredients used: Maple Syrup, Mineral Water, Ogre Pumpkin
