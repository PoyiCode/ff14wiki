**Loaghtan Steak** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Loaghtan Steak.

A tough cut of loaghtan meat soaked in a marinade of paprika, sherry, and thyme and grilled over an open flame.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Cooking Sherry, Dravanian Paprika, Fermented Butter, Loaghtan Fillet, Thyme
