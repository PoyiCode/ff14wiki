**Honey Muffin** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Honey Muffin.

A traditional Midlander cake made with a generous portion of honey.

Ingredients used: Aldgoat Milk, Chicken Egg, Honey, Mineral Water, Smooth Butter, Sunset Wheat Flour
