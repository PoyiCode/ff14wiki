**Lomo Saltado** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Lomo Saltado.

Stir-fried strips of marinated rroneek shoulder are served with popotoes in this spicy home-cooked favorite.

Ingredients used: Aji Amarillo, Giant Popoto, Night Vinegar, Rroneek Chuck, Turali Corn Oil
