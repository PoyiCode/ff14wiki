**Coconut Cod Chowder** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Coconut Cod Chowder.

A Near Eastern soup, brimming with shallows cod simmered in silky coconut milk.

Ingredients used: Blood Tomato, Carrot of Happiness, Coconut Milk, Thavnairian Paprika
