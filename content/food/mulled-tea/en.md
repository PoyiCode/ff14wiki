**Mulled Tea** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mulled Tea.

A pungent infusion brewed from several exotic spices and herbs. Enjoyed in the sultanate of Ul'dah.

Ingredients used: Cinnamon, Cloves, Mineral Water, Pearl Ginger, Thanalan Tea Leaves
