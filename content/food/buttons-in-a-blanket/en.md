**Buttons in a Blanket** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Buttons in a Blanket.

Button mushrooms wrapped in parboiled cabbage and stewed in a savory soup.

Ingredients used: Button Mushroom, Desert Saffron, Midland Cabbage, Ruby Tomato, Table Salt, Tomato Sauce
