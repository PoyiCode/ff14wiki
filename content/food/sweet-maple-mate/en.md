**Sweet Maple Mate** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sweet Maple Mate.

This sweet drink, brewed from the finest mate leaves, is a firm favorite among peddlers. A single sip is enough to vanquish even the most deep-seated fatigue.

Ingredients used: Coconut Milk, Lemonette, Royal Maple Syrup, Splendid Mate Leaves
