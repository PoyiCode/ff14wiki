**Gateau au Chocolat** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Gateau au Chocolat.

This decadently moist cake is made with downright *sinful* amounts of kukuru bean powder.

Ingredients used: Egg of Elpis, Fermented Butter, Palm Sugar, Sweet Kukuru Bean, Upland Wheat Flour, Whipped Cream
