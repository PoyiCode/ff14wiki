**Golden Pineapple Juice** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Golden Pineapple Juice.

Tart and invigorating juice freshly squeezed from golden pineapples.

Ingredients used: Amber Cloves, Golden Honey, Golden Pineapple, Lemonette, Yak Milk
