**Boiled Bream** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Boiled Bream.

A whole bream boiled until thoroughly cooked, and then covered in a thick sauce.

Ingredients used: Garlean Garlic, Midland Cabbage, Olive Oil, Wild Onion, Wizard Eggplant
