**Amra Lassi** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Amra Lassi.

A blend of yoghurt and spices, this refreshing drink is infused with the mellow sweetness of the amra fruit.

Ingredients used: Amra, Ovibos Milk, Palm Sugar, Peppermint
