**Dragonfruit Blend** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Dragonfruit Blend.

Mashed dragonfruit is blended together with cloud banana and coconut milk to create this creamy, refreshing drink.

Ingredients used: Cloud Banana, Coconut Milk, Dragonfruit, Garden Beet Sugar, Ovibos Milk
