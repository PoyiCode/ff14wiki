**Persimmon Pudding** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Persimmon Pudding.

A rich steamed dessert made with puréed persimmons and eggs.

Ingredients used: Beet Sugar, Gagana Egg, Gyr Abanian Flour, Nutmeg, Persimmon, Steppe Milk
