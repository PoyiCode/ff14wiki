**Glory Be Soup** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Glory Be Soup.

This stew, consisting of lentils and chestnuts boiled in red wine, derives its name from Garlean soldiers singing the praises of Emperor Solus, who shared a warm meal with them after a round of military exercises one chilly night.

Ingredients used: Eblan Lentils, Gridanian Chestnut, Night Pepper, Peppermint, Perilla Oil
