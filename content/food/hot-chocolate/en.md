title: "Hot Chocolate"
summary: "A dish of Eorzea."
---

**Hot Chocolate** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Hot Chocolate.

A sweet beverage commonly drunk in the Holy See, but rarely seen in Foundation due to inability of most of Ishgard's poorer residents to procure the relatively rare and expensive ingredients.

Ingredients used: Birch Syrup, Heavenly Kukuru Powder, Yak Milk
