**Roast Canard** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Roast Canard.

A canard breast roasted to perfection, drowned in a sweet and sour sauce made from two kinds of oranges.

Ingredients used: Blood Orange, Canard Breast, Honey, La Noscean Orange, Popoto, Smooth Butter
