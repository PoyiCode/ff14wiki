**Sunset Carrot Nibbles** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sunset Carrot Nibbles.

Ribbons of thinly sliced sunset carrots dressed with vinegar make for a healthful and serenely wholesome dish.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Cucumber, Night Vinegar, Perilla Oil, Sunset Carrot
