**Prune-packed Fruitcake** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Prune-packed Fruitcake.

Dried plums steeped in liquor are stirred into the batter to create this wonderfully rich, fruit-laden cake.
