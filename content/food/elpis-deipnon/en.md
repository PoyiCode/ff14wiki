**Elpis Deipnon** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Elpis Deipnon.

This hearty dish takes ground bird of Elpis meat and sears it to tender, juicy perfection.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Bird of Elpis Breast, Blood Tomato, Dark Rye Flour, Northern Sea Salt, Upland Wheat Flour
