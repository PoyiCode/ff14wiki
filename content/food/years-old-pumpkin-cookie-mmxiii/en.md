**Years-old Pumpkin Cookie MMXIII** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Years-old Pumpkin Cookie MMXIII.

A devilish imp is thought to have made this slightly burnt biscuit shaped like a pumpkin head nigh on a decade ago. One can only wonder what ingredients it contains.
