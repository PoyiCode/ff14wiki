**Creamy Alpaca Pasta** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Creamy Alpaca Pasta.

The rustic handmade noodles are boiled until tender, then tossed in a pan with grilled alpaca fillet and broccoli florets in a cream sauce.

Ingredients used: Alpaca Fillet, Broccoli, Dark Rye Flour, Egg of Elpis, Frantoio Oil, Whipped Cream
