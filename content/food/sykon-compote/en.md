title: "Sykon Compote"
summary: "A dish of Eorzea."
---

**Sykon Compote** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sykon Compote.

Sykon are boiled in sugar water to create these sweet, bite-sized treats.

Ingredients used: Lemonette, Palm Sugar, Sour Red, Sykon
