**Roast Chicken** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Roast Chicken.

Roasted to crispy perfection, then flavored with garlic and a hint of lemon, a single scent of this dish is enough to make the mouth water.
