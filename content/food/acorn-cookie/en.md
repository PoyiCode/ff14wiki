**Acorn Cookie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Acorn Cookie.

A crisp cookie made with iron acorn paste. A favorite of the Qiqirn.

Ingredients used: Iron Acorn, Mineral Water, Rye Flour, Table Salt
