**Shepherd's Pie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Shepherd's Pie.

Pulled antelope shank covered with mashed popotoes and baked in a golden, buttery pie crust.

Ingredients used: Antelope Shank, Black Pepper, Coerthan Carrot, Popoto, Smooth Butter, Wild Onion
