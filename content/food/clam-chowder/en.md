**Clam Chowder** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Clam Chowder.

A milk-based soup made with various shellfish, but most often clams.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Bouillon Cube, Chives
