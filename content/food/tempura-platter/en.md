**Tempura Platter** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Tempura Platter.

A selection of seafood and vegetables dipped in batter and lightly fried in oil. A Hingan delicacy.

Ingredients used: Cottonseed Oil, Gagana Egg, Gyr Abanian Flour, Lotus Root
