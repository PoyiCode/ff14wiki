**Mezcal-marinated Swampmonk** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Mezcal-marinated Swampmonk.

Tender swampmonk thigh soaked overnight in mezcal, then grilled over an open flame.

Ingredients used: Cooking Mezcal, Mountain Salt, Perilla Oil, Swampmonk Thigh
