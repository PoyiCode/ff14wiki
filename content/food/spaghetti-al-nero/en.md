**Spaghetti al Nero** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Spaghetti al Nero.

A tangy dish of thin noodles tossed with squid ink and blood tomato sauce that has the unfortunate side effect of turning your teeth jet black.
