**Espresso con Panna** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Espresso con Panna.

The surprising addition of sour cream may catch some off guard, but they will invariably be won over by the subtle harmonies of this rich blend.

Ingredients used: Cinnamon, Coffee Beans, Garden Beet Sugar, Sour Cream
