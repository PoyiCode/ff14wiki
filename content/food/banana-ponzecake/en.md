**Banana Ponzecake** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Banana Ponzecake.

An otherwise ordinary ponzecake, infused with ripe bananas and aloe for a soft, moist texture—and a *heavenly* aroma while baking.

Ingredients used: Egg of Elpis, Royal Maple Syrup, Turali Aloe, Upland Wheat Flour, Wild Ja Tiika Bananas
