**Spicy Shakshouka** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Spicy Shakshouka.

A hearty and delicious dish for any mealtime, its base is a flavorful, rich tomato sauce filled with potent spices, in which an egg has been perfectly poached.
