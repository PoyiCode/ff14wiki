**Scrambled Eggs** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Scrambled Eggs.

A breakfast dish consisting of several puk eggs scrambled and fried with white truffles.

Ingredients used: Cottage Cheese, Puk Egg, Table Salt, White Truffle
