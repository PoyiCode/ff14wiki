**Grilled Sweetfish** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Grilled Sweetfish.

A simple dish created by piercing a lightly salted fresh sweetfish with a sharpened stick and slowly roasting it over an open flame.
