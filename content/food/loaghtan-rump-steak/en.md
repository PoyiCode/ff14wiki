**Loaghtan Rump Steak** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Loaghtan Rump Steak.

If this loaghtan had a heart as tender as its rump, its passing was a tragedy indeed.

Ingredients used: Night Pepper, Night Vinegar, Perilla Oil, Prime Loaghtan Rump, Thavnairian Paprika
