**Apple Strudel** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Apple Strudel.

A handful of chopped apples glazed with birch syrup are thinly wrapped in a warm flaky crust, topped with just a sprinkling of cinnamon.

Ingredients used: Birch Syrup, Cinnamon, Fermented Butter, Golden Apple, Highland Flour, Vanilla Beans
