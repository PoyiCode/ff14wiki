**Sykon Salad** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sykon Salad.

The sykon serves as the star of this fruit-filled dish, complex and nutritious.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Blood Tomato, Cucumber, Iceberg Lettuce, Sykon, Thavnairian Paprika
