**Forest Miq'abob** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Forest Miq'abob.

A traditional Keeper of the Moon dish consisting of freshly picked mushrooms on a stick, roasted to perfection.

Ingredients used: Aloe, Black Pepper, Button Mushroom, Lavender Oil, Ruby Tomato, Table Salt
