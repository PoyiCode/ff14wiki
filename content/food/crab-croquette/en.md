**Crab Croquette** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Crab Croquette.

Rich, buttery crab legs blended with fluffy mashed vegetables, rolled into a ball, covered in bread crumbs, and fried to a crispy, golden brown. Best served slathered in a sweet sauce.
