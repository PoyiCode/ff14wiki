**Sauerkraut** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Sauerkraut.

Chopped midland cabbage pickled with salt. A staple on all Lominsan trade ships, as it is said to prevent the scurvy.

Ingredients used: Dragon Pepper, Laurel, Midland Cabbage, Mineral Water, Table Salt
