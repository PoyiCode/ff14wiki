**Herring Pie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Herring Pie.

Chunks of seasoned herring baked into a flaky crust that's been shaped like a fish so as to inform the eater to expect savory instead of sweet.

Ingredients used: Creamtop Mushroom, Mist Spinach, Upland Wheat Flour
