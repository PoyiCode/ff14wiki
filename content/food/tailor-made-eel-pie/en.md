**Tailor-made Eel Pie** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Tailor-made Eel Pie.

The acetic odor emitted by this specially crafted pastry will turn away all but the most loyal of eel pie enthusiasts.
