**Cockatrice Meatballs** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Cockatrice Meatballs.

Ground lean cockatrice meat hand rolled into bite-sized balls and fried in oil.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Cockatrice Thigh, Cyclops Onion, Tomato Sauce
