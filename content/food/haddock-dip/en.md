**Haddock Dip** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Haddock Dip.

A thick salty dip made from a purée of haddock, raw egg, and mashed popotoes. A favorite of Roegadyn tribes hailing from the north seas.
