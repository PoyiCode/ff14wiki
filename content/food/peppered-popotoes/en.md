**Peppered Popotoes** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Peppered Popotoes.

A pleasing plate of popotoes peppered and prepared to perfection, playing pleasurably on the palate.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt, Curly Parsley, Night Pepper, Russet Popoto
