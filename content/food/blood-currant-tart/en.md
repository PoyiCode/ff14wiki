**Blood Currant Tart** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Blood Currant Tart.

A sweet and sour pastry made with a generous portion of blood currants.

Ingredients used: Blood Currants, Gelatin, Maple Sugar, Pie Dough, Smooth Butter
