**Pineapple Orange Jelly** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pineapple Orange Jelly.

Made from Turali pineapples and navel oranges, the refreshing flavor of this jelly will remind you of Tural no matter where you are.

Ingredients used: Gelatin, Navel Orange, Royal Maple Syrup, Turali Pineapple
