**Silkie Pudding** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Silkie Pudding.

The distinctively smooth texture and rich taste of this lightly sweet, boiled dessert will have your mouth watering.

Ingredients used: Garden Beet Sugar, Lemonette, Nutmeg, Silkie Egg, Yak Milk
