**Grilled Platinum Bream** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Grilled Platinum Bream.

A single platinum bream, seasoned simply with rock salt and grilled to a golden brown to seal in all the juices.

Ingredients used: Abalathian Rock Salt
