**Creamy Hot Chocolate** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Creamy Hot Chocolate.

Brewed with cocoa powder created from sweet kukuru beans, this warm winter beverage is made all the sweeter with a dollop of whipped cream on top.

Ingredients used: Ovibos Milk, Palm Sugar, Sweet Kukuru Bean, Whipped Cream
