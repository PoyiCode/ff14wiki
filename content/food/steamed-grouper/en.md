**Steamed Grouper** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Steamed Grouper.

A large Zekki grouper steamed whole, then doused in fragrant boiling oil.

Ingredients used: Cooking Sake, Cottonseed Oil, Green Leek, Jhammel Ginger, Oriental Soy Sauce
