**Pumpkin Potage** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pumpkin Potage.

A creamy soup that highlights the rich and nutty flavor of the giant pumpkin.

Ingredients used: Alien Onion, Giant Pumpkin, Northern Sea Salt, Ovibos Milk, Potent Spice
