**Pasta Ortolano** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Pasta Ortolano.

A plate of sautéed eggplant and zucchini mixed into soft pasta and drizzled with a rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients used: Dravanian Paprika, Ramhorn Zucchini, Solstice Garlic, Tomato Sauce, Vermicelli, Wizard Eggplant
