**Rock-fisted Popoto Stew** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Rock-fisted Popoto Stew.

Based on a traditional Alexandrian recipe that does *not* call for the addition of any oglops, despite what some may have pondered.
