**Bubble Chocolate** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Bubble Chocolate.

A luscious bonbon made from dark chocolate infused with lighter-than-air bubbles.

Ingredients used: Buffalo Milk, Kukuru Butter, Kukuru Powder, Maple Sugar
