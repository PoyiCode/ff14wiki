**White Chocolate** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: White Chocolate.

A heart-shaped morsel of pure white chocolate. Those adventurers allergic to normal chocolate can rest at ease, for this silky smooth confectionary does not actually contain any kukuru powder.
