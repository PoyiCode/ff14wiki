title: "Gameni"

---

**Gameni** is a dish residents cook and eat — served at taverns and home tables across Eorzea. In-game name: Gameni.

A hodgepodge of boldly sliced tubers, mushrooms, and hornbill tenders, stewed slowly in a soy-based broth.

Ingredients used: Bamboo Shoot, Gyr Abanian Carrot, Hornbill Tenderloin, Lotus Root, Oriental Soy Sauce, Shiitake Mushroom
